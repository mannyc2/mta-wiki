B47 bus

route_b47-grand-ave-2024

The B47 bus is one of ten bus routes that operates out of the MTA Grand Av Bus Depot in Queens. listed as one of 10 routes from the depot The depot serves 1,032 buses pulling in and out every weekday, including over 80 movements during the busiest hour. 1,032 buses, 80+ movements Truck traffic and congestion from the Grand St Bridge cause significant operational difficulties for pull-in and pull-out, resulting in longer wait times and less reliable service for riders. operational difficulties from truck traffic and Grand St Bridge congestion Improvements along Grand Avenue that prioritize bus service and depot access are expected to deliver more reliable, faster buses in an area that lacks direct subway service. area lacking direct subway service

The same findings appear consistently in presentations delivered to Queens Community Board 4 on November 26, 2024, Queens Community Board 5 on December 17, 2024, and Brooklyn Community Board 1 on November 12, 2024, as part of the Grand St/Grand Av Safety & Bus Priority Improvements initiative by NYC DOT and MTA.
