The Q47 is a Queens bus route that served LaGuardia Airport as of June 2011, one of five bus routes (alongside the M60, Q33, Q48, and Q72) serving the airport at that time LGA bus routes 2011. At a November 2, 2011 public workshop for the LaGuardia Airport Access Alternatives Analysis—a study led by NYCDOT that had selected Bus Rapid Transit as the locally preferred mode—participants raised several concerns about the Q47 Public Meeting #2 date and format BRT selected as locally preferred mode.
