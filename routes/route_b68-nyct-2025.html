B68 Bus Route

route_b68-nyct-2025

The B68 is a Brooklyn bus route operating on Coney Island Avenue and Brighton Beach Avenue ACE lists B68 on Coney Island Av / Brighton Beach Av. On December 8, 2025, the MTA expanded the Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program to include the B68, along with the B60 in Brooklyn and the M57 in Manhattan, with a 60-day warning phase during which vehicles blocking bus lanes, bus stops, or double-parked would receive warning notices instead of fines December 8, 2025 ACE expansion to B68, B60, M57. The ACE program is administered jointly by the MTA, the New York City Department of Finance, and the New York City Department of Transportation ACE partnership description. The cited ACE program page describes coverage on 60 bus routes 60 routes and benefits for over one million average weekday riders 1000000 riders ACE active on 60 routes, over 1M riders.

The B68 joins 51 Brooklyn and citywide routes already covered by ACE as of late 2025 51 routes already covered by ACE, B68 among three new additions. During the 60-day warning phase, motorists receive educational notices; fine-bearing violations begin after the warning period ends 60-day warning notices before fines begin. ACE violations carry escalating civil penalties starting at $50 and increasing by $50 per violation up to a maximum of $250, with each violation reviewed by a city employee before issuance ACE fine structure and city employee review.
