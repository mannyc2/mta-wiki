QM12 Express Bus Route

route_qm12-qbb-study

The QM12 Express Bus Route was included in the Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study conducted by NYC DOT and presented to local Community Boards in spring-summer 2010 The Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study. The study documented a daily ridership of 220 passengers on the QM12, contributing to the total of 7,920 daily express bus riders on all routes using the Queensboro Bridge Express Bus Routes table — a figure that can be related to the Express Bus Total Daily Ridership on QBB 7920 riders per day metric. A map of the Third Avenue and 57th Street intersection shows a bus stop serving QM12 together with QM4, QM10, and QM24 Third Avenue at 57th Street map.

The study identified five focus areas, including Manhattan-side locations near 60th Street and 2nd Avenue (bus safety) and 57th Street and 3rd Avenue (bus priority opportunity) Study Focus Areas. The study schedule ran from spring 2010 data collection through fall 2011 implementation of short-term recommendations Spring 2010 data collection Fall 2011 implementation, with Recommendations #1-4 implemented in fall 2011 Recommendations #1-4 implementation. Recommendation #5, which examined express bus lane options on the Thomson Avenue ramps, remained pending additional analysis by NYC DOT Recommendation #5.

The QM12 operates between Manhattan and Queens as express bus service Third Avenue at 57th Street map and was one of several QM-series express routes evaluated in the study, along with QM1, QM1A, QM2, QM2A, QM3, QM4, QM10, QM15, QM16, QM17, QM18, QM21, and QM24 Express Bus Routes table.
