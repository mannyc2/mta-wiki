B24

route_b24

B24 is a Williamsburg Bridge Plaza-bound local bus route serving Brooklyn along the Marcy Avenue corridor. Its average daily ridership was 2,359 in 2024 CB1 June 2025 presentation. The B24 is one of three bus routes — along with the B60 and Q54 — that converge on Marcy Av between Borinquen Pl and Broadway, where each route experiences delays and slower speeds along this tail-end stretch; the delays compound and result in buses arriving late to their terminal, delaying the next scheduled run CB1 June 2025 presentation. The BQE North and South (2024) study recommended providing a bus priority lane to alleviate these delays CB1 June 2025 presentation.

Traffic congestion along this segment is common, especially on weekday late afternoons before the PM rush hour lane along the east curb goes into effect CB1 June 2025 presentation. Buses must also navigate a very tight right turn when continuing from southbound Marcy Av onto westbound Broadway, competing for space with other turning vehicles and improperly stopped traffic on the eastbound side CB1 June 2025 presentation. On the west side of Marcy Av between S 4 St and S 5 St, multiple vehicles load and perform deliveries daily while occupying the bus stop for extended periods CB1 June 2025 presentation.

A Bus Priority Street Improvement Project (SIP ID: 12263) was presented to Community Board 1's Transportation Committee in June 2025 and re-presented with a detailed design in September 2025 CB1 June 2025 presentation. The September 2025 design proposes a bus priority lane on Marcy Av from Borinquen Pl to Broadway (all hours), a travel lane widened to facilitate safer turns for trucks and buses, the bus lane merging into the general travel lane, a new Neighborhood Loading Zone, and a part-time parking lane replaced with a full-time general travel lane CB1 September 2025 pavement markings drawing CB1 September 2025 treatment summary.
