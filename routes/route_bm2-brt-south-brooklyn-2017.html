The BM2 Express is an MTA Bus Company-operated express route running between Canarsie-Avenue H in Brooklyn and Midtown/Downtown Manhattan BM2 service description. Along Pennsylvania Ave, the BM2 is one of five bus routes (B82 Local, B82 Limited, B83, BM2, BM5) serving a corridor with slow bus speeds, long pedestrian crossings, and narrow concrete medians Pennsylvania Ave corridor conditions. The BM2 is also a secondary route on Flatbush Ave, providing connections to midtown Manhattan alongside the B41, B9, B67, B103, and Q35 Flatbush Ave secondary routes.

In Summer 2024, the BM2 was among six express bus routes selected for frequency increases in anticipation of Congestion Pricing, with one Manhattan-bound trip added in the AM peak at an annualized cost of $254,000 for MTA Bus routes BM2 summer 2024 trip addition MTA Bus cost detail. In Spring 2025, under a broader congestion-pricing service enhancement package, the BM2 again received one additional Manhattan-bound AM peak trip as part of frequency increases on eight express bus routes BM2 spring 2025 trip addition.
