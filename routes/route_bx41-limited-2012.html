The Bx41 Limited was a limited-stop bus route operating on Webster Avenue between Melrose Avenue and East Gun Hill Road in the Bronx Bx41 Limited stops on Webster Avenue. It served as the limited-stop variant of the Bx41 Limited alongside the Bx41 local route, and also shared the corridor with the Bx55 Limited north of Fordham Plaza Existing Bx41 LTD listed among Webster Avenue routes. The Bx41 Limited was replaced by Bx41 Select Bus Service (SBS) on June 30, 2013, while the Bx41 local route remained unchanged Bx41 SBS replaces the Bx41 Limited Bx41 SBS launched on June 30, 2013. The SBS replacement featured half-mile stop spacing, off-board fare collection, and service running frequently all day Bx41 LTD to SBS conversion details.

Prior to replacement, the Bx41 Limited operated only on weekdays during peak hours on 12-minute headways Bx41 Limited operated weekdays peak hours on 12-minute headway. After the SBS launch, the Bx41 SBS operated 19 to 23 percent faster than the Limited service it replaced Bx41 SBS 19-23% faster than Bx41 Limited.
