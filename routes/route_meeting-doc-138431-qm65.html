QM65

route_meeting-doc-138431-qm65

The QM65 is a proposed new express bus route introduced as part of the Queens Bus Network Redesign, operating from southeast Queens to downtown Manhattan QM65 proposed new express route. Alongside the QM65, proposed express-network changes included the discontinuation of the QM3, plus other routing, bus stop, and frequency adjustments QM3 discontinuation. The route is documented in an MTA Board Action Request staff summary dated April 4, 2024, which sought authorization to hold a public hearing on the Queens Bus Network Redesign April 4, 2024 staff summary.

The Queens Bus Network Redesign process included an Existing Conditions report in August 2019 August 2019 Existing Conditions report, an Original Draft Plan in December 2019 that generated Over 11,000 comments on Original Draft Plan 11000 before the COVID-19 pandemic paused the project over 11,000 comments on Original Draft, a New Draft Plan in March 2022 New Draft Plan March 2022, and a Proposed Final Plan in December 2023 Proposed Final Plan December 2023. The MTA partnered with NYC DOT to identify 24 priority corridors in Queens identified for bus priority 24 for potential bus priority improvements 24 priority corridors identified. A phased implementation was targeted for mid-2025, with a Proposed Final Plan Addendum to follow the public hearing and precede the required MTA Board vote mid-2025 phased implementation Proposed Final Plan Addendum.
