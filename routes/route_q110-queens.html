Q110 bus route

route_q110-queens

The Q110 bus route in Southeast Queens is described in the Queens Bus Network Redesign equity evaluation as a route that has been streamlined for more direct routing More Direct Routing. The existing Q110 connects passengers from Parsons Boulevard in Jamaica to Belmont Park Q110 existing routing. Under the Proposed Final Plan Addendum, the Q110 is rerouted and extended to Floral Park, offering a more direct path and expanded coverage Q110 redesign proposal. This change would add a connection to the Queens Village LIRR station Queens Village LIRR connection. The redesign serves an area where 94% of the population is non-white and 40% is transit dependent, and is intended to enhance travel efficiency in this Southeast Queens corridor Southeast Queens demographics. Passengers on the discontinued portion of Hempstead Avenue would be served by the new Q82 route, ensuring continuity of service Q82 replacement service.
