Bx20

route_bx20-ace

The Bx20 is a local MTA bus route that operates on Broadway and Henry Hudson Parkway in the Bronx and Manhattan Bx20 listed as ACE route with major streets. The route serves the Broadway corridor from approximately 157th Street to 220th Street, alongside the Bx7, M5, M4, and M100 Bx20 identified among Broadway corridor bus routes. These routes connect to the 1, A, and C subway lines and Metro-North Railroad routes connect to 1 A C and Metro North.

Weekday ridership in 2018 was 813, based on New York City Transit data cited in the NYC DOT Better Buses Broadway Improvements presentation to Community Board 8 Bx20: 813 2018 weekday ridership. Across all five bus routes operating on the corridor, the Broadway study area carries over 15,000 daily riders 15000 riders between 171st and 186th streets, where speeds are lowest—as slow as 4 MPH ridership and speed data for Broadway between 171st and 186th St.

The NYC Department of Transportation is pursuing bus and safety improvements on Broadway from 157th Street to 220th Street, a Tier 1 Priority Investment Area under the NYC Streets Plan Broadway bus priority corridor context. Existing conditions were presented to Community Board 12 in March 2025, with DOT and MTA planning to develop a design proposal with continued community feedback next steps for Broadway corridor improvements. The Bx20 is also listed on the MTA's automated camera enforcement (ACE) page as a route where bus lane and bus stop camera enforcement applies Bx20 on ACE route list.
