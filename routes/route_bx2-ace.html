The Bx2 is a Bronx bus route listed on Grand Concourse in the Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program ACE route list. The route is one of 60 bus routes in the ACE program, a bus-mounted camera system that issues civil penalties to vehicles blocking bus lanes, bus stops, or double-parked along bus routes ACE program description. The ACE program, administered jointly by the MTA, NYC Department of Finance, and NYC Department of Transportation, expanded through 2023 state legislation and benefits over one million average weekday riders across all participating routes over 1M weekday riders 1000000 riders ACE program scope. On each ACE route, including the Bx2, the MTA and City issue warning notices for 60 days before fine-bearing violations begin 60-day warning period.
