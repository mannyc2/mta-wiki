Q20A/B is a local bus route in Queens running from Jamaica to College Point via Main Street Route description. The Q20A/B operated on Main Street together with the Q44 limited-stop service, and both were characterized by "long and slow bus trips" in the Flushing-to-Jamaica corridor identified in early BRT studies Corridor context. Combined weekday ridership for the Q20 was 13,609 with a peak frequency of 12 minutes Ridership data.

When the Q44 limited was upgraded to Q44 Select Bus Service, the Q20A/B local service continued in its existing pattern to maintain local stop coverage Service plan. All bus stops removed from the Q44 SBS route within Community Board 7 would still be served by the Q20 Stop coverage. The route was also part of earlier Jamaica Bus Improvement Study proposals, with a shared bus stop for Q20A/Q20B/Q44 and Q43 at Hillside Avenue and Sutphin Boulevard identified for potential reconfiguration Bus stop location.
