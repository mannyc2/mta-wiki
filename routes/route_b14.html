B14

route_b14

The B14 is a Brooklyn local bus route operating along Eastern Parkway that has been the focus of two related NYC DOT transit priority proposals. An Eastern Parkway Transit Priority Proposal covering the segment from Schenectady Avenue to Ralph Avenue was presented to Brooklyn Community Board 8 in March and April 2025 Eastern Parkway Transit Priority Proposal. Eastbound B14 operators have informally shifted to using the Eastern Parkway mainline instead of the service road to avoid traffic congestion and double parking at Utica Avenue, a change that has improved performance but was never formalized with updated signage B14 eastbound main line operations. As a result, B14 stops at Eastern Parkway Main Line/Utica Avenue and at the service road at Rochester Avenue and Buffalo Avenue operate informally and remain unsigned informal B14 stop locations.

Over 3,000 people board the B14 or the B17 at Eastern Parkway and Utica Avenue on a typical weekday, and bus service on Eastern Parkway dates to 1925 3,000+ weekday boardings at Utica Avenue. Under the proposal, B14 bus stops would be relocated to the southern pedestrian malls, landscaping would be replaced with hexagon or smooth granite pavers for ADA accessibility, and the Rochester Avenue stop would be removed in coordination with the MTA's Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Team coordination with Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Team.

A separate Linden Boulevard Traffic Safety and Bus Priority Improvements proposal presented to Brooklyn Community Board 5 in April 2026 also affects the B14. On the Linden Boulevard corridor, the proposal records a low-end bus-speed metric for existing conditions Linden Boulevard low-end bus speed 4 mph bus speeds as low as 4 mph. The proposal would place B14 service on Eldert Lane in both directions between Sutter Avenue and Stanley Avenue, replacing the current split routing that uses Linden Boulevard for only one block northbound B14 proposed route adjustment. New boarding island bus stops on medians are proposed at Euclid Avenue, Crescent Street, Eldert Lane, and 79th Street B14 routing and boarding island stops.
