Q3

route_meeting-doc-133291-q3

The Q3 is a local bus route in Queens. A February 2024 MTA staff summary proposes modifications to the weekday schedule of the Q3, along with the B82, Bx19, and Q29 Service Changes: Spring 2024 Bus Schedules. Under the proposal, the frequency of service on the Q3 would decrease and running time would increase Staff Summary discussion. The Q3 is operated by New York City Transit and, together with the B82 and Bx19, the combined cost impact is $89,400 Budget Impact. The proposed implementation date is April 2024 Proposed Implementation Date.

On the Q3, the Q3 AM Peak headway current - 8 min 8 minutes would improve to Q3 AM Peak headway proposed - 7 min 7 minutes, while the Q3 AM Peak capacity current - 75% 75 percent would decrease to Q3 AM Peak capacity proposed - 67% 67 percent Attachment 1 schedule table. Midday, PM Peak, and Evening headways and capacity percentages would remain unchanged Attachment 1 schedule table. The Q29, noted as having running time changes only, shows dashes across all table cells, indicating no headway or capacity changes in the table Attachment 1 schedule table Q29 footnote.
