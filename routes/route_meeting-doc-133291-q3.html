Service Changes: Spring 2024 Bus Schedules and Terminal Stop

The Q3 is a local bus route in Queens. A February 2024 MTA staff summary proposes modifications to the weekday schedule of the Q3, along with the B82, Bx19, and Q29 Service Changes: Spring 2024 Bus Schedules. Under the proposal, the frequency of service on the Q3 would decrease and running time would increase Staff Summary discussion. The Q3 is operated by New York City Transit and, together with the B82 and Bx19, the combined cost impact is $89,400 Budget Impact. The proposed implementation date is April 2024 Proposed Implementation Date.
