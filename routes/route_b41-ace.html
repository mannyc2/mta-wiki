The Proposed B41 Local is the primary bus route on Flatbush Avenue, operating local and limited-stop service between Downtown Brooklyn and Kings Plaza/Bergen Beach B41 route description from kickoff meeting. Under the Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Draft Plan, it would be a 24-hour route with proposed frequencies of 8 minutes in the AM Peak, 10 minutes Midday, 8 minutes in the PM Peak, 10 minutes in Early Evening, and 20 minutes in Late Evening; improvements include more direct routing, improved stop spacing, improved frequency, and fewer route patterns with service only to Kings Plaza B41 proposed route improvements B41 proposed service hours and frequency.

Ridership on Proposed B41 Local was reported at 22,500 average daily riders in early 2023 presentations B41 ridership from CB14 meeting and over 28,000 average daily riders in mid-2024 materials B41 ridership from CB8 meeting. Nearly 40% of all B41 trips start, end, or travel through the Flatbush Avenue bus priority project area, and 80% of those trips begin or end south of Grand Army Plaza B41 trip patterns from CB8 June 2025. The route is part of the Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program on Flatbush Av / Livingston St B41 ACE route listing.

The Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project planning has occurred over several years with more than 45 community, stakeholder, and elected meetings Four years of outreach summary. The B63 route revision at Atlantic Avenue was designed to avoid blocking the more frequent B41 and B67 buses traveling straight in bus lanes along Flatbush Avenue B63 revision reasoning. The B41 also saw over a 3% increase in Customer Journey Time Performance (CJTP) as of mid-2023, one of two routes (along with the Q66) to achieve that benchmark B41 CJTP improvement from MTA operations report.

Planned treatments for the Flatbush Avenue corridor include center-running bus lanes with bus boarding islands that would benefit the B41 by providing physically separated space for buses in the middle of the roadway Center-running bus lane benefits described Center-running lanes allow buses to move freely. Construction is anticipated to begin in Spring 2026 with bus lanes opening in Late Fall 2026 Spring 2026 construction start Late Fall 2026 bus lane opening.
