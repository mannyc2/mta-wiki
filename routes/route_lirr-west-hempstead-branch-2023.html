West Hempstead Branch

route_lirr-west-hempstead-branch-2023

The West Hempstead Branch is a commuter rail branch operated by the MTA Long Island Rail Road LIRR. In December 2022, during Montauk Branch switch maintenance between Valley Stream and Rockville Centre, bus service replaced train service on the West Hempstead Branch for approximately 8 hours each overnight period from December 6-8 West Hempstead Branch bus replacement. In April 2023, Montauk Branch track maintenance between Valley Stream and Jamaica required West Hempstead Branch trains to operate on adjusted schedules West Hempstead adjusted schedules. During the Van Wyck Bridge replacement on weekends of April 15-16 and April 22-23, West Hempstead Branch trains originated and terminated at Grand Central Madison Van Wyck Bridge adjustment.

The LIRR Annual Operating Report for 2023 reports the West Hempstead Branch achieved an overall on-time performance of 95.8%, compared to 94.1% in 2022 and an annual goal of 98.4% West Hempstead OTP table. PM Peak performance was 81.8% in 2023, the lowest period, while Total Peak reached 86.1% West Hempstead OTP table. Off Peak Weekday OTP was 95.2% and Weekend OTP was 97.8% West Hempstead OTP table. The branch completed 99.6% of scheduled trips in 2023, consistent with the prior year Percent Scheduled Trips Completed. The system-wide LIRR Overall On Time Performance 2023 94 percent was 94.0%, meaning the West Hempstead Branch outperformed the system average System OTP table.
