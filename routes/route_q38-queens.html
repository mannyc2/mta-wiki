Q38 bus route

route_q38-queens

The Q38 bus route is a Queens bus route described in the redesign equity appendix as operating as a loop, with its start and end points approximately a third of a mile apart between Rego Park and Corona — neighborhoods classified within Tier 1 (Significant Equity Consideration) and Tier 2 (Moderate Equity Consideration) areas under the Queens Bus Network Redesign equity framework Q38 route split description.

Under the Proposed Final Plan Addendum of the Queens Bus Network Redesign, the Q38 is identified for a route-split treatment intended to enhance service reliability Route Improvements Summary table. The proposal would divide the existing Q38 loop into two separate routes. Service along 62/63 Drs, Penelope Avenue, Juniper Valley Road, and Metropolitan Avenue between Rego Park and Maspeth is proposed to be maintained Q38 route split description. The northern segment of the existing Q38 would be replaced by the proposed Q14, a new route that would also incorporate the northern portion of the existing Q23 in Corona and East Elmhurst and the existing Q39 on Forest Avenue, creating a new connection between East Elmhurst and Ridgewood and expanding access for residents in several Tier 1 block groups Q38 route split description.

The split-routes strategy, as applied to the Q38, is part of a broader set of redesign improvements aimed at addressing persistent service issues such as unreliable routes and confusing service patterns, with particular attention to moderate and significant equity areas Route Improvement Assessment methodology.
