The M16 Bus Route on 34th Street originated as the M16 local bus before being renamed to M34A Select Bus Service on November 13, 2011, for clearer passenger communication 34th Street SBS Fall 2011 Open Houses - Renaming. Service levels and route alignment remained the same after the rename Service levels and route unchanged. The route operates as a crosstown bus on 34th Street in Manhattan, running between the Port Authority Bus Terminal (Ninth Avenue/West 42nd Street) and Waterside Plaza (FDR Drive/25th Street), using 34th Street between Eighth/Ninth Avenues and the FDR Drive/Second Avenue area M16 route description.

The 34th Street corridor is approximately 2 miles long 2 miles, making a 4-mile round trip 4 miles across Manhattan Corridor length description. Improvements planned alongside the SBS launch included off-board fare collection and bus lane camera enforcement 2011 planned improvements - off-board fare collection and camera enforcement.
