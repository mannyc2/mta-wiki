MTA Bus Bx3

route_bx3

The MTA Bus Bx3 is a Bronx bus route operating on Sedgwick Avenue, University Avenue, and West 181st Street, identified as part of the Automated Camera Enforcement program ACE lists Bx3 with Sedgwick Av / University Av / W 181 St. It operates on the University Avenue corridor (Kingsbridge Road to Washington Bridge), where it and the Bx36 together carry 55,000 weekday riders and 12,700 weekday riders on University Avenue, with average bus speeds of 6.3 mph during the AM peak and 5.6 mph during the PM peak University Ave corridor data. The Bx3 also operates on the Washington Bridge / 181 St corridor (Amsterdam Avenue to University Avenue), one of five routes (Bx3, Bx11, Bx13, Bx35, Bx36) serving 111,400 combined weekday riders, with 36,800 crossing the bridge, where average bus speeds are 9.9 mph during the AM peak and 8.2 mph during the PM peak Washington Bridge corridor data. The 181st Street Busway, a half-mile bus priority corridor along 181st Street between Broadway and Amsterdam Avenue in Washington Heights, launched on April 26, 2021, serving five MTA bus routes including the Bx3 181st Street Busway launch. However, a bus rider survey conducted in April 2022 and presented in June 2022 reported that survey respondents indicated bus service on the Bx3 (along with the Bx11 and Bx13) remained slow and inadequate as a top challenge of the Busway survey comments about slow Bx3 service. Potential street treatments studied for University Avenue segments near the Bx3 route include bus lane reconfigurations and boarding islands aimed at increasing bus speeds University Ave south of Tremont treatment concept University Ave south of Tremont boarding treatment University Ave north of Tremont treatment concept University Ave north of Tremont boarding treatment.
