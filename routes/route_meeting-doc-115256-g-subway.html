G Subway Line

route_meeting-doc-115256-g-subway

The G Subway Line (G Subway Line) serves Brooklyn and Queens as the Crosstown Line. On June 29, 2023, President Richard Davey, Senior Vice President of Subways Demetrius Crichlow, and local officials announced at Hoyt-Schermerhorn Sts Station that weekend service would increase on the G, J, and M lines, and a second batch of R211 subway cars went into service the same day G/J/M weekend service announcement June 2023. Customer satisfaction on the G line reached 75% in June 2023, up 5% from May, the highest among subway lines cited in the same metrics report G line satisfaction June 2023 (see G line customer satisfaction June 2023 75 percent).

The G line has been positioned as a modernization benchmark. An September 2024 MTA Board Meeting slide described a "Model G Train Project" that "Sets a New Standard" Model G Train Project, and another presentation slide titled "Replicating G Train Success on the A Line" indicates the G's operational model is being extended to other routes Replicating G Train Success on the A Line. A Crosstown Line Signal Modernization project was underway, with G trains scheduled to resume service between Church Av and Court Sq starting September 3 at 5 AM Crosstown Line Signal Modernization.
