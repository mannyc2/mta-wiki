Far Rockaway Branch

route_lirr-far-rockaway-branch-2023

According to the LIRR 2023 Annual Operating Report, the Far Rockaway Branch posted an overall on-time performance of 96.6% for 2023, up from 95.3% in 2022, against an annual goal of 98.0% 2023 OTP data for Far Rockaway Branch. Peak-period OTP was 94.2% (Total Peak), while weekend OTP reached 96.6% Performance Summary table.

The branch's elevator availability was 98.9% in 2023, down from 99.4% in 2022 Elevator Availability table. Escalator availability was 92.2% in 2023, a decline from 93.2% in 2022, and was the lowest among LIRR branches with escalators Escalator Availability table.

During December 2023 weekends (December 2-3, 9-10, and 16-17), Far Rockaway Branch trains were diverted from Penn Station to Grand Central due to West Side Yard switch installation work December 2023 service adjustment notice. Similar approximately 48-hour diversions to Grand Central Madison occurred on February weekends in 2024 February 2024 diversion notice. In response to the 2025 Rockaway Line 17-week outage for the Rockaway Line Rehabilitation and Resiliency Project, LIRR discounted the peak ticket price 7 USD of $7.00 and the off-peak price 5 USD of $5.00 to a single discounted price 2.75 USD of $2.75 for trips to and from Far Rockaway Station, intended to allow A Train customers to use LIRR service Fare discount proposal.
