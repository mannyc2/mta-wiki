Update on Extending Transit's Reach Strategic Action Plan - Service Changes: Bus Service EnhancementsImproving Bicycle, Pedestrian & Micromobility Access to MTA

The Bx23 is a local bus route operated by MTA Bus Company (MTABC) connecting Co-op City with the Pelham Bay Park Station in the Bronx Bx23 service description. In a 2023 update on the Extending Transit's Reach Strategic Action Plan, MTA reported that the Bx23 was one of four local bus routes equipped with front-of-bus bike racks, joining the S53, S93, and Q50 front-of-bus bike racks on local routes. A separate October 2022 presentation on bicycle, pedestrian, and micromobility access confirmed that permanent bike racks had been installed on the same four bus routes crossing MTA bridges permanent bike racks installed.

As part of a congestion pricing-related service enhancement proposal, the Bx23 was identified for running time adjustments among a package covering eight express bus routes and sixteen local bus routes/route combinations service enhancement recommendation Bx23 running time adjustments.
