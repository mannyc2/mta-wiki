Long Island Rail Road Annual Operating Report 2023

The Oyster Bay Branch is an LIRR branch line whose on-time performance (OTP) data appears in the MTA Long Island Rail Road's 2023 Annual Operating Report Annual Operating Report 2023. For the year through December 2023, the branch reported an overall OTP of 94.1%, compared to 94.3% in 2022, against an annual goal of 95.5% Oyster Bay Branch OTP table. Peak-period performance was notably lower than the overall figure: the AM peak registered 92.5% and the PM peak 91.7%, while off-peak weekday service achieved 95.2% and weekend service 95.1% Oyster Bay Branch OTP table.
