BM4

route_bm4-church-ave-cb12

The BM4 is an express bus route that provides direct service along Ocean Avenue between Brooklyn and Midtown & Downtown Manhattan, as documented in NYC DOT's 2019 Church Ave Transit & Traffic Improvements presentations to Community Boards 12 and 14 BM3 & BM4 express buses. The route is listed alongside the BM3 express bus and the B35 and B103 local/limited routes in materials describing current service on the Church Ave corridor BM3 & BM4 express buses.

The Church Ave corridor (Flatbush Ave to Ocean Parkway) was identified by NYC DOT as a 2019 priority project due to slow bus speeds of 4.25 mph during peak (compared to a 6.7 mph Brooklyn average) and high combined ridership of 45,000 daily riders across all routes Church Ave 2019 priority project. Along this corridor, bus passengers make up the dominant traveler share during both the AM and PM peaks AM peak bus traveler share 72 percent PM peak bus traveler share 69 percent Bus passengers 72% AM, 69% PM peak. The BM4 contributes to this ridership as one of the express bus options serving the corridor via Ocean Avenue BM3 & BM4 express buses.

The treatment components proposed for the Church Ave project — curbside bus lanes, loading zone adjustments, and metered parking changes — focused on the local B35 route rather than the BM4, but the project materials consistently reference the BM4 as part of the existing transit network along the corridor Curbside bus lanes.
