The BX19 is a local Bronx bus route operating along Southern Boulevard and East 149th Street, as listed by the MTA's Automated Camera Enforcement program Bx19, Southern Blvd / E 149 St. It is included in the ACE Routes Dataset with route_id "BX19" item BX19.

The BX19 was one of 21 routes on which Automated Bus Lane Enforcement (ABLE) mobile cameras operated through 2023 ABLE cameras operated on 21 routes through 2023, alongside routes such as B25, B26, B42, B62, BX12-SBS, BX35, BX36, BX41-SBS, M14-SBS, M15-SBS, M23-SBS, M34-SBS, M86-SBS, Q43, Q44-SBS, Q54, Q58, and S79-SBS BX19 listed among ABLE routes. The ABLE program, fully implemented in 2019, uses bus-mounted cameras to capture rear license plates of vehicles stopped illegally in bus lanes, issuing violations only after two separate buses observe the same vehicle at the same GPS location at least five minutes apart ABLE violation mechanism.

In June 2024, New York City Transit launched the Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program, which expanded enforcement to also cover double parking and bus stop blocking ACE launched June 2024. The BX19 is an ACE-enforced route ACE route listing Bx19. According to the March 2025 NYCT Key Performance Metrics report, the Bx19 saw a 12% speed increase along 145th Street and a 20% reduction in collisions since ACE cameras were activated Bx19 12% speed increase, 20% collision reduction. The same report also noted a 12% speed gain for the Bx19 along 145th Street in its special feature on the ACE program Bx19 12% speed increase along 145 St.

The BX19 has also been subject to scheduled service adjustments. Meeting document 133291 noted proposed modifications to the weekday schedules of the Bx19, along with the B82, Q3, and Q29 Bx19 weekday schedule modifications.
