SIM4X and SIM8X Discontinuation and Conversion

SIM4X is a weekday peak-period express bus variant operating between Staten Island's South Shore along Richmond Avenue and Downtown Manhattan, serving only two stops on Staten Island (the Staten Island Mall Park-and-Ride and one other stop) but all same stops as the SIM4 in Manhattan route description route detail. It was introduced as part of the August 2018 Staten Island Express Bus Redesign to leverage the Park-and-Ride facility to attract ridership, and in January 2019 one additional Staten Island stop was added in each direction 2018 redesign 2019 stop addition.
