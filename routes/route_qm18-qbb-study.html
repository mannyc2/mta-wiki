QM18 Express Bus Route

route_qm18-qbb-study

The QM18 Express Bus Route is an express bus service between Manhattan and Queens documented in the Queensboro Bridge (QBB) Bus Priority Study, published by NYC DOT in 2011 NYC DOT QBB study. During the study period, the route carried approximately 110 daily riders, contributing to a total of 7,920 daily express bus riders crossing the QBB QM18 daily ridership of 110. The study identified that congestion on the QBB caused significant bus delay, with over 75% of riders reporting regular delays over 75% report delay.

The QM18 shared a bus stop with the QM15, QM16, QM17, and QM21 at Third Avenue and 57th Street in Manhattan, where a bus weave maneuver was identified as a safety concern bus weave at 3rd Ave & 57th St. The study's Recommendation #2 addressed this intersection with a Leading Pedestrian Interval that also created an opportunity for eastbound bus signal priority LPI and bus signal priority.
