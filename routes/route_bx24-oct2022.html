New York City Transit Key Performance Metrics - October 2022

The Bx24 is a Bronx local bus route that was simplified as part of the Bronx Local Bus Network Redesign, which launched in late June 2022. Bronx Redesign launch Alongside other simplified routes (Bx11, Bx36, and Bx40/Bx42), the Bx24 saw reduced travel times during the first two weeks of the 2022 school year compared to the same period in September 2019.
