Bx24

route_bx24-oct2022

The Bx24 is a Bronx local bus route that was simplified as part of the Bronx Local Bus Network Redesign, which launched in late June 2022. Bronx Redesign launch Alongside other simplified routes (Bx11, Bx36, and Bx40/Bx42), the Bx24 saw reduced travel times during the first two weeks of the 2022 school year compared to the same period in September 2019. Reduced travel times for simplified routes Daily and weekly average speeds across the 13 modified routes in the redesign increased 4%. 4% speed increase on 13 modified routes The redesign also brought improvements in reliability and travel time for Bronx local bus performance overall.
