B63

route_b63-ace

B63 is a local bus route operating between Bay Ridge and Cobble Hill in Brooklyn, with major streets including Atlantic Avenue and 5th Avenue MTA Automated Camera Enforcement lists B63 as ACE route with major streets Atlantic Av / 5 Av. The route operates along Flatbush Avenue for parts of its alignment and is one of several routes—including B41, B67, B69, B45, and B103—that use the Flatbush Avenue corridor Map of Flatbush Avenue corridor showing B63 and other bus routes.

The Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project, a joint MTA and NYC DOT initiative, will introduce center-running bus lanes on Flatbush Avenue, which require revisions to the B63 route B63 Route Revision. The changes address operational conflicts: left-turning B63 buses at Atlantic Avenue would have blocked the more frequent B41 and B67 buses traveling straight in the bus lanes Left-turning buses would have blocked B41 and B67. Under the proposed Cobble Hill-bound (northbound) revision, B63 buses will turn left from 5th Avenue onto Bergen Street, right onto 4th Avenue, and left onto Atlantic Avenue to continue along the regular route Cobble Hill-bound detour route. For Bay Ridge-bound (southbound) buses, B63 will turn right onto 4th Avenue from Atlantic Avenue, left onto Dean Street, and right onto 5th Avenue Bay Ridge-bound detour route.

An alternative option considered swapping the B63 and B45 terminals to prevent the left-turn conflict on Flatbush Avenue Swapping the B45 and B63 terminals prevents left turning B63 from blocking B41 on Flatbush Ave.. According to the September 2025 community presentation, the alternate-routing approach would have the northbound B63 stop at 5th Avenue and Bergen Street as the only missed stop Alternate routing prevents left turning B63 from blocking B41 at Atlantic Ave.

The Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project is the result of four years of outreach, including over 45 community meetings Project Design is the Result of Four Years of Outreach and Support. Concrete and signal construction is projected to begin in spring 2026, with bus lanes opening for operation in late fall 2026, followed by a monitoring and adjustment period through fall 2027 Spring 2026: Begin concrete and signal construction Late Fall 2026: Bus lanes open for operation Fall 2026 – Fall 2027: Monitor and adjust as necessary.
