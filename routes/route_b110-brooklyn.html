B110 Bus Route

route_b110-brooklyn

The B110 Bus Route is a Brooklyn local bus route described in the source as the last NYC DOT-supervised route not under MTA control and as operated by Private Transportation Corporation Public comment from Tran Jacinto, Jan 19 2026. The route is proposed to operate along Bedford Avenue connecting Borough Park and Williamsburg Proposal for new B110 route. The record classifies the B110 as a local route with route ID B110, based in Brooklyn Route record evidence.

The evidence for this route comes from a public comment submitted to the MTA Finance Committee meeting of March 23, 2026, as part of the FFY 2026 Capital Projects Public Hearing online comments MTA Capital Projects Public Hearing FFY 2026 - Online Comments Received. The commenter, Tran Jacinto of Brooklyn, proposed that the MTA acquire Private Transportation Corporation and integrate the B110 into a unified NYCTA network, with a new B110 route along Bedford Avenue or its stops folded into existing routes Integration proposal. As a public proposal rather than an official MTA action, the route described may not reflect adopted service plans or current operations. The scope of this record is therefore limited to what the comment asserts, and no MTA Board action, service change notice, or adopted plan has been identified confirming or implementing this route at the time of record creation.
