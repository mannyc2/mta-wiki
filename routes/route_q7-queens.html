Q7 bus route

route_q7-queens

The Q7 bus route appears in the Queens Bus Network Redesign as one of 16 routes receiving a frequency increase and one of 8 routes receiving a span increase as part of the Proposed Final Plan Addendum Frequency Increases list Span Increases list. The span increase is specifically intended to address the lack of east-west overnight service along the Rockaway Boulevard corridor Q7 span increase rationale. In the areas served by the Q7, nearly 30% of residents do not own a vehicle, making reliable overnight service critical for equitable access to jobs, healthcare, and other essential services Q7 vehicle access statistic. The frequency and span improvements are prioritized in routes serving Tier 1 and Tier 2 equity areas, where transit-dependent and historically underserved populations are most concentrated Frequency/span prioritization Tier 1 definition.
