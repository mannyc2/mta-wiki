M14 A/D Select Bus Service is the Select Bus Service operating on 14th Street in Manhattan, serving the corridor between the Lower East Side, Union Square, and the Meatpacking District 14th Street is a major connector. M14 A/D SBS services began in July 2019 M14 A/D SBS services started in July 2019. In October 2019, the New York City Department of Transportation implemented the 14th Street Transit & Truck Priority Pilot Project along the corridor between 9th Avenue and 3rd Avenue, aiming to improve M14 A/D SBS operations and increase safety on this Vision Zero Priority Corridor while maintaining the street as an important truck route TTP Pilot Project implemented by NYCDOT in October 2019.

Under the TTP pilot, 14th Street functions as a busway every day from 6 AM to 10 PM, during which only buses and trucks may make through trips between 9th Avenue and 3rd Avenue Only buses and trucks may make through trips. All other vehicles may make local trips but must turn at the next available right All other vehicles may make local trips. Left turn bans are in effect off 14th Street between 9th Avenue and 3rd Avenue to keep buses from getting stuck behind turning vehicles and to increase pedestrian safety Left turns will not be allowed off of 14th Street Left turn bans keep buses from getting stuck behind turning vehicles. Restrictions are enforced through automated cameras along the corridor Restrictions are enforced through automated cameras.

After implementation, eastbound and westbound M14 A/D SBS travel times between 3rd Avenue and 8th Avenue improved by 24%, saving 2.9 minutes in both directions combined between January 2018 and January 2020 Bus travel time improvement 24% 24 percent Bus travel time 2.9 minutes saved 2.9 minutes 24% improvement in travel times 2.9 minutes faster Combined for both directions: 3rd Avenue to 8th Avenue from January 2018 to January 2020.
