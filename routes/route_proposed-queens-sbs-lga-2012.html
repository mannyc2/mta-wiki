Proposed Queens SBS route to LGA

route_proposed-queens-sbs-lga-2012

The proposed Queens SBS route to LGA (Proposed Queens SBS route to LGA) was developed through the LaGuardia Airport Access Alternatives Analysis, a partnership among the New York City Department of Transportation, MTA New York City Transit, MTA Bus Company, and the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey that began in May 2011 Study partnership. The study identified Bus Rapid Transit, branded as Select Bus Service, as the best mode to improve airport access BRT identified.

The Queens route would offer speeds over 40% faster than current local service Over 40% faster, with features including limited stops, highway operation, and transit signal priority Improvement features. Benefits cited for the route included improved regional connectivity via LIRR and subways and improved local route reliability Improved regional connectivityImproved local route reliability.

The proposed Queens SBS route was part of a broader LGA transit improvement package.
