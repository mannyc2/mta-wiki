B69

route_b69-flatbush-ave-apr2026

The B69 is a Brooklyn local bus route that operates along Flatbush Avenue for parts of its route B69 on Flatbush Ave map. As part of the Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project, the B69 would be subject to a temporary detour during construction, active Monday through Friday and Sunday B69 Temporary Detour.

The reroute is designed to prevent B69 buses from blocking B41 buses on Flatbush Avenue, since turning B69 buses at Atlantic Avenue would interfere with the more frequent B41 service operating straight in the bus lanes Potential Reroutes – B69. Northbound (Downtown Brooklyn-bound), B69 buses would turn right onto Park Place from 7th Avenue, then left onto Vanderbilt Avenue Downtown BK-bound. Southbound (Kensington-bound), buses would turn right from Vanderbilt Avenue onto Sterling Place, then left onto 7th Avenue Kensington-bound. Under this plan, the B69 would no longer serve the Grand Army Plaza 2/3 subway station, and southbound customers would have a longer walk to the 7th Avenue B/Q station Potential Reroutes – B69.

Construction of the Flatbush Avenue bus priority treatments is scheduled to begin in Spring 2026, with bus lanes opening for operation in Late Fall 2026 construction start bus lanes open. After opening, a monitoring and adjustment period is planned from Fall 2026 through Fall 2027 monitoring period.
