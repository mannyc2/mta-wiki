The M103 is a local bus route in Manhattan that appears as a sample data row in the MTA Bus Route Segment Speeds Dataset, showing a speed of 6.00 mph between 3 AV/E 23 ST and 3 AV/E 42 ST at hour 8 on Thursday, May 1, 2025 segment speed dataset sample row. The route also serves as the schedule example (Figure 1) in the methodology article announcing that dataset M103 schedule figure reference.

On 125th Street, the M103 duplicates the route of other buses and was noted as contributing to congestion at a 2012 public workshop on bus service along that corridor M103 duplicates other buses on 125th St. On Third Avenue, the M103 was one of three local routes (together with the M101 and M102) that together carried 59,000 daily riders along a curbside bus lane from 36th St to 56th St, which NYC DOT converted to an offset bus lane in Fall 2014 to improve speeds and reliability 59,000 daily riders on M101, M102, M103 offset bus lane conversion improves speeds.
