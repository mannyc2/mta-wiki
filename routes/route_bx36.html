The BX36 is a local bus route in the Bronx, designated as item "BX36" in the ACE routes dataset ACE routes dataset. It operates on Tremont Avenue from University Avenue to White Plains Road and is the 5th busiest route in the Bronx, carrying approximately 34,000 bus riders per day Tremont Av Bus Priority CB5 Nov 2024. It serves as a critical east-west transit connection across the borough Tremont Av Bus Priority CB6 Feb 2025.

The route is part of the MTA's Automated Bus Lane Enforcement (ABLE) program, with cameras operating on the route through at least 2023 along E Tremont Avenue and White Plains Road MTA Automated Camera Enforcement Bus Lane Camera Report 2024. The BX36 serves over 39,000 daily riders according to the Tremont Avenue Busway project page, which aims to increase bus speeds and reliability for these riders Tremont Avenue Busway.

At E Tremont Avenue and Webster Avenue, the BX36 provides a transfer point to the Bx41, as documented during the Webster Avenue Select Bus Service community planning process Webster SBS CAC3 Meeting Summary. The route also serves the University Avenue corridor, where combined weekday ridership across all routes (Bx3, Bx18, Bx36) reaches approximately 39,000 Bronx Bus Network Final Plan 2019 2020 Bus Priority Improvements CB5.

BX36 bus stops were proposed for relocation to Valentine Avenue as part of the Webster Avenue SBS project design Webster SBS CB5 2013. A June 2022 Tremont Avenue bus priority project straightened BX36 routing so that it serves Tremont Avenue from University Avenue to White Plains Road, while Bx40/Bx42 serves Tremont Avenue east of Rosedale Avenue; the routes no longer switch streets at Webster Avenue Tremont Av Bus Priority CB6 Feb 2024. The simplified routing has been associated with reduced travel times Meeting Doc 98311.
