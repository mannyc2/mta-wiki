Q58 - ABLE route

route_q58

The Q58 is a Queens local bus route operated by MTA New York City Transit under the ABLE automated bus lane enforcement program ABLE route list including Q58. It runs along Fresh Pond Road, Corona Avenue, and College Point Boulevard serving Ridgewood, Maspeth, Elmhurst, Corona, and Flushing ACE page listing Q58 major streets route description.

Multiple sources describe the Q58 as Queens' highest-ridership bus route Q58 highest ridership route in Queens Q58 highest ridership route in Queens high ridership route. On Broadway between Roosevelt Avenue and Grand Avenue, the Q58 shares corridor with the Q53 SBS; this segment carries approximately 17,000 daily bus trips combined and has been identified as the slowest portion of the Q53 route Broadway carries Q53 and Q58 with 17,000 daily trips.

The route is listed in the ACE Routes Dataset as route ID "Q58" with two entries in the dataset, and it is categorized under the ABLE program ACE dataset Q58 item ACE dataset Q58 count of 2. ABLE bus lane camera enforcement operated on this route through at least 2023 Q58 listed among ABLE camera routes. The Q58 also appears in MTA New York City Transit Key Performance Metrics reporting and has been shown with a delay alert in the MTA app Q58 in NYCT KPI metrics Q58 delay alert.

Several recent Grand Street and Grand Avenue safety and bus priority improvement presentations to Queens Community Boards 4 and 5 in late 2024 reference the Q58 as the primary route on that corridor, noting its status as the highest-ridership route in Queens Grand Ave project mentions Q58 CB5 presentation mentions Q58.
