Key Performance Metrics - March 2025

The Rockaway Shuttle train is a Queens-based subway shuttle service that has delivered 99% on-time performance since January 2025 Rockaway Shuttle 99% OTP since January. It operates as part of the Rockaway Resiliency project, maintaining transit access to the Rockaway peninsula while it is separated from the rest of the subway system Rockaway Resiliency project service. Because the shuttle cars cannot reach maintenance facilities during the separation, the NYCT team executed a complicated fleet swap — moving the cars to Pitkin Yard for routine maintenance fleet swap for maintenance. New York City Transit President Demetrius Crichlow rode the shuttle with New York City Council Member Selvena Brooks-Powers, who represents the district; riders told them the new service pattern improved their daily commute ride with Council Member Brooks-Powers.
