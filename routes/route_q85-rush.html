The Q85 Rush Route is a proposed rush route serving the Merrick Blvd corridor in southeastern Queens, documented in the Queens Bus Network Redesign Proposed Final Plan Addendum Q85 Rush Route Service description. The route would run from Rosedale to Jamaica, beginning at the Jamaica Center subway station, traveling east along Archer Avenue, turning south onto Merrick Boulevard, and continuing to Hook Creek Boulevard Map description. A discontinued segment at the southern end of the route is to be replaced by a new Q89 route Map description.

The Q85 Rush Route is one of 25 Rush Routes included within the addendum's 94 Local routes proposed 94 as part of the overall plan of 124 total routes proposed 124 94 Local routes including 25 Rush Routes. Rush Routes, shown in purple on the network map, are designed to connect outer borough neighborhoods quickly to bus and subway hubs Rush Routes (Purple) Rush Routes description.

The route would feature a rush segment on Merrick Boulevard, local stop spacing between Rosedale and the Merrick Boulevard corridor, and serve key destinations along Merrick Boulevard to provide faster service to key transfer points in Jamaica Service description. Underlying local service along the corridor would continue to be provided by the Q5 Service description.
