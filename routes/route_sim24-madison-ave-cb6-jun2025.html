SIM24 Express Bus

route_sim24-madison-ave-cb6-jun2025

The SIM24 Express Bus is an NYCT-operated express bus route connecting Prince's Bay and Huguenot Avenue on Staten Island to Midtown Manhattan SIM24 route description. It is among the bus routes that operate on Madison Avenue within the East 23rd to East 42nd Street study area, as shown in the Madison Avenue bus route diagram Madison Avenue bus routes diagram.

Starting in 2022, the MTA took over operation of the SIM24 and SIM23 routes, extending standard Staten Island express bus features to riders on these lines MTA takeover of SIM23 and SIM24. The two routes together transport about 300 passengers daily to and from midtown Manhattan SIM23/SIM24 daily ridership.

A proposed service enhancement would add 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks on the SIM24, as part of a package adding trips to express bus routes with the highest single-hour utilization Add trips to express bus routes SIM24 trip additions. This same trip addition was carried into a Spring 2025 express bus service enhancement proposal that provided frequency increases or running time adjustments on eight express routes Spring 2025 express bus enhancements.
