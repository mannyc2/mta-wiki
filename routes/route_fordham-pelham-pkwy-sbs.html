Fordham Road-Pelham Parkway Select Bus Service began service in June 2008 in the Bronx

route_fordham-pelham-pkwy-sbs

This was the first Select Bus Service route implemented in New York City, launching in June 2008 along Fordham Road and Pelham Parkway in the Bronx Fordham Road-Pelham Parkway SBS launched June 2008. The route has since increased speeds by 20% speed increase of 20%, and achieved a rider satisfaction rate of 98% of riders stating that they were satisfied or very satisfied with the new service 98 percent 98% rider satisfaction. As New York City's first SBS corridor, it established a model for the 15 subsequent SBS routes that followed across the boroughs 16 SBS routes implemented citywide.
