BxM11 Express Bus

route_bxm11

BxM11 Express Bus is a Bronx-Manhattan express bus route that appears in NYC DOT's 116th Street, Morningside Avenue, and Pleasant Avenue bus priority corridor study. The February 2025 CB10 study area presentation lists the BxM11 among 10 bus routes serving over 65,000 daily passengers in the 2.2-mile corridor along 116th Street, Manhattan Avenue, Pleasant Avenue, and Morningside Avenue BxM11 in 116th St study area. By the May and June 2025 CB10 presentations, the study area was refined to 1.1 miles along 116th Street, Manhattan Avenue, and Morningside Avenue, still including the BxM11 among routes serving over 64,000 daily bus passengers May 2025 study area listing BxM11 June 2025 study area listing BxM11.

The CB11 portion of the study, focused on 116th Street and Pleasant Avenue, lists BxM11 among 7 bus routes serving over 36,000 daily passengers CB11 May 2025 listing BxM11 CB11 June 2025 listing BxM11. The BxM11 is also referenced in on-bus ridership leave-loads data collected from routes traveling within the study area On-bus ridership data includes BxM11.

The corridor study is led by NYC DOT under the Better Buses program and is focused on addressing slow bus speeds through proposed treatments including offset bus lanes, curbside bus lanes, left-turn lanes, concrete pedestrian islands, and signalized mid-block crossings Proposed treatments for the corridor Better Buses program Project summary of proposed improvements. The project timeline indicates a draft proposal was presented to Community Board 9 in June 2025, with implementation tentatively planned for summer 2025 Project timeline.

The BxM11 is an express bus operated by the MTA NYCT Department of Buses between the Bronx and Manhattan 116th Street study description listing BxM11 as express bus route. Its inclusion in the 116th Street corridor study reflects that it shares the corridor with local and other express routes experiencing congestion-related delays, with buses moving at less than 4 mph in some segments Slow bus speeds on 116th Street corridor.
