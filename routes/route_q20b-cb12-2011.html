The Q20B bus route is a local bus route in Queens, operating between Jamaica and College Point as part of the Main Street corridor Select Bus Service candidates Q20A/B: Jamaica to College Point. During the planning for the Q44 SBS upgrade, a May 2015 community presentation stated that "Q20A/B Local Service Will Continue As Is" while the Q44 Limited was upgraded to Select Bus Service Q20A/B local service to continue unchanged. The route serves a bus stop at the intersection of Hillside Avenue and Sutphin Boulevard, where Q20A/Q20B/Q44 and Q43 routes share a stop location as documented in the 2011 Jamaica Bus Improvement Study presented to Queens Community Board 12 Q20A/Q20B/Q44 bus stop at Hillside Av and Sutphin Blvd. That study identified challenges in the Jamaica area including narrow streets with heavy bus and local traffic, bus stop patterns causing excess weaving and congestion, and misplaced layover locations narrow streets claim bus stop patterns claim. The Main Street corridor carrying Q20A/B and Q44 served approximately 42,000 daily riders, with a 2015 SBS implementation goal 42,000 daily riders on Main Street corridor.
