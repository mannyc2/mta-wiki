MTA Capital Program 2020-2024 Amendment #2

The SIM 23 / SIM 24 Express Bus Routes are express bus routes in Staten Island that were previously operated by the City of New York and subsequently taken over by the MTA MTA Capital Program 2020-2024 Amendment #2, page 16. The MTA's takeover of these routes is funded through the proposed Amendment #2 to the MTA 2020-2024 Capital Program, which adds $7 million in City Capital funds specifically for bus purchases associated with the transfer City Capital funding for SIM 23/24 bus purchases. This amendment, proposed to the MTA Board on July 27, 2022, encompasses a total program of $55.442 billion across all MTA agencies Proposed to MTA Board July 27, 2022Total Program $55.442 billion. The $7 million in City Capital funds is part of a broader $100 million increase in State of New York Capital funds and $542 million in other sources included in the amendment State funds increase $100 millionOther sources $542 million. The routes, which the source identifies as SIM23 and SIM24, represent Staten Island express bus service that transitioned from city to MTA operational control during the 2020-2024 capital planning period SIM 23/24 previously operated by City of New York.
