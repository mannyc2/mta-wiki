The S53 is one of four local bus routes that MTA documents with permanent front-of-bus bike racks, alongside the S93, Q50, and Bx23 S53, S93, Q50, and Bx23 bike racks. A separate bicycle, pedestrian, and micromobility access presentation describes the same four routes as bus routes crossing bridges, where the bike racks were installed to improve bicycle access on bridge-linked trips permanent bike racks on four bridge-crossing routes.

The S53 bike-rack work sits within the MTA's broader Extending Transit's Reach Strategic Action Plan, which was published in January 2023 to improve bicycle, pedestrian, and micromobility access across the system Extending Transit's Reach plan publication. The same update distinguishes the local-route bike racks from front-of-bus bike racks on Select Bus Service routes including Q44 SBS, S79 SBS, and M60 SBS SBS routes with front-of-bus bike racks.
