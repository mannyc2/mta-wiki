The B25 is a Brooklyn local bus route operating primarily on Fulton Street. It is listed in the ACE Routes dataset with route identifier "B25" ACE dataset B25 item and is part of a dataset containing 84 route entries ACE dataset cardinality of 84.

The B25 was a participant in the MTA's ABLE (Automated Bus Lane Enforcement) mobile camera program through 2023, one of 21 routes equipped with on-bus enforcement cameras ABLE cameras on 21 routes through 2023. The ABLE system captures no-standing and no-parking violations by recording vehicles stopped in bus lanes using onboard cameras, requiring two buses to observe the same vehicle at the same GPS location at least five minutes apart before a violation is issued ABLE violation capture mechanism.

The B25 was among the final three routes activated in this push, alongside the B62 and B42 final three routes B25, B62, B42.
