B46 (Utica Avenue)

route_b46-local-limited-20110915

The B46 (Utica Avenue) is a local and limited-stop bus route in Brooklyn, running from Williamsburg Bridge Plaza in the north to Kings Plaza in the south, primarily along Broadway and Malcolm X Blvd. Route map It is the second busiest bus route in New York City, carrying over 49,000 passengers per weekday, and most Utica area residents lack direct subway access. Ridership and subway access

The B46's average speed was 6.8 mph as of 2012-2013, below the Brooklyn average, with congestion causing slower buses and bus bunching. Speed and congestion Eastern Parkway is a major transfer point to the 3 and 4 subway trains. Eastern Pkwy transfer The route was studied under the Utica Avenue Bus Priority and Safety Study beginning in fall 2011, which evaluated offset bus lanes, left turn treatments, and Transit Signal Priority. Study timeline Proposed improvements One proposed treatment, an offset bus lane from St. Johns Place to Carroll Street, was designed to reduce bus delays while yielding a net gain of 19 peak period parking and loading spaces 19 spaces near Eastern Parkway. Parking gain The study stated that these improvements would lay the groundwork for potential future Select Bus Service on the B46. SBS groundwork
