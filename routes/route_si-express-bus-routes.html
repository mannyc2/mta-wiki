Staten Island Express Bus Routes

route_si-express-bus-routes

The Staten Island Express Bus Routes are six express services — SIM8, SIM8X, SIM22, SIM25, SIM26, and SIM30 — that use 42nd Street in Manhattan. Map showing SIM8/8X/22/25/26/30 on 42nd St They are among eight MTA bus routes across three boroughs operating on the corridor, carrying approximately 16,000 daily passengers 16000 passengers with up to 210 buses per hour 210 buses per hour. 8 routes, 3 boroughs, 16,000 daily passengers, 210 buses/hr

The 42nd Street Transit Improvements project, a Better Buses Action Plan priority, listed these SIM express routes among the services it serves along the 2.0-mile corridor from 12th Avenue to FDR Drive. Routes served and corridor length At the time of the 2019 plan, average bus speeds on 42nd Street were 4.2 mph in the AM peak and 2.9 mph in the PM peak, well below the Manhattan peak bus speed average 5.6 MPH of 5.6 mph and the citywide peak bus speed average 7.7 MPH of 7.7 mph. Average bus speeds: 4.2 mph (AM) / 2.9 mph (PM) Buses carry 29% of people on the street while using 33% of street space, and pedestrians account for 63% of people. Mode share: Bus 29%, Pedestrian 63%

This information comes from a June 2019 NYC DOT and MTA presentation to Manhattan Community Board 5 as part of the Better Buses Action Plan outreach. 42nd Street Transit Improvements presentation
