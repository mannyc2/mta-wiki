B42 - ABLE route

route_b42

The B42 is a Brooklyn local bus route operating on Rockaway Parkway and was included in the MTA's ABLE (Automated Bus Lane Enforcement) camera program through 2023 ABLE cameras operated on the following twenty-one routes through 2023 B42 included in ABLE route list B42, Rockaway Pkwy. The ABLE system uses onboard mobile cameras that capture rear license plates of vehicles stopped in bus lanes; two buses must observe the same vehicle at the same GPS location at least five minutes apart before a violation is issued ABLE camera system operation description. Bus lane enforcement cameras were activated on the B42 on December 12, 2022, as one of three final routes (along with the B25 and B62) in a broader expansion that added cameras to 300 additional buses and 9 new routes final three routes activated on December 12th include B25, B62, and B42. In the ACE (Automated Camera Enforcement) routes dataset, the B42 is identified with the route value "B42" under the ABLE program item: B42 program item: ABLE.
