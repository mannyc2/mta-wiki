Bx17 (NYCT) is a local Bronx bus route operated by MTA New York City Transit, serving the Fordham Road corridor between Port Morris and Fordham Plaza Bx17 service description Fordham corridor routes. On the Fordham Road corridor, which carries multiple bus routes, average bus speeds were measured at 6.8 mph during the AM peak and 5.9 mph during the PM peak Fordham corridor bus speeds.

The Fordham Road corridor carries approximately 85,000 average daily bus riders across five routes — Bx12 SBS, Bx12 Local, Bx9, Bx17, and Bx22 85,000 daily riders on 5 routes same figure. The Bx17 itself has an estimated 7,600 daily riders Bx17 daily ridership.
