The QM3 Express Bus Route was a Queens express bus service with a recorded daily ridership of 90 passengers, as documented in a 2011 Queensboro Bridge approach analysis QM3 daily ridership of 90. The QM3 was proposed for discontinuation as part of the Queens Bus Network Redesign, which recommended eliminating the route alongside the introduction of a new route (the QM65) and other routing, bus stop, and frequency changes across the Queens express network QM3 proposed discontinuation. The QM3's very low daily ridership of 90 placed it far below every other QM express route in the QBB corridor; for context, the next-lowest QM routes carried 110 and 130 daily riders, while the highest (QM1A) carried 2,020 QM route ridership comparison. The Express Bus Total Daily Ridership on QBB 7920 riders per day across all QM and X routes combined was 7,920 daily passengers total express bus daily ridership.
