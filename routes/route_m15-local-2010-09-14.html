The M15 Local was the existing local bus service on First and Second Avenues between South Ferry and 125th Street that remained unchanged when the M15 Select Bus Service launched on October 10, 2010 M15 Local unchanged Currently served by M15 bus route. At the time of the SBS implementation, the M15 Local operated 24 hours a day M15 Local 24 hours, with peak frequency of 5–8 minutes and off-peak frequency of 7–9 minutes M15 Local 5-8 minutes / 7-9 minutes. Transfers were permitted between the M15 SBS and M15 Local services Transfers permitted between SBS and Local.

In April 2011, the M15 Local had approximately 22,000 daily riders M15 Local is approximately 22,000. By November 2011, the progress report noted that M15 Local ridership had been dropping as some riders transitioned to the SBS, and that the M15 Local and M15 Limited services had roughly equal ridership totals M15 local ridership has been dropping.
