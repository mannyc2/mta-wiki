BxM9 Express Bus

route_bxm9

The BxM9 Express Bus is a Bronx-Manhattan express bus route that appears in NYC DOT's 116th Street Bus & Pedestrian Priority Project study area. The February 2025 CB10 presentation lists the BxM9 among 10 bus routes serving over 65,000 daily passengers on the corridor between 114th and 113th Streets along 116th Street, Manhattan Avenue, Pleasant Avenue, and Morningside Avenue 116th Street study area description. By June 2025 the CB10 study area was refined to 1.1 miles, still carrying over 64,000 daily passengers across the same set of routes June 2025 CB10 study area. In the CB11 portion of the project area, the BxM9 is one of 7 bus routes serving over 36,000 daily passengers May 2025 CB11 study area. The BxM9 also appears in NYC DOT's Better Buses program context Better Buses page listing BxM9 among express routes. The 116th Street project includes proposed treatments such as offset and curbside bus lanes, left-turn lanes, concrete pedestrian islands, and signalized mid-block crossings that would affect the BxM9 and other routes traveling through the corridor.
