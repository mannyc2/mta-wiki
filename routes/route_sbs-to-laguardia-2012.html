SBS to LaGuardia Airport

route_sbs-to-laguardia-2012

The SBS to LaGuardia Airport was a proposed Select Bus Service route considered as part of the Webster Avenue SBS planning process in 2012–2013. It appeared in community board presentations and a Community Advisory Committee presentation as one of several proposed Webster Avenue corridor bus services Proposed bus services. The service would have used off-board fare collection, served the same stops as the Bx41 SBS north of The Hub, and connected to the 6 train SBS to LaGuardia Airport description. The proposed route was listed in the same service grouping as the Bx41 SBS and Bx41 Local in multiple Bronx community board presentations from February and March 2013 Service listing, Service listing, Service listing. Map depictions from the November 2012 CAC presentation show the route as "SBS to LGA" running from the Webster Avenue corridor to LaGuardia Airport, labeled as a proposed Bronx to LaGuardia Airport SBS connection Map showing SBS to LGA route, Proposed service plan map. The route was included alongside the proposed conversion of the Bx41 Limited to Bx41 SBS, suggesting it was envisioned as a complementary service extending SBS-style amenities to the airport SBS to LaGuardia Airport description. It is not clear from these planning documents whether the route was ever implemented or advanced beyond the proposal stage; the available source record consists only of planning-stage presentations from 2012–2013.
