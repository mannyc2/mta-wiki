M60 Select Bus Service Launch Flyer

The Q19 is documented in the 2014 M60 Select Bus Service launch flyer published by NYC DOT for the May 25, 2014 service launch on 125th Street M60 SBS launch flyer. The flyer notes that the Q19 makes all local stops on Astoria Blvd, while the M60 SBS serves 125th Street in Manhattan Q19 local stops on Astoria Blvd. Along with the Bx15, M100, and M101—which continued making all local stops on 125th Street—the Q19 continued to serve all local stops on its own corridor when the new Select Bus Service began operation Q19 continued local stops.
