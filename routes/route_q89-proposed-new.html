Q89

route_q89-proposed-new

Q89 is a proposed new Local route in the Queens Bus Network Redesign Proposed Final Plan Addendum, part of a system that recommends 124 total routes proposed 124 (including 94 Local routes proposed 94) compared to 113 existing routes 113 124 total routes. The route would replace a discontinued southern segment of the Q85 Rush Route; a callout box on the published map states: "Discontinued segment will be served by the new Q89" Discontinued segment will be served by the new Q89. The existing Q85 Rush Route begins at the Jamaica Center subway station, travels east along Archer Av, turns south onto Merrick Blvd, continues through Bedell St and Locust Manor, turns east onto South Conduit Av through Rosedale, and ends at Hook Creek Blvd; the black dashed line on the map marks the discontinued section at the southern end Q85 Rush Route map.

The Q89 is classified as a Local route (green type), which the addendum describes as "connecting local neighborhoods" Local Routes Connecting local neighborhoods. The Queens Bus Network Redesign Proposed Final Plan Addendum was published in December 2024, with a planned MTA Board vote in winter 2025 and implementation in two phases aligned with regular service changes Developed / Published Addendum Summer 2024 - December 2024 MTA Board Vote January/February 2025 Phase I Phase II. Route type, frequency, span, and exact alignment details for the Q89 beyond the map description are not specified in the addendum.
