B67

route_b67-flatbush-ave-apr2026

The B67 is a secondary local bus route on Flatbush Avenue in Brooklyn. Multiple community outreach presentations describe the B67 as a secondary route that operates on Flatbush Ave for parts of its route, alongside B9, B103, Q35, and BM2, while the B41 is identified as the primary route on the corridor Flatbush Ave secondary routes CAB1 secondary routes CB9 secondary routes CB14 secondary routes. A map of the corridor shows the B67 running southbound on or near Flatbush Avenue CB2 corridor map.

Under the Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project, the B67 was a design consideration: left-turning buses at Atlantic Avenue would have blocked the more frequent B41 and B67 buses traveling straight in the bus lanes along Flatbush Avenue, a constraint that drove route revisions for other services such as the B63 MTP briefing on bus lane design. The corridor is a Vision Zero priority corridor, with 164 killed or seriously injured on Flatbush Ave in 5 years 164 people KSI metric on Flatbush Ave. The bus priority project design was shaped by four years of outreach including 45+ community meetings and 375 survey respondents outreach summary outreach numbers.
