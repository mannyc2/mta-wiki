SIM23 Express Bus

route_sim23-madison-ave-cb6-jun2025

The SIM23 Express Bus is an NYCT-operated express bus route serving Staten Island between Annadale (Arden Avenue) and Midtown Manhattan SIM23 (NYCT): Staten Island: Annadale-Arden Av-Midtown. Starting in 2022, the MTA took over operation of the SIM23 and the SIM24 routes, bringing riders the same features available to other Staten Island express bus riders; the two routes together transport about 300 passengers daily to and from Midtown Manhattan MTA took over SIM23 and SIM24 in 2022.

The SIM23 is one of 29 express bus routes serving Madison Avenue between East 23rd and East 42nd Street, and is represented as a southbound route beginning at 34th Street and continuing south to Madison Square Park on route diagrams SIM23 shown as southbound route on Madison Avenue. As one of the six express bus routes with the highest single-hour utilization on an average weekday, the SIM23 was selected for additional service in Summer 2024 as part of the MTA's response to anticipated congestion pricing demand, adding one peak-period, peak-direction trip in both the AM and PM peaks Add trips to SIM23 and five other express routesSIM23: 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks.

The Spring 2025 bus service enhancements continued these service levels on the SIM23 SIM23 Spring 2025: 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks. The total annualized operating cost increase of the Spring 2025 bus service enhancement package is approximately $8 million across express and local routes Annualized operating cost increase - total 8000000 USD.
