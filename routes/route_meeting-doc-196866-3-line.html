3 line

route_meeting-doc-196866-3-line

The 3 line is a subway route operated by NYCT that is part of proposed minor weekday schedule changes on the 2 3 4 5 lines, intended to better align scheduled service with shifts in passenger demand and improve operational reliability p001_c0005. The proposed adjustments, which would be implemented in May 2026, are expected to be cost neutral p001_c0009p001_c0011. These changes would spread peak service out and reduce the potential for delays through the Nostrand Junction bottleneck p002_c0010.

The trip shifts would result in a net increase of one round trip on the 3 line p003_c0003. On the northbound 3 line, trips would be added to the 7:00 AM, 7:00 PM, and 9:00 PM hours and removed from the 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM hours p003_c0004. The northbound 7:00 AM average headway would decrease from 10.0 to 8.6 minutes (3 NB 7 AM headway change 10.0 to 8.6 minutes) p005_c0004. On the southbound 3 line, trips would be added to the 5:00 AM, 7:00 AM, 6:00 PM, and 7:00 PM hours and removed from the 8:00 AM, 9:00 AM, and 4:00 PM hours p003_c0004. The southbound 7:00 AM average headway would decrease from 6.7 to 6.0 minutes (3 SB 7 AM headway change 6.7 to 6.0 minutes) p005_c0004.

Additionally, to reduce delays caused by 3 trains terminating at 34 St-Penn Station on the downtown express track, 2 line trips would operate local between Times Sq-42 St and Chambers St during late-night hours p003_c0006.
