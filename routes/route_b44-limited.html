B44 Limited was a limited-stop bus route in Brooklyn that operated along Nostrand Avenue and New York Avenue before being replaced by Select Bus Service in November 2013 B44 SBS Progress Report 2016. Multiple community presentations from 2010 through 2012 described the planned conversion, stating that the "B44 Limited will become SBS" and that the "B44 Local will continue as before" CB1 Meeting June 2010CB3 Meeting March 2011CAC Meeting 5 June 2012. The November 2013 Nostrand SBS flyer confirmed that "B44 SBS replaces B44 limited bus service" Nostrand SBS Flyer 2013.

As part of the SBS conversion, northbound B44 SBS service was rerouted from New York Avenue to Rogers Avenue, which decreased the northbound route length from 9.9 to 9.5 miles, while B44 Local service was maintained on New York Avenue Nostrand SBS Progress Report June 2016. The B44 Limited had been a historically high-ridership route, serving as both a subway feeder and a neighborhood connector between Williamsburg and Sheepshead Bay B44 SBS Progress Report 2016. Before SBS implementation, the B44 Limited was often slow, unreliable, and crowded due to traffic congestion, high passenger boardings, and double parking B44 SBS Progress Report 2016.
