Service Changes: Bus Service Enhancements

The Q28 (NYCT) is a local bus route serving Bayside and Flushing in Queens, identified for running time adjustments as part of a December 2024 MTA Staff Summary recommending bus service enhancements in support of Congestion Pricing Q28 listed as Bayside-Flushing running time adjustments. The summary, prepared by MTA Operations Planning under Chief Christopher Pangilinan and Project Manager Robert Lai, proposed running time adjustments on 16 local bus routes/route combinations where utilization is high or where routes have reliability issues staff summary author details16 local routes selected for utilization or reliability. Operated by New York City Transit as a local service, the Q28's specific running time adjustments were under development at the time of the summary and planned for implementation in the summer 2025 schedule details under development, summer 2025.

Across the local bus improvement program of which the Q28 is a part, approximately 29 additional buses are needed, secured through rehabilitation of buses planned for retirement, at a one-time first-year cost of roughly $5 million 29 additional buses via rehabilitation$5M first-year rehab cost.
