5 Subway

route_5-subway-flatbush

NYCT proposed minor weekday schedule changes on the 5 Subway along with the 2, 3, and 4 lines, to be implemented in May 2026, reallocating trips to better match shifts in ridership by time of day and improve operational reliability NYCT schedule review May 2026 implementation. The cumulative effect is intended to spread peak service for more even headways and reduce delays through the Nostrand Junction bottleneck Nostrand Junction bottleneck relief. The adjustments are expected to be cost neutral cost neutral.

For the northbound 5 Subway at 14 St-Union Square, proposed changes include adding two trips each to the 7:00 AM, 8:00 AM, and 6:00 PM hours, adding one trip to the 7:00 PM hour, removing two trips each from the 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM hours, and removing one trip each from the 11:00 AM, 5:00 PM, and 8:00 PM hours 5 NB trip bullet list 5 NB table. Key headway changes include the 7:00 AM hour going from 8.6 to 6.7 minutes (5 NB 7 AM headway change 8.6 to 6.7 minutes) and the 8:00 AM guideline load dropping from 102% to 84% (5 NB 8 AM guideline load change 102% to 84% percent) 5 NB 7 AM and 8 AM table rows.

For the southbound 5 Subway at Grand Central-42 St, the proposal moves one trip each from the 8:00 AM and 3:00 PM hours to the 6:00 AM hour 5 SB trip bullet list 5 SB table. The 6:00 AM headway would improve from 7.5 to 6.0 minutes (5 SB 6 AM headway change 7.5 to 6.0 minutes) 5 SB 6 AM table row.

Combined 4/5 corridor metrics also reflect the changes. Northbound at 14 St-Union Square, the 7:00 PM hour would gain 3 trips (4/5 combined NB 7 PM trip change 3 trips) and the 8:00 AM combined headway would tighten from 2.9 to 2.6 minutes (4/5 combined NB 8 AM headway change 2.9 to 2.6 minutes), while southbound 8:00 AM combined headway would ease from 2.4 to 2.6 minutes (4/5 combined SB 8 AM headway change 2.4 to 2.6 minutes) 4/5 combined table.

The 5 Subway serves Flatbush Avenue station, where a memorial event honoring Garrett Goble was held and a mural of Goble was installed in the station memorial event description memorial event and station sign showing 5 mural at Flatbush Avenue station.
