The M79 Crosstown Local Bus M79 Crosstown Local Bus runs east along 79th Street from Broadway to East End Avenue in Manhattan M79 bus route. It carries over 18,000 passengers per day over about 2 miles M79 description, recorded as M79 daily passengers 18000 passengers_per_day and M79 corridor length 2 miles. The route connects to 1, 6, B, and C subway trains and to 16 bus routes including the M15 SBS M79 description.

The M79 experiences heavy congestion: only 43% of trip time is in motion M79 trip breakdown, with 26% at bus stop, 21% in delays, and 9% crawling below 2.5 mph, totaling over 50% of the trip spent not moving well M79 performance claim.
