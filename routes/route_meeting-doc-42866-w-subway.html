W Subway

route_meeting-doc-42866-w-subway

The W Subway route was documented at the 57 Street / 7 Av ADA Subway Station, where a station sign visible at an MTA accessibility event listed the W train among the routes serving this station, alongside the N, Q, and R trains 57 Street Station sign showing N Q R W routes. This station appearance is part of an MTA Board Update presented on June 23, 2021, by MTA Construction & Development, which also covered the completion of major construction on the East Side Access project, ADA enhancements at Floral Park station on the Long Island Rail Road, and utilities work at the East End Gateway MTA Board Update June 23, 2021. The 57 Street / 7 Av station was highlighted as an ADA improvement project with an outdoor ceremony featuring construction workers, officials, and community members 57 Street / 7 Av ADA Subway Station heading.

No additional detail about W train service patterns, frequency, or operations is available in this source beyond the route being listed among the station's serving lines. The station entrance is recorded in Manhattan, consistent with the W route's operating territory 57 Street Station scene description.
