Bx10 (NYCT)

route_meeting-doc-160441-bx10

The Bx10 is an NYCT local bus route operating between Riverdale and Norwood that was identified for running time adjustments as part of an MTA service enhancement package in support of Congestion Pricing Bx10 running time adjustments Congestion Pricing rationale. It was among 16 local bus routes and route combinations selected for frequency increases and/or running time adjustments where utilization is high or where routes have reliability issues local route selection criteria. The details of the adjustments were under development at the time of the staff summary and were planned for implementation in the summer 2025 schedule summer 2025 planned local routes summer 2025. To support these local improvements, an increase in the overall fleet by approximately 29 additional buses was needed, secured by rehabilitating buses planned for retirement (First year cost - rehabilitate 29 local buses 5000000 USD) 29 buses via rehabilitation.
