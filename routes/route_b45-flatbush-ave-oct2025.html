B45

route_b45-flatbush-ave-oct2025

Under the Flatbush Avenue Bus Priority project, a potential terminal swap between B45 and the B63 is being explored Potential Terminal Swap – B45 & B63. Swapping the B45 and B63 terminals would prevent the left-turning B63 from blocking the B41 on Flatbush Avenue Terminal swap detail. Under the proposal, the westbound B45 would cross Flatbush Avenue and continue along Atlantic Avenue to terminate at Brooklyn Bridge Park WB B45 routing detail. The northbound B63 would turn left onto Flatbush Avenue from 5th Avenue and proceed along Flatbush Avenue and Livingston Street to terminate in Downtown Brooklyn NB B63 routing detail. All existing stops would continue to be served under the swap All stops served.

The B45 appears on corridor maps as one of several bus routes that operate on or near Flatbush Avenue, alongside the B63, B69, B103, B67, B65, and B41 Flatbush Ave corridor map. The terminal swap is part of a broader package of bus priority improvements, including center-running bus lanes, boarding islands, and pedestrian safety treatments, proposed by NYC DOT and the MTA along the Flatbush Avenue corridor Center-running bus lanes Bus routing and stop consolidation.
