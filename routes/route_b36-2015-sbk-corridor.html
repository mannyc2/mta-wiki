route_b36-2015-sbk-corridor

The B36 was identified as one of thirteen east-west bus routes serving south Brooklyn as part of the South Brooklyn Crosstown SBS corridor study presented at a June 2015 NYC DOT kick-off meeting B36 listed among east-west routes. These routes carried a combined daily ridership of 178,000 178,000 per day. The Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study (2009) had previously identified the south Brooklyn east-west corridor as a priority service need, based on public workshops that raised concerns about slow, long bus trips and underserved areas BRT Phase II Study identified priority issues raised at 2009 workshops.
