Long Beach Branch

route_lirr-long-beach-branch-2023

The Long Beach Branch is a LIRR rail branch between Valley Stream and Long Beach where scheduled track maintenance occurred in February 2023 LIRR plan to adjust schedules for track maintenance on Long Beach Branch. On Saturday, February 11, 2023, both main tracks between Valley Stream and Long Beach were taken out of service for approximately 24 hours while track maintenance was performed, with bus service replacing trains in both directions Track Maintenance – Long Beach Branch February 11 bus replacement.

In the LIRR Annual Operating Report 2023, the Long Beach Branch recorded an overall on-time performance of 95.9% for 2023, compared to 92.3% in 2022, against an annual goal of 97.0% Performance Summary - Long Beach Branch OTP 2023. By comparison, the system-wide LIRR Overall On Time Performance 2023 94 percent was 94.0%, against an annual goal of LIRR Annual Goal OTP 2023 95.8 percent of 95.8% System Overall OTP 2023. Branch-specific peak performance was lower, with AM Peak at 91.3% and PM Peak at 91.7%, while weekend service achieved 96.2% Long Beach Branch OTP by period 2023.
