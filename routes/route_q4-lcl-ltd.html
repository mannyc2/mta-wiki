Q4

route_q4-lcl-ltd

The Q4 is a local and limited bus route in Queens, connecting Jamaica to Cambria Heights, and was one of five routes selected for the MTA Fare-Free Bus Pilot mandated by the 2023 New York State budget Q4 route map description. The pilot ran from September 24, 2023 through August 2024, with fare collection resuming on September 1, 2024 pilot start and end dates pilot concludes at end of August.

During the pilot, weekday ridership on the Q4 increased from a pre-pilot baseline of 10,762 (September 2022 – May 2023) to a total pilot ridership of 14,506 (September 2023 – May 2024), a 35% increase—the highest among the five pilot routes weekday ridership table. Weekend ridership rose from 4,371 pre-pilot to 6,378 during the pilot, a 46% increase weekend ridership table.

Service performance metrics showed mixed results. The Q4's Service Delivered rate (percent of scheduled peak buses actually provided) dipped marginally from 97.5% pre-pilot to 97.4% during the pilot, a change of -0.1 percentage points service delivered table. End-to-end bus speeds decreased from 7.8 mph to 7.4 mph, a 4.8% decline—the largest speed decrease among the five pilot routes speed table. Customer Journey Time Performance (riders arriving within 5 minutes of schedule) fell from 78.1% to 74.6%, a 3.5 percentage point drop customer journey time table.
