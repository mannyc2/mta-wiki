The Q11 Local is a local bus route that operates along the Woodhaven Boulevard and Cross Bay Boulevard corridor in Queens, New York. It appeared on planning maps alongside the proposed Q52 and Q53 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes during public design workshops for the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard SBS project route reference. The route was also labeled as "Q11 Route" on discussion materials used at design workshops covering Woodhaven Boulevard, Cross Bay Boulevard, and the Broad Channel / Rockaways area Q11 on map Q11 on DW4 map Q11 on Cross Bay map.

Community feedback recorded during the SBS planning process specifically noted the importance of maintaining local bus service along the corridor, including the Q11 and Q21 routes important to maintain local bus service. At a public workshop in April 2014, participants identified the Q11 (along with Q21, Q52, and Q53) as a route that would benefit from transit signal priority improvements signal priority benefit.

At the intersection of Woodhaven Boulevard and Union Turnpike, the combined daily bus boardings for Q11, Q21, Q52, and Q53 totaled 3,627 as of a 2015 presentation, making it the third busiest Q52/Q53 bus stop on the corridor 3,627 daily bus boardings. The Q11 Local route was included in the route inventory materials for the series of public design workshops held in April 2015: Woodhaven Boulevard South (April 16), Woodhaven Boulevard North (April 23), and Cross Bay Boulevard (April 29) Q11 Route on slide Q11 Route on DW2 slide Q11 Route on DW3 slide.
