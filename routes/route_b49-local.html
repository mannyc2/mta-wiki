The B49 Local is a Brooklyn local bus route operating on Rogers Avenue and Bedford Avenue, providing local service on Rogers Avenue while the B44 SBS serves the same corridor with limited-stop Select Bus Service B49 service on Rogers Avenue. The B44 northbound SBS operates on Rogers Avenue alongside the B49 between Flatbush Avenue and Fulton Street B44 SBS operating with B49 on Rogers Avenue.

Although the B49 had no route or stop changes as part of the B44 SBS implementation in November 2013, bus lane and signal improvements on Rogers Avenue reduced travel times for B49 riders B49 benefitted from bus lane and signal improvements. In the bus lane section between Rogers Avenue at Foster Avenue and Bedford Avenue at Fulton Street, northbound B49 travel times decreased by 7% in the AM peak (from 26.5 to 24.6 minutes) and 11% in the PM peak (from 30 to 26.7 minutes), as measured between fall 2012 and fall 2015 B49 travel time table B49 travel time change by period.
