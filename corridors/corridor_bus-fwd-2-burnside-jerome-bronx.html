W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy, Bronx

corridor_bus-fwd-2-burnside-jerome-bronx

This corridor is listed in the BUSFORWARD 2017 plan as a local bus priority corridor where NYC DOT and the MTA planned to address issues affecting bus speed NYC DOT and the MTA will be addressing issues affecting bus speed on corridors such as the following. The corridor runs from W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy in the Bronx W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy.

This corridor is distinct from the 21 candidate SBS corridors identified elsewhere in the same plan; it was part of a separate local bus priority track that used tools from the bus priority toolkit such as bus lanes, bus stop spacing, and signal priority to improve travel speed and reliability Using tools from the bus priority toolkit, such as bus lanes, bus stop spacing and signal priority. The first round of these priority corridors was expected to undergo planning and outreach with implementation in 2018 and 2019 expected implementation in 2018 and 2019.
