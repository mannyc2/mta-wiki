Park Avenue between 46th and 50th Streets (Sector 2 area) designates an approximate 183,500 square foot Sector 2 Train Shed portion 183500 square_feet underneath Park Avenue and on certain side streets from East 46th Street to East 50th Street in Manhattan LOCATION block. The Train Shed functions as a bridge supporting the at-grade streets and avenues above the train platforms, with structural steel from the early 20th century that has significantly deteriorated Train Shed description.
