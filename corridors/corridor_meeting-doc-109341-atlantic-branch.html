Atlantic Branch (Atlantic Terminal to East New York)

corridor_meeting-doc-109341-atlantic-branch

This corridor covers the LIRR Atlantic Branch (Atlantic Terminal to East New York) segment where Rail Replacement and Maintenance occurred during the May 22 through September 5, 2023 timetable change. Atlantic Branch rail replacement description One of two main tracks was taken out of service on midday/weekdays between Atlantic Terminal and East New York to support rail replacement and maintenance. Atlantic Branch track outage description The resulting temporary service adjustments affected Brooklyn-bound trains in both directions. projects supported during this timetable Several related metrics document the specific service impacts: westbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 18 minutes earlier 18 minutes, some eastbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 11 minutes later 11 minutes, some eastbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 6 minutes earlier 6 minutes, and service to/from Nostrand Avenue and East New York reduced from 3 to 2 trains per hour reduced from 3 trains an hour to 2 trains an hour trains per hour. temporary service adjustments on Atlantic Branch The timetable change was presented to the Long Island Committee on April 24, 2023. LIRR informs Long Island Committee of schedule adjustments Continuing projects supported during this period included East Side Access, Main Line Expansion, West Side Yard Overbuild, and Mid-Suffolk Yard Construction. continuing projects listed Funding for the work was drawn from the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budgets. funding source
