E 163rd Street is a Bronx corridor included in the broader East 161st Street / Bx6 capital improvement project, with limits from Washington Avenue to Tiffany Street and a construction segment from Intervale Avenue to Tiffany Street March 2026 press release. The corridor serves approximately 25,000 daily riders 25000 riders_per_day on the Bx6 Select Bus Service (SBS) line and passes near Yankee Stadium, which draws more than three million visitors annually 3000000 visitors_per_year March 2026 press release.
