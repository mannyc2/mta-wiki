18 Av / Coney Island Av

corridor_18av-coney-island-av

18 Av / Coney Island Av was identified as one of 49 Brooklyn corridors studied for potential bus priority street improvements, and is among 17 top-ranked corridors in the MTA Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Draft Plan 49 corridors identified. The corridor was selected through a process that evaluated demand for bus service, bus performance, feasibility of street treatments, corridor role in the transit network, and neighborhood demographics evaluation criteria. It appears alongside other top-ranked Brooklyn corridors such as Flatbush Av, Nostrand Av, Utica Av, and Fulton St 17 top-ranked corridors list. The MTA is working with NYC DOT through the NYC Streets Plan to expand bus priority improvements on this and other corridors identified in the Draft Plan MTA-NYC DOT partnership NYC Streets Plan.

No route-level proposals or specific bus priority treatments for this corridor have been published in the Draft Plan beyond its identification as a study corridor. Further details may be developed through future public workshops and the Proposed Final Plan public workshops.
