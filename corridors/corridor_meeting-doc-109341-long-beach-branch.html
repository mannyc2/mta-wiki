LIRR Winter Trackwork ProgramsMay Timetable Change & Track Work Programs

The Long Beach Branch (Valley Stream to Long Beach) is an LIRR rail corridor on which the MTA Long Island Rail Road performs scheduled track maintenance and rail replacement work. In February 2023, both main tracks between Valley Stream and Long Beach were taken out of service for approximately 24 hours on Saturday, February 11, for track maintenance, with bus service replacing train service in both directions during that period February 2023 track maintenance on Long Beach Branch.

Later in spring 2023, rail replacement and signal maintenance was scheduled on weekends April 29-30, May 6-7, and May 20-21, with one of two main tracks on the Long Beach Branch out of service for 48-hour periods Spring 2023 rail replacement weekends on Long Beach Branch. Bus service replaced train service between Valley Stream and Far Rockaway during those 48-hour windows Spring 2023 rail replacement weekends on Long Beach Branch. These trackwork programs were presented to the Long Island Committee as part of the May 2023 timetable change and trackwork programs, with funding contained in the LIRR Operating and MTA Capital Program budget Funding source for trackwork projects.
