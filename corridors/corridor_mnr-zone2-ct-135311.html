Metro-North Railroad Procurements - March 2024

MNR Zone 2 (CT) is a geographic service zone defined by Metro-North Railroad for its emergency and scheduled bus services procurement, covering the territory from Bridgeport to the Connecticut state line along the Danbury and New Canaan Branches MNR Zone 2 (CT) definition. It is the second of five MNR zones established under Contract #426684, a competitively solicited miscellaneous service contract awarded in March 2024 MNR bus services procurement request.

The zone corresponds to MNR's Connecticut branch lines east of Bridgeport serving Danbury and New Canaan. Under this contract, the Connecticut Department of Transportation (CDOT) will reimburse MNR 100 percent for the costs of bus service on the New Canaan and Danbury branches (along with the Waterbury branch in Zone 1) CDOT reimbursement.

The overall five-year procurement carries a total estimated budget of $55 million 55000000 USD, with MNR's aggregate share at $30 million 30000000 USD and LIRR's at $25 million 25000000 USD aggregate budget breakdown. The RFP was advertised on June 13, 2023, 13 proposals were received 13 proposals on July 24, 2023, and the Selection Committee unanimously recommended 13 firms for award 13 firms RFP advertisement date proposals received recommended firms. Board approval was obtained on March 27, 2024 board approval date.

Five bus companies were designated to serve MNR-only territory under this contract: Leprechaun Lines, Inc. and Peter Pan Bus Lines, Inc., while eight additional firms serve both MNR and LIRR territories bus company territory table. The zone system was designed to leverage private resources for better geographic response and cost containment during both emergency disruptions and planned service outages emergency vs scheduled busing definitions.
