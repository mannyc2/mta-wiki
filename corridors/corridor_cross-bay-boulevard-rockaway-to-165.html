Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue

corridor_cross-bay-boulevard-rockaway-to-165

Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue is a Queens corridor segment that was covered by draft plans for the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard Select Bus Service project, presented at Public Design Workshop #3 on April 29, 2015 Draft plans for Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue. The corridor segment runs from Rockaway Boulevard to 165 Avenue in Queens Draft plans for Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue.

The Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard SBS project serves the Q52 and Q53 bus routes along this corridor Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard (Q52/Q53) Select Bus Service. The draft plans included specific treatment locations at Liberty Avenue, 109 Av, Linden Blvd, Pitkin Av, 157 Avenue, 160 Avenue, 163 Avenue, and 164 Avenue, as well as a traffic signage diagram for the segment between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue A Cross Bay Boulevard at Liberty Avenue B Cross Bay Boulevard at 109 Av A Cross Bay Boulevard at Linden Blvd B Cross Bay Boulevard at Pitkin Av A Cross Bay Boulevard at 157 Avenue B Cross Bay Boulevard at 160 Avenue Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue Diagram showing traffic signage for Cross Bay Boulevard between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue.

At 163 Avenue and 164 Avenue, the draft plans compared existing and proposed street layouts, each showing a total corridor width of 110 feet with lane configurations that included a bus stop and a bus lane in the proposal Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue.
