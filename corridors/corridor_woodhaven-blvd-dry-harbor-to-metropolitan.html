What's Happening Here? Woodhaven Boulevard Bus Lane ImprovemCB6 Transportation Committee Handout | February 3, 2016

This segment of Woodhaven Boulevard: Dry Harbor Road to Metropolitan Avenue in Queens received offset bus lanes (one lane away from the curb) installed by NYC DOT in August 2015, following the Woodhaven Blvd Congested Corridors Study DOT offset bus lane description bus only lanes installed August 2015. The bus lanes operate Monday through Friday from 7-10 AM and 4-7 PM, with parking remaining at the curb and vehicles permitted to enter the lane for right turns, curb cuts within 200 feet, parking access, or quick passenger pick-ups and drop-offs bus lane operating rules.
