May Timetable Change & Track Work Programs

This corridor segment covers the Montauk Branch between Amityville and Babylon, where LIRR performed Third Rail State of Good Repair work during the May 22, 2023 through September 5, 2023 timetable period Staff Summary purpose. One of two main tracks was taken out of service on midday/weekdays to allow third rail state of good repair work and maintenance Montauk Branch Amityville to Babylon work description. As a result, eastbound and westbound Babylon Branch trains were adjusted approximately 20 minutes earlier, and some Babylon Branch trains terminated or originated at Massapequa, with transfer opportunities available for customers traveling to stations Massapequa Park through Babylon Temporary service adjustments on Babylon Branch.

The work on this segment was part of a broader slate of projects supported during the same timetable period, which also included Signal Hut Installation on the Montauk Branch between Babylon and Sayville, and Rail Replacement and Maintenance on the Atlantic Branch between Atlantic Terminal and East New York Projects supported during this timetable. Continuing projects during this period included East Side Access, Main Line Expansion, West Side Yard Overbuild, and Mid-Suffolk Yard Construction Continuing projects. Funding for these projects was contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget Impact on funding.
