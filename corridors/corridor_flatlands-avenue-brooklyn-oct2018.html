B82 Southern Brooklyn Select Bus Service

The Flatlands Avenue corridor is a segment of the B82 Select Bus Service (SBS) route in Southern Brooklyn, spanning Flatlands Avenue from Avenue I to Ralph Avenue Existing: Flatlands Ave (Ave I to Ralph Ave). The corridor is served by both B82 SBS and B82 Local routes B82 SBS service launched on 10/01/18.

The B82 SBS service launched on October 1, 2018, replacing the B82 Limited and serving approximately 28,000 daily riders 28000 riders on the SBS and Local combined Serves 28,000 daily riders. Citywide, Select Bus Service has delivered 10-30% faster bus speeds 10-30% faster bus speeds percent, about a 10% increase in ridership 10 percent, and customer satisfaction of 95% 95 percent 10-30% faster bus speeds.

The intersection of Flatlands Avenue and Ralph Avenue was a Vision Zero Priority Intersection with the highest number of crashes on the B82 corridor, recording 103 crashes 103 crashes and 134 injuries 134 injuries from 2010 to 2014 103 crashes 134 injuries. The existing five-leg configuration, complicated by Paerdegat Avenue South, created long pedestrian crossings and poorly synchronized traffic signals Large 5-leg intersections.

Proposed treatments for the Flatlands Avenue corridor include closing a slip lane at Ralph Avenue to simplify the intersection, adding left-turn bans (northbound and eastbound), increasing pedestrian crossing time by 4 to 13 seconds at all crosswalks, installing a pedestrian island, and adding a curbside bus lane active 7 AM to 7 PM Monday through Saturday Increased pedestrian crossing time at all crosswalks. Between Avenue I and Ralph Avenue, the plan also includes making Avenue I one-way with new angled parking, longer green lights with better signal coordination, a bus lane/right-turn lane, and shorter pedestrian crossings Bus lane/right turn lane.
