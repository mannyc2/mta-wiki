MNR Zone 1 (CT)

corridor_mnr-zone1-ct-135311

MNR Zone 1 (CT) covers the territory from New Haven to Bridgeport, serving the Waterbury Branch, as defined in a March 2024 MNR procurement agenda for emergency and scheduled bus services MNR Zone 1 definition. This is one of five MNR operating zones established for bus service coverage across the railroad's Connecticut and New York territories MNR Zone table. The Connecticut Department of Transportation reimburses MNR 100 percent for the costs of bus service on the Waterbury Branch (along with the Danbury and New Canaan branches in Zone 2) CDOT 100% reimbursement. The broader bus-services procurement, Contract #426684, carries a total estimated aggregate budget of $55,000,000 55000000 USD, with $30,000,000 30000000 USD allocated to MNR across all zones and $25,000,000 25000000 USD to LIRR, over a five-year contract term 5 years Budget and term summary. MNR proposed the procurement on behalf of itself and LIRR to furnish emergency and scheduled bus services during track outages and service disruptions MNR procurement request.
