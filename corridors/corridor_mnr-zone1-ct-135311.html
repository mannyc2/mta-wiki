Metro-North Railroad Procurements - March 2024

MNR Zone 1 (CT) covers the territory from New Haven to Bridgeport, serving the Waterbury Branch, as defined in a March 2024 MNR procurement agenda for emergency and scheduled bus services MNR Zone 1 definition. This is one of five MNR operating zones established for bus service coverage across the railroad's Connecticut and New York territories MNR Zone table. The Connecticut Department of Transportation reimburses MNR 100 percent for the costs of bus service on the Waterbury Branch (along with the Danbury and New Canaan branches in Zone 2) CDOT 100% reimbursement.
