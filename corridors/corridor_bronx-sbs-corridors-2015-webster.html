Bronx SBS Corridors

corridor_bronx-sbs-corridors-2015-webster

This corridor record is derived from a map legend page within the 2015 Webster SBS Service Plan, published by NYC DOT LEGEND. The plan's legend identifies a set of Bronx SBS Corridors that encompass the Bx41 and Bx12 routes, showing both local and SBS variants Bronx SBS Corridors. On the map, the Bx41 route is depicted in dark purple and the Bx12 route in light blue Map description.

Transit treatments labeled on the corridor map include SBS Stations (purple circles with a white plus sign), Subway Connections (green circles), and Metro-North Connections (black circles with the MTA logo) SBS Station Subway Connection Metro-North Connection. The Bx41 Local and Bx41 SBS are both listed as corridor routes, as are Bx12 Local and Bx12 SBS Bx41 Local Bx41 SBS Bx12 Local Bx12 SBS.

The map covers the Webster Avenue corridor through the Bronx, including major streets such as East 149th Street, East Tremont Avenue, East Fordham Road, and East Gun Hill Road, with landmarks like Claremont Parkway, Bedford Park Boulevard-Botanical Garden, and Williams Bridge noted Map description. This source is a single-page legend and overview map, not a detailed service or implementation plan. The corridor record's scope is narrow — the routes and treatments shown reflect what was depicted on this specific planning map rather than a comprehensive corridor inventory.
