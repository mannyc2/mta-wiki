Metro-North Railroad Procurements - March 2024

MNR Zone 4 (NY) is the southernmost New York MNR service zone, covering the Hudson line from Croton Harmon and south and the Harlem line from White Plains and South MNR Zone 4 definition. It is one of five MNR zones defined in a March 2024 procurement for emergency and scheduled bus services (Contract #426684), alongside Zone 1 (CT), Zone 2 (CT), Zone 3 (NY), and Zone 5 (NY) zone table. The zone boundaries serve as the geographic basis for distributing bus service assignments among the 13 recommended vendor firms selected through a competitive RFP process 13 recommended firms zone-based distribution.

The zone was defined as part of a broader procurement led by MNR on behalf of itself and the LIRR, with a total estimated aggregate budget of $55 million over a five-year term ($30 million for MNR, $25 million for LIRR) total budget. The MNR Zone 4 (NY) corridor is adjacent to MNR Zone 3 to the north and the LIRR service area to the south and east, which covers Nassau, Western Suffolk, Eastern Suffolk, Queens, King, and New York Counties adjacent zones.

Since the zone definition originates from a procurement agenda document rather than a formal service planning study, the precise station-by-station limits within the described line segments are not specified in the source source limits.
