The Roosevelt Av / Broadway Av segment is the northernmost portion of the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards SBS corridor in Queens Corridor design summary map. As presented to Community Board 6 in May 2015, this segment does not include bus lanes; instead, the proposed treatment consists of improved curbside bus stops No bus lanes – Improved curbside bus stops. Unlike the Woodhaven Boulevard mainline to the south, which received main-road bus lanes and median stations Main road bus lanes – All buses use median stations, the Roosevelt Av / Broadway Av segment is shown as a grey (no-treatment-change) segment on the corridor design summary map Roosevelt Av / Broadway Av: The northernmost segment, shown in grey. The overall corridor serves the Q52/Q53 limited route and carries approximately 30,000 daily bus riders 30000 riders/day 30,000 daily bus riders.
