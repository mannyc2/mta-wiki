Amtrak Hell Gate Line

corridor_hell-gate-line-amtrak

This corridor is Amtrak's right of way for the Hell Gate Line (the "Hell Gate ROW") in the Morris Park section of the Bronx, adjacent to Bassett Avenue. Amtrak Hell Gate Line corridor The Hell Gate ROW will serve as the trackage for the Penn Access Project, a new Metro-North Railroad link to Penn Station that will provide one-seat passenger rail service for New Haven Line customers. PSA project description

The platform for the future Morris Park Station will be sited within the Hell Gate ROW, with a pedestrian overpass connecting the platform to the south station entrance. Station platform location The overpass is the only viable connection above Bassett Avenue, which is a private road serving as the sole ingress and egress to 1502 Bassett Avenue—a property improved with a 367,000 square foot 367000 square feet last-mile distribution facility leased to Amazon. Property and Bassett Avenue description
