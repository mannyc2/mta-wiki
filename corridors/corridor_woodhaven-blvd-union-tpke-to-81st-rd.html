Woodhaven Boulevard: Union Turnpike to 81st Road

corridor_woodhaven-blvd-union-tpke-to-81st-rd

This corridor lies along Woodhaven Boulevard in Queens from Union Turnpike to 81st Road Woodhaven Boulevard from Union Turnpike to 81st Rd. It is the segment on which NYC DOT proposed to reconstruct the roadway and implement bus and safety improvements as part of the Q52/Q53 Select Bus Service Project Q52/Q53 SBS improvements. The stated project goals are to improve bus travel times, improve pedestrian safety, organize vehicle movements, and simplify traffic Improve Bus Travel TimesImprove Pedestrian SafetyOrganize Vehicle MovementsSimplify Traffic.

The planned design calls for removing service road medians to create a simpler central median with pedestrian refuge, maintaining three vehicular traffic lanes in each direction, installing curbside bus-only lanes that allow right turns to the next intersection or driveway, restricting left turns from Woodhaven Boulevard to Union Turnpike, and creating a turn bay for U-Turns at 81st Road to maintain access to Union Turnpike list of proposed treatments. Implementation was announced to begin mid-September, though the flyer does not specify the year Implementation begins Mid-September. The flyer was published by the New York City Department of Transportation NYC DOT logo.
