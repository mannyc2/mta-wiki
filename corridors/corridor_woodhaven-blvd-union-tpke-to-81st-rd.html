What's Happening Here? - Woodhaven Boulevard Q52/Q53 SBS Imp

This corridor lies along Woodhaven Boulevard in Queens from Union Turnpike to 81st Road Woodhaven Boulevard from Union Turnpike to 81st Rd. It is the segment on which NYC DOT proposed to reconstruct the roadway and implement bus and safety improvements as part of the Q52/Q53 Select Bus Service Project Q52/Q53 SBS improvements. The stated project goals are to improve bus travel times, improve pedestrian safety, organize vehicle movements, and simplify traffic Improve Bus Travel TimesImprove Pedestrian SafetyOrganize Vehicle MovementsSimplify Traffic.
