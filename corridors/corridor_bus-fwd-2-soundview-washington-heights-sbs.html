Soundview to Washington Heights (SBS candidate F)

corridor_bus-fwd-2-soundview-washington-heights-sbs

The Soundview to Washington Heights (SBS candidate F) corridor is a proposed Select Bus Service candidate in the Bronx, identified in NYC DOT's BUSFORWARD 2017 plan BUSFORWARD 2017 as candidate F in the Bronx borough section Soundview to Washington Heights candidate corridor. It was one of 21 candidate SBS corridors citywide selected through data analysis and public feedback by NYC DOT and the MTA for the next phase of SBS expansion 21 candidate corridors identified. The candidate list was developed in response to 2015 and May 2017 City Council legislation calling for an updated SBS plan and broader transit improvements 2015 legislation May 2017 legislation.

Across the SBS program, existing SBS routes have demonstrated 10-30% faster bus speeds, about 10% ridership increases, and 95% customer satisfaction SBS performance results. The existing SBS network carried over 309,000 daily riders (12% of city bus ridership), and with the proposed additions, over 800,000 riders would be on routes with fast, reliable service, representing over 32% of city bus ridership SBS ridership metrics. No specific route alignment, length, or ridership data for this corridor has been published in the BUSFORWARD 2017 plan beyond its identification as a candidate corridor. The adjacent Bronx corridors identified in the same plan include Tremont Avenue (candidate D), University Avenue (candidate C), and Harlem to Southern Boulevard (candidate E).
