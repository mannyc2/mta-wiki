Flatlands Av / Glenwood Rd / Cozine Av

corridor_flatlands-av-glenwood-rd

Flatlands Av / Glenwood Rd / Cozine Av is one of 17 top-ranked corridors among 49 Brooklyn corridors identified by NYC DOT for potential bus priority street improvements. 49 corridors studied 17 top-ranked corridors list The NYC DOT evaluation, conducted in collaboration with the MTA, ranked corridors based on demand for bus service, bus performance, feasibility of implementing new street treatments, the corridor's role in the transit network, and neighborhood demographics and equity metrics. Selection criteria NYC DOT-MTA collaboration This corridor is listed alphabetically among the top-ranked group and joins other Brooklyn corridors such as Flatbush Av, Fulton St, Nostrand Av, and Utica Av. Listed alphabetically Full corridor list The identification of these corridors supports the NYC Streets Plan goal of expanding bus priority street improvements such as dedicated bus lanes, busways, queue jump intersections, and transit signal priority. Bus priority toolkit As of the December 2022 Draft Plan, no specific bus priority treatments or implementation timeline had been announced for this corridor; further study, planning, and public outreach would be needed to advance specific street interventions.
