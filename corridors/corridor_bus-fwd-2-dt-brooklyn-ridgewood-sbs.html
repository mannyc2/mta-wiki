This corridor is one of 21 candidate corridors identified by NYC DOT and the MTA for the next phase of Select Bus Service, as part of the Bus Forward 2017 citywide plan 21 candidate SBS corridors identified. Listed as corridor "I" and labeled "Downtown Brooklyn to Ridgewood," it is one of five Brooklyn candidate SBS corridors in the plan Brooklyn corridors list. The corridor was identified through data analysis and public feedback including workshops, on-street surveying, and online outreach identification process.
