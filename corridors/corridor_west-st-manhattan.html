West St

corridor_west-st-manhattan

West St is a street in Lower Manhattan adjacent to Battery Pl, where curb regulations were updated to keep the curb lane clear, a treatment that was marked as completed Improved TEA placement and instruction / Updated curb regulations on West St. An aerial photograph of the Battery Park area shows annotations including three blue stars along West St and a "NO STOPPING" red box overlaid on the street West St on aerial photograph of Battery Park area.

Traffic delays accessing West Street were identified as one of several issues in the Battery Pl area during an interagency study by MTA, NYCDOT, and NYPD Issues Identified on Battery Pl. The study found that the Battery Pl corridor serves 15 express bus routes with 190 trips during the 4-7 pm peak period 190 trips and over 5,500 daily customers 5500 customers 4-7 pm peak period sees 190 trips serving over 5,500 daily customers.

The adjacent Battery Pl was identified as a Better Buses Action Plan priority project, part of a citywide effort to improve bus speeds 25% by 2020 25 percent launched in April 2019 Better Buses Action Plan released April 2019. A presentation covering these efforts was made to Manhattan Community Board 1 on October 21, 2019 Manhattan Community Board 1 | 10.21.2019. Beyond the curb regulation update on West St and improved TEA placement, further study recommendations included stop consolidation and a queue jump signal at Battery Pl and Broadway Stop consolidation on Broadway, Battery Pl / Queue jump signal on Battery Pl @ Broadway.
