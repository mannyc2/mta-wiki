BUSFORWARD 2017

This corridor is SBS candidate corridor "A" in the Bronx, listed as "Broadway to Central Bronx" in the Bus Forward 2017 plan 21 SBS candidate corridors. It was identified by NYC DOT and the MTA as one of 21 candidate corridors across all five boroughs for the next phase of the Select Bus Service program, selected through data analysis, public workshops, on-street surveying, and online feedback 21 candidate corridors identified. The broader Bus Forward effort to grow the SBS network focused on improving job access and providing service on trips not well served by subway and rail systems key issues for SBS growth. A separate Bus Forward listing of corridors for local bus-speed interventions mentions a "Broadway, Bronx" corridor with segments from W 225th St to W 231st St and from W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy; that list addresses issues affecting bus speed and is distinct from the SBS candidate corridor A labeled "Broadway to Central Bronx" corridors for addressing bus speed issues. The existing SBS network carried over 309,000 daily riders (12% of citywide bus ridership), and the proposed expansion, including this corridor, would bring SBS service to over 800,000 daily riders — 32% of city bus ridership existing and proposed SBS ridership. SBS routes citywide have shown 10-30% faster bus speeds, about a 10% ridership increase, and 95% customer satisfaction SBS performance metrics.
