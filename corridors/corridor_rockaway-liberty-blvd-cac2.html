Rockaway / Liberty Boulevard

corridor_rockaway-liberty-blvd-cac2

Rockaway / Liberty Boulevard is a Queens intersection corridor identified in NYC DOT discussion materials for the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards Select Bus Service project Woodhaven / Cross Bay Blvd & Rockaway / Liberty Blvd. The corridor appears in the SBS Community Advisory Committee Meeting #2 discussion materials dated October 22, 2014, alongside the main Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards corridor Meeting #2 title. At this intersection, three conceptual bus priority designs were presented: Concept 1 (Offset Bus Lanes) Concept 1 label, Concept 2 (Main Road Bus Lanes) Concept 2 label, and Concept 3 (Median Busway) Concept 3 label. All three concepts were explicitly labeled as "CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION" CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION, indicating they were early-stage proposals not yet finalized. The corridor record's borough is Queens located in Queens. This corridor is closely related to the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards SBS project, which serves the Q53 route.
