Interborough Express Route

corridor_interborough-express-route-meeting-doc-171486

Interborough Express Route is documented on page 13 of an MTA Board Meeting presentation from April 30, 2025. The corridor runs through Brooklyn, starting at Roosevelt Avenue and proceeding south through Grand Avenue, Eliot Avenue, Metropolitan Avenue, Myrtle Avenue, Wilson Avenue, Atlantic Avenue, Sutter Avenue, Livonia Avenue, Linden Blvd, Remsen Avenue, Utica Avenue, Flatbush Av., Nostrand Av., East 16 Street, McDonald Avenue, New Utrecht Avenue, 8 Avenue, and 4 Avenue, ending at Brooklyn Army Terminal.

The Interborough Express was presented under the "Strengthen & Expand the Network" theme and is listed as a sample project in the 2025-2029 MTA Capital Plan alongside other vital transportation projects. The presentation indicates the NYS Budget to Fund Capital Plan as the funding mechanism.

The same Board Meeting presentation reported that the MTA budget has shrunk 3% since 2019, with annual spending declining from $16.6 billion in 2019 16.6 billion dollars to $16.1 billion in the 2025 Final Proposed Budget (in real 2019 dollars).

This corridor page is sourced from a single presentation slide; the route description comes from OCR alt-text describing a map and no precise station locations or alignment type (e.g., rail, bus, or dedicated guideway) are specified in the source.
