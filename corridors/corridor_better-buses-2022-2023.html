The Better Buses corridor installations 2022-2023 covers 23.1 miles of new and upgraded bus lanes installed by NYC DOT in 2022 and 2023, following the program's launch in 2019 Better Buses report. Installations included University Avenue and Gun Hill Road in the Bronx, 1st Avenue, 3rd Avenue, and Avenues A and D in Manhattan, 21st Street and Northern Boulevard in Queens, and Livingston Street in Brooklyn Better Buses report. The 23.1-mile total (Bus lane miles installed 2022-2023 23.1 miles) documented in the NYC DOT Bus Lane Camera Enforcement 2024 report covers both new and upgraded bus lane mileage Better Buses report. DOT stated that it prioritizes bringing bus-mounted and stationary cameras to new bus priority projects as soon as feasible after implementation Better Buses report.

The Better Buses program was designed by DOT to extend the goals of Select Bus Service to a broader set of bus corridors beyond those that received the full SBS treatment Better Buses report.
