LIRR Service County - NYC Boroughs

corridor_lirr-counties-nyc-135311

This corridor captures the New York City borough counties served by the Long Island Rail Road as defined in a March 2024 MTA Board procurement action for emergency and scheduled bus services under Contract #426684 Discussion of MNR and LIRR bus service procurement. The LIRR territory within NYC is listed as Queens, King, and New York Counties, corresponding to the boroughs of Queens, Brooklyn, and Manhattan LIRR service territory table. These three counties are part of a six-county LIRR service area that also includes Nassau, Western Suffolk, and Eastern Suffolk on Long Island LIRR service territory table.

The corridor is documented as a zone definition in a competitively solicited miscellaneous service contract with a Total Estimated Budget - $55,000,000 55000000 USD and a Five-Year Contract Term 5 years, of which the LIRR portion is LIRR Estimated Budget - $25,000,000 25000000 USD Budget breakdown. Metro-North Railroad (MNR) administered the procurement on behalf of both railroads, with the MNR share estimated at MNR Estimated Budget - $30,000,000 30000000 USD MNR acting on behalf of LIRR. The RFP attracted 13 Proposals Received 13 proposals by July 24, 2023, leading to 13 Recommended Firms 13 firms covering various territory combinations across MNR zones and LIRR counties Selection committee recommendation.

The bus companies assigned to LIRR-only territory include Bus Around Town Corp. d/b/a Coachman Luxury Transport, D&J Service, Inc., Huntington Coach Corp., M&V Corporate Transportation, and Suffolk Transportation Services, Inc., while Academy Express, Charter Bus Pros, Community Coach, DPV Transportation, J&F Tours Transportation, and Red Carpet Coach Ltd. serve both MNR and LIRR territories Territory coverage table.
