M60 Select Bus Service - Community Board 1 Briefing

The Astoria Boulevard corridor in Queens carries the M60 SBS route between the RFK Bridge and the LaGuardia Airport area. As presented at a June 2013 Community Board 1 briefing, the proposed M60 SBS route begins in Manhattan at 125th Street, crosses the RFK Bridge, and follows Astoria Boulevard before branching toward the LGA terminals M60 proposed SBS route map. The M60 SBS was planned to stop at major intersections in Astoria and East Elmhurst along this corridor M60 SBS stops at major intersections in Astoria and East Elmhurst.

As of the June 2013 briefing, other street design changes on Astoria Boulevard were still in development Other street design changes on Astoria Blvd still in development. The next steps listed for the project included developing Astoria Boulevard recommendations Develop Astoria Boulevard recommendations. The M60 SBS service context at the time showed growing ridership — from 14,408 average weekday riders in 2007 to 17,358 in 2012 Average Weekday M60 Ridership table — and buses were stopped 45% of the time, with heavy double-parking in Manhattan contributing to bunching and delays that affected Queens operations Buses are stopped 45% of the time Heavy double parking in Manhattan leads to bus bunching/delays in Queens.
