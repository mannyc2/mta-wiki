Flatlands Avenue is a corridor in Brooklyn running from E 103 Street to Pennsylvania Avenue Key design pieces. This segment was identified as part of the B82 Select Bus Service project and is described as a wider portion of Flatlands with concrete medians Existing: Flatlands Ave. Three city bus routes and one express bus route use this portion of Flatlands Avenue, providing a transit connection from Pennsylvania Avenue/Starrett City to the Canarsie-Rockaway Parkway station wider portion of Flatlands.

The proposed design for the B82 SBS project included bus lanes on Flatlands Avenue from E 103 Street to Pennsylvania Avenue, operating 7 AM to 7 PM Monday through Saturday Bus Lanes on Flatlands Ave. The corridor was also slated for a widen/reconfigure travel lanes treatment as part of the project's street design package map illustrates proposed bus lane treatments. The B82 SBS route traverses Flatlands Avenue eastward after running north along Kings Highway, continuing to Pennsylvania Avenue proposed B82 SBS route.

The corridor was presented to Brooklyn Community Board 5 in April 2017 and again in January 2018 as part of iterative community outreach on the B82 SBS design CB 5 Transportation Committee January 22, 2018 presentation.
