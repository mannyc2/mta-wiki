Far Rockaway Branch

corridor_meeting-doc-109341-far-rockaway-branch

The Far Rockaway Branch is a LIRR branch that experienced a complete weekend service suspension for rail replacement and signal maintenance in spring 2023. In a staff summary to the Long Island Committee dated April 11, 2023, the MTA Long Island Rail Road described plans for track work on the weekends of April 29-30, May 6-7, and May 20-21 during which both main tracks on the Far Rockaway Branch would be out of service for 48-hour periods while rail was replaced and signal maintenance was performed Far Rockaway Branch rail replacement and maintenance description. During those weekends, bus service replaced train service between Valley Stream and Far Rockaway bus replacement service between Valley Stream and Far Rockaway. This Far Rockaway Branch track work was part of a set of Special Programs described alongside a broader May 22, 2023 through September 5, 2023 timetable change that also included Third Rail State of Good Repair work on the Montauk Branch, Signal Hut Installation, and Atlantic Branch rail replacement projects supported during timetable. Funding for the track work was contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget funding source.
