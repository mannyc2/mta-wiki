This segment of the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards corridor at Queens Blvd and Hoffman Dr was described in a May 2015 presentation to Community Board 6 as part of the proposed Select Bus Service (SBS) project, with planned improvements including designated bus-only station areas and enhanced bus stops and transfers Queens Blvd and Hoffman Dr – Designated bus-only station areas – Improved bus stops / transfers. The corridor design summary placed this segment between Roosevelt Av / Broadway Av (which had no bus lanes) to the north and Woodhaven Blvd (which received main road bus lanes with median stations) to the south Roosevelt Av / Broadway Av Woodhaven Blvd.
