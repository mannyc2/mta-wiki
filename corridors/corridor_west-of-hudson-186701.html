West of Hudson

corridor_west-of-hudson-186701

West of Hudson is the Metro-North Railroad service territory west of the Hudson River, with operations distinct from the railroad's three East-of-Hudson lines (Harlem, Hudson, and New Haven). The July 2025 Mid-Year Forecast from MTA Metro-North Connection reports West of Hudson Farebox Revenue 11.754 $ in millions of $11.754 million for the January-through-December period, alongside West of Hudson Ridership 1.134 millions of 1.134 million riders over the same span p007_c0002. For context, the combined East-of-Hudson farebox revenue totaled $643.975 million and ridership reached 70.898 million over the same period, making West of Hudson service a relatively small share of Metro-North's overall financial picture p007_c0002. The corridor receives subsidy support through both MTA Subsidy 710.867 $ in millions ($710.867 million across all Metro-North cash operations) and ODOT Subsidy 265.187 $ in millions ($265.187 million), with the ODOT funding reflecting a reimbursement arrangement between the Ohio Department of Transportation and Metro-North p005_c0002. The corridor is served by a dedicated maintenance workforce reported as "Metro-North West" positions, which ranged from 29 to 31 monthly throughout the forecast year p008_c0002.
