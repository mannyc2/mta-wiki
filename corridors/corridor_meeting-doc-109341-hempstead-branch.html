Hempstead Branch (Floral Park to Hempstead)

corridor_meeting-doc-109341-hempstead-branch

The Hempstead Branch segment between Floral Park and Hempstead is a LIRR corridor where bridge waterproofing work was performed at Cherry Valley Bridge in Garden City in May 2023 Cherry Valley Bridge waterproofing on Hempstead Branch. This corridor runs from Floral Park to Hempstead, and the work was part of a Special Program within the broader May 22, 2023 timetable change that also included rail replacement, signal hut installation, and continuing projects such as East Side Access and Main Line Expansion May 22 timetable change period projects supported during this timetable.

On Saturday, May 13, 2023, both main tracks on the Hempstead Branch between Floral Park and Hempstead were taken out of service for approximately 24 hours while the Cherry Valley Bridge in Garden City was waterproofed both main tracks out for 24-hour waterproofing. During that period, bus service replaced train service in both directions bus replacement during bridge work. The corridor's relevance to this document is limited to this bridge waterproofing event; the document otherwise primarily describes service adjustments on the Atlantic, Babylon, Montauk, Long Beach, and Far Rockaway branches Hempstead Branch Cherry Valley Bridge waterproofing mentioned.

Funding for all projects described in the document, including the Hempstead Branch bridge work, was contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budgets funding source.
