Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn Corridor

corridor_bushwick-downtown-brooklyn-brt-phase2

The Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn Corridor was identified as one of 16 potential future BRT corridors during the BRT Phase II planning study conducted by MTA New York City Transit and the New York City Department of Transportation Table listing Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn as BRT Phase II candidate. The study, initiated in fall 2008, evaluated more than 40 potential corridors against five criteria: community feedback, ridership potential, network benefits, bus lane and busway feasibility, and geographic diversity Phase II study initiation Evaluation criteria.
