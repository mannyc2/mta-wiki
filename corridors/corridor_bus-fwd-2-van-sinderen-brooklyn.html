Van Sinderen Av/Fulton St to Jamaica Av/Georgia Av, Brooklyn

corridor_bus-fwd-2-van-sinderen-brooklyn

This corridor appears in the BUSFORWARD 2017 report as one of several local bus priority corridors in Brooklyn where NYC DOT and the MTA planned to address issues affecting bus speed. The report, published by NYC DOT p1, identified the corridor as running from Van Sinderen Av/Fulton St to Jamaica Av/Georgia Av p11 table. It was listed under a separate "Improving Local Bus Corridors" initiative, distinct from the 21 candidate SBS corridors identified elsewhere in the same report p5. The Bus Forward program slated a first round of these local priority corridors for planning and outreach over the following year, with expected implementation in 2018 and 2019 p10. At the time of the report, average bus speed citywide was 7.4 mph 7.4 mph and dropped to less than 4 mph in busy commercial districts p3. The corridor list was noted as subject to change throughout the planning and evaluation process p11. No specific implementation status or further details about this particular corridor have been recorded beyond its inclusion in the initial priority list.
