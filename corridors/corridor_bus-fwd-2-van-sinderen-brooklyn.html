BUSFORWARD 2017

This corridor appears in the BUSFORWARD 2017 report as one of several local bus priority corridors in Brooklyn where NYC DOT and the MTA planned to address issues affecting bus speed. The report, published by NYC DOT p1, identified the corridor as running from Van Sinderen Av/Fulton St to Jamaica Av/Georgia Av p11 table. It was listed under a separate "Improving Local Bus Corridors" initiative, distinct from the 21 candidate SBS corridors identified elsewhere in the same report p5. The Bus Forward program slated a first round of these local priority corridors for planning and outreach over the following year, with expected implementation in 2018 and 2019 p10.
