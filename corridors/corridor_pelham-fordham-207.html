Pelham Parkway, Fordham Road, West 207 Street Corridor

corridor_pelham-fordham-207

The Pelham Parkway, Fordham Road, and West 207 Street corridor runs approximately 3.1 miles from Eastchester Avenue to Broadway, spanning three connected streets that form a major east-west bus corridor across the Bronx Corridor profile in 2019 Final Plan. It serves a combined daily route ridership of 86,000 across routes including the Bx9, Bx12, Bx12 SBS, Bx17, Bx22, and Bee-Line routes 60 through 62 Routes and ridership. Both the 2019 Bronx Bus Network Redesign Final Plan and its 2021 Addendum classify this corridor as a "Future Plan" corridor, meaning dedicated bus priority treatments had not yet been implemented as of the addendum's publication Status table listing Future Plan.
