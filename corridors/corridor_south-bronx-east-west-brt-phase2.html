Bus Rapid Transit Phase II: Future CorridorsBUS RAPID TRANSIT PHASE II: Future Corridors

The South Bronx East-West Corridor (Hunts Pt./Soundview) was identified as a BRT Phase II candidate through a joint NYCT and NYC DOT planning study initiated in fall 2008 a planning study of a citywide BRT network was initiated in the fall of 2008. The study compiled over 40 potential BRT corridors 40 corridors from a citywide transit needs analysis and public feedback, then evaluated them on five primary criteria: community feedback, ridership potential, network benefits, bus lane and busway feasibility, and geographic diversity NYCT and DOT compiled a list of over 40 potential BRT corridors.

Public outreach in spring 2009 included seven workshops across the city with over 325 workshop participants 325 people, representatives from 47 community and business groups 47 groups, staff from 23 local elected officials' offices 23 offices, and over 600 online survey respondents 600 people Over 325 people participated in the workshops, including representatives from 47 community and business groups, and staff members from the offices of 23 local elected officials.

From this effort, NYCT and DOT identified 16 potential future corridors, with the South Bronx East-West Corridor listed as number 4 among the BRT Phase II Candidates — distinct from the short list of three corridors (Webster Avenue/Third Avenue, Hillside Avenue, and LaGuardia/East Elmhurst) selected for the next detailed planning effort Table of Routes ... 4 South Bronx East-West Corridor (Hunts Pt./Soundview). The corridor was identified in both the full "Future Corridors" report and the "BRT Phase II Future Corridors" report Table of Routes ... 4 South Bronx East-West Corridor (Hunts Pt./Soundview). Further planning would depend on available funding, community support, and progress on related transit projects, with each corridor expected to receive a Community Advisory Committee and public workshop process For each corridor, NYCT and DOT will create a Community Advisory Committee.
