Staten Island stationary camera corridors

corridor_stationary-staten-island

Through 2023, Hylan Boulevard was the only Staten Island corridor where New York City DOT installed stationary bus lane cameras as part of the citywide enforcement program Staten Island Hylan Boulevard*. The asterisk indicates that mobile ABLE (Automated Bus Lane Enforcement) cameras were also deployed on this corridor through 2023, supplementing the fixed camera coverage Mobile (ABLE) cameras also in use through 2023. Citywide, 188 fixed camera locations were installed at stationary sites through 2023, with 371 cameras (two per site) monitoring bus lane compliance fixed bus lane cameras 188 locations 990 unique cameras 371 stationary 619 on-bus. The report also lists the S79-SBS among routes with mobile ABLE camera coverage, so Staten Island appears in both the fixed-corridor and on-bus enforcement inventories ABLE routes S79-SBS.
