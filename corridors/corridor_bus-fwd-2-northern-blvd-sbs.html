Northern Boulevard (SBS candidate Q)

corridor_bus-fwd-2-northern-blvd-sbs

Northern Boulevard was identified as a candidate corridor for the next phase of Select Bus Service (SBS), labeled as corridor "Q," in the BUSFORWARD 2017 plan published by NYC DOT Queens corridor list Q Northern Boulevard. The 21 candidate corridors citywide were selected by NYC DOT and the MTA based on data analysis and public feedback through workshops and on-street surveying 21 candidate corridors identified. At the time of the plan, the existing SBS network carried over 309,000 daily riders (12% of city bus ridership), and the proposed expansion would bring that to over 800,000 daily riders (32% of city bus ridership) SBS network ridership figures. The SBS program had demonstrated the ability to deliver 10-30% faster bus speeds, roughly 10% ridership increases, and 95% customer satisfaction across implemented routes SBS performance metrics. This corridor is one of several Queens candidates in the plan, alongside Ridgewood to Flushing (M), Flushing to Springfield Boulevard (N), Hillside Avenue (O), Union Turnpike (P), Southeast Queens (R), Jamaica to Flushing via Kissena Boulevard (S), and Queens Access to JFK (T) Queens candidate corridors M through T. No specific route alignment, treatment plan, or implementation timeline for Northern Boulevard was detailed in the BUSFORWARD 2017 document beyond its identification as a candidate SBS corridor.
