Cross Bay Boulevard

corridor_cross-bay-blvd-liberty-109av-2015

This corridor is the Cross Bay Boulevard segment within the larger Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards Select Bus Service planning effort, as documented in draft plans presented at Public Design Workshop #1 on April 16, 2015 Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard (Q52/Q53) Select Bus Service. The corridor lies in Queens, with two locations shown on the page 4 plan sheets: at Liberty Avenue and at 109 Avenue A Cross Bay Boulevard at Liberty Avenue B Cross Bay Boulevard at 109 Av. The draft plan document covers Woodhaven Boulevard between Union Turnpike and Rockaway Boulevard, and the Cross Bay Boulevard segment represents the southern portion of the combined corridor served by Q52 and Q53 buses Draft plans for Woodhaven Boulevard between Union Turnpike and Rockaway Boulevard. This corridor record is scoped to the two Cross Bay Boulevard cross-section locations shown in the Design Workshop #1 materials; additional locations along the same corridor may be represented in companion corridor records.
