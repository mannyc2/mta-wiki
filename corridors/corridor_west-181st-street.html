Bronx Bus Network Redesign Final Plan Addendum

West 181st Street, between Amsterdam Avenue and Broadway, was one of the first corridors to receive a bus priority treatment under the Bronx Bus Network Redesign, with a Busway completed in April 2021 by NYC DOT completed bus priority projects. The corridor table in the Final Plan Addendum lists its status as "Completed 2020" and identifies five routes operating along the segment: Bx3, Bx11, Bx13, Bx35, and Bx36 West 181st Street table row. The West 181st Street Busway was one of three bus priority projects NYC DOT completed in the Bronx between fall 2020 and spring 2021, alongside bus lanes on East 149th Street (October 2020) and center-running bus lanes on E.L. Grant Highway (November 2020) completed bus priority projects.

The corridor was selected through an NYC DOT analysis of 46 corridors in the Bronx and Manhattan, which identified 10 of the highest-ranking corridors for bus priority treatments including bus lanes, transit signal priority, bus boarders, and curb management 46-corridor analysis. A note on the source evidence: the corridor status field reads "Completed 2020," while the narrative on page 6 states the Busway was completed in April 2021, suggesting the table date may reflect the analysis or designation year rather than the construction completion date.
