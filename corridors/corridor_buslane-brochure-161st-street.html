The NYC DOT "Bus Lanes in NYC" brochure identifies 161st Street as an example of a street with center-running or median bus lanes in the Bronx Center-running or median bus lanes heading. The brochure describes center-running bus lanes as being "in the middle of the roadway" and states that "vehicles are not permitted to enter the bus lanes for any reason while the bus lane is in effect," with right-turns, pick-ups, and drop-offs directed to the service road Center-running bus lane description. The other streets cited alongside 161st Street as examples of this bus lane type are Woodhaven Boulevard and Kings Highway Center-running bus lane description.
