LIRR Service County - Long Island

corridor_lirr-counties-long-island-135311

This corridor represents the LIRR Service County - Long Island territory as defined in a March 2024 MTA Board procurement request for emergency and scheduled bus services LIRR Long Island zone definition. The source block establishes a broader LIRR territory than the page title suggests: Nassau, Western Suffolk, Eastern Suffolk, Queens, King, and New York Counties all appear in the same zone definition LIRR Long Island zone definition. That makes the record useful for the Long Island counties, but the cited procurement table also reaches into the NYC borough counties in the same source row corridor zone table.

This corridor was referenced in the procurement justification for Contract No. 426684, which sought Board approval to award competitively solicited contracts for emergency and scheduled bus services MNR on behalf of LIRR request. Metro-North Railroad proposed the procurement on behalf of both MNR and LIRR, collectively referred to as "the Railroads" MNR on behalf of LIRR request. The contracts carry a five-year term with an estimated aggregate budget of $55 million 55000000 USD, of which $25 million 25000000 USD is allocated to LIRR aggregate budget breakdown. The RFP was publicly advertised on June 13, 2023, and 13 proposals were received by July 24, 2023 RFP advertisement dateproposals received date. The Selection Committee recommended 13 firms for contract award 13 recommended firms.
