This corridor covers Manhattan Avenue between 110th Street and 116th Street in Manhattan, with a study area that also includes Morningside Avenue between 113th and 114th Streets Proposed bus lane map Study area description.

The corridor carries over 40,000 daily bus passengers across the M3, M7, and M116 routes Bus passenger volume. The M116 bus travels at less than 4 mph in some segments due to congestion and double-parking Bus speed problem Speed map.
