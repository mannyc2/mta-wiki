East Gun Hill Road Corridor

corridor_east-gun-hill-rd

The East Gun Hill Road Corridor runs on East Gun Hill Road from Bainbridge Avenue to Bartow Avenue in the Bronx, covering 2.3 miles. Corridor metrics The corridor serves routes Bx28, Bx30, and Bx38, with a combined daily route ridership of 23,000. Corridor metrics The 2021 Bronx Bus Network Redesign Addendum lists this corridor as a "Future Plan" corridor, with Bx30 noted as a current route on the alignment. Corridor status table

For context on the broader network, the existing average bus stop spacing across the Bronx 882 feet was 882 feet. Average stop spacing metric
