The corridor_kings_highway_e23_st_to_ave_k is the segment of Kings Highway from E 23 St to Ave K in Brooklyn, described as a wide, boulevard-style portion of the street with narrow, deteriorating medians that serve as bus stops and large, complex intersections p029_c0001 p029_c0002. It functions as a major east-west transit corridor, serving the B82 Local, B82 Limited, B7 Local, school buses, and coach buses; during AM and PM rush hours, buses run approximately once per minute along Kings Highway p029_c0002.
