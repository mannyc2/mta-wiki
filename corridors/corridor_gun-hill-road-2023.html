BUSFORWARD 2017Minutes of Regular Meeting Committee on Operations November New York City Transit Key Performance Metrics - November 202

The wave 13 meeting-doc evidence treats Gun Hill Road as a completed Bronx bus-priority corridor, not just a generic location reference. NYCT reported standing with NYC DOT for the October 31 completion of 3.1 miles of bus lanes on Gun Hill Road, serving the Bx28, Bx38, Bx41, and Bx41-SBS and linking Co-Op City to Montefiore Hospital. [meeting_doc_127471#p005_c0007] [meeting_doc_127471#p006_c0002]

A later committee summary repeats the completion point in broader bus-performance language: bus priority lanes and busways were described as key tools to speed trips and reduce waits, with the Gun Hill Road lane expected to benefit nearly 40,000 crosstown customers. The same wave produced other Gun Hill project pages, so the corridor/project identities should be manually reviewed before any future deduplication pass. [meeting_doc_128931#p003_c0011]
