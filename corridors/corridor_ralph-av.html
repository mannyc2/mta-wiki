Ralph Av / E 98 St / New Lots Av

corridor_ralph-av

Ralph Av / E 98 St / New Lots Av is a top-ranked Brooklyn bus priority corridor identified by NYC DOT, in collaboration with the MTA, as part of the Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Ralph Av / E 98 St / New Lots Av listed among top-ranked corridors MTA and NYC DOT collaboration on bus priority corridors. The corridor was one of 17 that ranked highest in the NYC DOT evaluation process, drawn from a broader set of 49 corridors studied for potential bus priority street improvements 49 corridors identified, 17 top-ranked corridors ranked highest in evaluation. The NYC DOT analysis assessed corridors based on demand for bus service, bus performance, feasibility of implementing street treatments, the corridor's role in the transit network, and neighborhood demographics and equity metrics criteria for corridor identification. As a top-ranked corridor, Ralph Av / E 98 St / New Lots Av is a candidate for future study, public outreach, design, and implementation of bus priority treatments such as dedicated bus lanes, busways, queue jump intersections, and transit signal priority toolkit of potential bus priority improvements.
