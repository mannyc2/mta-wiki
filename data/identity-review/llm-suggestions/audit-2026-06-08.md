## Policy Follow-Up

Add an explicit identity rule for route ids with `+` suffixes:

- Treat ACE/BusTime-style `+` route ids as probable SBS-coded variants, not as a generic limited-stop or separate "plus" service, when the same base route has an SBS record and source context is ACE, ABLE, bus-mounted enforcement, or MTA route datasets.
- Still keep local/limited/base-route records distinct from SBS route records unless source evidence says the service converted and the record should be merged or marked predecessor/successor.
- Composite labels such as `Q10/Q80` should not become a single route identity; split or model them as coverage/context records.
