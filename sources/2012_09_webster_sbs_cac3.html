### [p018_c0001] SBS Overview
- [p018_c0002] ■ Bx41 LTD → Bx41 SBS - ½ mile stop spacing - Service will run frequently all day ■ Bx41 Local - No change to stop spacing - Service every ~10 minutes ■ 4 miles of offset bus lanes with bus bulb stations ■ For the entire route: - Low-floor buses - Off board fare collection - Station and bus branding - Transit signal priority - Pedestrian safety improvements
