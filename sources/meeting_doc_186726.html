### [p001_c0008] PURPOSE:
### [p001_c0009] Hudson, Harlem and New Haven Lines – Effective Sunday, October 5, 2025
[p001_c0010] 
Metro-North is updating schedules on the Hudson, Harlem, and New Haven Lines to improve service reliability and accommodate ongoing infrastructure projects. These changes support major switch replacements, track and tie renewals, and the continuation of the Park Avenue Viaduct and Walk Bridge replacement projects.
### [p001_c0011] OCTOBER 5, 2025, TIMETABLE CHANGE
### [p001_c0012] Service Improvements
- [p001_c0013] HUDSON LINE: New “Super-Express” Trains to and from Poughkeepsie Station: Starting Sunday, October 5, five trains will make the trip in under 90 minutes. PM Peak Adjustments: Select weekday evening trains to be adjusted for better spacing during Park Avenue Viaduct track work. Weekend Adjustments: Minor modifications to weekend outbound trains for better spacing during Park Avenue Viaduct two track outages.

### [p002_c0003] Holidays
### [p002_c0004] THANKSGIVING HOLIDAY WEEKEND – 11/26/2025 – 11/30/2025
[p002_c0005] 
Metro-North will operate a special four-day service plan for the Thanksgiving holiday weekend (Wednesday, November 26, through Sunday, November 30). The schedule is designed to meet the travel needs of our customers and will be available via our website, our Call Center/IVR, and third-party apps such as TrainTime. As in prior years, off-peak fares will apply throughout the entire four-day period.

### [p003_c0001] Staff Summary
### [p003_c0002] • Thanksgiving Day – 11/27/2025
[p003_c0003] 
Metro-North will provide:
- [p003_c0004] ○ Expanded inbound morning service for customers traveling to the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade. ○ Additional outbound service late morning through mid-afternoon for customers traveling to suburban destinations. ○ Expanded evening service for those returning to New York City.
[p003_c0005] 
Between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, fares from Grand Central will be collected before customers board trains ("gate collection"). This process ensures every customer has a valid ticket prior to boarding. Metro-North anticipates providing more than 100,000 rides on Thanksgiving Day.
### [p003_c0006] • Friday, the day after Thanksgiving – 11/28/2025

Cited by 1 record

[p003_c0007] 
With fewer commuters and significantly higher discretionary travel, Metro-North will operate on a modified schedule:
- [p003_c0008] ○ A special expanded Saturday Schedule will be in effect, including additional service during Traditional AM and PM peak hours. ○ On the Harlem Line, trains that normally originate or terminate at Crestwood will instead extend to/ from Scarsdale, Hartsdale, White Plains, and North White Plains.
### [p003_c0009] • Saturday, November 29 & Sunday, November 30, 2025 – Thanksgiving Weekend
[p003_c0010] 
Saturday and Sunday service will operate as scheduled, with additional cars added to select trains to accommodate holiday travel demand.
### [p003_c0011] • CHRISTMAS-NEW YEAR'S WEEK (Tuesday 12/22 – Monday 12/30)
[p003_c0012] 
Historically, commuter ridership decreases during the week between Christmas and New Year's. However, mid-day inbound ridership between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM increases by more than 100% compared with typical weekdays. This increase reflects higher discretionary travel, as schools are on holiday recess and many shoppers take advantage of after-Christmas sales.

Cited by 2 records

[p003_c0013] 
To accommodate this shift in demand, Metro-North will:
- [p003_c0014] ○ Suspend select morning rush-hour trains. ○ Operate 6 additional mid-day trains (2 Hudson, 1 Harlem, 3 New Haven). ○ Adjust train lengths to accommodate ridership levels.
