## Page 11

### [p011_c0001] Corridor Description
[p011_c0002] 9.3 miles from Williamsburg Bridge to Sheepshead Bay
[p011_c0003] Currently served by B44 bus route
- [p011_c0004] • 44,000 weekday riders – 5 th busiest bus route in city • 11,000 people get on in CB3 • Buses travel at an average speed of 7-8 mph
[p011_c0005] Within a ¼ mile:
- [p011_c0006] • 300,000 residents • 62% of households do not own a car (71% in CB3) • 60% of residents commute by transit (66% in CB3)

### [p012_c0001] Project Features
[p012_c0002] B44 Limited will become SBS; B44 Local will continue as before
[p012_c0003] Offset bus lanes with bus bulbs at stations for 5 miles
[p012_c0004] Transit Signal Priority for 3.8 miles from Flatbush Avenue to Sheepshead Bay

- [p012_c0006] • New low-floor buses • Next generation fare collection • Branding of stations and buses
