### [p003_c0007] Overview
- [p003_c0008] YTD January costs were $17M or 18% below the Adopted Budget. Major drivers of the favorable non-reimbursable YTD variance, including underruns in COVID-19 response, lower SAP requirements and departmental controls at NYCT; lower maintenance requirements, tightened management procedures and weather at the LIRR; lower maintenance, less traffic congestion and unscheduled service at MTA Bus; favorable weather, revised T&E crew requirements, and a budget allocation adjustment at MNR; lower requirements and managerial efficiencies at B&T; and revised MTAPD deployment assumptions at MTAHQ. The favorable reimbursable YTD variance is resulted of lower capital support at NYCT; less capital project activity at MNR and the LIRR; and higher coverage requirements for MTAPD at MTA HQ.
