### [p003_c0001] Bx6 Background
[p003_c0002] 
The Bx6 South Bronx corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p003_c0003] • Serves nearly 25,000 daily riders • Connects to 1 , C , 4 , B , D , 2 , 5 , 6 subway lines, Metro-North, and 20 bus routes, including Bx41 SBS • 76% of households within a quarter-mile of the route do not own a vehicle

### [p004_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p004_c0002] 
Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
[p004_c0003] 
SBS has brought:
- [p004_c0004] • 10-30% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p004_c0005] 
There are 12 Select Bus Service routes in operation serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p004_c0006] Map of New York City showing 12 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes in operation, serving all 5 boroughs. The map highlights the following routes: Bx41 (Webster Av) Bx12 (Fordham Rd) Q44 (Main St / Cross Bronx Service Rd) M60 (125th St / Astoria Blvd / LGA) M86 (86th St) M34/M34A (34th St) M23 (23rd St) M15 (1st Av / 2nd Av) B46 (Utica Av) B44 (Nostrand Av / Rogers Av) S79 (Hylan Blvd) Q70 (BQE / LGA) The map also shows the boroughs: The Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Staten Island, and Manhattan.
