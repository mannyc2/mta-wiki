### [p001_c0002] Service Changes: Spring 2024 Bus Schedules and Terminal Stop Changes
[p001_c0003] Sarah Wyss , Interim Chief, Operations Planning
### [p001_c0004] Service Issue
[p001_c0005] To ensure that customers are provided with efficient bus service and that resources are effectively allocated, bus schedules and stops are regularly reviewed, evaluated based on current rider demand, operating and traffic conditions, other relevant issues, and MTA Board-adopted loading guidelines.
[p001_c0006] The changes being proposed at this time are modifications to the weekday schedules of the B82, Bx19, Q3 and Q29, as shown in Attachment 1.
### [p001_c0007] Recommendation
[p001_c0008] Implement the proposed changes to the weekday schedules of the B82, Bx19, Q3 and Q29 as shown in Attachment 1.
### [p001_c0009] Budget Impact
[p001_c0010] For the B82, Bx19, and Q3, which are operated by New York City Transit, the total cost impact of the proposed changes is $89,400. For the Q29, which is operated by MTA Bus, the cost impact of the proposed changes is $167,600.
### [p001_c0011] Proposed Implementation Date
[p001_c0012] April 2024

### [p002_c0006] Purpose
[p002_c0007] To obtain the President's approval for and to inform the New York City Transit and MTA Bus Committee of proposed schedule changes to the B82, Bx19, Q3 and Q29.
### [p002_c0008] Discussion
[p002_c0009] To ensure that customers are provided with efficient bus service and that resources are effectively allocated, bus schedules and stops are regularly reviewed, evaluated based on current rider demand, operating and traffic conditions, other relevant issues, and MTA Board-adopted loading guidelines.
[p002_c0010] The proposed schedule changes are modifications to the weekday schedules of the B82, Bx19, Q3 and Q29, as shown in Attachment 1. The frequency of service will increase on the Bx19 and decrease on the B82 and Q3. Running time will increase on the B82, Q3 and Q29.
### [p002_c0011] Recommendation
[p002_c0012] Implement the proposed changes to the weekday schedules of the B82, Bx19, Q3 and Q29, as shown in Attachment 1.
### [p002_c0013] Alternatives
[p002_c0014] Do not make the proposed changes.
