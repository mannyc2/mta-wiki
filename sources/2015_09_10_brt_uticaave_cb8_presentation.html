### [p002_c0001] Project Background
[p002_c0002] The Utica Avenue B46 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p002_c0003] • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Busiest bus route in Brooklyn, carrying nearly 50,000 passengers per day • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains and 30 bus routes • Vision Zero Priority Corridor • 7 pedestrians killed and 57 pedestrians severely injured (2009-2013)
> [p002_c0004] Residents' Mode to Work Mode Percentage Transit 61% Driving 24% Walk / Bicycle 10% Other 5% Source: 2008-2012 ACS 5-Year Estimates ¼ mile from B46 corridor

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p003_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
[p003_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p003_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p003_c0005] There are 8 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p003_c0006] The map illustrates the Select Bus Service (SBS) routes across New York City. The routes are depicted as blue lines on a map of the city and its surrounding areas. The boroughs shown are Staten Island, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Manhattan. The surrounding counties are Westchester, Nassau, and Nassau County. The eight SBS routes are labeled as follows: Bx12 : Fordham Rd / Pelham Pkwy (in Westchester County) Bx41 : Webster Av (in The Bronx) M60 : 125 St / Astoria Blvd (in Queens) M86 : 86th St (in Queens) M34/34A : 34th St (in Queens) M15 : First Av / Second Av (in Queens) B44 : Nostrand Av / Rogers Av (in Brooklyn) S79 : Hylan Blvd (in Staten Island) A north arrow is located in the bottom right corner of the map.

### [p005_c0001] Existing Street Design Plan
### [p005_c0002] St. Johns Place to Church Avenue (Implemented Summer 2014)
- [p005_c0003] • Added bus lanes, left-turn bays, and turn restrictions • B46 Limited and local travel times decreased 8% to 15%* • Vehicle travel times decreased 20% to 25% in peak direction*
### [p005_c0004] Church Avenue to Fillmore Avenue (Implementing August-September 2015)
- [p005_c0005] • Adding bus lanes, left-turn bays, turn restrictions, and pedestrian islands • Adding truck loading zones, updating curb regulations
[p005_c0006] *AM and PM peak
