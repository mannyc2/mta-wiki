## Page 25

### [p025_c0001] Ticketing policies on commuter railroads
- [p025_c0002] ▶ A reduced fare ticket for seniors, people with disabilities, and people on Medicare will be valid 24/7, even in the morning peak ▶ Allow children ages 5-17 to ride for $1 when accompanied by a fare-paying adult, even in the morning peak ▶ Maintain manual activation on one-way tickets, valid until 4 a.m. the next day ▶ A new Day Pass for unlimited daily travel ▶ After 10 one-way trips, automatically get an 11 th one-way ticket for free in 14 days ▶ Recurring late mobile ticket purchasers and late activators will be subject to a surcharge (just like paper tickets purchased onboard)
[p025_c0003]
