### [p003_c0007] Overview
- [p003_c0008] YTD February costs were $4M or 2% below the Adopted Budget. Major drivers of the unfavorable non-reimbursable YTD variance include higher-than-expected weather-related events and higher vacancy/absentee coverage requirements, partially offset by lower maintenance requirements, and reduced traffic congestion/lower unscheduled service at MTA Bus; revised MTAPD deployment assumptions at MTAHQ; lower requirements and managerial efficiencies at B&T; and revised T&E crew requirements at MNR. The favorable reimbursable YTD variance results from lower capital support at NYCT; less capital project activity at MNR and the LIRR; partially offset by higher coverage requirements for MTAPD at MTA HQ.
