AGENCY: MTA Metro-North Railroad ("MNR") PERMITTEE: Town of Ossining ("Town") LOCATION: Ossining Station, Town of Ossining, Westchester County, New York ("Station") PREMISES: Parking lots 14a and 14b (112 spaces total) on the West Side of the Station USE: Use of Station Parking Lot for Ingress and Egress to Public Street TERM: Saturday, April 20, 2024, 5:00 am to 7:30 pm COMPENSATION: $1; payment waived
### [p002_c0009] COMMENTS:

Cited by 5 records

[p002_c0010] 
Pursuant to the Board-approved policy on October 24, 2002, governing the short-term licensing of railroad facilities by municipal and not-for-profit corporations for non-commercial activities which allows for the issuance of short-term permits for these types of uses provided that no single event exceeds 72 hours, the Town has been granted permission to utilize 112 parking spaces in Lots 14a and 14b at the Station for the Town's annual Earth Day event on Saturday, April 20, 2024, scheduled from 5:00 am to 7:30 pm.

Cited by 3 records

[p002_c0011] 
MTA Legal drafted the Short-Term Permit Agreement as to form, the Town has provided appropriate insurance coverage and indemnification, and MTA Real Estate has fully executed the agreement.
