### [p003_c0006] 5. PRESIDENT’S REPORT
[p003_c0007] 
President Davey delivered the President’s Report.

Cited by 1 record

[p003_c0008] 
President Davey reported that the MTA had received an FTA “Champion of the Challenge” award for its Zero-Emission Bus Transition Plan.

Cited by 4 records: Zero-Emission Bus Transition Plan +3 more

[p003_c0009] 
President Davey commented that the Faster, Cleaner Safer initiatives continue to put the focus on customer experience. He noted as an example that the coming expansion of the automated bus lane enforcement camera program will increase bus speeds and bus customer satisfaction. He also referred to record high subway on-time performance for three straight months.

Cited by 4 records: Automated Bus Lane Enforcement Camera Program Expansion, Faster, Cleaner and Safer Strategic Action Plan +2 more

[p003_c0010]
