## Page 8

### [p008_c0001] Woodhaven / Cross Bay SBS corridor
- [p008_c0002] • Based on the existing Q52/53 Limited bus route • 30,000+ daily bus riders • 14 miles long from Woodside to the Rockaways • Important north/south transit corridor for Queens • Provides connections to 8 subway lines, 20+ bus routes, and LIRR • CB9 covers 2 of the top 5 busiest Q52/53 stops
> [p008_c0003] A map of the Woodhaven / Cross Bay SBS corridor in Queens, New York. The route is highlighted with a blue dashed line, starting at Woodside (LIRR) in the northwest, passing through Elmhurst, Rego Park, and Woodhaven, then crossing the Cross Bay Bridge to the Rockaways. The map shows major roads like Broadway, Roosevelt Ave, Queens Blvd, Myrtle Ave, Atlantic Blvd, and Rockaway Blvd. Key locations include Jackson Heights, Middle Village, Ozone Park, Howard Beach, and Arverne. The CB9 bus line is specifically highlighted in blue. The map also shows the Jamaica Bay and the Broad Channel.

[p011_c0002] Transform Woodhaven and Cross Bay Boulevards into a complete street where:
