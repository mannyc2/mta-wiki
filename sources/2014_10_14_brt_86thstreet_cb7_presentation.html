### [p004_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p004_c0002] 
Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p004_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p004_c0004] • 15-20% faster bus speeds • 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Improved passenger comfort and convenience

Cited by 1 record

[p004_c0005] 
There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
