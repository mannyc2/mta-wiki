### [p003_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p003_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p003_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p003_c0004] • 15-20% faster bus speeds • 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Improved passenger comfort and convenience
[p003_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p003_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 7 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes. ### [p010_c0001] Project Background
[p010_c0002] The Utica Avenue (B46) bus corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the “Bus Rapid Transit: Phase II Study” (2009).
- [p010_c0003] • Ranked by community as one of two corridors most in need of bus improvements • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Carries nearly 50,000 passengers per day, making it the second-busiest bus route in NYC • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains • Connection to 30 bus routes including B35, B6, B12 • B35-to-B46 transfer is the most frequent bus-to-bus transfer in NYC

### [p011_c0001] Project Background Safety Concerns
[p011_c0002] Utica Avenue has among the highest rates of injury from motor vehicle crashes in Brooklyn.
- [p011_c0003] • 5 fatalities and 54 severe injuries between 2008 and 2012 • 60% of vehicles travel above the 30mph speed limit

### [p012_c0001] 2014 Bus Priority and Safety Improvements
### [p012_c0002] Transit
- [p012_c0003] • Offset and curbside bus lanes on 1.3 miles of Utica Av corridor from St. John's Pl to Church Av
### [p012_c0004] Traffic and Safety
- [p012_c0005] • Left-turn restrictions to improve pedestrian safety and left-turn bay additions to maintain traffic flow
### [p012_c0006] Parking
- [p012_c0007] • +19 parking spaces near Eastern Parkway (within 3 blocks) • -22 parking spaces along southern part of corridor (~1 per block) • New loading zones to accommodate various businesses

### [p014_c0001] Utica Avenue Select Bus Service
[p014_c0002] Build on existing Bus Priority and Safety Project to upgrade B46 Limited to B46 SBS. Utica Avenue SBS will include:
- [p014_c0003] • Off-board fare collection for full route • Additional bus lanes • Transit signal priority • Service planning changes to improve reliability • Capital improvements at SBS stations ▪ Real-time passenger information ▪ Bus bulbs (sidewalk extensions at bus stops) ▪ New shelters, plantings, benches, etc.

[p016_c0003] The Community Advisory Committee (CAC) will be composed of:

- [p016_c0007] 1. Act as consistent representatives during the project planning process. 2. Provide input as design and implementation progresses. 3.
