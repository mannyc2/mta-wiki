## Page 2

### [p002_c0001] What's changed since the December financial plan?
[p002_c0002] In the State budget, Governor Hochul and the legislature provided $65 million to bring the planned 2023 fare change in line with the standard biennial 4% increase in fare revenue , rather than 5.5%.
[p002_c0003] The planned 5.5% toll revenue increase remains in place.
