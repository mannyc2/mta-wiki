### [p002_c0001] Nostrand Ave/Rogers Ave Corridor
[p002_c0002] 9.3 miles from Williamsburg Bridge to Sheepshead Bay
[p002_c0003] Currently served by B44 bus route
- [p002_c0004] • 42,000 weekday riders – 7th busiest bus route in city • 3,500 people board in CB 8 • Buses travel at an average speed of 7-8 mph
[p002_c0005] Within a 1/4 mile:
- [p002_c0006] • 300,000 residents • 62% of households do not own a car (70% in CB 8) • 60% of residents commute by transit (67% in CB 8, compared to 19% by car, truck, or van)
