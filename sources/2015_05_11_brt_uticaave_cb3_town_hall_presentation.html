### [p004_c0001] Project Background
[p004_c0002] The Utica Avenue B46 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p004_c0003] • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Busiest bus route in Brooklyn, carrying nearly 50,000 passengers per day • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains and 30 bus routes • Vision Zero Priority Corridor • 7 pedestrians killed and 57 pedestrians severely injured (2009-2013)

## Page 9

### [p009_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p009_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p009_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p009_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p009_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p009_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 7 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes. The routes are highlighted in blue and labeled as follows: Bx12: Fordham Rd/ Pelham Pkwy (Westchester County) Bx41: Webster Av (The Bronx) M60: 125 St./Astoria Blvd. to LGA (The Bronx) M34/M34A: 34 St (The Bronx) M15: First Av/ Second Av (Queens) B44: Nostrand Ave/ Rogers Ave (Brooklyn) S79: Hylan Blvd (Staten Island) The map also shows the boroughs: Westchester County, The Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Bay Ridge, Staten Island, and Nassau County. ### [p015_c0001] Utica Avenue Street Design
### [p015_c0002] Summer 2014
- [p015_c0003] • Added bus lanes, left-turn bays, and turn restrictions from St. Johns Place to Church Avenue • B46 Limited and Local travel times decreased 8% to 15%* • Vehicle travel times decreased 20% to 25% in peak direction*
### [p015_c0004] Summer 2015
- [p015_c0005] • Add bus lanes, left-turn bays, turn restrictions from Church Avenue to Fillmore Avenue • Add three-block queue jump northbound-only curbside bus lane from Bergen Street to Atlantic Avenue • Add truck loading zones, update curb regulations • No bus lanes for Malcolm X Boulevard
[p015_c0006] *AM and PM peak
