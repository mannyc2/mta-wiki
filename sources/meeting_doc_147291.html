## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Budget results for first six months of 2024
- [p003_c0002] • 2024 first half overall operating revenue and expenses are modestly better than budget by 0.7% and 1.1%, respectively
### [p003_c0003] Revenues
- [p003_c0004] • Subway and bus revenue below budget by 5.5% primarily due to fare evasion • Commuter rail revenue better than budget driven by MNR at 10% above budget • B&T revenues better than budget by 4% due to greater traffic on bridges and tunnels • Dedicated tax receipts are below budget by 5.5% - continued decline in real estate related taxes in the MTA region
### [p003_c0005] Expenses
- [p003_c0006] • Overall operating expenses below budget by 1.1% • Operating efficiencies of over $400 million on target to be delivered
