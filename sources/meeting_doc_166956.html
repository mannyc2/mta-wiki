### [p001_c0008] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0009] Schedules will change on the Hudson, Harlem and New Haven lines effective Sunday, March 30, 2025.
[p001_c0010] The March 30, 2025, Schedules continue to support major C&D, CDOT and Maintenance of Way projects that require extensive track outages on all three lines including Switch replacements, Track and Tie renewals, the continuation of Park Avenue Viaduct Span renewal and Walk Bridge replacement.
### [p001_c0011] March 30, 2025, TIMETABLE CHANGE
### [p001_c0012] Service Improvements
- [p001_c0013] • HUDSON LINE: ○ Train 818 will now depart Poughkeepsie Station at 6:08 AM and will have a total run time of 94 minutes between Poughkeepsie and GCT. ○ Train 710 will now make a Regular Station Stop (S) at Marble Hill Station. ○ Train 736 will now make Regular Station Stop (S) at Yankee Stadium – 153 rd ST. ○ Minor adjustments to weekday outbound trains later in the PM-Peak for better run times. ○ Two additional inbound trains added from Croton-Harmon to Yankees – 153 rd ST during Yankee Schedule K/L (Trains 7772 & 7774) ○ The following weekend inbound Poughkeepsie trains will have stops removed from Ossining and Tarrytown Stations: 8820, 8824, 8828, 8832, 8856 & 8860. ○ The following weekend Outbound Poughkeepsie Trains will have stops removed from Ossining and Tarrytown Stations: 8807, 8811, 8815, 8819, 8839, 8841, 8845, 8849, 8851 & 8855.
