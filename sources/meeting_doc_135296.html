### [p009_c0001] Grand Central Terminal Elevator & Escalator Map
> [p009_c0002] The map illustrates the layout of Grand Central Terminal, with elevators and escalators marked by blue callouts. The terminal is bounded by Madison Ave to the west, Lexington Ave to the east, Park Ave to the south, and 42nd St to the north. Key areas include the Hyatt Hotel, Vanderbilt Hall, Grand Central Market, and the Train Master's Office. Elevators are labeled NE-1 through NE-12, and escalators are labeled T-6, 7, 8, T-13, and T-18, 19, 20. The map also shows the 47th Street Cross Passage and the 45th Street Cross Passage (below). Street names and their corresponding numbers are provided along the perimeter of the terminal.
