### [p014_c0001] Project Benefits
[p014_c0002] 
Over the length of the corridor, bus lanes, transit signal priority, and quicker boarding would lead to 25% faster trips.

Cited by 1 record

[p014_c0003] 
A trip from Empire Blvd to Flatbush Ave would be about 6 minutes faster, from 24 minutes today to 18 minutes with SBS.

Cited by 2 records

[p014_c0004] 
Bus bulbs at SBS stations would allow easy, level boarding, with more pedestrian space and greenery.

Cited by 2 records

[p014_c0005] 
Bus service would be more reliable, with less bus bunching.
