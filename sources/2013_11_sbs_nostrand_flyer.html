## Page 2

### [p002_c0001] USER GUIDE
### [p002_c0002] BUS RIDERS
- [p002_c0003] ▪ B44 SBS replaces B44 limited bus service ▪ B44 local service remains the same ▪ SBS fare = local bus fare
### [p002_c0004] How do I pay my fare on SBS?
- [p002_c0005] ▪ Pay on the sidewalk at a Metrocard or coin machine (shown above) before boarding the bus ▪ Take your receipt and keep it while you ride ▪ Board the bus from any of the three doors—no need to show your receipt to the bus operator
[p002_c0006] A roving team of fare inspectors board buses to check receipts. Passengers without a valid receipt are subject to a $100 fine.
### [p002_c0007] DRIVERS
[p002_c0008] When a bus lane is in effect, stay out of the lane except to:
- [p002_c0009] ▪ Park on that block ▪ Turn right at the next corner ▪ Quickly drop off or pick up passengers
### [p002_c0010] Bus Lane Hours
- [p002_c0011] ▪ Most bus lanes are one lane out from the curb, and are in effect from 7 AM to 7 PM, Mon-Fri ▪ Some areas have different bus lane hours. Always follow the posted signs.
### [p002_c0012] Truck Loading Windows
- [p002_c0013] ▪ To reduce truck doubleparking in commercial areas of Nostrand Avenue, the curb will be reserved for truck loading and unloading: ◦ 10 AM to noon on the east side of the street ◦ Noon to 2 PM on the west side
### [p002_c0014] NYC enforces bus lanes with:
- [p002_c0015] ▪ Video cameras ▪ Increased police enforcement
[p002_c0016] $115 to $150 fine if you drive, park, or stand in an active bus lane.
### [p002_c0017] NORTHBOUND BUS SERVICE
> [p002_c0018] The diagram shows the Northbound Bus Service route. It starts at Flatbush Av and travels north on NEW YORK AVENUE . Stops on New York Avenue include St. Johns Pl Stations , Empire Blvd Stations , Clarkson Av Stations , Church Av Stations , and Avenue D/ Newkirk Stations . A dashed green line indicates the Northbound B44 Local will remain on NEW YORK AVENUE . The route then branches onto ROGERS AVENUE , with stops at Fulton St Stations , Medgar Evers College , and Brooklyn College . A blue line with arrows indicates the direction of travel. A blue square with a white 'H' represents Kings County & SUNY Downstate Hospitals .
[p002_c0019] ----- Northbound B44 Local will Remain on New York Avenue
- [p002_c0020] ▪ Northbound B44 SBS will travel on Rogers instead of New York Avenue for faster service. ▪ The B44 local will continue to serve all stops on New York Avenue, while the B49 will provide local service on Rogers Avenue
### [p002_c0021] ONGOING CONSTRUCTION
> [p002_c0022] The diagram shows the area of ongoing construction on NOSTRAND AVENUE . The route is shown as a blue line with arrows. Stops include Williamsburg Bridge Plaza/ Bus Transfer Hub , Taylor St Stations , Hewes St Stations , Flushing Av Stations , DeKalb Av , Fulton St Stations , and St. Johns Pl Stations . Streets shown include ROEBLING STREET , TAYLOR STREET , LEE AVENUE , BEDFORD AVENUE , and LAFAYETTE Av . A blue shaded area indicates the construction zone on Nostrand Avenue.
- [p002_c0023] ▪ SBS service will operate through the ongoing street reconstruction on Nostrand Avenue from Flushing to Atlantic Avenues ▪ This project will build SBS stations at DeKalb Avenue and Fulton Street, as well as bus lanes.
