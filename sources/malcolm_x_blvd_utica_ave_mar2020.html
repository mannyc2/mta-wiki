### [p004_c0001] BACKGROUND: B46 SELECT BUS SERVICE
- [p004_c0002] • B46 is Brooklyn's busiest bus route (38,000 daily riders) • Connects Bed-Stuy to 3 4 A C J Z + 30 bus routes • Upgraded to Select Bus Service in 2016 • B46 SBS upgraded to articulated buses in January 2020
