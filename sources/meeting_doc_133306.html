### [p002_c0011] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0012] Key Performance Metrics Reports
[p002_c0013] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p002_c0014] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0015] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0016] Work Plan
[p002_c0017] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0018] President's Report
[p002_c0019] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0020] Chief's Report
[p002_c0021] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0022] Safety Report
[p002_c0023] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators
### [p002_c0024] Police Report
[p002_c0025] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.

[p003_c0001] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0002] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0003] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0004] Financial Report
[p003_c0005] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0006] Procurements
[p003_c0007] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0008] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0009] FEBRUARY 2024
### [p003_c0010] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2024
[p003_c0011] The Agency will present its revised 2024 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2024 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2024 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2024 Adopted Budget.
### [p003_c0012] 2023 Year in Review
[p003_c0013] A review of the prior year's performance of railroad service will be provided to the Committee.
### [p003_c0014] March Timetable Change & Spring Trackwork Programs
[p003_c0015] The Committee will be advised of plans to adjust schedules.
### [p003_c0016] MARCH 2024
### [p003_c0017] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p003_c0018] Annual report to the Committee on system-wide availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
### [p003_c0019] Spring Trackwork Programs
[p003_c0020] The Committee will be advised of plans to adjust schedules.
### [p003_c0021] APRIL 2024
### [p003_c0022] 2023 Annual Ridership Report
[p003_c0023] A report will be presented to the Committee on Agency ridership trends during 2023 based on monthly ticket sales data and the results of train ridership counts conducted by the Agency.
### [p003_c0024] May Timetable Change & Trackwork Programs
[p003_c0025] The Committee will be advised of plans to adjust schedules.
### [p003_c0026] Diversity & EEO Report– 4 th Quarter 2023
[p003_c0027] Quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key EEO and Human Resources indicators relating to LIRR's Equal Employment Opportunity and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
