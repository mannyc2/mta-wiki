### [p006_c0001] SBS Results in NYC
[p006_c0002] 
SBS has been implemented on Fordham Rd in the Bronx in 2008, and on 1 st and 2 nd Aves in Manhattan in 2010, providing significant benefits:
- [p006_c0003] • 15% to 20% faster trips • Over 90% customer satisfaction • 10% ridership increase on the entire route, including SBS and local
