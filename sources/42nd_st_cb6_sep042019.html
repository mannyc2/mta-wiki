### [p004_c0001] 42 ND STREET PROJECT EXTENTS
- [p004_c0002] • Plan extends from 12 th Ave to FDR Drive • AM/PM bus speeds along 42 nd St are 4.0 MPH , compared to citywide average of 7.7 MPH • Important crosstown transit corridor carrying 8 MTA bus routes with 16,000 daily riders

## Page 7

### [p007_c0001] BUS RELIABILITY ISSUES
- [p007_c0002] • Bus running time is unreliable even within the same time period due to varying traffic conditions day to day • Runs can vary by 20-40 minutes, leading to bus bunching, long/unpredictable waits at the bus stop, and crowding on the bus
[p007_c0003] 
_M42 Running Times, Weekdays EB_
> [p007_c0004] The scatter plot displays the running times of M42 buses on weekdays for the EB route. The vertical axis, labeled 'Running Time (min)', ranges from 10 to 70 in increments of 10. The horizontal axis represents time, with labels at 03:00:00, 06:00:00, 09:00:00, 12:00:00, 15:00:00, 18:00:00, and 21:00:00. The data points are represented by a dense cloud of blue dots. The running times are highly variable, with many points clustered between 20 and 40 minutes, but also showing significant outliers, such as a point near 65 minutes around 09:00:00 and another near 55 minutes around 15:00:00. ### [p019_c0001] MODELED TRAFFIC & BUS IMPACTS
- [p019_c0002] • Better organization of all traffic with right-sized lanes, bus only lanes, turning lanes • Traffic analysis for the planned changes on 42 St showed: • Traffic will operate at current or improved levels of service along 42 St (up to 7% improvement in travel times, up to 9% reduction in delay) • Improved bus travel times along 42 St (up to 13% improvement in travel times, up to 27% reduction in delay) • Maintained or improved travel times for general traffic on side streets surrounding 42 St
