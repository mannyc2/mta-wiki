### [p014_c0001] MTAPD is leveraging technology to enhance its operations
[p014_c0002] 
Our Strategic Decision Centers have been instrumental in reducing both time and costs associated with police-related train delays
> [p014_c0003] A collage of images showing a control room operator at a desk with multiple monitors displaying train schedules and maps, and a close-up of a person on a radio call.
- [p014_c0004] • In 2023, Metro-North attributed 19,066 minutes of train delays to police-related incidents • In 2024, the first full year of the initiative, that number dropped by 71% to 5,422 minutes • The total number of trains delayed due to police incidents decreased by 55%, from 1,035 in 2023 to 463 in 2024 • These improvements played a significant role in Metro-North Railroad achieving its highest On-Time Performance (OTP) metrics in its 41-year history
