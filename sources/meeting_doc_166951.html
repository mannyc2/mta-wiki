### [p002_c0003] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0004] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0005] Key Performance Metrics Reports
[p002_c0006] 
The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p002_c0007] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0008] 
The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0009] Work Plan
[p002_c0010] 
The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics anticipated to be presented to the Committee for action or for information. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
[p002_c0011] 
In addition to Work Plan items, it is anticipated that the railroads will continue to update the committees on other items of interest on a regular basis and will submit action items to the committee for its approval as needed.
### [p002_c0012] President's Report
[p002_c0013] 
A monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0014] Operations Report
[p002_c0015] 
A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0016] Safety Report
[p002_c0017] 
A monthly report will be provided highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators.

### [p003_c0003] Information Items (if any)

Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0005] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0006] 
Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0007] Financial Report
[p003_c0008] 
A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0009] Procurements
[p003_c0010] 
List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
