Demonstrating sound judgment and adherence to protocol, they approached the individuals to investigate and request identification. Cited by 2 records

### [p016_c0001] ABOUT THE METROPOLITAN TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY & NEW YORK CITY TRANSIT
[p016_c0002] 
The Metropolitan Transportation Authority is North America's largest transportation network, serving a population of 15.3 million people across a 5,000-square-mile travel area surrounding New York City through Long Island, southeastern New York State, and Connecticut.

Cited by 3 records

[p016_c0003] 
New York City Transit and MTA Bus operate all subways and buses in New York City. Our 45,000 employees serve 4,500,000 passengers a day. We operate nearly 6,700 subway cars and 5,800 buses, and we maintain 472 subway stations, 640 miles of track, 27 bus depots and 70 shops and yards.

Cited by 12 records

[p016_c0004] 
The MTA is governed by a 23-member Board of Directors, organized in eight committees. Members of the New York City Transit Committee include:
