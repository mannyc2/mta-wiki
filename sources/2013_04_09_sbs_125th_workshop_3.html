### [p005_c0001] Parking Regulations
### [p005_c0002] No Standing / Bus Stop
- [p005_c0003] • Stopping to expeditiously drop off or pick up passengers allowed • No loading of packages or merchandise • No parking
### [p005_c0004] Unmetered Parking / Alternate Side Parking
- [p005_c0005] • Parking allowed at any time except as indicated • Vehicles may stop or stand at this location during street cleaning times but driver may not leave the vehicle
### [p005_c0006] Metered Parking
- [p005_c0007] • Parking allowed during indicated times and for indicated time period • Requires paying parking fee at Muni-Meter
### [p005_c0008] Commercial Loading Zones
- [p005_c0009] • No standing regulations apply to all non-commercial vehicles • Commercial vehicles can load and unload goods • Common parking regulation in commercial areas • Specified parking period can be as short as 1 hour to encourage turnover
### [p005_c0010] Special Zones / Permit Parking
[p005_c0011] 
Examples:
- [p005_c0012] • Hotels: Hotel Loading Zones • Medical Facilities: No Standing except ambulettes or passenger pick-up and drop-off • Houses of Worship

### [p012_c0001] Traffic Analysis - Level of Service: Existing
[p012_c0002] 
Traffic volumes, signal timing and progression, number of lanes, and curb activity determine average delay per vehicle at an intersection (Level of Service or LOS)
[p012_c0003] 
LOS A = no or minor delay crossing an intersection
[p012_c0004] 
LOS D to F = more than 45 seconds to cross; often waiting through one or more green lights
