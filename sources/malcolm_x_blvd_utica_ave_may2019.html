### [p004_c0001] BACKGROUND: B46 SELECT BUS SERVICE
- [p004_c0002] • B46 is Brooklyn's busiest bus route (38,000 daily riders) • Connects Bed-Stuy to 3 4 A C J Z and 30 bus routes • B46 SBS launched in July 2016 • Southbound ridership has increased in Bed-Stuy, but bus speeds have slowed on blocks without bus lanes
