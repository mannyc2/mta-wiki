### [p006_c0003] Proposed SBS Stop Locations on 125 th Street
> [p006_c0004] The map illustrates the proposed SBS route for the M60 bus service. The route is shown as a purple line connecting various stops. Westbound stops are marked with blue circles, and eastbound stops are marked with yellow circles. The route starts in Manhattan at 125th Street, crosses the RFK Bridge into Queens, and continues along Astoria Boulevard. It then branches into two paths: one heading towards the LGA Delta/US Airways Terminal and Central Terminal, and another heading towards the Marine Air Terminal. The map also shows the surrounding streets and landmarks, including the East River, the RFK Bridge, and various terminals and intersections in Queens. Legend: Westbound M60 SBS Stops (Blue circle) Eastbound M60 SBS Stops (Yellow circle) M60 SBS Route (Purple line)
[p006_c0005] M60 SBS stops at major intersections in Astoria and East Elmhurst
[p006_c0006] Stops at every subway/rail station on 125 th St plus Amsterdam Av and Second Av
[p006_c0007] Some bus stops will be lengthened to make it easier for buses to get to the curb
