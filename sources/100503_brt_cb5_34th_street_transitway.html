### [p014_c0001] Transitway Overview
> [p014_c0002] The map illustrates the proposed 34th Street Transitway. A yellow double-lined corridor runs along the center of 34th Street, extending from Pier 79 on the left to E 34th St Pier on the right. The corridor is divided into two main sections: a green section on the left for General Traffic Lanes and a red section on the right for Bus Lanes. A legend in the bottom right corner identifies the green arrows as General Traffic Lanes and the red arrows as Bus Lanes.
- [p014_c0003] • Two-way Transitway for length of 34 th Street aligned along curb • Single side alignment selected based on street width and corridor needs – street is too narrow to place Transitway down center of street, and allow parking on either side of the street. • Transitway occupies half of street, north half east of Fifth Avenue, south half west of Sixth Avenue, with a new plaza from Fifth to Sixth • General Traffic: One-Way Eastbound only east of Fifth Avenue One-Way Westbound only west of Sixth Avenue
