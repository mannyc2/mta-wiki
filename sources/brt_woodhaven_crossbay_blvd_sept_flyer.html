[p001_c0007] 
DOT will reconstruct Woodhaven Boulevard from Union Turnpike to 81st Rd, and implement bus and safety improvements as part of the Q52/Q53 Select Bus Service Project:
- [p001_c0008] ▪ Remove service road medians to create simpler central median with pedestrian refuge ▪ Maintain three vehicular traffic lanes in each direction ▪ Install curbside bus-only lanes that allow for right turns to the next intersection or driveway ▪ Restrict left turns from Woodhaven Blvd to Union Turnpike ▪ Create turn bay to allow for U-Turns at 81st Rd for access to Union Turnpike
### [p001_c0009] Implementation begins Mid-September
