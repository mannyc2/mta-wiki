### [p017_c0001] 34 th Street Long Term Concept
### [p017_c0002] Potential Transitway Features:
- [p017_c0003] • River-to-river fully separated two way transitway will be built on one side of 34 th street • Options for the rest of the right of way include: – One-way traffic outbound from midtown – One-way traffic westbound – New plaza and pedestrian spaces – Reserved commercial loading space • Transitway will decrease travel time by up to 45% for crosstown bus service
