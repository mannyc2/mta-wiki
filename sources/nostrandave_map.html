> [p001_c0001] This map illustrates the subway line connecting Queensboro Plaza in Manhattan to Flatbush Avenue in Queens. The route is shown as a red line with white dashed segments for SBS in Bus Lane and solid segments for SBS in Mixed Traffic. The map also shows the East River, Williamsburg Bridge, and surrounding neighborhoods like Downtown Brooklyn, Prospect Park, and Marine Park.
