### [p008_c0003] Definitions:

Cited by 7 records

[p008_c0004] 
Mechanical includes sudden changes in speed, handrail, alignment. Human Factor includes lost balance, encumbrances, Slip/Trip/Fall, pushed/shoved, intoxication, caught between, etc. Entrapment is defined when customers are removed from an elevator with special tools or training. These events require intervention but not necessarily involve a customer injury.
