### [p007_c0001] Sharpen management focus on cost drivers
[p007_c0002] 
Insourced cleaning positions to increase customer satisfaction at lower cost
[p007_c0003] 
During COVID, NYCT rapidly increased cleaning of Stations and Cars by contracting out for support
[p007_c0004] 
After reviewing results, NYCT determined it could improve results by insourcing. Changes include:
- [p007_c0005] • More cost effective • Greater specialization (e.g., power washing) • New training
[p007_c0006] 
NYCT will continue to monitor performance and revise its operating model (e.g., ideal supervisor to staff ratio) as we continue to balance costs and cleanliness
[p007_c0007] 
Customer satisfaction has increased ~10pp for both Station and Train Cleanliness

### [p009_c0001] Develop common standards and best practices
### [p009_c0002] Scale LEDs across the Subway system
[p009_c0003] 
LEDs provide multiple benefits compared to traditional fluorescent bulbs:
- [p009_c0004] • Lower energy consumption • Longer-lasting (resulting in lower material and labor costs for replacements) • Brighter light and greater feeling of safety
[p009_c0005] 
Subways has successfully piloted LEDs on cars, in stations, and in tunnels
[p009_c0006] 
As part of the Faster, Cleaner, Safer plan, Subways will be rolling out LEDs at scale over the coming years, driving higher customer satisfaction at lower cost
[p009_c0007] 
On average, LEDs last over 3x as long and consume ~50% less energy vs. fluorescents

### [p017_c0001] Develop common standards and best practices
[p017_c0002] 
M7 Standard Work optimization is a key productivity enabler that can create value from job time reduction and increased predictability
- [p017_c0003] • M7s are 30% of Metro-North's fleet & 65% of LIRR's • Utilizing Lean Six-Sigma principles to determine optimal sequencing and timing of the work to reduce overall time cars spend in the shop • Initial task-by-task review of FRA required 92-day Periodic Inspection will be performed in Croton-Harmon • Implementation teams will build skills and methodology for scaling this approach to other types of work across both railroads
