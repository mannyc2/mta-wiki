### [p020_c0001] SUMMARY
- [p020_c0002] • Add offset bus lane on southbound Broadway from 230 St to 225 St • Restrict right turn from northbound Broadway to Exterior St where queue jump will be installed – Combine bus only signal with Leading Pedestrian Interval (LPI) to give pedestrians and buses a head start • Expand commercial loading access on Broadway service road, provide commercial parking opportunities on 228 St • Directly improve bus speeds for 5,500 daily riders and provide a more reliable commute for 38,500 weekday bus riders
