### [p003_c0002] Procurements
[p003_c0003] 
List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.

[p003_c0005] 
A monthly report will be provided highlighting key operating and performance statistics and indicators.
### [p003_c0006] Finance
[p003_c0007] 
A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast both on an accrual and cash basis.
### [p003_c0008] Ridership
[p003_c0009] 
A monthly report will be provided that includes a comparison of actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues with the budget and prior year results.

### [p003_c0011] FEBRUARY 2023
### [p003_c0012] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2023
[p003_c0013] 
The Agency will present its revised 2023 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2023 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2023 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2023 Adopted Budget.
### [p003_c0014] 2022 Annual Operating Results
[p003_c0015] 
A review of the prior year's performance of railroad service will be provided to the Committee.
### [p003_c0016] Diversity & EEO Report– 4 th Quarter 2022
[p003_c0017] 
A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p003_c0018] MARCH 2023
### [p003_c0019] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p003_c0020] 
Annual report to the Committee on system-wide reliability and availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
### [p003_c0021] APRIL 2023
### [p003_c0022] 2022 Annual Ridership Report
[p003_c0023] 
A report will be presented to the Committee on Metro-North's ridership trends during 2022 based on monthly ticket sales data and the results of train ridership counts conducted by Metro-North.
### [p003_c0024] MAY 2023
### [p003_c0025] Final Review of 2022 Operating Results
[p003_c0026] 
A review of the prior year's budget results and their implications for current and future budget performance will be presented to the Committee.
