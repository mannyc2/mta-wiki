### [p003_c0001] Metropolitan Transportation Authority
### [p003_c0002] 2022 Transit & Bus Committee Work Plan
### [p003_c0003] Detailed Summary
### [p003_c0004] I. RECURRING
### [p003_c0005] Approval of Minutes
[p003_c0006] An official record of proceedings which occurred during the previous month's Committee meeting.
### [p003_c0007] NYCT Work Plan
[p003_c0008] A monthly update of any edits and/or changes in the work plan.
### [p003_c0009] Operations Performance Summary
[p003_c0010] Summary presentation on the performance of Subway and Bus Service, including a discussion on Safety, Finance and Ridership.
[p003_c0011] Information includes discussion on key indicators for Subway and Bus Customer-Focused and Performance metrics.
### [p003_c0012] Procurements
[p003_c0013] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0014] Service Changes
[p003_c0015] Service proposals presented for Committee information and for Board approval, when required. Proposals outline various subway service initiatives.
### [p003_c0016] Tariff Changes
[p003_c0017] Proposals presented to the Board for approval of changes affecting NYCT fare policy structure.
### [p003_c0018] Capital Budget Modifications
[p003_c0019] Proposals presented to the Board for approval of changes to NYCT's 5-Year Capital Program.
### [p003_c0020] Action Items
[p003_c0021] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices

### [p004_c0003] NYCT 2023 Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2023-2026
[p004_c0004] NYCT will present its revised 2023-2026 Financial Plan.
