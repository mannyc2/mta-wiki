AGENCY: MTA Long Island Rail Road Company (“LIRR”) SELLER: Rose-Breslin Associates, LLC / AVR Realty Company LOCATION: Town of Brookhaven, Suffolk County, NY PREMISES: Approximately 5.33 acres of vacant land USE: Construction and relocation of LIRR Yaphank Station COMPENSATION: None
### [p004_c0009] COMMENTS:

Cited by 3 records

[p004_c0010] 
LIRR acquired an approximately 5.33-acre parcel in the Town of Brookhaven in Suffolk County, NY in support of MTA C&D’s project to construct a new station and relocate the existing LIRR Yaphank Station. The current Yaphank station is in a sparsely populated area and sees a low level of service. The relocated station will better meet current and future ridership demands of central Suffolk County. The Premises was appraised and valued $2 million and acquired from the Seller for no compensation as the site also benefits the Seller’s future adjacent development plans.

Cited by 1 record

[p004_c0011] 
The Premises was acquired pursuant to MTA Board Policy, dated September 15, 2015, and modified July 19, 2023, which allows MTA Real Estate to acquire property rights in support of MTA capital projects without MTA Board approval when aggregate compensation is less than $100,000.
