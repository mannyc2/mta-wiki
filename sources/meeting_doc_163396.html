## Page 7

### [p007_c0001] The Crowe forensic audit outlined important themes to aid in driving efficiency
### [p007_c0002] Recommendations
[p007_c0003] 
1. Require immediate master planning
[p007_c0004] 
2. Adopt alternative management approaches to control future costs
[p007_c0005] 
3. Provide active risk management of projects
[p007_c0006] 
4. Consolidate projects/procurements across multiple sub-projects
[p007_c0007] 
5. Enhance linkages between capital projects
### [p007_c0008] What we changed
[p007_c0009] 
Rigorous upfront planning and operating agency buy-in in early stages
[p007_c0010] 
Strategic use of delivery models, including Design-Build and early completion incentives
[p007_c0011] 
Empowered role of Program CEOs and change order scrutiny
[p007_c0012] 
Larger bundled contracts to fully realize economies of scale and scope
[p007_c0013] 
Robust 20-Year Needs Assessment across nearly 6 million MTA assets to lay out priority capital investments
### [p007_c0014] Select examples of impact
- [p007_c0015] ▪ $1B saved on bids compared to estimate since 2020 across 430 projects 1 ▪ 4.5x pace of ADA accessibility projects through bundling ▪ Major projects like LIRR Third Track and Canarsie Tube come in under budget and on schedule ▪ Significant scope savings – $300M in design for Second Avenue Subway Phase 2 and $40M for ADA Woodhaven Blvd
[p007_c0016] 
_1. Comparing bid costs versus engineers' estimate. This includes an average of 4 months in schedule savings compared to estimate_

## Page 8
[p008_c0001] 
The consolidation of five duplicative sets of admin functions into HQ has provided cost savings and process improvements
### [p008_c0002] HR
- [p008_c0003] ▪ Improved “time to hire” ▪ Timely employee separations processes
### [p008_c0004] IT
- [p008_c0005] ▪ Right-sized devices, licenses, and subscriptions ▪ Renegotiation of large vendor contracts
### [p008_c0006] Procurement
- [p008_c0007] ▪ Revitalized rolling stock strategy ▪ Optimizing common supply chain practices (better forecasting, improved vendor management, and updated minimum quantities, reorder points, etc.)
### [p008_c0008] Finance
- [p008_c0009] ▪ Efficient and timely accounts payable practices ▪ Unified and streamlined expense approval process
