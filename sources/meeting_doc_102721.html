### [p002_c0014] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0015] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0016] Performance Metrics Report
[p002_c0017] A monthly report consisting of a Message from the President, Ridership, Finance Safety, Operations and Police Major Projects and Customer & Communities statistics.
### [p002_c0018] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0019] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0020] Work Plan
[p002_c0021] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0022] President's Report
[p002_c0023] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0024] Safety Report
[p002_c0025] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0026] Police Report
[p002_c0027] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p002_c0028] Action Items (if any)
[p002_c0029] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0002] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0003] Procurements
[p003_c0004] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0005] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0006] JANUARY 2023
### [p003_c0007] Approval of 2023 Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0008] The Committee will approve the Proposed Long Island Rail Road Committee Work Plan for 2022 that will address initiatives to be reported on throughout the year.
### [p003_c0009] M/W Quarterly Update
[p003_c0010] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
### [p003_c0011] FEBRUARY 2023
### [p003_c0012] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2023
[p003_c0013] The Agency will present its revised 2023 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2022 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2022 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2022 Adopted Budget.
### [p003_c0014] 2022 Annual Operating Results
[p003_c0015] A review of the prior year's performance of railroad service will be provided to the Committee.
### [p003_c0016] March Timetable/Spring Trackwork Programs
[p003_c0017] The Committee will be advised of plans to adjust schedules.
### [p003_c0018] MARCH 2023
### [p003_c0019] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p003_c0020] Annual report to the Committee on system-wide availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
### [p003_c0021] Spring Trackwork Programs
[p003_c0022] The Committee will be advised of Agency plans to adjust schedules to support construction projects during the Spring of 2023.
### [p003_c0023] Diversity & EEO Report– 4 th Quarter 2022
[p003_c0024] Quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key EEO and Human Resources indicators relating to LIRR's Equal Employment Opportunity and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
