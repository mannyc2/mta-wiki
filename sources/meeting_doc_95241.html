### [p001_c0003] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0004] 
This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to adjust schedules for a Switch Installation in Queens Interlocking in support of the Queens Reconfiguration Project, A Signal Cutover in Mineola in support of the Main Line Expansion Project, Switch Maintenance in Valley Stream, Concrete Tie Replacement near Floral Park, and Track Maintenance near Patchogue.
### [p001_c0005] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS

Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0007] • Main Line – Switch Installation – On the weekend of September 24-25, all main tracks on the Main Line will be out of service between New Hyde Park and Hicksville for approximately 48 hours for a switch installation in Queens Interlocking as part of the Queens Reconfiguration Project. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: ▪ Eastbound, no MU service will operate between Penn Station and Hicksville, and two-hourly Oyster Bay Branch diesel service will operate between East Williston and Oyster Bay. For Eastbound customers from western terminals traveling to Ronkonkoma Branch stations Farmingdale through Ronkonkoma, and to Port Jefferson Branch stations Hicksville through Huntington/Port Jefferson, customers will board electric MU express service to Babylon, where they will transfer to diesel shuttle trains operating between Babylon and Hicksville via the Central Branch, then transfer at Hicksville to trains operating to either Ronkonkoma or Huntington/Port Jefferson. Eastbound Bethpage customers will board the electric MU express service to Babylon, then transfer to the diesel shuttle train, which will stop at Bethpage on its way to Hicksville.

Train service will operate between Ronkonkoma and Greenport, but on adjusted schedules. • Main Line – Signal Cutover – On the weekend of October 1-2, both tracks on the Oyster Bay Branch and all main tracks on the Main Line will be out of service between New Hyde Park and Hicksville for approximately 48 hours for a signal cutover in support of the Main Line Expansion Project. The Elmont Station Project will also be piggybacking this outage to perform track maintenance and track surfacing in Queens Interlocking. Following this weekend outage, the LIRR will be able to service the new Elmont Station from the Main Line in both eastbound and westbound directions for events. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: ▪ Eastbound, no MU service will operate between Penn Station and Hicksville. Eastbound Bethpage customers will board the electric MU express service to Babylon, then transfer to the diesel shuttle train, which will stop at Bethpage on its way to Hicksville. ▪ Westbound, no electric MU service will operate between Hicksville and Penn Station. Additionally, schedule adjustments will be required on both the Babylon Branch and Montauk Branches. • Hempstead Branch – Concrete Tie Installation – On the weekend of October 15-16, both main tracks on the Hempstead Branch will be out of service for approximately 48 hours while new concrete ties are installed, and the existing rail is replaced. Installing the new concrete ties will offer greater reliability versus traditional wooden ties, and a longer lifespan, thus reducing the need for future outages. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: On the weekend of October 15-16, buses will replace train service at Bellerose Station and also between Stewart Manor and Hempstead on the Hempstead Branch. Regularly scheduled Hempstead Branch Trains will depart Atlantic Terminal making stops at Nostrand Avenue, East New York and Jamaica. Customers for stations Hollis through Hempstead will be accommodated by Ronkonkoma trains that will make added stops at Hollis, Queens Village, Floral Park and Mineola. Hempstead Branch customers will detrain at Mineola for bus service to all stations between Stewart Manor and Hempstead. Customers at Bellerose Station will be accommodated by buses to/from Queens Village Station.
