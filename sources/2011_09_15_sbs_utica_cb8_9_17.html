### [p004_c0001] B46 Bus Service
- [p004_c0002] • Most Utica area residents do not have subway access • B46 is the second busiest bus route in NYC • Over 49,000 passengers per weekday • Eastern Pkwy a major transfer point to 3 & 4 train • Congestion slows buses approaching Eastern Pkwy

### [p007_c0001] Proposed Improvement
> [p007_c0002] The diagram illustrates a proposed bus lane improvement at the intersection of Utica Ave and Eastern Parkway. The map shows a grid of streets including St. Johns Pl, Lincoln Pl, Eastern Parkway, Union St, President St, and Carroll St. Utica Ave runs vertically through the center, with traffic flow indicated by arrows. Eastern Parkway runs horizontally, with a blue line indicating a proposed bus lane shift from the current location to the southbound (SB) approach. The map uses color coding: green for Loading/Parking, yellow for Bus Stop, and red for Bus Only Lane. The proposed changes include a new bus-only lane on the SB side of Eastern Parkway, a bus stop on the northbound (NB) side of Eastern Parkway, and a loading/parking area on the northbound side of Utica Ave. The legend on the right side of the diagram defines these color codes.
