### [p001_c0006] HOLIDAY SERVICE
### [p001_c0007] Thanksgiving Day Program (Thursday, November 27, 2025)

Cited by 4 records

[p001_c0008] 
To help usher in the 2025 holiday season and provide a better travel experience for those attending the parade in NYC, the Long Island Rail Road Thanksgiving Day program includes six extra westbound trains (4 to Penn Station and 2 to GCM) on Thanksgiving morning and nine early afternoon eastbound extra trains (4 from Penn Station and 5 from GCM) for those returning home from the parade or heading to Long Island for Thanksgiving gatherings.

Cited by 8 records

[p001_c0009] 
The westbound Thanksgiving morning extras operate as follows: three from Babylon, two from Ronkonkoma, and one from Farmingdale. The eastbound Thanksgiving afternoon extras operate as follows: four to Babylon, three to Ronkonkoma, one to Farmingdale, and one to Huntington.
### [p001_c0010] IMPACT ON FUNDING

Cited by 7 records

[p001_c0011]
