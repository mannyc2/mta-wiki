[p003_c0006] AGENCY: MTA Metro-North Railroad ("MNR") PERMITTOR: County of Westchester LOCATION: Willson's Woods Park 8 Bradford Avenue, Mount Vernon, New York 10553 PREMISES: Approximately 17,530± sq. ft of Access Road and Staging Area Adjacent to the New Haven Line Right-Of-Way Near the Existing Substation C-16 USE: Access and Staging for the Construction of a New Substation TERM: Approximately forty (40) months COMPENSATION: N/A
### [p003_c0007] COMMENTS:
[p003_c0008] MNR is seeking to replace the existing Pelham Substation C-16, located adjacent to the Willson's Woods Park and on the New Haven line right-of-way near the Pelham Station in the City of Mount Vernon, New York. In order to realize this project, Westchester County has agreed to provide MNR with a permit to utilize an access road and staging area within Willson's Woods Park. The construction is estimated to take forty (40) months, and there is no fee associated with the permit.
[p003_c0009] This permit was issued pursuant to the MTA Board-approved policy dated November 15, 1999, and last modified June 23, 2021, which authorizes MTA Real Estate to enter directly into construction access agreements in support of MTA operating agencies for capital projects, maintenance, or repairs of operating facilities. MTA Legal drafted the Short-Term Permit, approved the agreement as to form, and it was fully executed on December 4, 2023.

[p006_c0008] AGENCY: Metropolitan Transportation Authority (“MTA”) and Metro-North Railroad (“MNR”) PURCHASER: South Bronx Development I LLC (“South Bronx Development”), an entity owned and controlled by MADD Equities (“MADD Equities”) LOCATION: Webster Avenue and East 197 th Street, Bronx, New York - Block 3273, Lot 101 (the “Subject Property”) ACTIVITY: Substitution of MADD Equities as the purchaser of the MTA Property Interests associated with the Subject Property
### [p006_c0009] COMMENTS:
[p006_c0010] At its February 2023 meeting, the Board authorized MTA Transit Oriented Development (“TOD”) to negotiate and enter into a Land Development and Disposition Agreement (“LDDA”) with Webster Development LLC (“Webster Development”) for the sale of certain MTA property interests associated with the Subject Property (the “MTA Property Interests”). A copy of the Staff Summary and the Board’s authorization to enter into the LDDA and consummate the sale of the MTA Property Interests to Webster Development is attached for the Board’s reference. Webster Development intends to utilize the MTA Property Interests to facilitate the development of an approximately 550-unit low-income housing development adjacent to the Subject Property (the “Project”). The purchase price for the MTA Property Interests is $3,969,056 (the “Purchase Price”), subject to adjustment if Webster Development elects to purchase additional transferrable development rights for the development of the Project.
[p006_c0011] Webster Development has advised TOD that it has transferred a controlling interest in the Project to South Bronx Development, which is owned and controlled by MADD Equities. MADD Equities is an established real estate development firm that acquires, owns, develops, and operates properties across many asset classes, including low-income and work force housing. In TOD’s opinion, MADD Equities role in the Project meaningfully increases the likelihood that the Project as proposed will be successfully completed.
[p006_c0012] All other aspects of the transaction to sell the MTA Property Interest as previously approved by the Board remain unchanged, including the Purchase Price payable for the MTA Property Interests.
