### [p002_c0019] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0020] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0021] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0022] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0023] 2023 Work Plan
[p002_c0024] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0025] President's Report
[p002_c0026] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0027] Safety Report
[p002_c0028] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators
### [p002_c0029] Police Report
[p002_c0030] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0002] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0003] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0004] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0005] Procurements
[p003_c0006] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0007] Police Activity Report
[p003_c0008] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p003_c0009] PERFORMANCE SUMMARIES
### [p003_c0010] Operations Report
[p003_c0011] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p003_c0012] Financial Report
[p003_c0013] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0014] Ridership Report
[p003_c0015] A monthly report will be provided that compares actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues against prior year results.
### [p003_c0016] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0017] JUNE 2023
### [p003_c0018] Joint Railroad Strategic Priorities Update
[p003_c0019] In alignment with MTA's strategic priorities, a biannual report to the Committee on the Railroads' progress in delivering safe and reliable transportation and excellent customer service.
### [p003_c0020] Summer Track Work Programs
[p003_c0021] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road's plans to adjust schedules to support various trackwork programs, Main Line Second Track construction and East Side Access Readiness projects
### [p003_c0022] Diversity & EEO Report– 1 st Quarter 2023
[p003_c0023] Quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key EEO and Human Resources indicators relating to LIRR's Equal Employment Opportunity and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p003_c0024] JULY 2023
### [p003_c0025] Trackwork Programs & Fall Schedule Adjustments
[p003_c0026] The Committee will be advised of Agency plans to adjust schedules to support construction projects during the fall of 2023.
