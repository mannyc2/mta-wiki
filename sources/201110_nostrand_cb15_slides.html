### [p007_c0001] SBS Results in NYC
[p007_c0002] 
SBS has been implemented on Fordham Rd in the Bronx in 2008, and on 1 st and 2 nd Aves in Manhattan in 2010, providing significant benefits:
- [p007_c0003] • 15% to 20% faster trips • Over 90% customer satisfaction • 10% ridership increase on the entire route, including SBS and local

### [p012_c0001] Traffic Changes
### [p012_c0002] In CB 15
- [p012_c0003] • In bus lane area, a small increase (12 seconds) in northbound delay in the morning peak hour. • South of Flatbush Avenue, Transit Signal Priority will allow buses to travel more quickly, and will help general traffic flow.
### [p012_c0004] North of CB 15
