### [p002_c0011] SBS USER GUIDE
### [p002_c0012] BUS RIDERS
- [p002_c0013] ▪ All S79 service becomes Select Bus Service, stopping only at SBS stations ▪ Local stops will continue to be served by the S59 and S78, with added service ▪ SBS fare = local bus fare ▪ Board through the front door and pay your fare for S79 using the farebox on the bus ▪ Express buses can also use bus lanes

### [p002_c0017] Bus lanes on Hylan Boulevard
- [p002_c0018] ▪ Between Lincoln Avenue and Steuben Street ▪ In effect on weekdays from 6-9am northbound and 3-7pm southbound
### [p002_c0019] Bus lanes on Richmond Avenue
- [p002_c0020] ▪ Between Platinum Avenue and Forest Hill Road, and between Shirley Avenue and Hylan Boulevard ▪ In effect at all times ▪ Located to the left of the curb lane
