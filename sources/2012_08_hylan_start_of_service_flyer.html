## Page 2

### [p002_c0001] S79 SELECT BUS SERVICE BEGINS SEPTEMBER 2, 2012
### [p002_c0002] IMPROVEMENTS COMING SEPTEMBER 2ND
[p002_c0003] 1. Streamlined S79 bus service will operate with fewer stops and a more direct route to save travel time.
[p002_c0004] 2. Bus lanes at key locations bring faster and more reliable service to all bus riders.
- [p002_c0005] ▪ On Hylan Boulevard between Lincoln Avenue and Steuben Street. ▪ On Richmond Avenue between Platinum Avenue & Forest Hill Road. ▪ On Richmond Avenue between Shirley Avenue & Hylan Boulevard.
[p002_c0006] 3. Optimized signal timing improves progression of traffic.
[p002_c0007] 4. Transit Signal Priority (TSP) extends the green light or shortens the red light to keep buses moving (coming in 2013).
[p002_c0008] 5. Pedestrian refuge islands improve safety.
[p002_c0009] 6. New and repaired sidewalks enhance safety and access to bus stops.
[p002_c0010] 7. Left turn bays on Richmond Avenue help reduce traffic congestion.
### [p002_c0011] SBS USER GUIDE
### [p002_c0012] BUS RIDERS
- [p002_c0013] ▪ All S79 service becomes Select Bus Service, stopping only at SBS stations ▪ Local stops will continue to be served by the S59 and S78, with added service ▪ SBS fare = local bus fare ▪ Board through the front door and pay your fare for S79 using the farebox on the bus ▪ Express buses can also use bus lanes
### [p002_c0014] DRIVERS
[p002_c0015] When a bus lane is in effect, stay out of the lane except to:
- [p002_c0016] ▪ Turn right at the next corner OR ▪ Quickly drop off or pick up a passenger
### [p002_c0017] Bus lanes on Hylan Boulevard
- [p002_c0018] ▪ Between Lincoln Avenue and Steuben Street ▪ In effect on weekdays from 6-9am northbound and 3-7pm southbound
### [p002_c0019] Bus lanes on Richmond Avenue
- [p002_c0020] ▪ Between Platinum Avenue and Forest Hill Road, and between Shirley Avenue and Hylan Boulevard ▪ In effect at all times ▪ Located to the left of the curb lane
### [p002_c0021] NYC enforces bus lanes with:
- [p002_c0022] ▪ Video cameras ▪ Increased police enforcement
[p002_c0023] $115 - $150 fine if you drive, park, or stand in an active bus lane.
[p002_c0024] More information is available at: www.nyc.gov/buslanes
