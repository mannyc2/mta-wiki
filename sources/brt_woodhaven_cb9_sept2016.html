## Page 4

### [p004_c0001] Woodhaven / Cross Bay SBS corridor
- [p004_c0002] • Based on the existing Q52/53 Limited bus route • 30,000+ daily bus riders • 14 miles long from Woodside to the Rockaways • Important north/south transit corridor for Queens • Provides connections to 8 subway lines, 20+ bus routes, and LIRR • CB9 covers 2 of the top 5 busiest Q52/53 stops
> [p004_c0003] A map of the Woodhaven / Cross Bay SBS corridor in Queens, New York. The route is highlighted in a thick blue line, starting from Roosevelt Av and 75 St in the north, heading south through Whitney Av, Grand Av, Queens Blvd, Penelope Av, Metropolitan Av, Myrtle Av, Jamaica Av, 91 Av, 103 Av, Rockaway Blvd, Pitkin Av, 157 Av, and 163 Av. The route then continues south along Cross Bay Blvd to Noel Rd / E 9 Rd and W 15 Rd / E 16 Rd, ending at Rockaway Beach Blvd. The map also shows the CB9 subway line in light blue, which runs parallel to the SBS corridor. Other landmarks include Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge, Wildlife Refuge, Jamaica Bay, JFK Int'l Airport, AirTrain JFK, and AirTrain JFK. The map also shows various bus stops and subway stations along the route.
