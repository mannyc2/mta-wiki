### [p002_c0004] Dedicated Bus Lanes
[p002_c0005] 
B46 buses now have their own dedicated lane , moving passengers faster while making the street more predictable for drivers.
### [p002_c0006] Bus Priority at Lights
[p002_c0007] 
B46 SBS buses are equipped with transit signal priority (TSP) technology which communicates with traffic signals so the bus spends less time waiting at traffic lights.
### [p002_c0008] Coming Spring 2016
### [p002_c0009] Faster Fare Payment

[p002_c0010] 
With off-board fare payment, riders will pay their fare before boarding the bus with a MetroCard or coins and board through any door, greatly reducing the time waiting to pay at the front of the bus.

### [p003_c0002] How Does Off-Board Fare Payment Work?
[p003_c0003] 
B46 SBS riders must dip their MetroCards or use coins to get a ticket to ride before boarding the bus. The cost is the same as a Limited bus, Local bus or the subway. Transfers are stored on the MetroCard. If paying by coins, show the ticket to the bus operator to get a paper transfer MetroCard. Riders then board the bus at any door, greatly reducing wait times at bus stops.
