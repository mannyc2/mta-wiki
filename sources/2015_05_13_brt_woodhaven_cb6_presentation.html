### [p029_c0001] Proposed design - traffic benefits
- [p029_c0002] • 3 lanes continuously along corridor reduces merging/diverging behavior • Banning key left turns (particularly Union Turnpike SB) helps thru traffic flow • Longer left-turn bays where left turns are allowed keep turns out of through traffic • More consistent roadway design allows for better traffic signal timing and coordination • Service road design separates thru traffic from local access / parking
