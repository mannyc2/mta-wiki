### [p002_c0001] Marcy Av, Borinquen Pl to Broadway
- [p002_c0002] Confluence of three Williamsburg Bridge Plaza-bound bus routes: B24 – Avg. Daily Ridership 2,359 (2024) B60 – Avg. Daily Ridership 12,226 (2024) Q54 – Avg. Daily Ridership 13,411 (2024) Each bus route experiences delays and slower speeds along this tail end stretch. These delays compound on top of delays earlier in the routes' journeys and result in buses arriving late to their terminal, delaying the next scheduled run. The BQE North and South (2024) study recommended providing a bus priority lane to alleviate these delays.

### [p005_c0001] Marcy Av & Broadway – Difficult Right Turns
> [p005_c0002] A high-angle photograph of a busy urban intersection. A blue MTA bus is in the middle of a tight right turn from Marcy Av onto westbound Broadway. The bus has 'clear-energy bus' and 'ELECTRIC' branding. To its left, a white sedan is stopped. In front of the bus, a black SUV is also stopped. The intersection is marked with white crosswalk lines. Above the street, a large, rusted steel structure, likely part of a train or elevated roadway, spans the area. Buildings and storefronts are visible in the background.
> [p005_c0003] A high-angle photograph of the same intersection. A blue MTA bus is shown from a different angle, having completed its right turn onto westbound Broadway. The bus has 'NOT IN SERVICE' written on its side. A white sedan is stopped in the lane to the left of the bus. A black SUV is visible further ahead on the street. The scene is similar to the first image, with the same steel structure overhead and surrounding buildings.
[p005_c0004] Buses must navigate a very tight right turn when continuing onto westbound Broadway from southbound Marcy Av; usually competing for space with other turning vehicles as well as improperly stopped traffic on the eastbound side of Broadway.
