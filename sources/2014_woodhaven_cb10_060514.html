### [p008_c0001] Curbside Bus Lanes
[p008_c0002] Substantial congestion approaching Rockaway Boulevard delays buses getting to the subway.
### [p008_c0003] Proposed
- [p008_c0004] • Curbside bus lane northbound from Plattwood Ave to Liberty Ave, and southbound from 101 Ave to Rockaway Blvd • Proposed in effect 7am-7pm on the last block, and peak periods only on other blocks • Allows bus to reach subway connection, getting past backups without removing travel lanes

### [p013_c0001] SBS and Traffic Flow
