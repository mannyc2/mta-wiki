### [p003_c0001] B82 Project Background
[p003_c0002] The Southern Brooklyn B82 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p003_c0003] • Serves 32,000 daily riders on B82 Limited and Local • Heavily used route with slow trips • Important east-west transit connection across Southern Brooklyn • Connects to D N F B Q L subway lines and 21 bus routes, including B44 Nostrand SBS and B46 Utica SBS • Serves areas far from the subway

### [p023_c0001] B82 Route to Canarsie-Rockaway Pkwy
- [p023_c0002] • Westbound buses will be re-routed via Glenwood Rd bus lane • Faster, more direct routing to Canarsie-Rockaway Pkwy station • More customer-friendly routing by matching both directions • Reduces bus traffic on local streets and at major intersections • B82 SBS will temporarily stop outside of Canarsie-Rockaway Pkwy station on Glenwood Rd, due to new MTA station reconstruction project (until approximately 2020)
