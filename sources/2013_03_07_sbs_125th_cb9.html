### [p022_c0004] Potential Changes to Parking Regulations
### [p022_c0005] Alternate-Side Parking (ASP) between Amsterdam Av and Morningside Av
[p022_c0006] allows free parking for as long as 4 days
[p022_c0007] makes it more difficult for shoppers and delivery trucks to find short-term parking
[p022_c0008] leads to double parking
[p022_c0009] DOT proposes converting ASP to 1-Hour Metered Parking from 8 AM to 7 PM
[p022_c0010] DOT would also add 1-Hour Metered Commercial Loading from 8 AM to 12 PM where needed
[p022_c0011] _New York City Transit_
