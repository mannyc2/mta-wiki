### [p003_c0001] Project Background
[p003_c0002] The Utica Avenue B46 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p003_c0003] • Ranked by community as one of two corridors most in need of improvements • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Second-busiest bus route in NYC, carrying nearly 50,000 passengers per day • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains and 30 bus routes • Bus lanes implemented in 2014 in portion of corridor, improving bus and vehicle travel times

## Page 5

### [p005_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p005_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p005_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p005_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p005_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p005_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 7 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes. The routes are highlighted in blue and labeled as follows: Bx12: Fordham Rd/ Pelham Pkwy (Westchester County) Bx41: Webster Av (The Bronx) M60: 125 St./Astoria Blvd. to LGA (The Bronx) M34/M34A: 34 St (The Bronx) M15: First Av/ Second Av (Queens) B44: Nostrand Ave/ Rogers Ave (Brooklyn) S79: Hylan Blvd (Staten Island) The map also shows the boroughs: Westchester County, Nassau County, The Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Bay Ridge, Staten Island, and Staten Island Mall.
