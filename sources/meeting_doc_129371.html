### [p002_c0001] Recap: Extending Transit's Reach
[p002_c0002] 
In January 2023, MTA published a Strategic Action Plan to improve bicycle, pedestrian, and micromobility access across the entire MTA system through:
- [p002_c0003] • Improved bike parking at subway and commuter rail stations • Enhanced bike infrastructure on buses • Safer routes to transit and bridges • Better biking and pedestrian lanes on bridges

### [p007_c0001] MTA's Personal Electric Vehicle policy
> [p007_c0002] Personal electric vehicles on buses • No charging • Turn it off and keep it off • Fold it • Don't block seats, doors, or emergency equipment Personal electric vehicles are not allowed on MTA Express Buses.
