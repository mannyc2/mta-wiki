Re-statement of MTA Real Estate licensing-in policy governing construction access agreements N/A N/A The existing policy for construction access agreements, adopted November 15, 1999 and amended on November 12, 2013 and June 23, 2021, allows MTA Real Estate to negotiate, acquire or grant third parties access rights and privileges required by MTA Agencies for capital projects or maintenance or repairs of operating facilities. These access rights are typically for the duration of the project and subject to a $10,000 per month limit. Such permits or licenses are typically obtained from other public entities. The restated policy, as modified to remove the authorization for MTA Real Estate to grant third parties and is limited to the acquisition of rights.
