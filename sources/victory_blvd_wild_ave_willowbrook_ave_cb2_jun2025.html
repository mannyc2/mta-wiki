## Page 4

### [p004_c0001] Why Victory Boulevard?
[p004_c0002] NYC DOT and MTA seek to improve bus service on Victory Boulevard because:
- [p004_c0003] • There are 25,789 average daily riders on 16 routes that facilitate connections to the Staten Island Ferry. • 619 injuries on the corridor in the past 5 years, including 60 who were severely injured. Victory Boulevard is a Vision Zero priority corridor. • According to a 2024 MTA survey, Staten Island bus riders would like MTA to prioritize reducing wait times, provide more reliable service, and minimize delays.
