### [p005_c0002] Adding service
- [p005_c0003] • More off-peak service across 12 subway lines • Quicker trips on 16 local bus routes starting in June; 8 express bus routes took effect in March • Implementing a comprehensive bus redesign in Queens, the most bus-dependent borough • Huge increase in LIRR service thanks to Third Track and Grand Central Madison
