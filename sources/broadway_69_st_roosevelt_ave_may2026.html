### [p007_c0001] Proposal: Center Running Bus Lanes
- [p007_c0002] • The project will: – Convert channelized median and left turn bays in to a 24/7 eastbound center-running bus lane between the BQE and Roosevelt Ave. – Retain one travel lane in each direction for general traffic. – Improve turns on to Broadway at 73rd Street through minor parking removal. – Ban left turns at 72nd Street. – Update curb regulations to encourage turnover and reduce double parking on Broadway and adjacent side streets.
