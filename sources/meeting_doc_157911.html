### [p002_c0001] Budget results for first ten months of 2024
- [p002_c0002] • 2024 overall revenue and expenses are on budget with expenses below budget by 2.1%
### [p002_c0003] Revenues
- [p002_c0004] • Subway and bus revenue below budget by 4.5% primarily due to fare evasion, but covered by loss provision built into budget • Commuter rail revenue better than budget by almost 5% • B&T revenues on budget • Dedicated tax receipts are slightly above the reforecast
### [p002_c0005] Expenses
- [p002_c0006] • Overall operating expenses below budget by 2.1% • Operating efficiencies of over $400 million on target to be delivered
