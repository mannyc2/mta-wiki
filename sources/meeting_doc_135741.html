### [p002_c0001] Fast Facts
- [p002_c0002] • New mental health co-response teams: NYC clinicians & MTAPD working together • Focus 100% on the most troubled, most troubling clients : acutely psychotic and/or deeply entrenched • “Help right now” approach. So far: • ~ 90 days, 90+ clients to hospital or shelter – same day • Including 16 involuntary transports to hospitals for psychiatric evaluation • Front end of pipeline to new NYS/NYC psych beds & supportive housing options • Ahead: $20 million expansion per Governor Hochul

### [p012_c0001] SCOUT So Far
- [p012_c0002] • 2 teams, weekdays, since fall 2023 • > 90 placements: • 63% voluntary to shelter • 19% “aided” (medical transport) • 18% involuntary psych transport • Reaching the hard cases • Involuntary transports • Leading to hospital psych admissions • With connections to ongoing care & supportive housing options
