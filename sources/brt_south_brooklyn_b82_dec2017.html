### [p003_c0001] B82 Project Background
[p003_c0002] 
The Southern Brooklyn B82 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p003_c0003] • Serves 32,000 daily riders on B82 Limited and Local • Heavily used route with slow trips • Important east-west transit connection across Southern Brooklyn • Connects to D N F B Q L subway lines and 21 bus routes, including B44 Nostrand SBS and B46 Utica SBS • Serves areas far from the subway

### [p004_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p004_c0002] 
Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
[p004_c0003] 
SBS has brought:
- [p004_c0004] • 10-30% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p004_c0005] 
There are 15 Select Bus Service routes in operation serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p004_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 15 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes in operation. The routes are highlighted in blue and labeled with their respective route numbers and locations.
