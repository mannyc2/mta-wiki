This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to temporarily adjust schedules to support a series of Switch Installations in West Side Yard.
### [p001_c0005] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS

Cited by 5 records

[p001_c0006] 
Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0007] • West Side Yard – Switch Installation – On the weekends of February 3-4, February 10-11, February 17-18-19, and February 24-25, all yard tracks within West Side Storage Yard, which is the primary storage facility for Penn Station, will be out of service to support a series of switch installations. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the tracks in West Side Yard out of service each weekend, Far Rockaway Branch trains will be diverted from Penn Station to Grand Central Madison for a period of approximately 48-hours.
### [p001_c0008] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p001_c0009] 
Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
