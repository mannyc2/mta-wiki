### [p002_c0010] METRO-NORTH RAILROAD COMMITTEE WORK PLAN
### [p002_c0011] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0012] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0013] Key Performance Metrics Reports
[p002_c0014] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p002_c0015] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0016] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0017] Work Plan
[p002_c0018] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0019] President's Report
[p002_c0020] A monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0021] Chief's Report
[p002_c0022] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0023] Safety
[p002_c0024] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0025] Police Activity Report
[p002_c0026] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p002_c0027] Information Items (if any)
[p002_c0028] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p002_c0029] Action Items (if any)
[p002_c0030] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Financial Report
[p003_c0002] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0003] Procurements
[p003_c0004] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0005] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0006] OCTOBER 2024
### [p003_c0007] 2025 Preliminary Budget
[p003_c0008] Public comment will be accepted on the 2025 Budget.
### [p003_c0009] NOVEMBER 2024
[p003_c0010] None
### [p003_c0011] DECEMBER 2024
### [p003_c0012] 2025 Final Proposed Budget
[p003_c0013] The Committee will recommend action to the Board on the Final Proposed Budget for 2025.
### [p003_c0014] 2025 Proposed Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0015] The Committee Chair will present a draft Metro-North Committee Work Plan for 2025 that will address initiatives to be reported throughout the year.
### [p003_c0016] Diversity & EEO Report– 3 rd Quarter 2024
[p003_c0017] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p003_c0018] Review of Committee Charter
[p003_c0019] Annual review and approval of the MNR Committee Charter.
### [p003_c0020] JANUARY 2025
### [p003_c0021] Approval of 2025 Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0022] The Committee will approve the Proposed Metro-North Railroad Committee Work Plan for 2025 that will address initiatives to be reported on throughout the year.
### [p003_c0023] FEBRUARY 2025
### [p003_c0024] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2025
[p003_c0025] The Agency will present its revised 2025 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2025 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2025 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2025 Adopted Budget.
### [p003_c0026] 2024 Year in Review
[p003_c0027] A review of the prior year's performance of railroad service will be provided to the Committee.
### [p003_c0028] MARCH 2025
### [p003_c0029] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p003_c0030] Annual report to the Committee on system-wide reliability and availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
