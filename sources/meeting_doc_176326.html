### [p003_c0001] President's Report
[p003_c0002] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p003_c0003] Safety Report
[p003_c0004] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators
### [p003_c0005] Police Report
[p003_c0006] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p003_c0007] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0008] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0009] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0010] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0011] Procurements
[p003_c0012] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0013] Police Activity Report
[p003_c0014] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p003_c0015] PERFORMANCE SUMMARIES
### [p003_c0016] Operations Report
[p003_c0017] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p003_c0018] Financial Report
[p003_c0019] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0020] Ridership Report
[p003_c0021] A monthly report will be provided that compares actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues against prior year results.
### [p003_c0022] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS

### [p003_c0024] Approval of 2025 Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0025] The Committee will approve the Proposed Long Island Rail Road Committee Work Plan for 2025 that will address initiatives to be reported on throughout the year.
### [p003_c0026] DECEMBER 2025
### [p003_c0027] Review Committee Charter
