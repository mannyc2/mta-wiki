### [p004_c0001] About Select Bus Service
[p004_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit
[p004_c0003] SBS brings:
[p004_c0004] Buses 15-20% faster
[p004_c0005] 10%+ increases in ridership in first year
[p004_c0006] 95%+ customer satisfaction
[p004_c0007] Improved passenger comfort & convenience
