### [p002_c0001] Total Collision Rate vs Traffic Volume
> [p002_c0002] This line chart illustrates the relationship between monthly traffic volume and the collision rate per million vehicles from 2019 to 2026. The left Y-axis represents Monthly Traffic Volume (millions), ranging from 0 to 35. The right Y-axis represents the Collision Rate per Million Vehicles, ranging from 0 to 8. The X-axis shows the years from 2019 to 2026. The 'Collision Rate per Million Vehicles' (solid blue line) shows a sharp decline from approximately 7.5 in 2019 to about 2.5 in 2026. The 'Traffic Trend Line' (dashed blue line) shows a steady increase from approximately 5.2 in 2019 to about 7.0 in 2026. Two yellow stars mark key points: 'BWB geometry improvements' at the intersection of the two lines in early 2020, and 'VNB roadway widening' at the end of the collision rate line in mid-2024.
