### [p004_c0001] 2020 - Pension Support Program
### [p004_c0002] Pension Quality Assurance Support Program:
- [p004_c0003] • The Quality Assurance (QA) group continued to review calculations for new retirees, Retroactive Wage Adjustments (RWA) and other calculation adjustments. • Plans reviewed include the LIRR's Defined Benefit (DB) Open and Closed Pension Plan, MNR DB Pension Plan, MTA Police DB Pension Plan, and MTA Bus Plan. • The Pension QA Group reviewed 1,470 pension files from January 2020 through December 2020.

### [p005_c0001] 2020 - On Board Program
### [p005_c0002] Railroad Onboard Revenue Program:
- [p005_c0003] • MTA Audit Services perform observations onboard the LIRR and MNR trains to ensure that conductors are collecting the correct fare and they are in compliance with operating processes. • Monthly reporting is provided to both agencies for: 1) fare not collected, 2) incorrect fare collected, 3) conductor not seen and, 4) other special observation request results. • The program was put on hold from March 17, through June 30, 2020, due to the Covid-19 emergency; and resumed July 1 st .
