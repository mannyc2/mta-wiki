### [p002_c0002] Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study Overview
### [p002_c0003] Study Goal:
- [p002_c0004] • Identify cost-effective ways to improve bus speed and reliability for local and express buses that use the QBB.
### [p002_c0005] Study Principles:
- [p002_c0006] • Consult stakeholders • Ensure pedestrian and driver safety • Balance the needs of all bridge users • Evaluate benefits and potential impacts • Minimize impact on adjacent neighborhoods

### [p003_c0002] Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study Project Need
- [p003_c0003] • The QBB is a major bus corridor – 16,000 bus trips a day – 110 buses during PM rush hour – Local service between Manhattan and Queens • Congestion on QBB is a major cause of bus delay – Significant bus delay during rush hour – Over 75% of QBB bus riders report regular delays – Congestion slows buses, making transit less attractive
