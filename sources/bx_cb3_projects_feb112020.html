### [p008_c0001] Corridor Background & Issues
> [p008_c0002] A map of the Bx35 bus corridor in Manhattan. The route is highlighted in blue, starting at 167th Street and 1st Avenue, heading south through the Bronx, crossing the East River, and continuing south through Manhattan. Key landmarks include Crotona Park, Morrisania, and the Bronx Museum of the Arts. Various bus stops are marked with colored circles and numbers. A black rectangle highlights the section of the route between 167th Street and Morris Street.
- [p008_c0003] • Bx35 carries 22,000 weekday riders • Important crosstown connections to 8 bus routes and the 4, B/D Trains, and Manhattan • Average bus speeds: – 4.7 mph AM peak – 4.3 mph PM peak • Key issues: – Congested corridor w/ slow bus speeds – Double parking and loading – Vehicles blocking bus stops
