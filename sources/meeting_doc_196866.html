### [p002_c0006] Purpose
[p002_c0007] To obtain Presidential approval, and to inform the NYC Transit and MTA Bus Committee, of weekday schedule adjustments on the 2 3 4 5 to address shifts in demand, to provide improved service to customers, and to make service more reliable.
### [p002_c0008] Discussion
[p002_c0009] NYCT routinely reviews and revises schedules in order to provide passengers with efficient, reliable and effective service. We are proposing minor schedule changes to improve service and operations on the 2 3 4 5 on weekdays. Basic information about these proposed adjustments is outlined below, and additional details are shown in Attachment 1.
[p002_c0010] On both the Seventh Avenue (2 3) and Lexington Avenue (4 5) express lines, shifts in the times that passengers are traveling on the subway suggest a need to change weekday schedules. Train trips would be reallocated to better match changes in ridership by time of day, as well as to improve reliability, by shifting trips earlier or later across all four lines. These changes would generally spread peak service out, resulting in more even headways in the AM and PM peak hours and reducing the potential for delays through the Nostrand Junction bottleneck.
