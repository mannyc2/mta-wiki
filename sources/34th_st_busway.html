### [p002_c0001] Transit & Truck Priority
[p002_c0002] 34th Street will become a busway:
- [p002_c0003] • Eastbound from Ninth Ave. to Third Ave. • Westbound from Third Ave. to Ninth Ave.
[p002_c0004] This busway is designed to:
- [p002_c0005] • Increase bus speeds and reliability for 28,000+ daily bus riders on M34/M34A SBS and 22 express bus routes. • Improve truck movement and deliveries to local businesses along 34th Street.
### [p002_c0006] New 34th Street Busway Regulations
[p002_c0007] 6 AM – 10 PM / 7 days a week
[p002_c0008] Only buses, trucks with 6+ wheels, Access-A-Ride vans, and emergency vehicles, may travel all the way through on 34th Street between Third Ave. and Ninth Ave. in both directions.
[p002_c0009] Passenger vehicles, including for-hire vehicles, may make local trips by turning onto the busway from an avenue. Passenger vehicles may expeditiously drop off and pick up passengers at every block along the busway, and park where allowed per posted parking regulations.
[p002_c0010] After turning onto the busway, passenger vehicles must make the first possible right turn off the busway following all posted signage. All left turns on the busway are prohibited, except for authorized buses at select intersections. The map on the other side of this brochure shows how vehicles can access each block of the busway.
### [p002_c0011] Frequently Asked Questions
[p002_c0012] Are passenger vehicles, including for-hire vehicles, allowed on the busway?
[p002_c0013] Yes, all passenger and forhire vehicles are allowed to enter the 34th Street Busway for access to all blocks. Vehicles must turn onto the busway from an avenue and make the next available right turn.
[p002_c0014] How will I know what the rules are when I'm driving on 34th Street?
[p002_c0015] Posted signage on 34th Street and intersecting avenues explain the rules. Red paint and “Bus Truck Only” markings will also indicate the busway.
[p002_c0016] If I can't drive all the way down 34th Street, what other routes can I use?
[p002_c0017] If you are traveling westbound, you can drive on 31st St., 35th St., 37th St., or other routes. If you are traveling eastbound, you can drive on 30th St., 36th St., 38th St., or other routes.
[p002_c0018] Is parking still allowed on the busway?
[p002_c0019] Yes, parking is allowed on the busway. Follow the posted parking regulations.
[p002_c0020] Which vehicles are considered trucks?
[p002_c0021] A truck is any vehicle designed to transport property that has more than two axles or six or more tires.
[p002_c0022] How is the busway enforced?
[p002_c0023] Restrictions are enforced through NYPD traffic agents and automated cameras, both of which may issue warnings and summonses. Camera violations incur a $50 fine for a first violation and up to $250 for the fifth or subsequent violation. NYPD bus lane moving violations are $150.
[p002_c0024] How are curb regulations enforced?
[p002_c0025] NYPD traffic agents will handle enforcement.
[p002_c0026] Learn more at nyc.gov/34Busway
