[p001_c0003] Draft plans for Woodhaven Boulevard between Queens Boulevard and Union Turnpike
> [p001_c0004] The figure is an aerial map of Woodhaven Boulevard, showing the proposed bus service layout. The map includes the following street names and bus service symbols: Queens Blvd: Features a 'ONLY' turn lane symbol, a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol, and a bus stop sign for 'SBS Local Express Woodhaven Boulevard'. Wetherole Street: Features a 'ONLY' turn lane symbol, a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol, and a bus stop sign for 'Local Express Woodhaven Boulevard'. 60 Av: Features a 'ONLY' turn lane symbol and a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol. Eliot Av: Features 'NO TURN' symbols for both directions and a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol. 61 Road / Alderton Street: Features a 'ONLY' turn lane symbol, a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol, and a bus stop sign for 'Local Express Woodhaven Boulevard'. Dry Harbor Road: Features a 'ONLY' turn lane symbol and a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol. 62 Rd: Features a 'NO PARKING' symbol, a 'NO STANDING' bus stop symbol, and a bus stop sign for 'Local Woodhaven Boulevard'. The map also shows landmarks such as Queens Center Mall, Hoffman Park, and various local streets like Hoffman Dr, Wetherole St, 60 Av, Eliot Av, 61 Rd, Alderton St, and Dry Harbor Rd. A large 'DRAFT' watermark is overlaid on the map. Two detailed street cross-sections are provided at the bottom: A Woodhaven Boulevard at Queens Boulevard: Compares 'Existing' and 'Proposed' street layouts. The 'Proposed' layout includes a dedicated bus lane and a bus stop. B Woodhaven Boulevard at 62 Road: Compares 'Existing' and 'Proposed' street layouts. The 'Proposed' layout includes a dedicated bus lane and a bus stop.
