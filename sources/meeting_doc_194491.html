### [p014_c0004] Some examples of what we did to increase productivity:
- [p014_c0005] • Decreased days lost due Workers' Comp claims by 10% since 2024 • Reduced our “time to hire” new staff – NYCT down from 166 days in 2023 to 105 days in 2025 • Installed new OMNY Card Vending Machines, which go out-of-service 70% less frequently than MetroCard Vending Machines • Reduced our cash collection efforts as we collect 68% less cash than we did in 2018 • Reduced our procurement time frames – processing orders for goods procured through informal bids in our operating agencies (goods under $1M) have gone from 9 days to 7 days
