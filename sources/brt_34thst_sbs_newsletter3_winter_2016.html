### [p001_c0004] Faster Buses and Safer Streets Come to 34th Street!
[p001_c0005] 
Since 2008, NYCDOT and MTA New York City Transit have been improving 34th Street. In November 2015, the section between 7th Avenue and Lexington Avenue was completed with four new bus bulbs, freshly painted bus lanes, new parking/loading space, and resurfaced streets. A bus bulb is where the sidewalk is widened to meet the offset bus lane at a bus stop. These bus bulbs provide more room for people to wait and allow buses to travel in and out of the stop without pulling over.

- [p002_c0007] • Eight bus bulbs at bus stops* • One curb extension • Repaving and restripping for new bus lanes and loading zones • New signage
### [p002_c0008] Benefits
- [p002_c0009] • Faster and more reliable bus service • Added pedestrian space • More comfortable waiting area for bus riders

* Two additional bulbs will open in the future. The bus bulb at the northwest corner of 8th Avenue will be completed upon removal of New Yorker Hotel scaffolding and the bus bulb at the southeast corner of 11th Avenue will open upon the completion of 55 Hudson Yards.
### [p002_c0011] East Section
### [p002_c0012] Lexington Avenue to FDR Service Road

Cited by 2 records

[p002_c0013] 
Construction of the 34th Street Select Bus Service capital project from Lexington Avenue to FDR Service Road is expected to be completed by December 2016 by New York City Department of Design and Construction.
### [p002_c0014] Project Components
- [p002_c0015] • Three bus bulbs at bus stops • Two curb extensions • Repaving and restripping for new bus lanes and loading zones • New distribution water main between First Avenue and Third Avenue and new truck water mains at the intersections with Second Ave and Third Ave (part of the NYC Department of Environmental Protection's Third Water Tunnel project) • Replacement of aging sewer segments • Relocation of other utilities as needed • New signage Work Hours Work hours are limited to minimize impacts on traffic flow and neighborhood quality of life. The work hours for this section are: • Monday-Friday 7:00AM to 3:30PM (E. 33rd St to E. 35th St; Monday - Friday 10:00AM to 5:00PM) • Saturday and Sunday 8:00AM to 4:30PM
