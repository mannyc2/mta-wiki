### [p010_c0001] Project Benefits
[p010_c0002] 
Over the length of the corridor, bus lanes, transit signal priority, and quicker boarding would lead to 25% faster trips.
[p010_c0003] 
A trip from Taylor St to Fulton St would be about 3 minutes faster, from 16 minutes today to 13 minutes with SBS.
[p010_c0004] 
More reliable service and less bus bunching.
[p010_c0005] 
With both local and SBS buses traveling the full route, the area north of Flushing Avenue would have more frequent bus service than today.
> [p010_c0006] The map illustrates the proposed Nostrand Ave Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) route.
