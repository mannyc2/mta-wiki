### [p001_c0001] MTA LONG ISLAND RAIL ROAD FEBRUARY FINANCIAL PLAN 2026-2029 2025 FINAL ESTIMATE AND 2026 ADOPTED BUDGET
[p001_c0002] 
In accordance with MTA-approved budget procedures, the following information presents MTA Long Island Rail Road's (LIRR) 2025 Final Estimate, 2026 Adopted Budget and the Financial Plan for 2026-2029. The Adopted Budget reflects the inclusion of MTA Adjustments that were presented in the November Plan and adopted by the MTA Board in December 2025 as well as other technical adjustments.

Cited by 3 records

[p001_c0003] 
Technical adjustments include:
- [p001_c0004] • GASB 101 expense realignment.

Cited by 1 record

[p001_c0005] 
The MTA Plan adjustments from Volume 1 include:
- [p001_c0006] • MTA Operating Efficiencies for various new initiatives that have been identified and are being implemented in 2026.

Cited by 2 records

[p001_c0007] 
The attached also includes schedules detailing the monthly allocation of financials, including headcount and utilization data based on the 2026 Adopted Budget for the purpose of reporting actual results on a monthly basis to the MTA Board.
