## Page 11

### [p011_c0001] BUS RELIABILITY ISSUES
- [p011_c0002] • Bus running time is unreliable even within the same time period due to varying traffic conditions day to day • Runs can vary by 20-40 minutes, leading to bus bunching, long/unpredictable waits at the bus stop, and crowding on the bus
[p011_c0003] _M42 Running Times, Weekdays EB_
> [p011_c0004] The scatter plot displays the running times of M42 buses on weekdays for the EB route. The vertical axis, labeled 'Running Time (min)', ranges from 10 to 70 in increments of 10. The horizontal axis represents time, with labels at 03:00:00, 06:00:00, 09:00:00, 12:00:00, 15:00:00, 18:00:00, and 21:00:00. The data points are represented by a dense cloud of blue dots. The running times are highly variable, with many points clustered between 20 and 40 minutes, but also showing significant outliers, such as a point near 65 minutes around 09:00:00 and another near 55 minutes around 15:00:00. This wide range of running times within a single hour illustrates the unreliability of the bus service.

- [p015_c0003] • Existing roadway conditions do not meet current DOT standards for lane widths. Typical lane widths are 10'-11' for general travel lanes and 11'-12' for bus lanes. • 42 St is marked for 2 travel lanes with 1 curbside bus lane in each direction (in effect 7-10 AM, 4-7 PM except Sunday). However, it rarely functions that way due to loading needs and traffic conditions.
