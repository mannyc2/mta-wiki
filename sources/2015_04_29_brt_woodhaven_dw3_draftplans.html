> [p004_c0004] Diagram showing traffic signage for Cross Bay Boulevard between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue. The signs indicate the following traffic flow and bus service: 161 Avenue: Two-way traffic (straight and right-turn lanes). Bus stop: Local (Cross Bay Boulevard). 162 Avenue: Two-way traffic (straight and right-turn lanes). 163 Avenue: One-way traffic (left-turn lane only). Bus stop: SBS Local (Cross Bay Boulevard). 164 Avenue: Two-way traffic (straight and right-turn lanes). 165 Avenue: One-way traffic (right-turn lane only).
> [p004_c0005] Aerial photograph of Cross Bay Boulevard showing the proposed bus service locations. The image is overlaid with a large 'DRAFT' watermark. Points A and B are marked on the photograph to indicate the locations of the detailed diagrams below.
[p004_c0006] _A Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue_
> [p004_c0007] Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue. Existing: Shows a 20' parking/travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 11' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 24' parking/travel lane, and a 110'-0" total width. Proposed: Shows a 20' parking/travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 11' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 24' parking/travel lane, and a 110'-0" total width. It also includes a bus stop and a bus lane.
[p004_c0008] _B Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue_
> [p004_c0009] Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue. Existing: Shows a 20' parking/travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 11' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 24' parking/travel lane, and a 110'-0" total width. Proposed: Shows a 20' parking/travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 11' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 12' travel lane, 24' parking/travel lane, and a 110'-0" total width. It also includes a bus stop and a bus lane.
