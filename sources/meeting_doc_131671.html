[p003_c0004] 
Net preliminary operating results for YTD December were favorable to the November Forecast by $517 million, with the month of December favorable by $224 million. The $517 million YTD favorable variance reflects favorable expenses of $539 million (approximately 3.3% of the 2023 expense budget). Based on a preliminary review, the MTA estimates that approximately half of the favorable expense variance is timing related, and the real/timing analysis will be revised as 2023 financials are finalized.

### [p013_c0002] Triborough Bridge and Tunnel Authority Sales Tax Revenue Bonds, Series 2024A (TBTA Capital Lockbox - City Sales Tax)
[p013_c0003]
