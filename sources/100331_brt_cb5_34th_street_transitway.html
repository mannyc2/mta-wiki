## Page 13

### [p013_c0001] Transitway Overview
> [p013_c0002] The map illustrates the proposed 34th Street Transitway. A yellow double-lined corridor, representing the transitway, runs along the east curb of 34th Street. Green arrows indicate General Traffic Lanes, and red arrows indicate Bus Lanes. The transitway is shown as a two-way system. Key landmarks include Pier 79, Javits Center, Penn Station/MSG, and E 34th St Pier. A legend in the bottom right corner identifies the green arrow as 'General Traffic Lanes' and the red arrow as 'Bus Lanes'.
- [p013_c0003] • Two-way Transitway for length of 34 th Street aligned along curb • Single side alignment selected based on street width and corridor needs – street is too narrow to place Transitway down center of street, and allow parking on either side of the street. • Transitway occupies half of street, north half east of Fifth Avenue, south half east of Sixth Avenue, with a new plaza from Fifth to Sixth • General Traffic: One-Way Eastbound only east of Fifth Avenue One-Way Westbound only west of Sixth Avenue
[p013_c0004] _New York City Transit_
