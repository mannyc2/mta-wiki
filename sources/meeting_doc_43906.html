### [p003_c0006] Overview
- [p003_c0007] YTD June costs were $3M below Budget. Major drivers of the unfavorable non-reimbursable YTD variance include higher vacancy/absentee coverage requirements and higher-than-expected weather-related events at NYCT, partially offset by lower maintenance requirements, and reduced traffic congestion/lower unscheduled service at MTA Bus; lower requirements for maintenance, and tightened management procedures at the LIRR; revised T&E crew requirements at MNR; revised MTAPD deployment assumptions at MTA HQ; and managerial efficiencies and rescheduled maintenance at B&T. The favorable reimbursable YTD variance results from lower capital support requirements at NYCT, and less capital project activity at the LIRR and MNR, partially offset by higher coverage requirements for MTAPD at MTA HQ.
