### [p001_c0004] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0005] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to temporarily adjust schedules to support a Switch Installation near Floral Park, Maintenance on the Port Washington Branch, and Platform Repairs at Bellerose,
### [p001_c0006] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0007] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0008] • Switch Installation – Main Line – On the weekend of June 1-2 and June 22-23, two of three main tracks on the Main Line will be taken out of service between Queens and Merillon Avenue for a switch installation. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With two of three main tracks out of service on the Main Line, Ronkonkoma Branch service will be reduced to hourly in both directions, and service adjustments will be required on the Huntington/Port Jefferson, Oyster Bay, Hempstead, and Babylon/Montauk Branches. • Port Washington Branch – Track Maintenance – On Saturday, June 15 th , the single main track between Great Neck and Port Washington will be out of service to support track maintenance. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the single main track out of service between Great Neck and Port Washington, bus service will replace train service for a period of approximately 30 hours. • Main Line – Bellerose Platform Repairs – On Saturday, June 15 th , one of the two main tracks that service Bellerose Station will be taken out of service for a period of 24 hours as repairs will be made to the station platform. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With one main track out of service through Bellerose, and with routing limitations through Queens Interlocking, eastbound Main Line trains will bypass Elmont – UBS Arena Station for a period of approximately 30 hours. The work at Bellerose Station will not begin until after the event at UBS Arena on Friday evening.
### [p001_c0009] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p001_c0010] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
