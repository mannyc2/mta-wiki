### [p001_c0002] October 2022 Crime Report
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this report is to provide Committee Members with statistical information regarding the number of major felonies including: homicide, robbery, assault, rape in addition to hate crime incidents occurring in the NYCT Subway system. The report is submitted by NYPD's Transit Bureau on a monthly basis for the month ending prior to the reporting period.
