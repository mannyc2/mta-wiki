### [p001_c0003] New Truck Loading Zones on 125th Street New Parking on 124th and 126th Streets
> [p001_c0004] A map of the 125th Street area in New York City. The map shows a grid of streets including Amsterdam Av, Morningside Av, St Nicholas Av, Frederick Douglass Blvd, Adam Clayton Powell Blvd, Lenox Av, Madison Av, Park Av, Lexington Av, and 5 Av. 125th Street runs horizontally across the map, with 126th Street above it and 124th Street below it. The map highlights several new features: 10 blocks of 125th Street are marked with black rectangles for 'AM Commercial Loading Mon-Fri'. New 'Alternate-Side Parking' is indicated by green dashed lines along 126th Street and 124th Street. New 'Metered Parking' is indicated by blue dashed lines along 124th Street. A legend in the bottom right corner defines these symbols. A note '* not to scale' is present in the bottom right corner.
> [p001_c0005] A red rectangular sign with a white border. It features a large white number '1' in the top left corner, followed by the text 'hour metered parking'. Below this, in bold red text, it says 'COMMERCIAL VEHICLES ONLY'. Underneath, in smaller red text, it says 'OTHERS NO STANDING'. At the bottom, in red text, it says 'Monday - Friday'. A red double-headed arrow is at the bottom of the sign.
### [p001_c0006] Coming in May 2014:
- [p001_c0007] • Morning commercial loading zones on 10 blocks of 125th Street to reduce double parking • 227 new parking spaces on 124th and 126th Streets to provide better access for shoppers and residents • Longer bus stops on 125th Street to improve pedestrian safety • Faster, more reliable bus service for over 32,000 daily passengers on 125th Street
