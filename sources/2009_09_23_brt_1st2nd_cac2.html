### [p018_c0001] First Avenue/Second Avenue SBS
### [p018_c0002] Station Locations - Placement
[p018_c0003] Manhattan blocks offer 200 feet of usable curb space. This allows for SBS Stations and local stops to be on the same block.
[p018_c0004] Trees, café seating, cellar doors, subway gratings, newsstands, fire hydrants, driveways, etc. can limit usable space on many blocks.
