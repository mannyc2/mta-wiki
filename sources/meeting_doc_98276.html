### [p002_c0016] METRO-NORTH RAILROAD COMMITTEE WORK PLAN
### [p002_c0017] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0018] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0019] Summary of Actions (if any)
[p002_c0020] A summary is provided by agency of any procurement or action item included in the monthly agenda.
### [p002_c0021] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0022] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0023] Work Plan
[p002_c0024] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0025] President's Report
[p002_c0026] A monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0027] Safety
[p002_c0028] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0029] Police Activity Report
[p002_c0030] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p002_c0031] Information Items (if any)
[p002_c0032] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0002] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0003] Procurements
[p003_c0004] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0005] Operations
[p003_c0006] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key operating and performance statistics and indicators.
### [p003_c0007] Finance
[p003_c0008] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast both on an accrual and cash basis.
### [p003_c0009] Ridership
[p003_c0010] A monthly report will be provided that includes a comparison of actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues with the budget and prior year results.
### [p003_c0011] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0012] OCTOBER 2022
### [p003_c0013] 2023 Preliminary Budget
[p003_c0014] Public comment will be accepted on the 2023 Budget.
### [p003_c0015] LIRR/MNR PTC Project Update
[p003_c0016] The Committee will be briefed on the status of project implementation and close-out following full Positive Train Control functionality for both railroads going into effect in December 2020.
### [p003_c0017] Track Program Quarterly Update
[p003_c0018] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
### [p003_c0019] NOVEMBER 2022
### [p003_c0020] Progress on Way Ahead Strategic Plan
[p003_c0021] A biannual report to the Committee on Metro-North's progress in setting the standard for safety, reliability and innovation in the delivery of excellent customer service.
### [p003_c0022] DECEMBER 2022
### [p003_c0023] 2023 Final Proposed Budget
[p003_c0024] The Committee will recommend action to the Board on the Final Proposed Budget for 2023.
### [p003_c0025] 2023 Proposed Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0026] The Committee Chair will present a draft Metro-North Committee Work Plan for 2023 that will address initiatives to be reported throughout the year.
### [p003_c0027] Diversity & EEO Report– 3 rd Quarter 2022
[p003_c0028] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.

### [p004_c0004] Approval of 2023 Committee Work Plan
[p004_c0005] The Committee will approve the Proposed Metro-North Railroad Committee Work Plan for 2023 that will address initiatives to be reported on throughout the year.
### [p004_c0006] Track Program Quarterly Update
[p004_c0007] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
### [p004_c0008] FEBRUARY 2023
### [p004_c0009] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2023
[p004_c0010] The Agency will present its revised 2023 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2023 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2023 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2023 Adopted Budget.
### [p004_c0011] 2022 Annual Operating Results
[p004_c0012] A review of the prior year's performance of railroad service will be provided to the Committee.
### [p004_c0013] MARCH 2023
### [p004_c0014] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p004_c0015] Annual report to the Committee on system-wide reliability and availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
### [p004_c0016] Diversity & EEO Report– 4 th Quarter 2022
[p004_c0017] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p004_c0018] APRIL 2023
### [p004_c0019] Final Review of 2022 Operating Results
[p004_c0020] A review of the prior year's budget results and their implications for current and future budget performance will be presented to the Committee.
### [p004_c0021] 2022 Annual Ridership Report
[p004_c0022] A report will be presented to the Committee on Metro-North's ridership trends during 2022 based on monthly ticket sales data and the results of train ridership counts conducted by Metro-North.
### [p004_c0023] MAY 2023
### [p004_c0024] Track Program Quarterly Update
[p004_c0025] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
