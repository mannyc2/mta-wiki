Board Member Zuckerman expressed his interest in the number of transit police currently in the system as well as the homeless population trends. Chair Lieber stated both of those topics are on the table and will be sure to provide a response.

Board Member Tessitore commented on the importance of employee safety and how important it is for perpetrators to be held accountable for any violence toward an MTA employee. Chair Lieber pointed out we are in control of policing with MTAPD on the commuter railroads and partner with NYPD in the subways, and work to deter employee assaults. Chair Lieber highlighted that emotionally disturbed individuals have a disproportionate impact regarding assaults within our system and we seek help from the city and Mayor in that area.
