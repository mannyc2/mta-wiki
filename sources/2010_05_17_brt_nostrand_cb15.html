## Page 11

### [p011_c0001] Corridor Description
[p011_c0002] 9.3 miles from Williamsburg Bridge to Sheepshead Bay
[p011_c0003] Currently served by B44 bus route
- [p011_c0004] • 44,000 weekday riders – 5 th busiest bus route in city • 6,000 people get on in CB15 • Buses travel at an average speed of 7-8 mph
[p011_c0005] Within a ¼ mile:
- [p011_c0006] • 300,000 residents • 62% of households do not own a car (41% in CB15) • 60% of residents commute by transit (48% in CB15, compared to 42% by car, truck, or van)

### [p012_c0001] Project Features

## Page 14

### [p014_c0001] Project Benefits
[p014_c0002] Bus lanes, transit signal priority, and quicker boarding would lead to 25% faster trips.
[p014_c0003] A trip from Emmons Ave to Flatbush Ave would be about 6 minutes faster, from 25 minutes today to 19 minutes with SBS.
[p014_c0004] Signal optimization will help all traffic to flow more smoothly.
[p014_c0005] More reliable service and less bus bunching.
[p014_c0006] With both local and SBS buses traveling the full route, the area south of Avenue U would have more frequent bus service than today.
