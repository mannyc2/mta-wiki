### [p001_c0001] SELECT BUS SERVICE IN NYC
[p001_c0002] Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) is a cost-effective approach to transit service that cities around the world use to make riding the bus more like a subway. BRT improves speed, reliability, and passenger comfort/convenience.
[p001_c0003] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for BRT. The first route launched in 2008, and a total of seven routes have been implemented throughout the City:
- [p001_c0004] • Fordham Road (Bx12 SBS) • Webster Avenue (Bx41 SBS) • First Avenue/Second Avenue (M15 SBS) • Nostrand Avenue (B44 SBS) • 34th Street (M34/M34A SBS) • 125th Street-LGA (M60 SBS) • Hylan Boulevard (S79 SBS)
### [p001_c0005] SBS results include:
- [p001_c0006] • 15-23% faster bus service • 95% rider satisfaction • about 10% increase in ridership • up to 20% reduction in crashes

[p004_c0003] The Flushing to Jamaica CAC consists of elected officials, community boards, representatives from civic organizations, and bus riders. The CAC meets throughout the process to provide feedback on project design and the implementation process.

### [p011_c0001] MAIN STREET VISION ZERO PRIORITY CORRIDOR
### [p011_c0002] City-wide Initiative to Eliminate Traffic Fatalities
[p011_c0003] Main Street (from Northern Blvd to Queens Blvd) is in the top 10 most dangerous roadways in Queens.
[p011_c0004] From 2008-2012, 32 pedestrians were either killed or severely injured.
### [p011_c0005] Priority Intersections on Q44 SBS Route
- [p011_c0006] • Union St/Northern Blvd • Main St/Roosevelt Av • Main St/Elder Av • Main St/Booth Memorial Av • Main St/Union Tpke • Archer Av/Parsons Blvd • Sutphin Blvd/Archer Av
> [p011_c0007] VISION ZERO Safety Improvements ● Left turn restriction ★ Pedestrian Safety Island ⊕ Bus Bulb
### [p011_c0008] VISION ZERO Priority Areas
[p011_c0009] Left Turn Restrictions at key locations address one of the key causes of traffic injuries
[p011_c0010] Left Turn Bays at other locations provide well-defined space for turning vehicles
[p011_c0011] Pedestrian Safety Islands provide crossing refuge
[p011_c0012] Bus Bulbs at busy SBS stops add pedestrian and transit waiting space and shorten crossing distance
