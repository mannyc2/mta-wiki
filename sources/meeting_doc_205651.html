### [p002_c0011] President Sheridan's Remarks
[p002_c0012] 
President Sheridan stated that as Chair Lieber had noted in a recent editorial, TBTA passed the two-year mark of its targeted joint-agency enforcement operations against ghost plates, conducting its 125th joint-operation last week at the RFK Bridge. The task force has expanded, with now 20 regional law enforcement agencies with "boots on the ground" spread across the MTA service region. Since TBTA kicked off this multi-agency initiative with the Governor, Mayor, and Chair Lieber on March 12, 2024, the task force has issued 75,000 summonses, towed 7,000 vehicles, and made 1,700 arrests. Those interdicted are responsible for over $66 million dollars owed collectively to all member agencies. President Sheridan thanked all task force members and their dedicated employees for continuing this work, which is making a difference as shown by a decrease of unbillable tolls.

### [p003_c0003] Competitive Procurements
[p003_c0004] 
DCPO Hanley stated that there is one (1) procurement action sought for Board approval, as follows:
[p003_c0005] 
TBTA seeks approval to award four competitively solicited personal service contracts for Miscellaneous Intelligent Transportation System—or “ITS”—and Operations Systems Consultant Design Services on an as-needed basis. The awards will be to Henningson, Durham & Richardson Architecture and Engineering; PBS Engineering; M&J Engineering; and VHB Engineering. The contracts will be used through task-based work orders in the aggregate estimated amount of $15,000,000 over five years.

Cited by 8 records: Miscellaneous ITS & Operations Systems Consultant Design Services +7 more

[p003_c0006] 
The consultants will support TBTA with design services including the integration and maintenance of the systems to promote safe and efficient operations. This includes field ITS components such as sensors, signs, cameras, and communication links, as well as updates to control systems, cybersecurity needs, and other system integrations. The contracts also provide early-stage engineering and assessments to help plan future capital projects and ensure they align with TBTA's operational and technological needs.
