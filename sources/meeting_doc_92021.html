## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] What would encourage you to use the subway more often?
> [p003_c0002] Intervention Percent of responses Fewer people behaving erratically 17% More visible police and security presence 13.5% Shorter wait times on weekends 11.5% Personal security concerns addressed 10.5% Shorter wait times on weekdays 10.5%
[p003_c0003] Three of the top five interventions that would encourage more subway use are related to safety and security.

## Page 4

### [p004_c0001] What would encourage you to use the bus more often?
> [p004_c0002] Factor Percent of responses Shorter wait times on weekdays 22% Shorter wait times on weekends 15% More reliable service 13% Shorter travel times 9% Better fare incentives 7%
[p004_c0003] Safety and security are not major concerns on buses – shorter wait times and more reliable service will encourage more ridership
