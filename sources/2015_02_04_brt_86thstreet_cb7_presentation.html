### [p003_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p003_c0002] 
Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p003_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p003_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • ~10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p003_c0005] 
There are currently 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p003_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 7 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes. The routes are highlighted in blue and labeled as follows: Bx12: Fordham Rd/ Pelham Pkwy (Westchester County) Bx41: Webster Av (The Bronx) M60: 125 St./Astoria Blvd. to LGA (The Bronx) M34/M34A: 34 St (The Bronx) M15: First Av/ Second Av (Queens) B44: Nostrand Ave/ Rogers Ave (Brooklyn) S79: Hylan Blvd (Staten Island) The map also shows the boroughs: The Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn, Bay Ridge, Staten Island, and Westchester County. ### [p023_c0001] East 86 th St Traffic Analysis Conclusions
- [p023_c0002] • Existing double parking for loading and deliveries blocks travel lanes for long periods • Articulated M86 buses not currently reaching the curb at every stop • SBS fare collection will dramatically speed dwell time at stops • Current traffic conditions lead to lanes blocked for much longer than they would be with M86 SBS bus bulbs
