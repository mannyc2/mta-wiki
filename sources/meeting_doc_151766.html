### [p002_c0002] SECURITY
### [p002_c0003] Metro-North Railroad Security Department
[p002_c0004] 
Utilizing the Metropolitan Transportation Authority's (MTA) security model, the mission of the Metro-North Railroad (MNR) Security Department is to provide threat-based security protection, guidance and management of customers, employees and assets on our trains, at our stations and facilities and within Grand Central Terminal.
[p002_c0005] 
The MNR Security Department was created as a sole department at MNR in 2014. Upon inception, it became our mission to build a transportation security group that could serve as a model for best practices and build a security program that effectively transitions the railroad culture into a culture that embodies security. To enhance that pride in our departmental employees and to broadcast our commitment to the MNR organization, we developed a set of core values for each of our employees to internalize and work towards:
[p002_c0006] 
INTEGRITY • PROFESSIONALISM • SERVICE

### [p004_c0002] Right of Way Task Force (ROWTF) 2022 – 2024 YTD
### [p004_c0003] MISSION
[p004_c0004] 
The purpose of the Right of Way Task Force (RoWTF) is to proactively identify security vulnerabilities in MTA Metro-North Railroad (MNR) territory and create effective mitigation solutions to better secure the Right-of-Way.
### [p004_c0005] RoWTF PARTNERSHIP
[p004_c0006] 
The RoWTF Program is managed by the MNR Security Department and consists of a collaborative partnership between the MNR Security Department, the MTA Police Department (MTAPD), the MNR Maintenance of Way (MoW) Department, the MNR Transportation Department, and the MNR Office of System Safety (OSS).
### [p004_c0007] Typical items addressed:
- [p004_c0008] • Trespassing Issues • Fencing • Signage • Illegal dumping
