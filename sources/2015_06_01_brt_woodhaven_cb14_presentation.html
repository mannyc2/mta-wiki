### [p007_c0001] Woodhaven / Cross Bay SBS corridor
- [p007_c0002] • Based on the existing Q52/53 LTD bus route • 30,000 daily bus riders • 14 miles long from Woodside to the Rockaways • Within a 15-minute walk of the corridor: – 400,000 residents – 43% of households do not own a car – 60% of residents commute by transit
