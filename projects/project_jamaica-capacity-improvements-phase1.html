Jamaica Capacity Improvements - Phase 1

project_jamaica-capacity-improvements-phase1

What The Updates Show

In the November 2022 Long Island Rail Road Joint Committee update, Jamaica Capacity Improvements - Phase 1 appears in the LIRR ESA Support Projects table as "Beneficial Use" with a $321.6 million project budget. [meeting_doc_100321#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0002]

The project slide separates Phase 1 into Stage 1 "ESA Support" and Stage 2 "Universal Outlying Crossovers (Not ESA Support Effort)." [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0002] [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0004]

Stage 1 consists of Platform F and associated station tracks, which the 2022 slide says were in service in November 2021. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0003]

Stage 2 consists of MET Interlocking, Beaver Interlocking, and Union Crossover work. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0004]

In the 2022 slide, MET Interlocking final cutover is marked actual in October 2021, Beaver Interlocking final cutover is marked actual in August 2022, and Union Crossover track infrastructure is still a fourth-quarter-2022 forecast. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0005]

By the November 2023 update, the project remains listed as "Beneficial Use" with the same $321.6 million budget. [meeting_doc_127536#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0002]

The November 2023 project slide keeps Platform F and associated station tracks as in service in November 2021, keeps MET Interlocking and Beaver Interlocking as actual October 2021 and August 2022 final cutovers, and changes Union Crossover track infrastructure to a January 2023 actual. [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0003] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0005]

Timeline In The Source Records

Platform F and associated station tracks were in service in November 2021. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0003] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0003]

MET Interlocking final cutover was October 2021, marked actual in both updates. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0005] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0005]

Beaver Interlocking final cutover was August 2022, marked actual in both updates. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0005] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0005]

Union Crossover track infrastructure was forecast for fourth quarter 2022 in the November 2022 update, then reported as actual in January 2023 in the November 2023 update. [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0005] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0005]

Read source-time, the safest summary is that Phase 1 was already in "Beneficial Use" in the 2022 and 2023 project tables, while one Stage 2 component moved from forecasted to actual between the two updates. [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_100321#p004_c0005] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_127536#p004_c0005]
