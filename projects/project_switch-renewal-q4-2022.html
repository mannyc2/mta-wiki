Switch Renewal

project_switch-renewal-q4-2022

Switch Renewal is a LIRR systemwide track-maintenance program covering 916 switches, split between 572 main-line switches and 344 yard switches. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0005] In the Q4 2022 LIRR Maintenance of Way update, the program planned 16 switch renewals and reported 12 installed, or 75% of goal. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0006]

Most Q4 2022 listed locations were reported complete: Divide 21W/21E, Divide 13W, Nassau 2, Queens 221 Crossover, Montauk Jay 65 switch, and Hicksville Yard 1sw & 2sw. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0005] The main incomplete location was West Side Yard, where 2 of 9 planned switches were installed, reported as 22% complete. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0005]

By the LIRR Q2 2023 update, the same 916-switch systemwide frame appeared with 19 switches planned and 2 installed, or 10% of goal; West Side Yard was still listed as 2 installed and explicitly carried forward "from 2022." [meeting_doc_115196#p003_c0002]
