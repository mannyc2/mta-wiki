Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation

project_gcm-contractor-maintenance-cellular-124266

Overview

Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation is identified in LIRR timetable-change staff summaries as a project supported during cited timetable periods spanning from the November 13, 2023-March 3, 2024 period to the September 2-November 9, 2025 period. [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0005]

The cited blocks present the GCM item in recurring supported-project lists, and those lists repeatedly place it alongside Hall Interlocking Expansion, Atlantic or Atlantic Branch Tunnel Lighting, rail work, and West Side Yard work. [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_133331#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_138226#p001_c0006] [meeting_doc_155021#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0006] [meeting_doc_170846#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0005]

Timetable Appearances

In the October 5, 2023 staff summary for the November timetable change and fall trackwork programs, LIRR told the Long Island Committee it planned schedule adjustments from November 13, 2023 through March 3, 2024, and the supported-project list included Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation. [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0005]

In the February 8, 2024 staff summary for the March timetable change and spring trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from March 4, 2024 through May 19, 2024, and again listed Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation. [meeting_doc_133331#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_133331#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_133331#p001_c0005]

In the April 4, 2024 staff summary for the May timetable change and trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from May 20, 2024 through September 2, 2024, and listed Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation among supported projects. [meeting_doc_138226#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_138226#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_138226#p001_c0006]

In the October 9, 2024 staff summary for the November timetable and fall trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from November 11, 2024 through March 2, 2025, and the supported-project list used the shorter label Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance. [meeting_doc_155021#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_155021#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_155021#p001_c0005]

In the February 3, 2025 staff summary for the March timetable change and winter trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from March 3, 2025 through May 19, 2025, and listed Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation among supported projects. [meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0005] [meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0006]

In the March 31, 2025 staff summary for the May timetable change and trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from May 19, 2025 through September 1, 2025, and listed Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation in the same paragraph. [meeting_doc_170846#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_170846#p001_c0004]

In the June 26, 2025 staff summary for the September timetable and trackwork programs, LIRR described schedule adjustments from September 2, 2025 through November 9, 2025, and listed Grand Central Madison Contractor Maintenance and Wireless Cellular Installation in a project list framed around state of good repair and reliability. [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0005]

Context

The November 2023 staff summary also says the timetable included service improvements to reflect new customer travel patterns and demand following implementation of the Grand Central Madison service plan earlier that year. [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0005]

The September 2025 staff summary later describes the supported-project list as work to improve state of good repair and reliability. [meeting_doc_179526#p001_c0005]
