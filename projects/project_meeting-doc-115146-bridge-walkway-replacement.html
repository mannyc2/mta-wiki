Bridge Walkway Replacement Program

project_meeting-doc-115146-bridge-walkway-replacement

The Bridge Walkway Replacement Program appears in Metro-North Railroad Maintenance of Way Division quarterly materials for April, June, and September 2023. [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_115146#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_115146#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0004]

The recurring scope is three bridge-walkway locations. Purdy's Titicus River at HAR 46.26, Track 1, is listed as 100% complete in all three quarterly source pages, and Culvert-Indian Kill at PJ 39.55 is also listed as 100% complete in all three. [meeting_doc_111786#p013_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p013_c0005] [meeting_doc_115146#p013_c0004] [meeting_doc_115146#p013_c0005] [meeting_doc_124216#p013_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p013_c0005]

Breakneck-Wades Brook Railtop at HUD 59.26 is listed under the same program in April and June without a percent-complete note, then in September as 0% complete with work to begin in the fourth quarter. [meeting_doc_111786#p013_c0006] [meeting_doc_111786#p013_c0007] [meeting_doc_115146#p013_c0006] [meeting_doc_115146#p013_c0007] [meeting_doc_124216#p013_c0006] [meeting_doc_124216#p013_c0007]
