Overview

Right of Way Improvements is a Metro-North Maintenance of Way bundle tracked in quarterly Track, Structures, Stations and Facilities updates. The Q4 2022 page lists six component jobs: Mount Vernon West Yard Cleanup & Grading, Harmon Yard Track Compound Paving, North White Plains Work Equipment Area Paving, CP 235 Staircase for CIL Access, Hudson/Harlem Line Transmission Pole Repairs, and Danbury Line catenary pole removal.
