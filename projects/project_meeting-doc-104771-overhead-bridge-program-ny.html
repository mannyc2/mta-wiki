Overhead Bridge Program (NY)

project_meeting-doc-104771-overhead-bridge-program-ny

Overview

Overhead Bridge Program (NY) appears in Metro-North Railroad Track, Structures, Stations and Facilities Maintenance of Way updates as "Bridge Flag Repairs (Harlem, Hudson New Haven-NY/Port Jervis Line)." [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_104771#p007_c0002] The recurring repair scope is replacing deteriorated members, rebuilding deteriorated bridge seats, repairing abutment walls, repairing load-bearing supports, and timber deck replacements. [meeting_doc_104771#p007_c0003] [meeting_doc_115146#p007_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p007_c0003]
