Status And Scope

The Staten Island Express Bus Network Redesign sits within MTA's borough-by-borough Bus Network Redesign program, which IBO describes as intended to improve service in each borough by improving connections, simplifying routes, and speeding up buses. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p007_c0005] IBO's February 2025 report says the Staten Island Express and Bronx Local redesigns had both been completed and implemented, while Brooklyn and Queens were still in the planning stage. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p007_c0005]

An MTA performance report says the Staten Island Express and Bronx Local redesigns were showing positive trends after implementation, but the cited paragraph does not name Staten Island-specific measures or quantify those trends. [meeting_doc_100341#p005_c0002]

Performance Context

IBO identifies its speed analysis as a weekday peak-hour route-speed comparison using January 2019 as the pre-pandemic benchmark and December 2024 as the current comparison point. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p007_c0009] [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p007_c0010] Across all 316 bus routes, IBO found 7.6 mph in December 2024, consistent with 7.8 mph in January 2019. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p007_c0010] Its citywide trip-type table shows Express routes were the fastest category, but down from 13.7 mph in January 2019 to 12.8 mph in December 2024. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p008_c0006]

For Staten Island as a borough, IBO says December 2024 average bus speed was 13.9 mph, the highest by a notable margin; its all-trip-type borough table shows Staten Island at 14.3 mph in January 2019 and 13.9 mph in December 2024. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p008_c0004] [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p008_c0007] IBO also says Staten Island had the lowest borough bus ridership in 2023, at a little over 20 million passengers. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p005_c0008]

Bus-Lane Context

IBO reports that Staten Island had the least bus-lane density of the five boroughs, had no new bus lanes built since the Streets Plan began, and last received a new bus lane in 2020. [speeding_up_slowly_2025#p014_c0005]
