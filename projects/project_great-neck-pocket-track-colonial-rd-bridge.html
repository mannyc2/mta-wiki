Great Neck Pocket Track & Colonial Road Bridge

project_great-neck-pocket-track-colonial-rd-bridge

What The Updates Show

The November 2022 update is internally mixed: its project table lists Great Neck Pocket Track & Colonial Road Bridge in "Beneficial Use Forecast 12/2/22" with a $45.0 million budget, while the project slide itself says "Beneficial Use Achieved." [meeting_doc_100321#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0001] [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0002] The same November 2022 slide still treats the Neck 2 crossover, signal crossover, and commissioning as December 2, 2022 forecasts, even though Switch 12E and Switch 12W are already marked actual on November 6 and November 20, 2022. [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0004]

By the November 2023 update, the project table lists the same project in "Beneficial Use" with the same $45.0 million budget, and the project slide marks the Neck 2 crossover, signal cutover, and commissioning as actual on December 2, 2022. [meeting_doc_127536#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004] The November 2023 slide also keeps Neck 3 as an actual June 2022 crossover and keeps Switch 12E and Switch 12W as actual November 2022 milestones. [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004]

Timeline In The Source Records

  • Colonial Road Bridge: placed in service in May 2016 and bridge construction complete in August 2016, both labeled actual in the project slides. [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0004] [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004]
  • Great Neck Pocket Track: Neck 3 crossover marked actual in June 2022. [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0004] [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004]
  • Great Neck Pocket Track: Switch 12E and Switch 12W marked actual on November 6 and November 20, 2022. [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0004] [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004]
  • Great Neck Pocket Track: Neck 2 crossover, signal cutover, and commissioning move from December 2, 2022 forecasts in the November 2022 slide to December 2, 2022 actuals in the November 2023 slide. [meeting_doc_100321#p006_c0004] [meeting_doc_127536#p006_c0004]

Read source-time, the safest summary is that the project was still partly forecasted in the November 2022 table/milestone slide, but was reported as in beneficial use with the remaining December 2, 2022 milestones actual by the November 2023 update.
