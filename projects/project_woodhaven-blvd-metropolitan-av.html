Woodhaven Blvd – Metropolitan Av

project_woodhaven-blvd-metropolitan-av

Project Snapshot

Woodhaven Blvd - Metropolitan Av is listed as a Woodhaven Boulevard capital project from Yellowstone Blvd to 77 Av, with status "Scoping," Community Boards CB5 and CB6, and estimated construction in 2028. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p016_c0002]

The September 2024 CB5 and CB6 decks use the slash name "Woodhaven Blvd / Metropolitan Av" for the same Yellowstone Blvd-to-77 Av scope, status, community boards, and 2028 estimated construction window. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p016_c0002] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p016_c0002]

The December 2024 next-steps slide lists design start for Woodhaven Blvd - Metropolitan Av in early 2025 and the next community-board design review in late 2026. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p025_c0002]

Proposed Treatments

The September 2024 project slide says the concept would maintain the alignment and operating hours of the bus lanes. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p026_c0003]

The same slide lists upgrades to 5 bus stops, including 2 bus bulbs, 23 sidewalk extensions, 20 shortened crossings, and 6 reconstructed medians. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p026_c0003]

It also lists 7 additional crossings, 5 raised crosswalks, and 20+ street trees. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p026_c0003]

At Woodhaven Blvd and Metropolitan Av, the before/after focus slide identifies existing issues as a crowded bus stop or narrow sidewalk, a non-ADA-compliant median pedestrian refuge, and conflicting left turns at the intersection, while the after condition labels bus bulbs, a refurbished median with street trees, a shortened crossing with an ADA median pedestrian refuge, an offset left turn, and a sidewalk extension. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p026_c0004] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p026_c0005]

Focus Locations

The conceptual-plan slide for Woodhaven Blvd / Metropolitan Av shows a street layout with Woodhaven Blvd, Trotting Course Lane, Metropolitan Av, 73 Av, and Rutledge Av annotations. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p027_c0002]

The CB6 deck also includes a conceptual-plan sheet labeled Yellowstone Blvd from Woodhaven Blvd to Ingram St, dated September 3, 2024. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p028_c0002]

A Polo Pl focus slide shows proposed changes including SBS Stop 1407, landscaping, curb aprons, and a signal change for vehicles from Trotting Course Ln toward Cooper Av and southbound Woodhaven Blvd. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p030_c0003]

A Yellowstone Blvd and Alderton St focus slide shows proposed driveway accommodation, new pedestrian ramp work, ADA pedestrian-ramp upgrades, bus-stop removals tied to the bus network redesign, and conversion of bus-stop areas to parking. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p032_c0003]

A Yellowstone Blvd from Juno St to Ingram St focus slide shows proposed slip-lane closure with a concrete curb extension, sidewalk widening, an ADA pedestrian-ramp upgrade, and conversion of the Selfridge St slip lane and parking area to a two-way cul-de-sac. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p033_c0003]
