East Side Access Readiness

project_meeting-doc-east-side-access-readiness

Overview

East Side Access Readiness appears in these LIRR work-plan sources through the Summer Track Work Programs item, which says LIRR planned schedule adjustments to support various trackwork programs, Main Line Second Track construction, and East Side Access Readiness projects. [meeting_doc_100311#p005_c0010] [meeting_doc_100311#p005_c0011]

The same Summer Track Work Programs wording recurs across June 2023 and June 2024 work-plan entries, tying the Readiness reference to schedule adjustments rather than to a standalone project narrative. Overview

East Side Access Readiness appears in these sources as LIRR committee work-plan schedule-adjustment context, not as a standalone project briefing. In the 2024 work-plan entries, it appears under June 2024 Summer Track Work Programs: LIRR planned to adjust schedules to support various trackwork programs, Main Line Second Track construction, and East Side Access Readiness projects. The proposed 2025 plan lists October 2025 with November Timetable and Fall Trackwork Programs, and its detailed entry says LIRR planned schedule adjustments to support trackwork programs, Main Line Second Track construction, and East Side Access Readiness projects.
