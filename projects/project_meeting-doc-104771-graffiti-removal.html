Graffiti Removal Program

project_meeting-doc-104771-graffiti-removal

The Graffiti Removal Program appears in Metro-North Maintenance of Way Division updates for Track, Structures, Stations and Facilities, with source snapshots dated December 31, 2022, April 2023, and September 2023. [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0004]

The described work was on the Hudson and Harlem lines and involved cleaning graffiti from various buildings, bridges, retaining walls, and rock cuts. [meeting_doc_104771#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0002]

The December 31, 2022 update listed the Graffiti Removal Program as 100% complete. [meeting_doc_104771#p012_c0002]

The April 2023 update listed the same named program and scope as 25% complete, while the September 2023 update listed the same named program and scope as 50% complete. [meeting_doc_111786#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0002]

The project was reported on a page that also grouped Cos Cob Bridge work, Saga bridge work, Right of Way Fence work, and CP 271-to-CP 272 security fencing. [meeting_doc_104771#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0002]

The reported completion percentages should be treated as document-time status snapshots because each relevant page says the figures were subject to change due to current events. [meeting_doc_104771#p012_c0003] [meeting_doc_111786#p012_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0003]
