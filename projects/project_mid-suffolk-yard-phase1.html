Mid-Suffolk Yard (Phase 1)

project_mid-suffolk-yard-phase1

Overview

Mid-Suffolk Yard (Phase 1) is listed in the LIRR ESA Support Projects table as a Mid-Suffolk Yard project in Beneficial Use with a $136.0 million project budget. [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0002]

The project-specific slide labels Mid-Suffolk Yard (Phase 1) as having achieved beneficial use. [meeting_doc_100321#p007_c0001] [meeting_doc_100321#p007_c0002] [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0001] [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0002]

The November 2022 source listed the new yard support facility as complete in March 2020 and the yard and supporting infrastructure as complete in October 2020. [meeting_doc_100321#p007_c0003]

The November 2023 source repeated those two actual completion milestones and updated closeout to December 2022 actual. [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0003]

Timeline Notes

The new yard support facility completion was March 2020 actual. [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0003]

Yard and supporting infrastructure completion was October 2020 actual. [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0003]

Closeout was shown as a July 2023 forecast in the November 2022 presentation and as a December 2022 actual in the November 2023 presentation. [meeting_doc_100321#p007_c0003] [meeting_doc_127536#p007_c0003]

Source Context

The November 2022 and November 2023 presentations both frame Mid-Suffolk Yard within a set of LIRR ESA Support Projects that also includes Jamaica Capacity Improvements Phase 1, Massapequa Pocket Track, Great Neck Pocket Track & Colonial Road Bridge, and Port Washington Yard Track Extensions. [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0001] [meeting_doc_100321#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0001] [meeting_doc_127536#p003_c0002]
