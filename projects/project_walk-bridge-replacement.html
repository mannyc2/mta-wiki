Walk Bridge Replacement

project_walk-bridge-replacement

Walk Bridge Replacement is a Norwalk, Connecticut capital project on the New Haven Line; related records identify the current Norwalk River Railroad Bridge as "Walk Bridge," one of the oldest movable bridges on the Northeast Corridor and important to Amtrak and Metro-North New Haven Line service. [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0005] A May 2023 source says officials broke ground on May 12 for a new vertical-lift railroad bridge with two independent two-track decks, with expected benefits including less service vulnerability from a mechanical issue, higher maintainability, higher vertical clearance, faster operation over the bridge, and greater operational flexibility. [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0002] [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0003] [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0004]

Metro-North described prep work resuming in Norwalk as part of New Haven Line summer construction work in June 2023. [meeting_doc_115141#p005_c0003] An October 2023 schedule-change source said the Walk Bridge replacement at South Norwalk would begin in December 2023 and reduce capacity from four tracks to two tracks between CP 241 at South Norwalk and the new CP 243 interlocking east of East Norwalk. [meeting_doc_124251#p002_c0007] Related readiness work included Metro-North collaboration with CDOT on CP 243, intended to allow the two track outages needed for bridge replacement, plus Dock Yard improvements for added train storage and easier equipment turns. [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0006] [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0007]

By February 2024, sources described the $1 billion Walk Bridge Replacement Project as underway and tied it to East Norwalk work: CDOT would install an underground retention system in the north parking lot and perform utility work during the first of two three-week East Norwalk Station closures. [meeting_doc_135266#p004_c0005] The stated project scope includes replacing the railroad bridge over Fort Point Street, realigning Fort Point Street, replacing bridge structures at Osborne Avenue, East Avenue, and Strawberry Hill Avenue, and improving East Norwalk Station. [meeting_doc_135266#p004_c0005] Later schedule summaries continued to list Walk Bridge replacement among major C&D, CDOT, and Maintenance of Way projects requiring extensive track outages. [meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0008] [meeting_doc_166956#p001_c0010]
