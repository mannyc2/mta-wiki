NY Undergrade Bridge Repair Program

project_meeting-doc-104771-ny-undergrade-bridge-repair

Scope

NY Undergrade Bridge Repair Program appears in Metro-North Railroad Maintenance of Way updates under "Track, Structures, Stations and Facilities Metro-North Railroad." [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_115146#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0003] The program page tracks site-level undergrade bridge repair work, not a single percentage total: the listed work includes flag repairs, concrete vault repairs, masonry arch repairs, steel/concrete repairs, replacement work, and bridge removal across Hudson, Harlem, and Port Jervis locations. [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0002] [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0004] [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0006] [meeting_doc_115146#p009_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p009_c0003]

Reading Notes

Treat the percentages as document-time status snapshots, not final outcomes. The inspected NY Undergrade slide blocks provide site-level completion percentages, but do not state a budget, contract value, or target completion date. [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0004] [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0006] [meeting_doc_111786#p009_c0003] [meeting_doc_115146#p009_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p009_c0003]

The same quarterly decks repeat an "All subject to change due to current events" caveat on nearby project pages, so use the status percentages cautiously. [meeting_doc_104771#p008_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p008_c0004] [meeting_doc_115146#p008_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p008_c0004]
