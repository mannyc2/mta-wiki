Where this data comes from

Sources are public NYC/MTA government records obtained from public agency websites. Every structured record cites evidence blocks in the source documents, and source pages are exported as citation targets.

Extraction is LLM-assisted and may contain errors; extracted text and derived data are provided for research and reference. To request a correction or takedown, open a GitHub issue.
