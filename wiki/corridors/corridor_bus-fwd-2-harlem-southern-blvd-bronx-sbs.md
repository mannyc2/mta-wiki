The [[corridor:corridor_bus-fwd-2-harlem-southern-blvd-bronx-sbs|Harlem to Southern Boulevard (SBS candidate E)]] corridor is one of 21 candidate corridors identified by NYC DOT and the MTA for the next phase of Select Bus Service expansion as part of the Bus Forward 2017 plan [[cite:bus_forward_2#p005_c0001|21 candidate SBS corridors table]]. It was listed under the Bronx borough as candidate "E", named "Harlem to Southern Boulevard" [[cite:bus_forward_2#p005_c0001|Harlem to Southern Boulevard labeled E]]. The candidate corridors were selected through data analysis and public feedback including workshops, on-street surveying, and online feedback [[cite:bus_forward_2#p004_c0002|21 candidate corridors identified via analysis and feedback]]. The Bus Forward plan responded to City Council legislation from 2015 and May 2017 that called for an updated SBS implementation plan and broader transit improvements [[cite:bus_forward_2#p002_c0001|2015 legislation for SBS update]][[cite:bus_forward_2#p002_c0002|May 2017 legislation for transit improvements]]. The overall SBS program has demonstrated that SBS routes achieve [[metric:metric_bus-forward-2-sbs-speed-increase|10-30% faster bus speeds]], about [[metric:metric_bus-forward-2-sbs-ridership-increase|a 10% ridership increase]], and [[metric:metric_bus-forward-2-sbs-customer-satisfaction|95% customer satisfaction]] [[cite:bus_forward_2#p003_c0004|SBS program results]]. The proposed SBS network would serve [[metric:metric_bus-forward-2-proposed-sbs-daily-riders|over 800,000 daily riders]] — over 32% of city bus ridership — up from the [[metric:metric_bus-forward-2-existing-sbs-daily-riders|existing SBS network's 309,000 daily riders]] [[cite:bus_forward_2#p007_c0001|existing and proposed SBS ridership]]. No specific route alignments, terminus points, or implementation timeline for this corridor were detailed in the Bus Forward plan beyond its identification as a candidate corridor.
