The [[corridor:corridor_bushwick-downtown-brooklyn-brt-phase2|Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn Corridor]] was identified as one of 16 potential future BRT corridors during the BRT Phase II planning study conducted by MTA New York City Transit and the New York City Department of Transportation [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Table listing Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn as BRT Phase II candidate]]. The study, initiated in fall 2008, evaluated more than 40 potential corridors against five criteria: community feedback, ridership potential, network benefits, bus lane and busway feasibility, and geographic diversity [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p002_c0003|Phase II study initiation]] [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0003|Evaluation criteria]]. While the Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn Corridor was included on the list of 16 Phase II candidates, it was not among the three highest-priority corridors advanced to the next planning stage—Webster Avenue/Third Avenue, Hillside Avenue, and LaGuardia/East Elmhurst [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0005|Short list of three candidates]].

The BRT Phase II public outreach in spring 2009 included seven workshops with [[metric:metric_workshop-participants-325|325]] participants and over [[metric:metric_survey-respondents-600|600]] online survey respondents, along with representatives from [[metric:metric_community-business-groups-47|47]] community and business groups and [[metric:metric_elected-officials-offices-23|23]] local elected officials' offices [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p002_c0006|Public outreach details]]. From an initial pool of [[metric:metric_potential-corridors-over-40|over 40]] potential corridors, the list was narrowed to 16 based on the five evaluation criteria and public feedback [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0003|Over 40 corridors narrowed to 16]].

The corridor lies within Brooklyn and remains a Phase II candidate for future BRT development, listed on the same table as the short-list routes [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Bushwick to Downtown Brooklyn corridor table entry]]. The sources do not specify the exact street alignment, termini, or service planning details for this corridor, and no implementation timeline has been identified beyond its inclusion as a Phase II candidate [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Table of BRT Phase II candidates]].
