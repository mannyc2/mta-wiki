MNR Zone 1 (CT) covers the territory from New Haven to Bridgeport, serving the Waterbury Branch, as defined in a March 2024 MNR procurement agenda for emergency and scheduled bus services [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|MNR Zone 1 definition]]. This is one of five MNR operating zones established for bus service coverage across the railroad's Connecticut and New York territories [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|MNR Zone table]]. The Connecticut Department of Transportation reimburses MNR 100 percent for the costs of bus service on the Waterbury Branch (along with the Danbury and New Canaan branches in Zone 2) [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p008_c0004|CDOT 100% reimbursement]]. The broader bus-services procurement, Contract #426684, carries a total estimated aggregate budget of [[metric:metric_total-estimated-budget-55m-135311|$55,000,000]], with [[metric:metric_mnr-estimated-budget-30m-135311|$30,000,000]] allocated to MNR across all zones and [[metric:metric_lirr-estimated-budget-25m-135311|$25,000,000]] to LIRR, over a [[metric:metric_contract-term-5-years-135311|five-year contract term]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0006|Budget and term summary]]. MNR proposed the procurement on behalf of itself and LIRR to furnish emergency and scheduled bus services during track outages and service disruptions [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0005|MNR procurement request]]. The RFP was publicly advertised on June 13, 2023, and [[metric:metric_thirteen-proposals-received-135311|13 proposals]] were received by July 24, 2023 [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0010|RFP advertisement date]][[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0011|Proposals received date]]. The Selection Committee recommended [[metric:metric_recommended-firms-13-135311|13 firms]] for contract award, with bus companies assigned to territories that include MNR-only, LIRR-only, and joint MNR/LIRR coverage [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0003|13 recommended firms]][[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0004|Territory-bus company table]].
