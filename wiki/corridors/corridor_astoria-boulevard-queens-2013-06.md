The [[corridor:corridor_astoria-boulevard-queens-2013-06|Astoria Boulevard]] corridor in Queens carries the M60 SBS route between the RFK Bridge and the LaGuardia Airport area. As presented at a June 2013 Community Board 1 briefing, the proposed M60 SBS route begins in Manhattan at 125th Street, crosses the RFK Bridge, and follows Astoria Boulevard before branching toward the LGA terminals [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p006_c0004|M60 proposed SBS route map]]. The M60 SBS was planned to stop at major intersections in Astoria and East Elmhurst along this corridor [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p006_c0005|M60 SBS stops at major intersections in Astoria and East Elmhurst]].

As of the June 2013 briefing, other street design changes on Astoria Boulevard were still in development [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p007_c0006|Other street design changes on Astoria Blvd still in development]]. The next steps listed for the project included developing Astoria Boulevard recommendations [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p008_c0006|Develop Astoria Boulevard recommendations]]. The M60 SBS service context at the time showed growing ridership — from 14,408 average weekday riders in 2007 to 17,358 in 2012 [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p003_c0006|Average Weekday M60 Ridership table]] — and buses were stopped 45% of the time, with heavy double-parking in Manhattan contributing to bunching and delays that affected Queens operations [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p004_c0005|Buses are stopped 45% of the time]] [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p004_c0006|Heavy double parking in Manhattan leads to bus bunching/delays in Queens]].

The corridor's boarding and alighting patterns were concentrated in Manhattan: 49% of boardings and 51% of alightings occurred on 125th Street, while Queens accounted for 19% of boardings and 22% of alightings, and LaGuardia Airport for 11% of boardings and 10% of alightings [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p002_c0005|Boardings by Area pie chart]] [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p002_c0007|Alightings by Area pie chart]]. Travel time data showed an eastbound total trip of 56 minutes (31 minutes in motion, 10 stopped at red lights, 15 at bus stops) and a westbound total of 48.7 minutes [[cite:2013_06_06_sbs_125th_cb1#p004_c0004|M60 Bus Speed and Reliability table]].
