This corridor covers [[corridor:corridor_manhattan-av-110th-116th-cb9-jun2025|Manhattan Avenue between 110th Street and 116th Street]] in Manhattan, with a study area that also includes Morningside Avenue between 113th and 114th Streets [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p013_c0005|Proposed bus lane map]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p005_c0002|Study area description]].

The corridor carries over 40,000 daily bus passengers across the M3, M7, and M116 routes [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p005_c0002|Bus passenger volume]]. The M116 bus travels at less than 4 mph in some segments due to congestion and double-parking [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p006_c0002|Bus speed problem]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p006_c0003|Speed map]].

[[metric:metric_residents-10400-18-mile|10,400 residents live within 1/8 mile of the study area]], of whom [[metric:metric_car-free-households-76-percent|76% of households are car-free]] (above the [[metric:metric_car-free-households-city-avg-55|city average of 55%]]) and [[metric:metric_commute-public-transit-walk-bike-86|86% commute via public transit, walking, or biking]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p027_c0003|Demographics bullet list]]. Commute mode shares are [[metric:metric_transit-commute-69-percent|69% transit]], [[metric:metric_walk-commute-14-percent|14% walking]], [[metric:metric_commute-car-12-percent|12% car]], [[metric:metric_commute-bike-3-percent_2|3% commute by bike]], and [[metric:metric_commute-other-2-percent|2% other]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p027_c0006|Travel to work table]].

From 2020-2024, 37 people were injured in crashes in the study area, with 4 severely injured and 1 killed [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p008_c0002|Crash summary]]. KSI totals were 2 pedestrians, 2 bicyclists, and 1 motor vehicle occupant [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p008_c0007|KSI table]].

A draft proposal was presented to CB9 in June 2025 [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p023_c0002|Project timeline]]. The proposal adds [[metric:metric_project-bus-lane-miles-015|0.15 miles of bus lanes]], [[metric:metric_project-pedestrian-space-9800-sqft|9,800+ ft2 of painted pedestrian space]], [[metric:metric_project-concrete-pedestrian-space-880|880+ ft2 of concrete pedestrian space]], [[metric:metric_project-plaza-1-slip-lane|1 new plaza from a slip lane closure]], and [[metric:metric_project-tree-pits-2|2+ new street tree pits]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p021_c0002|Project summary]]. The bus lane would repurpose approximately [[metric:metric_parking-spaces-repurposed-25|25 parking spaces]] and operate 7am-7pm weekdays [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p017_c0002|Curb regulation changes]]. A consolidated bus stop at Manhattan Av./W. 112th St. would add ~5 overnight/weekend parking spots [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p015_c0003|Stop consolidation]]. At Morningside Ave. and 116th St., approximately [[metric:metric_parking-spaces-daylighting-3|3 parking spaces would be repurposed for daylighting]] with hardened safety treatments and a bike corral [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p018_c0002|Daylighting]]. A painted curb extension would reduce crossing distances by 39% and 34% [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p016_c0005|Curb extension crossing reduction]]. The slip lane closure on Morningside Avenue creates [[metric:metric_plaza-space-8600-sqft|8,600+ ft2 of programmable plaza space]] and a simpler two-phase signal that provides [[metric:metric_signal-green-time-increase-22s|+22 seconds of green time per 90-second cycle]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p019_c0002|Plaza benefits]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p019_c0007|Two-phase signal]] [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p019_c0009|+22s green time]].

The project was identified in the 2021 NYC Streets Plan. Tentative implementation is expected to begin in Summer 2025, pending engineering review and approval [[cite:116_st_morningside_ave_pleasant_ave_cb9_jun2025#p023_c0002|Full project timeline]].
