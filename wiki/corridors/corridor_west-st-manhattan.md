[[corridor:corridor_west-st-manhattan|West St]] is a street in Lower Manhattan adjacent to Battery Pl, where curb regulations were updated to keep the curb lane clear, a treatment that was marked as completed [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p005_c0003|Improved TEA placement and instruction / Updated curb regulations on West St]]. An aerial photograph of the Battery Park area shows annotations including three blue stars along West St and a "NO STOPPING" red box overlaid on the street [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p005_c0004|West St on aerial photograph of Battery Park area]].

Traffic delays accessing West Street were identified as one of several issues in the Battery Pl area during an interagency study by MTA, NYCDOT, and NYPD [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p004_c0004|Issues Identified on Battery Pl]]. The study found that the Battery Pl corridor serves 15 express bus routes with [[metric:metric_190-trips-peak-period|190 trips during the 4-7 pm peak period]] and [[metric:metric_5500-daily-customers|over 5,500 daily customers]] [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p004_c0004|4-7 pm peak period sees 190 trips serving over 5,500 daily customers]].

The adjacent Battery Pl was identified as a Better Buses Action Plan priority project, part of a citywide effort to [[metric:metric_improve-bus-speeds-25-percent|improve bus speeds 25% by 2020]] launched in April 2019 [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p002_c0002|Better Buses Action Plan released April 2019]]. A presentation covering these efforts was made to Manhattan Community Board 1 on October 21, 2019 [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p001_c0002|Manhattan Community Board 1 | 10.21.2019]]. Beyond the curb regulation update on West St and improved TEA placement, further study recommendations included stop consolidation and a queue jump signal at Battery Pl and Broadway [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p006_c0003|Stop consolidation on Broadway, Battery Pl / Queue jump signal on Battery Pl @ Broadway]].

The source presentation focuses on the Battery Place Bus Priority project, with West St references limited to the curb regulation update, the annotated aerial photograph, and the list of identified issues [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p005_c0003|Updated curb regulations on West St]] [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p005_c0004|Aerial photograph with West St annotations]] [[cite:battery_pl_oct2019#p004_c0004|Traffic delays accessing West Street]].
