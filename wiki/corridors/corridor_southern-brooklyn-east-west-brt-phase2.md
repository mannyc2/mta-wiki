The [[corridor:corridor_southern-brooklyn-east-west-brt-phase2|Southern Brooklyn East-West Corridor]] was identified as one of 16 potential future corridors during the Bus Rapid Transit Phase II planning study, which was initiated in the fall of 2008 by the New York City Department of Transportation and MTA New York City Transit [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p002_c0003|BRT Phase II study initiated]]. NYCT and DOT compiled a list of [[metric:metric_potential-corridors-over-40|over 40 potential BRT corridors]] based on a transit needs analysis and public feedback, then narrowed the field to 16 using five evaluation criteria: community feedback, ridership potential, network benefits, bus lane and busway feasibility, and geographic diversity [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0003|16 corridors identified from over 40]]. The corridor appears as route number 7 under "BRT Phase II Candidates" in the study's corridor table [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Table of Routes listing Southern Brooklyn East-West as candidate 7]].

The corridor was not selected for the short list of three priority candidates (Webster Avenue/Third Avenue, Hillside Avenue, and LaGuardia/East Elmhurst), which were identified for the next detailed BRT planning effort [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0005|Short list of 3 candidates identified]]. The Phase II public outreach effort, conducted in the spring of 2009, included seven citywide workshops and an online survey that drew [[metric:metric_workshop-participants-325|over 325 workshop participants]], [[metric:metric_community-business-groups-47|representatives from 47 community and business groups]], staff from [[metric:metric_elected-officials-offices-23|23 local elected officials' offices]], and [[metric:metric_survey-respondents-600|over 600 survey respondents]] [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p002_c0006|Public outreach metrics from spring 2009]]. This corridor is located in Brooklyn, as indicated by its borough designation [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Table listing showing borough context]]. Further details about the corridor's specific street alignments or endpoints are not available from the Phase II study documents.
