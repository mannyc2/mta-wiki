[[corridor:corridor_bus-fwd-2-fresh-pond-rd-queens|Fresh Pond Road, Metropolitan Avenue to Putnam Avenue]] was identified as a 2019 NYC DOT bus-priority project because the corridor had slow PM-peak bus speeds of 3.0 mph compared with a 6.4 mph Queens peak average, about 30,000 daily riders, and a high volume of buses [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p005_c0002|Fresh Pond Road 2019 priority project reasons]]. The project materials describe the corridor as a 1/2-mile segment with continuous southbound traffic backup in the afternoon and evening [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p007_c0002|Metropolitan Avenue to Putnam Avenue corridor conditions]].

The core proposal was a curbside southbound bus lane from Bleecker Street to Putnam Avenue, active only during PM peak hours with parking and loading allowed at other times [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_may2019#p018_c0002|southbound PM peak bus lane proposal]]. NYC DOT also proposed consolidating stops to four stop pairs roughly three blocks apart, retaining three pairs, moving one pair for even spacing, and eliminating one pair [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p022_c0002|four proposed stop pairs]] [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p022_c0003|three pairs retained]] [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p022_c0004|one pair moved]] [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p022_c0005|one pair eliminated]].

The curb-management plan said the bus lane would repurpose 70 spaces during the PM peak, including 55 metered and 15 alternate-side-parking spaces, while adding 61 new metered spaces on spurs and loading-zone changes at key locations [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p024_c0002|70 PM-peak spaces and 61 new metered spaces]] [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p025_c0002|loading zones and bus stop consolidation]]. The June 2019 timeline shows continuing community outreach after the May 21 CB 5 Transportation Committee meeting, including a May 30 Farmers Oval Civic Association meeting and a June 6 Ridgewood Property Owners and Civic Association meeting [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p027_c0002|Fresh Pond Road outreach timeline]].
