The [[corridor:corridor_meeting-doc-151786-new-haven-line|New Haven Line]] schedule changed effective October 6, 2024, with both AM and PM Peak trains seeing a 1-to-2 minute reduction in run time and weekend trains seeing a similar 1-to-2 minute reduction [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0011|October 6, 2024 schedule change details]]. The schedule change supports major construction and maintenance projects on all three Metro-North lines, including the continuation of Park Avenue Viaduct Span renewal and Walk Bridge replacement [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0008|infrastructure project support context]]. The October 6 schedule continues to accommodate extensive track outages tied to switch replacements, track and tie renewals, and CDOT projects [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0008|track outage context]].

A separate financial plan document (MTA Metro-North Railroad July Financial Plan - 2026 Mid-Year Forecast) reports that the [[metric:metric_new-haven-line-ridership|New Haven Line Total Ridership]] reached 34.237 million for the 12-month period (Jan-Dec) [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|ridership table]], while [[metric:metric_new-haven-line-farebox-revenue|New Haven Line Total Farebox Revenue]] appears as 322,211 in the source farebox table for the same period [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|farebox revenue table]]. For context, the [[metric:metric_harlem-line-farebox-revenue|Harlem Line Total Farebox Revenue]] appears as 175,405 and the [[metric:metric_hudson-line-farebox-revenue|Hudson Line Total Farebox Revenue]] appears as 146,359 in that table, making the New Haven Line the highest farebox revenue among the three East-of-Hudson lines; the table scale should be checked before converting those source values into dollars [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|farebox revenue by line table]]. The [[metric:metric_total-farebox-revenue-2025|Total Farebox Revenue (East of Hudson + West of Hudson)]] and [[metric:metric_east-hudson-ridership|Total Ridership (East of Hudson)]] are also drawn from this same financial table [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|combined revenue table]].
