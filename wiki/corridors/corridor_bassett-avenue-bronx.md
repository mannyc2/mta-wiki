Bassett Avenue is a private road in the Morris Park section of the Bronx that provides the sole means of vehicular and pedestrian ingress and egress to the property at 1502 Bassett Avenue [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0011|Bassett Avenue sole ingress/egress]]. The road runs between Eastchester Road and Metro Atrium Drive and lies adjacent to Amtrak's right of way for the Hell Gate Line, which will serve as the trackage for the Penn Access Project [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0008|licensed area description]].

The property at 1502 Bassett Avenue is improved with a [[metric:metric_property-size-367000-sqft|Property Building: 367,000 square feet]] last-mile distribution facility leased by the grantor RLF II BASSETT LLC to Amazon [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0011|property description and building size]]. As part of the Penn Access Project, a pedestrian overpass will be constructed above Bassett Avenue to connect the platform of the future Morris Park Station with the station's south entrance; the source states that the only viable location for this overpass is above Bassett Avenue [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p002_c0006|pedestrian overpass location]].

To build the overpass, the MTA seeks to acquire a [[metric:metric_easement-area-2203-sqft|Easement Area: 2,203 square feet]] permanent easement and a construction license from the grantor, with a total [[metric:metric_compensation-1m|Compensation: $1,000,000.00]] lump-sum payment plus the MTA's agreement to construct a new entrance to the property from an adjacent public street at an estimated cost of [[metric:metric_new-property-entrance-cost-310943|New Property Entrance Estimated Cost: $310,943]] [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0008|easement area and compensation summary]][[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p002_c0007|payment and new entrance condition]]. MTA Transit Oriented Development concluded, based on legal counsel and an independent appraisal, that the negotiated settlement is materially less expensive than condemnation and avoids associated litigation risk [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p002_c0008|cost comparison and TOD conclusion]]. The Finance Committee considered the item on December 15, 2025 and the MTA Board on December 17, 2025 [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0006|board action schedule]].
