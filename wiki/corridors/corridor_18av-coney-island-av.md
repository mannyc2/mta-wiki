[[corridor:corridor_18av-coney-island-av|18 Av / Coney Island Av]] was identified as one of 49 Brooklyn corridors studied for potential bus priority street improvements, and is among 17 top-ranked corridors in the MTA Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Draft Plan [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p020_c0002|49 corridors identified]]. The corridor was selected through a process that evaluated demand for bus service, bus performance, feasibility of street treatments, corridor role in the transit network, and neighborhood demographics [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p019_c0008|evaluation criteria]]. It appears alongside other top-ranked Brooklyn corridors such as Flatbush Av, Nostrand Av, Utica Av, and Fulton St [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p020_c0004|17 top-ranked corridors list]]. The MTA is working with NYC DOT through the NYC Streets Plan to expand bus priority improvements on this and other corridors identified in the Draft Plan [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p019_c0003|MTA-NYC DOT partnership]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p019_c0004|NYC Streets Plan]].

No route-level proposals or specific bus priority treatments for this corridor have been published in the Draft Plan beyond its identification as a study corridor. Further details may be developed through future public workshops and the Proposed Final Plan [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_without_route_profiles#p007_c0003|public workshops]].
