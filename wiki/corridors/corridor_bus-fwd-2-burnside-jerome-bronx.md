This corridor is listed in the BUSFORWARD 2017 plan as a local bus priority corridor where NYC DOT and the MTA planned to address issues affecting bus speed [[cite:bus_forward_2#p011_c0001|NYC DOT and the MTA will be addressing issues affecting bus speed on corridors such as the following]]. The corridor runs from W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy in the Bronx [[cite:bus_forward_2#p011_c0002|W Burnside Av/Jerome Av to E Tremont Av/Crotona Pkwy]].

This corridor is distinct from the 21 candidate SBS corridors identified elsewhere in the same plan; it was part of a separate local bus priority track that used tools from the bus priority toolkit such as bus lanes, bus stop spacing, and signal priority to improve travel speed and reliability [[cite:bus_forward_2#p010_c0003|Using tools from the bus priority toolkit, such as bus lanes, bus stop spacing and signal priority]]. The first round of these priority corridors was expected to undergo planning and outreach with implementation in 2018 and 2019 [[cite:bus_forward_2#p010_c0004|expected implementation in 2018 and 2019]]. Citywide context for the plan noted that buses citywide moved at an average of 7.4 mph and in busy commercial districts the average speed fell below 4 mph [[cite:bus_forward_2#p003_c0002|on average buses only move at 7.4 mph and in busy commercial districts, the average speed is less than 4 mph]].
