The [[corridor:corridor_bus-fwd-2-soundview-washington-heights-sbs|Soundview to Washington Heights (SBS candidate F)]] corridor is a proposed Select Bus Service candidate in the Bronx, identified in NYC DOT's BUSFORWARD 2017 plan [[cite:bus_forward_2#p001_c0001|BUSFORWARD 2017]] as candidate F in the Bronx borough section [[cite:bus_forward_2#p005_c0001|Soundview to Washington Heights candidate corridor]]. It was one of 21 candidate SBS corridors citywide selected through data analysis and public feedback by NYC DOT and the MTA for the next phase of SBS expansion [[cite:bus_forward_2#p004_c0002|21 candidate corridors identified]]. The candidate list was developed in response to 2015 and May 2017 City Council legislation calling for an updated SBS plan and broader transit improvements [[cite:bus_forward_2#p002_c0001|2015 legislation]] [[cite:bus_forward_2#p002_c0002|May 2017 legislation]].

Across the SBS program, existing SBS routes have demonstrated 10-30% faster bus speeds, about 10% ridership increases, and 95% customer satisfaction [[cite:bus_forward_2#p003_c0004|SBS performance results]]. The existing SBS network carried over 309,000 daily riders (12% of city bus ridership), and with the proposed additions, over 800,000 riders would be on routes with fast, reliable service, representing over 32% of city bus ridership [[cite:bus_forward_2#p007_c0001|SBS ridership metrics]]. No specific route alignment, length, or ridership data for this corridor has been published in the BUSFORWARD 2017 plan beyond its identification as a candidate corridor. The adjacent Bronx corridors identified in the same plan include Tremont Avenue (candidate D), University Avenue (candidate C), and Harlem to Southern Boulevard (candidate E).
