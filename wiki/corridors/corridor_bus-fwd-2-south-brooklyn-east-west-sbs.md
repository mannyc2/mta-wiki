The [[corridor:corridor_bus-fwd-2-south-brooklyn-east-west-sbs|South Brooklyn East-West (SBS candidate H)]] corridor was identified by NYC DOT and the MTA as one of 21 candidate corridors for the next phase of Select Bus Service across the city [[cite:bus_forward_2#p005_c0001|21 SBS candidate corridors listed]]. The list was developed through data analysis and public feedback including workshops, on-street surveying, and online input [[cite:bus_forward_2#p004_c0002|21 candidate corridors identified]]. This corridor is located in Brooklyn, grouped under the borough heading alongside Church Avenue, Downtown Brooklyn to Ridgewood, Brooklyn Access to JFK, and Flatbush Avenue [[cite:bus_forward_2#p005_c0001|Brooklyn corridors listed]]. Four key analyses guided where to target SBS expansion: access to rapid transit service, long and slow bus trips, slow bus speeds, and bus reliability [[cite:bus_forward_2#p013_c0002|four key analyses identified]] [[cite:bus_forward_2#p013_c0003|four key analyses listed]]. At the time of the Bus Forward 2017 plan, the existing SBS network carried over 309,000 daily riders, and proposed additions including this corridor would bring the total on SBS routes to over 800,000 daily riders [[cite:bus_forward_2#p007_c0001|existing and proposed SBS ridership]].
