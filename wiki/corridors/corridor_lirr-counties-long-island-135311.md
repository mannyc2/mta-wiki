This corridor represents the [[corridor:corridor_lirr-counties-long-island-135311|LIRR Service County - Long Island]] territory as defined in a March 2024 MTA Board procurement request for emergency and scheduled bus services [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|LIRR Long Island zone definition]]. The source block establishes a broader LIRR territory than the page title suggests: Nassau, Western Suffolk, Eastern Suffolk, Queens, King, and New York Counties all appear in the same zone definition [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|LIRR Long Island zone definition]]. That makes the record useful for the Long Island counties, but the cited procurement table also reaches into the NYC borough counties in the same source row [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|corridor zone table]].

This corridor was referenced in the procurement justification for Contract No. 426684, which sought Board approval to award competitively solicited contracts for emergency and scheduled bus services [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0005|MNR on behalf of LIRR request]]. Metro-North Railroad proposed the procurement on behalf of both MNR and LIRR, collectively referred to as "the Railroads" [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0005|MNR on behalf of LIRR request]]. The contracts carry a five-year term with an estimated aggregate budget of [[metric:metric_total-estimated-budget-55m-135311|$55 million]], of which [[metric:metric_lirr-estimated-budget-25m-135311|$25 million]] is allocated to LIRR [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0006|aggregate budget breakdown]]. The RFP was publicly advertised on June 13, 2023, and 13 proposals were received by July 24, 2023 [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0010|RFP advertisement date]][[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0011|proposals received date]]. The Selection Committee recommended 13 firms for contract award [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0003|13 recommended firms]].
