[[corridor:corridor_park-ave-46th-50th|Park Avenue between 46th and 50th Streets (Sector 2 area)]] designates an [[metric:metric_sector2-area|approximate 183,500 square foot Sector 2 Train Shed portion]] underneath Park Avenue and on certain side streets from East 46th Street to East 50th Street in Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p001_c0010|LOCATION block]]. The Train Shed functions as a bridge supporting the at-grade streets and avenues above the train platforms, with structural steel from the early 20th century that has significantly deteriorated [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p002_c0011|Train Shed description]].

JPMorgan Chase (JPMC) has agreed to expand its ongoing Train Shed rehabilitation work to include this Sector 2 corridor as the "Expanded Scope" or "Project" [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p001_c0011|ACTIVITY block]]. The total budget is estimated at approximately [[metric:metric_total-budget-900m|$900M]] [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p002_c0006|COMPENSATION section]], of which [[metric:metric_jpmc-contribution-50m|JPMC will contribute $50M]] with the remainder funded by the 2025-2029 Capital Program [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p003_c0014|FUNDING section]]. The work follows a model established by Sector 1, where [[metric:metric_sector1-contribution-25m|JPMC contributed $25M]] toward rehabilitation adjacent to its 270 Park Avenue headquarters; that work is [[metric:metric_sector1-completion-95pct|95% complete]] with completion scheduled for December 2026 [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p002_c0012|Sector 1 background]].

The Sector 2 work is structured in two phases under an Early Work Agreement ([[metric:metric_ewa-cap-50m|capped at $50M]]) and a forthcoming Sector 2 Amendment [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p001_c0013|EWA description]]. Phase 1 covers work south of 48th Street; Phase 2 covers work north of 48th Street, which the MTA must elect to direct JPMC to perform by June 15, 2026 [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p001_c0013|MTA election deadline]]. The Sector 2 work has an anticipated duration of [[metric:metric_sector2-duration-36months|36 months]] with an anticipated completion date of May 30, 2029 [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p003_c0012|construction schedule]]. The project also coordinates with NYC DOT on rehabilitation and reconfiguration of the Park Avenue Malls [[cite:meeting_doc_201771#p002_c0013|Expansion LOI details]].
