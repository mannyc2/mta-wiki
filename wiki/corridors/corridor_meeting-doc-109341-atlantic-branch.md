This corridor covers the LIRR [[corridor:corridor_meeting-doc-109341-atlantic-branch|Atlantic Branch (Atlantic Terminal to East New York)]] segment where Rail Replacement and Maintenance occurred during the May 22 through September 5, 2023 timetable change. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0001|Atlantic Branch rail replacement description]] One of two main tracks was taken out of service on midday/weekdays between Atlantic Terminal and East New York to support rail replacement and maintenance. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0001|Atlantic Branch track outage description]] The resulting temporary service adjustments affected Brooklyn-bound trains in both directions. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p001_c0005|projects supported during this timetable]] Several related metrics document the specific service impacts: [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-109341-atlantic-westbound-18-min-earlier-v2|westbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 18 minutes earlier]], [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-109341-atlantic-eastbound-11-min-later-v2|some eastbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 11 minutes later]], [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-109341-atlantic-eastbound-6-min-earlier-v2|some eastbound Brooklyn trains adjusted up to 6 minutes earlier]], and [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-109341-nostrand-ew-service-reduction|service to/from Nostrand Avenue and East New York reduced from 3 to 2 trains per hour]]. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0001|temporary service adjustments on Atlantic Branch]] The timetable change was presented to the Long Island Committee on April 24, 2023. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p001_c0004|LIRR informs Long Island Committee of schedule adjustments]] Continuing projects supported during this period included East Side Access, Main Line Expansion, West Side Yard Overbuild, and Mid-Suffolk Yard Construction. [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p001_c0005|continuing projects listed]] Funding for the work was drawn from the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budgets.
