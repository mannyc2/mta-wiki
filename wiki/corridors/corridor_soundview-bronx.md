The [[corridor:corridor_soundview-bronx|Soundview Corridor]] was identified as a priority corridor by Bronx participants during the BRT Phase II public workshops held in May and June 2009 [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0011|p016_c0011]]. Multiple participant tables characterized the area's existing transit options as unreliable and insufficient, and noted difficulty for pedestrians to reach subway stops and other parts of the Bronx [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0012|p016_c0012]]. Participants expressed a desire for better connectivity both within Soundview and to other areas of the Bronx [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0012|p016_c0012]].

The most prominent recommendation from the Bronx workshop was that four out of five participant tables wanted to extend the Soundview corridor east-west, potentially along 161st Street [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0012|p016_c0012]]. This desire for improved east-west connections was a recurring theme among Bronx participants more broadly, who noted that most subway service in the borough runs north-south [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0005|p016_c0005]].

The Bronx workshop, held May 28, 2009 at The Hub, drew 29 participants and was one of seven citywide BRT Phase II workshops [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p010_c0005|p010_c0005]]. Bronx participants who completed the questionnaire showed strong BRT support, with 74% supporting expansion outright, 24% supporting with reservations, and 2% opposed [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p014_c0007|p014_c0007]].

The Soundview Corridor was one of three Bronx priority corridors to emerge from the workshops, alongside the Webster Avenue/Third Avenue Corridor and Hunts Point Peninsula [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0007|p016_c0007]][[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p016_c0009|p016_c0009]]. A separate press release from 2021 documents a Soundview Bus Priority project, but the relationship between that project and the 2009 corridor identification is not yet clear from available evidence [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021]].
