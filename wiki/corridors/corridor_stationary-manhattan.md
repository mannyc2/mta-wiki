Streets in [[corridor:corridor_stationary-manhattan|Manhattan stationary camera corridors]] with fixed-location bus lane enforcement cameras through 2023, as listed in the NYC Bus Lane Camera Enforcement 2024 Report published by NYC DOT [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p007_c0006|Manhattan heading]]. The 16 listed corridors are: 181st Street, 125th Street, 14th Street, 1st Avenue, 207th Street, 23rd Street, 2nd Avenue, 34th Street, 3rd Avenue, 42nd Street, 5th Avenue, 86th Street, Allen Street, Broadway, Lexington Avenue, and Madison Avenue [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p007_c0007|Manhattan corridor list]]. An asterisk next to a street name indicates that mobile ABLE cameras were also in use on that corridor through 2023; asterisk-marked Manhattan corridors include 181st Street, 14th Street, 1st Avenue, 23rd Street, 2nd Avenue, 34th Street, 86th Street, and Allen Street [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p007_c0012|ABLE footnote]].

Citywide, DOT operated stationary cameras at 188 locations through 2023, using two cameras per site mounted above bus lanes that capture primarily driving-in-bus-lane violations [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p006_c0004|188 locations]] [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p006_c0003|fixed camera description]] [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p009_c0001|stationary captures driving violations]]. During 2022–2023, DOT stationary cameras recorded 36,117,200 total events and issued 1,248,543 notices of liability [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p010_c0003|stationary events]] [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p013_c0001|stationary NOLs]]. Of stationary violations issued, 92% were not challenged, 6% were challenged and ruled guilty, 2% were challenged and ruled not guilty, and 0.5% received fine reductions [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p015_c0005|stationary challenge breakdown]] [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p016_c0002|stationary challenge table]]. DOT operating costs for stationary cameras were $8,773,020 in 2022 and $8,761,035 in 2023 [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p008_c0001|DOT stationary costs]], and total stationary camera revenue collected was $91,127,285.46 across the two-year period [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p017_c0001|stationary revenue]].
