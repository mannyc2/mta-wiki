MNR Zone 5 (NY) covers the Metro-North Railroad territory west of the Hudson River, encompassing Rockland and Orange Counties in New York State, as defined in a March 2024 Board procurement document for emergency and scheduled bus services [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|Zone description table]]. The corridor is one of five defined MNR zones used to organize bus service procurement under Contract #426684, which carried an estimated aggregate budget of [[metric:metric_total-estimated-budget-55m-135311|$55,000,000]] across both MNR and LIRR territories [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0006|Budget breakdown]]. MNR's share allocated to Zones 1 through 5 was [[metric:metric_mnr-estimated-budget-30m-135311|$30,000,000]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0003|MNR budget table]]. The procurement sought qualified vendors to furnish emergency busing (immediate service due to sudden disruption) and scheduled busing (planned disruption from capital projects) on an as-needed basis throughout the Railroads' operating territories [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0007|Emergency vs scheduled definitions]]. A total of [[metric:metric_thirteen-proposals-received-135311|13 proposals]] were received by July 24, 2023, and the Selection Committee recommended [[metric:metric_recommended-firms-13-135311|13 firms]] for contract award, with territory assignments including firms serving MNR-only zones (such as Leprechaun Lines and Peter Pan Bus Lines) and firms serving both MNR and LIRR zones [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0011|Proposals received]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0003|13 firms recommended]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0004|Territory-bus company table]]. The contracts carried a [[metric:metric_contract-term-5-years-135311|five-year term]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p005_c0007|Contract term listing]]. Zone 5 is geographically distinct from the other MNR zones in that it covers the West of Hudson service area, while Zones 1-2 cover Connecticut branches and Zones 3-4 cover the Hudson and Harlem lines within New York east of the Hudson [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|Zone description table]].
