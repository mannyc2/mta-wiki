[[corridor:corridor_broadway-queens|Broadway, 69th Street to Roosevelt Avenue, Queens]] runs in Queens from the BQE near 69th Street to Roosevelt Avenue [[cite:better_buses#p001_b0001|Broadway in Queens between 69th Street and Roosevelt Avenue]] [[cite:broadway_69_st_roosevelt_ave_may2026#p007_c0002|Convert channelized median...between the BQE and Roosevelt Ave]]. As of April 2019, the Broadway segment was described as the slowest portion of the Q53 Select Bus Service route, carrying about 17,000 daily trips on Q53 and Q58 buses combined [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p006_c0002|Remains the slowest portion of the Q53 route]]. The Q70-SBS also uses the corridor, with nearly 9,000 daily riders and bus speeds reported as slow as 2.7 mph due to heavy congestion and double parking [[cite:broadway_69_st_roosevelt_ave_may2026#p004_c0003|Nearly 9,000 riders use the Q70-SBS daily...bus speeds are as slow as 2.7mph]].

In May 2026, NYC DOT presented a proposal to Queens Community Board 3 that would convert the channelized median and left turn bays into a 24/7 eastbound center-running bus lane between the BQE and Roosevelt Avenue, retaining one travel lane in each direction for general traffic [[cite:broadway_69_st_roosevelt_ave_may2026#p007_c0002|Convert channelized median and left turn bays in to a 24/7 eastbound center-running bus lane]]. The proposal also bans left turns from Broadway onto 72nd Street in both directions to keep the bus lane clear [[cite:broadway_69_st_roosevelt_ave_may2026#p008_c0003|Restrict left turn from Broadway onto 72nd St, in both the eastbound and westbound directions]].

An earlier April 2019 presentation to Queens Community Board 4 proposed bus queue jumps at 75th Street, 78th Street, Whitney Avenue, Corona Avenue, and Grand Avenue [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p011_c0003|75th St, 78th St, and Grand Av NB...Whitney Av, Corona Av, and Grand Av SB]] [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p010_c0002|Queue Jumps]], along with dedicated bicycle lanes replacing wide parking lanes [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p020_c0005|Wide parking lanes are replaced with bicycle lanes]]. Between the 2016 and 2018 morning peak periods on the full Q53 route (which includes Broadway), the [[metric:metric_q53-nb-total-before-2016|Q53 northbound total travel time (Feb-May 2016)]] was 30.9 minutes, improving to [[metric:metric_q53-nb-total-after-2018|27.8 minutes (Feb-May 2018)]], while southbound evening peak travel improved from 35.2 minutes ([[metric:metric_q53-sb-total-before-2016|Q53 Southbound Total Travel Time Before (Feb-May 2016)]]) to 32.0 minutes ([[metric:metric_q53-sb-total-after-2018|Q53 Southbound Total Travel Time After (Feb-May 2018)]]) [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p005_c0003|Total 30.9 27.8]] [[cite:brt_broadway_roosevelt_ave_queens_blvd_apr2019#p005_c0005|Total 35.2 32.0]].
