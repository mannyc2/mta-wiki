[[corridor:corridor_rockaway-liberty-blvd-cac2|Rockaway / Liberty Boulevard]] is a Queens intersection corridor identified in NYC DOT discussion materials for the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards Select Bus Service project [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p004_c0002|Woodhaven / Cross Bay Blvd & Rockaway / Liberty Blvd]]. The corridor appears in the SBS Community Advisory Committee Meeting #2 discussion materials dated October 22, 2014, alongside the main Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards corridor [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p001_c0003|Meeting #2 title]]. At this intersection, three conceptual bus priority designs were presented: Concept 1 (Offset Bus Lanes) [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p004_c0003|Concept 1 label]], Concept 2 (Main Road Bus Lanes) [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p005_c0003|Concept 2 label]], and Concept 3 (Median Busway) [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p006_c0006|Concept 3 label]]. All three concepts were explicitly labeled as "CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION" [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p003_c0008|CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION]] [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p006_c0007|CONCEPTUAL DESIGN FOR DISCUSSION]], indicating they were early-stage proposals not yet finalized. The corridor record's borough is Queens [[cite:2014_10_22_brt_woodhaven_cac2_discussionmaterials#p004_c0002|located in Queens]]. This corridor is closely related to the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevards SBS project, which serves the Q53 route.
