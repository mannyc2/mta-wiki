The corridor is Marcy Avenue from Borinquen Place to Broadway in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, a three-block stretch where three Williamsburg Bridge Plaza-bound bus routes converge [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p001_c0001|Marcy Av, Borinquen Pl to Broadway]] [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|confluence of three bus routes]]. The B24 (2024 average daily ridership 2,359), B60 (12,226), and Q54 (13,411) all experience delays and slower speeds along this tail-end stretch, compounding earlier en-route delays and causing late terminal arrivals [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|B24 B60 Q54 ridership and delays]]. The BQE North and South (2024) study recommended providing a bus priority lane to alleviate these delays [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|BQE study recommendation]].

Traffic congestion is common along the corridor, especially on weekday late afternoons before the PM rush hour lane along the east curb takes effect [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p003_c0008|traffic congestion timing]]. Buses must navigate a very tight right turn from southbound Marcy Avenue onto westbound Broadway, often competing for space with other turning vehicles and improperly stopped traffic on eastbound Broadway [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p005_c0004|difficult right turn]]. On the west side of Marcy Avenue between S 4 Street and S 5 Street, delivery vehicles frequently occupy the bus stop for extended periods [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p004_c0008|loading zone demand]].

NYC DOT has proposed a Bus Priority Street Improvement Project (SIP ID 12263) for the corridor under the Better Buses program, presented to Brooklyn Community Board 1 in June 2025 and September 2025 [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p001_c0002|SIP ID]] [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p001_c0003|CB1 Transportation Committee June 2025]] [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_sept2025#p001_c0003|CB1 Full Board September 2025]]. Proposed treatments include converting a general travel lane to a bus priority lane (all hours), having the bus lane merge into the general travel lane, adding a new neighborhood loading zone, replacing a part-time parking lane with a full-time general travel lane, and widening the travel lane to facilitate safer turns for trucks and buses [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_sept2025#p007_c0003|proposed pavement markings and treatments]].
