[[corridor:corridor_first-avenue-manhattan-2010|First Avenue]] is the northbound side of the First Avenue/Second Avenue Select Bus Service corridor, whose route diagram showed SBS running between South Ferry and 125th Street with paired First and Second Avenue station locations [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p010_c0011|First/Second Avenue SBS route diagram]]. The September 2010 CAC deck states that M15 Select Bus Service would begin on October 10, 2010, and its markings plan listed First Avenue bus-lane paint from Houston Street to 72nd Street alongside protected bike-lane paint from Houston Street to 34th Street [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p003_c0006|M15 SBS begins October 10, 2010]] [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p005_c0009|First Avenue markings and paint plan]].

The corridor used both offset and curbside bus-lane treatments: the CAC materials describe offset bus lanes as in effect at all times, while curbside bus lanes were paired with morning and afternoon bus-lane hours, midday commercial loading, and overnight parking [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p019_c0005|offset bus lane heading]] [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p019_c0006|offset bus lane in effect at all times]] [[cite:2010_09_14_brt_1st2nd_cac5#p020_c0006|curbside bus lane hours and curb use]]. Later CB6 materials added bus bulbs, with early action implementation in 2011, remaining bulbs in 2012, and stormwater management where feasible [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p018_c0006|early action bus bulbs in 2011]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p018_c0007|remainder in 2012]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p018_c0008|stormwater component where feasible]].

The May 2011 CB6 deck reported NYPD bus-lane summonses on First Avenue, including 2,009 parking-in-bus-lane summonses, 72 driving-in-bus-lane summonses, and 2,081 total First Avenue summonses [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p007_c0005|NYPD summons heading]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p007_c0012|First Avenue summons table]]. The same deck reported 5,800 bus-lane camera summonses through April 5, 2011, and recorded [[metric:metric_bike-first-ave-50th-street|First Avenue cycling volume at 50th Street]] as 828 riders, based on three single-day 7am-7pm counts in May, August, and September 2010 [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p007_c0013|5,800 camera summonses]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p007_c0014|through April 5, 2011]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p013_c0013|First Avenue 828 cycling count]] [[cite:2011_05_02_brt_1st2nd_cb6#p013_c0015|three single-day counts note]].
