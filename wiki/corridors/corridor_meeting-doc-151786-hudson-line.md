The [[corridor:corridor_meeting-doc-151786-hudson-line|Hudson Line]] is a Metro-North Railroad commuter rail line serving the Hudson Valley between Grand Central Terminal and Poughkeepsie. Effective Sunday, October 6, 2024, Metro-North implemented a schedule change on the Hudson Line to support major construction and maintenance-of-way projects, including the continuation of Park Avenue Viaduct span renewal and Walk Bridge replacement [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0007|October 6, 2024 Schedule Change]] [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0008|Projects supported by schedule changes]]. As part of that change, the Hudson Line received minor adjustments to weekday outbound trains later in the PM-Peak for better spacing during Park Avenue Viaduct two-track outages, and weekend trains saw a 1-to-2 minute reduction in run time [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0011|Hudson Line schedule adjustments]].

The MTA Metro-North Railroad July Financial Plan - 2025 Mid-Year Forecast reports the [[metric:metric_hudson-line-farebox-revenue|Hudson Line Total Farebox Revenue]] at $146.4 million for the 12-month period (January through December forecast), combining $41.3 million in commutation revenue and $105.1 million in non-commutation revenue [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|Hudson Line farebox revenue table]]. The same forecast shows [[metric:metric_hudson-line-ridership|Hudson Line Total Ridership]] of 14.6 million riders, comprising 5.6 million commutation trips and 9.0 million non-commutation trips [[cite:meeting_doc_186701#p007_c0002|Hudson Line ridership table]].

The Park Avenue Viaduct repair project, which drives the PM-Peak schedule adjustments on the Hudson Line, is part of the 2020-2024 Capital Program and is a state-of-good-repair effort that carries all of Metro-North's Grand Central Terminal trains [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0011|Viaduct outage impact on Hudson Line]] [[cite:meeting_doc_100291#p015_c0006|Viaduct carries 98% of GCT trains]]. The October 2024 schedule change also supported additional construction such as switch replacements, track and tie renewals, and CDOT projects [[cite:meeting_doc_151786#p001_c0008|Projects supported by schedule changes]].
