This corridor is Amtrak's right of way for the Hell Gate Line (the "Hell Gate ROW") in the Morris Park section of the Bronx, adjacent to Bassett Avenue. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0011|Amtrak Hell Gate Line corridor]] The Hell Gate ROW will serve as the trackage for the Penn Access Project, a new Metro-North Railroad link to Penn Station that will provide one-seat passenger rail service for New Haven Line customers. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0010|PSA project description]]

The platform for the future Morris Park Station will be sited within the Hell Gate ROW, with a pedestrian overpass connecting the platform to the south station entrance. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0011|Station platform location]] The overpass is the only viable connection above Bassett Avenue, which is a private road serving as the sole ingress and egress to 1502 Bassett Avenue—a property improved with a [[metric:metric_property-size-367000-sqft|367,000 square foot]] last-mile distribution facility leased to Amazon. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0011|Property and Bassett Avenue description]]

As part of the Penn Access Project, MTA Transit Oriented Development sought authorization from the MTA Board to acquire a permanent easement of [[metric:metric_easement-area-2203-sqft|2,203 square feet]] and a construction license from RLF II BASSETT LLC for the pedestrian overpass, with [[metric:metric_compensation-1m|$1,000,000]] in one-time compensation plus construction of a new entrance to the property from an adjacent public street at an estimated cost of [[metric:metric_new-property-entrance-cost-310943|$310,943]]. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0008|Easement and compensation details]] TOD concluded, based on legal counsel and an independent appraisal, that the negotiated settlement was materially less expensive than condemnation and avoided associated litigation risk. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p002_c0008|Negotiated settlement rationale]]

The MTA Board considered the item at its December 17, 2025 meeting, following a Finance Committee review on December 15, 2025. [[cite:meeting_doc_194041#p001_c0006|Board and committee schedule]]
