This corridor captures the New York City borough counties served by the Long Island Rail Road as defined in a March 2024 MTA Board procurement action for emergency and scheduled bus services under Contract #426684 [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0005|Discussion of MNR and LIRR bus service procurement]]. The LIRR territory within NYC is listed as Queens, King, and New York Counties, corresponding to the boroughs of Queens, Brooklyn, and Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|LIRR service territory table]]. These three counties are part of a six-county LIRR service area that also includes Nassau, Western Suffolk, and Eastern Suffolk on Long Island [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0009|LIRR service territory table]].

The corridor is documented as a zone definition in a competitively solicited miscellaneous service contract with a [[metric:metric_total-estimated-budget-55m-135311|Total Estimated Budget - $55,000,000]] and a [[metric:metric_contract-term-5-years-135311|Five-Year Contract Term]], of which the LIRR portion is [[metric:metric_lirr-estimated-budget-25m-135311|LIRR Estimated Budget - $25,000,000]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0006|Budget breakdown]]. Metro-North Railroad (MNR) administered the procurement on behalf of both railroads, with the MNR share estimated at [[metric:metric_mnr-estimated-budget-30m-135311|MNR Estimated Budget - $30,000,000]] [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p006_c0005|MNR acting on behalf of LIRR]]. The RFP attracted [[metric:metric_thirteen-proposals-received-135311|13 Proposals Received]] by July 24, 2023, leading to [[metric:metric_recommended-firms-13-135311|13 Recommended Firms]] covering various territory combinations across MNR zones and LIRR counties [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0003|Selection committee recommendation]].

The bus companies assigned to LIRR-only territory include Bus Around Town Corp. d/b/a Coachman Luxury Transport, D&J Service, Inc., Huntington Coach Corp., M&V Corporate Transportation, and Suffolk Transportation Services, Inc., while Academy Express, Charter Bus Pros, Community Coach, DPV Transportation, J&F Tours Transportation, and Red Carpet Coach Ltd. serve both MNR and LIRR territories [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p007_c0004|Territory coverage table]]. Funding sources include each railroad's operating and capital budgets as well as Connecticut Department of Transportation funds for Connecticut-based MNR branches [[cite:meeting_doc_135311#p008_c0004|Funding sources]].
