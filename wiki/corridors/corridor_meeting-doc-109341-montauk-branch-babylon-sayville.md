This corridor covers the Montauk Branch segment between Babylon and Sayville, where Signal Hut Installation work was carried out during a planned LIRR timetable change from May 22, 2023 through September 5, 2023 [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p001_c0004|informational purpose statement]]. The corridor was one of several branches where construction projects were accommodated under the timetable, alongside Third Rail State of Good Repair work between Amityville and Babylon and Rail Replacement on the Atlantic Branch [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p001_c0005|projects supported during timetable]]. On this segment, one of two main tracks was taken out of service during overnight periods between Babylon and Sayville to support the delivery and installation of signal huts [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0001|signal hut installation description]]. As a temporary service adjustment, one eastbound and one westbound train on the Montauk Branch were shifted approximately 20 minutes later, as captured in [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-109341-signal-hut-20-min-later|Montauk Branch signal hut installation trains adjusted 20 minutes later]] [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0001|signal hut service adjustments]]. Funding for the work was drawn from the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budgets [[cite:meeting_doc_109341#p002_c0006|funding source]]. The corridor's identity in this source is defined entirely by the temporary overnight service disruption; the page does not provide permanent infrastructure details, ridership data, or a full corridor profile for this stretch of the Montauk Branch.
