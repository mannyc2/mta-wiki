This corridor lies along Woodhaven Boulevard in Queens from Union Turnpike to 81st Road [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0007|Woodhaven Boulevard from Union Turnpike to 81st Rd]]. It is the segment on which NYC DOT proposed to reconstruct the roadway and implement bus and safety improvements as part of the Q52/Q53 Select Bus Service Project [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0007|Q52/Q53 SBS improvements]]. The stated project goals are to improve bus travel times, improve pedestrian safety, organize vehicle movements, and simplify traffic [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0002|Improve Bus Travel Times]][[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0003|Improve Pedestrian Safety]][[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0005|Organize Vehicle Movements]][[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0006|Simplify Traffic]].

The planned design calls for removing service road medians to create a simpler central median with pedestrian refuge, maintaining three vehicular traffic lanes in each direction, installing curbside bus-only lanes that allow right turns to the next intersection or driveway, restricting left turns from Woodhaven Boulevard to Union Turnpike, and creating a turn bay for U-Turns at 81st Road to maintain access to Union Turnpike [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0008|list of proposed treatments]]. Implementation was announced to begin mid-September, though the flyer does not specify the year [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0009|Implementation begins Mid-September]]. The flyer was published by the New York City Department of Transportation [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0016|NYC DOT logo]]. The flyer is a single-page document and contains no publication date or completion timeline [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0001|What's Happening Here?]][[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0014|contact info]]. This corridor segment sits within the larger Woodhaven Boulevard corridor served by the Q52 and Q53 Select Bus Service routes [[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0004|Woodhaven Boulevard Q52/Q53 SBS Improvements]][[cite:brt_woodhaven_crossbay_blvd_sept_flyer#p001_c0007|Q52/Q53 SBS project]].
