[[corridor:corridor_cross-bay-boulevard-rockaway-to-165|Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue]] is a Queens corridor segment that was covered by draft plans for the Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard Select Bus Service project, presented at Public Design Workshop #3 on April 29, 2015 [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p001_c0003|Draft plans for Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue]]. The corridor segment runs from Rockaway Boulevard to 165 Avenue in Queens [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p001_c0003|Draft plans for Cross Bay Boulevard between Rockaway Boulevard and 165 Avenue]].

The Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard SBS project serves the Q52 and Q53 bus routes along this corridor [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p001_c0001|Woodhaven / Cross Bay Boulevard (Q52/Q53) Select Bus Service]]. The draft plans included specific treatment locations at Liberty Avenue, 109 Av, Linden Blvd, Pitkin Av, 157 Avenue, 160 Avenue, 163 Avenue, and 164 Avenue, as well as a traffic signage diagram for the segment between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p001_c0009|A Cross Bay Boulevard at Liberty Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p001_c0010|B Cross Bay Boulevard at 109 Av]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p002_c0004|A Cross Bay Boulevard at Linden Blvd]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p002_c0005|B Cross Bay Boulevard at Pitkin Av]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p003_c0009|A Cross Bay Boulevard at 157 Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p003_c0010|B Cross Bay Boulevard at 160 Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0007|Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0009|Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0004|Diagram showing traffic signage for Cross Bay Boulevard between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue]].

At 163 Avenue and 164 Avenue, the draft plans compared existing and proposed street layouts, each showing a total corridor width of 110 feet with lane configurations that included a bus stop and a bus lane in the proposal [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0007|Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 163 Avenue]] [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0009|Diagram comparing the existing and proposed street layout at Cross Bay Boulevard at 164 Avenue]]. Between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue, the proposed traffic signage indicated two-way traffic at most intersections, with 163 Avenue designated one-way (left-turn lane only) and 165 Avenue one-way (right-turn lane only), and SBS bus stops planned at 161 Avenue and 163 Avenue [[cite:2015_04_29_brt_woodhaven_dw3_draftplans#p004_c0004|Diagram showing traffic signage for Cross Bay Boulevard between 161 Avenue and 165 Avenue]].
