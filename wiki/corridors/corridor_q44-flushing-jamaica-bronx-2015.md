The [[corridor:corridor_q44-flushing-jamaica-bronx-2015|Main St/Cross Bronx Service Road (Flushing to Jamaica)]] corridor was first identified in the initial NYC BRT study of 2006 and the Bus Rapid Transit Phase II report of 2009, and was characterized by "long and slow bus trips" [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p003_c0002|Project Background]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p003_c0003|Long and slow bus trips]]. The Q44 route, running from Jamaica to the Bronx Zoo along Main St/Cross Bronx Service Road, carries [[metric:metric_q44-daily-riders-28000|28,000 daily riders]] and functions as a vital Bronx-Queens inter-borough link; [[metric:metric_q44-29-percent-trips-start-end-bronx|29% of all Q44 trips start or end in the Bronx]] and [[metric:metric_q44-75-percent-bronx-trips-bound-queens|75% of Bronx trips are bound for Queens]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p007_c0002|Q44: Jamaica to Bronx Zoo]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p007_c0003|Main St/Cross Bronx Service Road]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p007_c0004|Corridor overview facts]].

Bus speeds on the corridor are low: [[metric:metric_q44-avg-speed-bronx-5-10-mph|Q44 average bus speed 5-10 mph in the Bronx]], and [[metric:metric_q44-less-5-mph-main-st-hillside-v3|Q44 less than 5 mph on Main Street and Hillside Avenue/Sutphin Boulevard]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p005_c0002|Average bus speed]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p005_c0003|Less than 5 mph]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p005_c0004|Main Street, Hillside Avenue/Sutphin Boulevard]]. A Q44 LTD bus delay analysis found that buses spend 51% of travel time in motion, 29% delayed by red lights or traffic, 18% at bus stops, and 2% by other causes [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p006_c0003|Q44 LTD Bus Delay pie chart]]. Bronx rider destinations split across Flushing (34%), the Bronx itself (25%), Jamaica (15%), Whitestone (15%), and points between Flushing and Jamaica (11%) [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p008_c0005|Bronx rider destination table]].

The April 2015 presentation to Bronx Community Board 9 described proposed SBS treatments including improved fare collection, bus lanes, transit signal priority, passenger information, stations and amenities, and branding [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0001|Select Bus Service Toolbox]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0003|Improved fare collection]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0005|Bus lanes]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0007|Transit signal priority]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0009|Passenger Information]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0011|Stations & Amenities]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p004_c0013|Branding]]. Proposed stop removals were presented based on stop spacing, ridership, bus/subway connections, and pedestrian crossing access over the Cross Bronx [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p010_c0001|Proposed Stop Removals]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p010_c0003|Stop removal criteria]]. The presentation noted that earlier SBS corridors saw [[metric:metric_sbs-10-percent-ridership-increase|about a 10% increase in ridership in the first year]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p002_c0004|About 10% increase in ridership]]. At the proposed SBS stops, [[metric:metric_q44-86-percent-nb-ridership-proposed-stops|86% of northbound ridership]] and [[metric:metric_q44-82-percent-sb-ridership-proposed-stops|82% of southbound ridership]] would be served [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p011_c0003|86% of northbound ridership]] [[cite:2015_04_02_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb9_presentation#p012_c0003|82% of southbound ridership]].
