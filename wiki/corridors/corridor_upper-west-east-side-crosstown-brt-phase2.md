The [[corridor:corridor_upper-west-east-side-crosstown-brt-phase2|Upper West Side/Upper East Side Crosstown Corridor]] was identified as one of 16 potential future BRT corridors during the BRT Phase II planning process, which evaluated over 40 candidate corridors based on five criteria: community feedback, ridership potential, network benefits, bus lane and busway feasibility, and geographic diversity [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0003|16 potential corridors identified]] [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p004_c0002|Phase II candidate listing]]. The corridor was not selected for the short list of three candidates (Webster Avenue/Third Avenue, Hillside Avenue, and LaGuardia/East Elmhurst) that were prioritized for the next planning effort [[cite:brt_phase2_future_corridors_rpt#p003_c0005|short list of three]].

At the Manhattan BRT workshop held on June 18, 2009 in the Garment District, which drew 97 participants—the highest turnout of any borough workshop [[metric:metric_manhattan-workshop-attendance|Manhattan Workshop Attendance]] [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p010_c0005|workshop attendance table]]—Manhattan residents voiced overwhelming support for better east-west crosstown service, particularly across Central Park [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p020_c0005|crosstown service support]]. Participants ranked this corridor as the highest priority among all Manhattan corridors under discussion [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p020_c0008|highest priority ranking]]. Within the corridor, 86th Street received the greatest attention; participants stated that the M86 bus is "very slow and over-crowded" [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p020_c0008|M86 slow and over-crowded]]. 96th Street, 72nd Street, and 66th Street were also mentioned as important crosstown streets within the corridor [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p020_c0008|other streets mentioned]].

The BRT Phase II public outreach effort overall found strong support for BRT expansion: 97% of online survey respondents and 98% of workshop attendees expressed support [[metric:metric_survey-respondents-total|Online Survey Respondents]] [[metric:metric_workshop-participants-total|BRT Workshop Participants]] [[cite:brt_phase2_outreach_rpt#p004_c0011|public support for BRT]].
