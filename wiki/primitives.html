Project and intervention boundary

For page writing, the route is the service identity. The M86 SBS implementation is a project bundle. The bus bulbs, queue jump lanes, off-board fare payment, and arrival information displays are interventions, represented by treatment component records.

Expanded evidence mode

Evidence mode keeps the same writing surface but expands citation cards inline so a reviewer can audit the exact blocks and roles while reading.

Rules of thumb

  • 1Prose says what matters. Primitives say where the claim came from and what kind of thing it is.
  • 2Citation chips should be small by default and expandable during review.
  • 3Projects are bundles with lifecycle. Interventions are treatment components unless the corpus proves otherwise.
  • 4Metrics render as rows first. Charts come after comparable grouping.
