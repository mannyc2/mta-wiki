The December 31, 2022 update listed the Graffiti Removal Program as 100% complete. [meeting_doc_104771#p012_c0002]

The April 2023 update listed the same named program and scope as 25% complete, while the September 2023 update listed the same named program and scope as 50% complete. [meeting_doc_111786#p012_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0002]
