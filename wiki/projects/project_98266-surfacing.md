Track Surfacing is a maintenance-performance record, not a bus-priority treatment. The current materialized identity combines LIRR "Track Surfacing" totals from quarterly Maintenance of Way updates with Metro-North "Surfacing" totals from separate quarterly updates, so agency-specific figures should be read separately. [meeting_doc_104766#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_104766#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0003]

For LIRR's 2022 Q4 Maintenance of Way update, Track Surfacing covered 501 track miles excluding yards, with 68 miles planned and 95 miles actual, reported as 140% of goal. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0003] [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0004] The same slide breaks the work into Atlantic Hall to Valley, Hempstead Park 2 to Garden, Hempstead Bellerose to Park 2, Mainline Queens to Carle Place, Oyster Bay East Williston to Locust, and other systemwide locations. [meeting_doc_104766#p003_c0003]

For LIRR's 2023 Q2 update, the Track Surfacing line again describes 501 track miles excluding yards, but with 25 miles planned and 28 miles actual, reported as 112% of goal. [meeting_doc_115196#p003_c0002] In that Q2 source, Port Jefferson PJ Single, Port Jefferson Divide 4 to Divide 5, and Mainline Divide 4 to Divide 6 were each listed at 0 actual miles, while "Other Locations Systemwide" accounted for 28 actual miles. [meeting_doc_115196#p003_c0002]

For Metro-North's April 2023 Q1 update, Surfacing was listed as 754 track miles systemwide with 33.9 miles planned; the table reported 10 miles completed, or 28% of goal, with completed mileage on the Hudson Line and New Haven and none yet on the Harlem Line or New Canaan. [meeting_doc_111786#p005_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p005_c0005] By the September 2023 Q3 update, the same Metro-North Surfacing program still showed 33.9 miles planned and reported 57 miles completed, or 90%, with completed mileage on Hudson, Harlem, New Haven, and New Canaan. [meeting_doc_124216#p005_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p005_c0005]
