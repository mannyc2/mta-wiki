## Summary

Grand Central Madison is the East Side Access terminal that gives LIRR passengers direct service to Grand Central Terminal through an 11.5-mile underground tunnel with a new station and concourse below the existing terminal. [meeting_doc_115231#p011_c0008] MTA/LIRR described its first customer service on January 25, 2023 as the first extension of LIRR service since the railroad's first train to Penn Station in 1910. [meeting_doc_105776#p002_c0008]

## Timeline

By November 2022, GCM was in readiness mode: employee orientation and training were underway, cross-agency safety and preparedness drills had taken place, and about 200 volunteers tested terminal wayfinding, signage, and passenger paths. [meeting_doc_100291#p004_c0008] [meeting_doc_100291#p004_c0009] [meeting_doc_100291#p025_c0003]

The first customer service began with the 10:45 AM Grand Central Direct train from Jamaica on January 25, 2023. [meeting_doc_105776#p002_c0008] Before full service, LIRR operated limited Jamaica-GCM shuttle service on weekdays and weekends, with customer ambassadors at Jamaica and on the GCM concourse and Penn Station tickets accepted because Penn and GCM were in the same fare zone. [meeting_doc_105776#p003_c0001] The full schedules were presented as an approximately 41% service increase. [meeting_doc_105776#p003_c0002] [meeting_doc_113906#p005_c0012] [meeting_doc_113906#p005_c0013]

A July 2023 capital update reported actual revenue service in January 2023, a $11.1B budget, and the project approximately 96% complete; after the January 25 revenue-service start, GCM had entered closeout. [meeting_doc_115231#p011_c0009] [meeting_doc_115231#p011_c0010]

## Service Effects

LIRR's 2023 ridership report says GCM's opening created new travel opportunities. In that same 2023 context, LIRR total ridership reached 65.2 million customers, up 24.2% from 2022 but still 71.6% of 2019 ridership. [meeting_doc_138216#p002_c0002] [meeting_doc_138216#p002_c0004]

The report also states that AM peak Manhattan service increased by 57%, Manhattan service was split roughly 60/40 between Penn Station and Grand Central Madison, 275 weekday trains were added to reach 943 daily trains, and almost 300 daily trains operated to or from GCM. [meeting_doc_138216#p003_c0007] A later outlook said trips to GCM were approaching a 40% share versus 60% to Penn, weekdays were routinely carrying more than 230,000 customers, and on-time performance had been above 95% for several months. [meeting_doc_138216#p009_c0002]

## Access And Follow-On Work

The Biltmore connection opened on May 8, 2023 with two escalators and an elevator between the Biltmore Room and Grand Central Madison. [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0010] It gave LIRR customers direct escalator access between GCM and Grand Central Terminal's Main Concourse, added elevator access for ADA customers, and was expected to handle about 22% of GCM passenger exits. [meeting_doc_111761#p007_c0002] [meeting_doc_111761#p007_c0005]

Operationally, a May 2023 PTC update listed Positive Train Control operations as implemented in Grand Central Madison. [meeting_doc_111741#p002_c0006] An October 2023 timetable staff summary said the November 13, 2023 schedule would include GCM contractor maintenance and wireless cellular installation, along with service improvements reflecting new customer travel patterns after the GCM service plan. [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124266#p001_c0005]

## Caveats

The inspected sources use multiple milestone dates: January 25, 2023 for first customer/revenue service, February 27 for "opening" in the 2023 ridership report, and May 8, 2023 for the Biltmore connection. These should not be collapsed into a single launch date without a source-specific qualifier. [meeting_doc_105776#p002_c0008] [meeting_doc_138216#p002_c0004] [meeting_doc_111761#p006_c0010]

The 2023 ridership and satisfaction figures are LIRR-wide report context; the cited blocks describe GCM-related service changes but do not isolate a GCM-only causal effect for all ridership or satisfaction improvements. [meeting_doc_138216#p002_c0002] [meeting_doc_138216#p003_c0007] [meeting_doc_138216#p005_c0003]
