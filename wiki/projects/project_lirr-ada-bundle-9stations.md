## Overview

The January 2023 source describes this as an LIRR Americans with Disabilities Act station-improvements bundle covering nine stations: Massapequa Park, Amityville, Copiague, Lindenhurst, Laurelton, Locust Manor, St. Albans, Valley Stream, and Auburndale. [meeting_doc_104746#p006_c0003]

The stated scope included accessibility and state-of-good-repair upgrades, architectural and electrical improvements, new signage and lighting, ADA path-of-travel and other site upgrades, seven new elevators, and replacement elevators at the other two stations. [meeting_doc_104746#p006_c0003]

The January 2023 source said work was expected to begin that summer and that completion of all nine stations would make 93% of LIRR's 126 stations fully ADA-accessible. [meeting_doc_104746#p006_c0003]

## Status Update

By the May 2023 source, work had advanced from design into construction activity, with "shovels" in the ground at the first four of nine stations in the ADA station improvements package. [meeting_doc_111761#p008_c0011]

Amityville, Copiague, Lindenhurst, and Massapequa Park were identified as the first four stations and were expected to be the first to reach beneficial use, with the project described as bringing all nine stations into ADA compliance through new or replaced elevators, site, sidewalk, and path-of-travel improvements. [meeting_doc_111761#p008_c0012]

The same May 2023 update said structural and architectural improvements, lighting, signage, and electrical enhancements were part of a single design-build contract. [meeting_doc_111761#p008_c0012]

Valley Stream, Laurelton, Locust Manor, St. Albans, and Auburndale were also slated for the upgrades, and substantial overall project completion was scheduled for September 2025. [meeting_doc_111761#p008_c0013]

A May 2023 summary block separately characterized the effort as nine LIRR stations slated for ADA improvements by fall 2025. [meeting_doc_111761#p005_c0012]
