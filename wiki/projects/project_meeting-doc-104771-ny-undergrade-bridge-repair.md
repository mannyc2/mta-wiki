## Scope

NY Undergrade Bridge Repair Program appears in Metro-North Railroad Maintenance of Way updates under "Track, Structures, Stations and Facilities Metro-North Railroad." [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_115146#p001_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0003] The program page tracks site-level undergrade bridge repair work, not a single percentage total: the listed work includes flag repairs, concrete vault repairs, masonry arch repairs, steel/concrete repairs, replacement work, and bridge removal across Hudson, Harlem, and Port Jervis locations.
