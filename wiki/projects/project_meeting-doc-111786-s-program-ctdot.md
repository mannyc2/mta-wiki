## Overview

S-Program (CTDOT Bridge Program) is tracked in Metro-North Maintenance of Way quarterly materials as a CTDOT/CDOT-linked bridge repair program. The source language uses "CTDOT" in the program name and "CDOT" for the funding status. [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_115146#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0002]

The available budget evidence is funding status, not a dollar amount: the Q4 2022 deck marked the program "Deferred to 2023 due to lack of funding," Q2 2023 still listed it as "Pending Funding from CDOT," and Q3 2023 reported that CDOT funding was approved in September. [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_115146#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0002]

## Scope And Schedule

In the December 31, 2022 Q4 update, the S-Program list covered five New Haven Line locations, all at 0% complete: Steamboat Road (NH 28.22), Sound Shore Drive (NH 29.68), Mill Plain Road (NH 50.29), Fairfield Creek (NH 51.68), and Union Street (NH 55.40). [meeting_doc_104771#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0003]

A related Q1 2023 materialized page shows the same program as "Pending Repair Plans & Funding from CDOT," with an eight-location list: Steamboat Road, Davis Avenue #2, Lukes Crossing / Sound Shore Drive, Greenwich Avenue, Compo Road, Myrtle Avenue, East Main Street, and Campbell Avenue, all at 0% complete. [meeting_doc_111786#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_111786#p011_c0003]

The June 2023 Q2 update kept that eight-location scope and still showed every listed location at 0% complete while funding remained pending from CDOT. [meeting_doc_115146#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_115146#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_115146#p011_c0003]

By the September 2023 Q3 update, the deck stated that CDOT funding had been approved in September, work would commence in October 2023, and the work would carry over to 2024. The same eight listed repair locations were still shown at 0% complete in that snapshot. [meeting_doc_124216#p001_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0003]

## Relationship To Other Bridge Work

The S-Program should be read separately from the NY bridge programs in the same Maintenance of Way decks. Those decks also track an "Overhead Bridge Program (NY)" for bridge flag repairs on the Harlem, Hudson, New Haven-NY, and Port Jervis lines, plus a "NY Undergrade Bridge Repair Program"; both are distinct from the CTDOT Bridge Program page. [meeting_doc_104771#p007_c0002] [meeting_doc_104771#p009_c0002] [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0002]

It is also distinct from CT bridge items on the following page, including Cos Cob Bridge NH 29.90 and Saga NH 44.32. In Q3 2023 those page-12 items had their own progress values, while the S-Program page-11 locations remained at 0% complete with a future work-start note. [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0002] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0003] [meeting_doc_124216#p012_c0002]

## Caveats

Treat these as document-time snapshots. Each inspected S-Program page carries the caveat "All subject to change due to current events," and the sources inspected here do not confirm actual post-October 2023 start, completion, or a dollar value for the approved CDOT funding. [meeting_doc_104771#p011_c0004] [meeting_doc_111786#p011_c0004] [meeting_doc_115146#p011_c0004] [meeting_doc_124216#p011_c0004]
