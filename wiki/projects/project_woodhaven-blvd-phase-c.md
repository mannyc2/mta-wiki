## Project Snapshot

Woodhaven Blvd Phase C is a conceptual Woodhaven Boulevard capital project from Queens Blvd to 66 Av, associated with CB5 and CB6, and the decks list its estimated construction as TBD. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p016_c0002] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p016_c0002] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p016_c0002]

It sits in a three-project capital sequence that also lists Phase B from Union Turnpike to 107 Av in design with estimated 2026 construction and Woodhaven Blvd / Metropolitan Av from Yellowstone Blvd to 77 Av in scoping with estimated 2028 construction. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p016_c0002]

The next-steps slide lists Phase C scoping start as early 2025 and design start as TBD. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p025_c0002] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p033_c0002] [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb6_sept2024#p036_c0002]

## Planned Treatment Direction

The CB5 Phase C slide says the project would maintain bus-lane alignment and operating hours and complement improvements made by Phase B and Woodhaven Blvd / Metropolitan Av. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p031_c0002]

The same Phase C slide lists upgrades to bus stops, sidewalk extensions, shortened crossings, and reconstructed medians. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p031_c0002]

It also lists new crossings, raised crosswalks, street trees and landscaping, and improved lighting. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb5_sept2024#p031_c0002]

## Corridor Context

Woodhaven Boulevard is described as a major bus corridor hosting Q52/Q53 SBS with 30,000+ daily riders, connections to Queens Boulevard and the Rockaways, 8 subway lines, and 20 local/express bus routes. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p004_c0002]

The same capital-project background slide says Woodhaven Boulevard has been a Vision Zero Priority Corridor since 2019 and identifies priority intersections at Myrtle Av, Jamaica Av, and Rockaway Blvd. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p004_c0002]

The 2017 Q52/Q53 SBS launch installed 6.85 miles of bus lanes on Woodhaven Blvd, including offset lanes from Queens Blvd to Park Ln South, median lanes from Park Ln South to Rockaway Blvd, and curbside lanes from Rockaway Blvd to N Conduit Av. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p005_c0002]

The deck reports prior corridor outcomes, including average bus travel-time improvement of 3 minutes in each direction, on-time performance improvement up to 80%, and northbound Q52/Q53 late arrivals shrinking from 52% to 10% in a 2019-vs-2017 comparison. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p011_c0002]

Safety metrics in the same source report pedestrian injuries down 20%, cyclist injuries down 80%, motor-vehicle crashes down 36%, and total incidents down 33%. [capital_woodhaven_blvd_capital_projects_cb10_dec2024#p012_c0003]
