The [[route:route_q78-queens|Q78 bus route]] was a proposed Bell-Springfield Boulevards route that appeared in earlier drafts of the Queens Bus Network Redesign but was omitted from the Proposed Final Plan and Addendum due to budgetary constraints [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p017_c0007|Q78 Bell-Springfield Boulevards Route]] [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p017_c0008|omitted from Proposed Final Plan]]. The route would have provided continuous service along Springfield and Bell Boulevards, connecting Laurelton to Bayside, enhancing connectivity across Southeast and Northeast Queens, and serving schools, Queensborough Community College, and numerous other bus routes [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p017_c0008|Q78 route description]]. The MTA characterized this and similar omitted proposals as valuable opportunities to further enhance equity within the Queens bus network as resources become available [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p017_c0009|equity opportunities]]. The Q78 does not appear in the Route Improvements Summary table that lists implemented or recommended route-level changes in the Proposed Final Plan Addendum [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p018_c0003|Route Improvements Q1-Q36]] [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p019_c0003|Route Improvements Q37-Q77]] [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p020_c0003|Route Improvements Q80-Q115]].
