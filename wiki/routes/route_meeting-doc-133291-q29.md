The [[route:route_meeting-doc-133291-q29|Q29]] was one of four routes included in proposed weekday schedule changes documented in a February 14, 2024 MTA staff summary seeking approval from the President and the NYCT/MTA Bus Committee [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0006|Q29 included in proposed schedule changes]]. Unlike the B82, Bx19, and Q3, the Q29 was flagged as a running-time-only change with no headway or capacity modifications shown in the attachment table [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0005|Routes with running time changes only - Q29]] [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0003|Q29 table row shows all dashes for headway and capacity]]. Running time on the Q29 was proposed to increase [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p002_c0010|Running time will increase on the B82, Q3 and Q29]]. The Q29 is operated by MTA Bus (not New York City Transit), and the cost impact of the proposed changes was estimated at $167,600 [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0010|Q29 operated by MTA Bus, cost impact $167,600]] [[metric:metric_q29-cost-impact-mtabus|Q29 cost impact - $167,600]]. The proposed implementation date was April 2024 [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0012|April 2024]]. Trip adjustments may also have occurred during time periods not shown in the attachment table [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0006|Trip adjustment may occur during time periods not shown above]].
