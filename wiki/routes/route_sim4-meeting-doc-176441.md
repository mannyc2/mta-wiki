The [[route:route_sim4-meeting-doc-176441|SIM4]] is a weekday peak period, peak direction express bus route operating between Staten Island's South Shore along Richmond Avenue and Downtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0005|SIM4 route description]]. The SIM4 and its variant SIM4X serve Downtown Manhattan, while the related SIM8 and SIM8X serve Midtown [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0009|SIM4/SIM4X serve Downtown, SIM8/SIM8X serve Midtown]].

The SIM4X variant was introduced as part of the Staten Island Express Bus Redesign in August 2018, making only two stops on Staten Island (at the Staten Island Mall Park-and-Ride and one other stop) but serving all the same Manhattan stops as the full SIM4 route [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0006|SIM4X established in 2018 redesign]]. In January 2019, one additional stop per direction was added on Staten Island [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0010|January 2019 stop addition]]. The June 2025 staff summary lists the SIM4X as operating 15 trips per weekday, with 7 morning trips to Manhattan and 8 afternoon/evening trips to Staten Island [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0011|SIM4X 15 trips/weekday]] ([[metric:metric_sim4x-15-trips-weekday|SIM4X 15 trips per weekday]], [[metric:metric_sim4x-7-morning-trips|SIM4X 7 morning trips to Manhattan]], [[metric:metric_sim4x-8-afternoon-trips|SIM4X 8 afternoon/evening trips to Staten Island]]).

The SIM4X trips are underutilized, running at an average load factor of approximately 15% [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0007|SIM4X 15% load factor]] ([[metric:metric_sim4x-load-factor-15pct|SIM4X average load factor 15%]]). A June 2025 staff summary recommends discontinuing both the SIM4X and SIM8X variants and reinvesting resources into 7 additional full-route SIM4 trips (3 to Manhattan and 4 to Staten Island) and 2 additional full-route SIM8 trips, with implementation planned for Fall 2025 [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0003|proposal for 7 SIM4 and 2 SIM8 trips]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0013|Fall 2025 implementation date]] ([[metric:metric_proposed-7-sim4-trips|Proposed 7 additional SIM4 trips]], [[metric:metric_proposed-3-sim4-manhattan-trips|Proposed 3 SIM4 trips to Manhattan]], [[metric:metric_proposed-4-sim4-si-trips|Proposed 4 SIM4 trips to Staten Island]], [[metric:metric_proposed-2-sim8-trips|Proposed 2 additional SIM8 trips]]). The net decrease in operating costs from this change is estimated at $2.5 million per year [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0011|$2.5M net decrease]] ([[metric:metric_net-decrease-operating-costs-2-5m|Net decrease in operating costs $2.5M/year]]).
