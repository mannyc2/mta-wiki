The [[route:route_q20b-cb12-2011|Q20B bus route]] is a local bus route in Queens, operating between Jamaica and College Point as part of the Main Street corridor Select Bus Service candidates [[cite:2015_06_09_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb12#p006_c0006|Q20A/B: Jamaica to College Point]]. During the planning for the Q44 SBS upgrade, a May 2015 community presentation stated that "Q20A/B Local Service Will Continue As Is" while the Q44 Limited was upgraded to Select Bus Service [[cite:2015_05_brt_flushing_jamaica_presentation_openhouse#p007_c0003|Q20A/B local service to continue unchanged]]. The route serves a bus stop at the intersection of Hillside Avenue and Sutphin Boulevard, where Q20A/Q20B/Q44 and Q43 routes share a stop location as documented in the 2011 Jamaica Bus Improvement Study presented to Queens Community Board 12 [[cite:201106_jamaica_cb12_slides#p026_c0006|Q20A/Q20B/Q44 bus stop at Hillside Av and Sutphin Blvd]]. That study identified challenges in the Jamaica area including narrow streets with heavy bus and local traffic, bus stop patterns causing excess weaving and congestion, and misplaced layover locations [[cite:201106_jamaica_cb12_slides#p005_c0004|narrow streets claim]] [[cite:201106_jamaica_cb12_slides#p006_c0004|bus stop patterns claim]]. The Main Street corridor carrying Q20A/B and Q44 served approximately 42,000 daily riders, with a 2015 SBS implementation goal [[cite:2015_06_09_brt_flushing_jamaica_cb12#p006_c0005|42,000 daily riders on Main Street corridor]].
