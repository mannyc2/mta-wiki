The QM65 is a proposed new express bus route introduced as part of the Queens Bus Network Redesign, operating from southeast Queens to downtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p004_c0007|QM65 proposed new express route]]. Alongside the QM65, proposed express-network changes included the discontinuation of the QM3, plus other routing, bus stop, and frequency adjustments [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p004_c0007|QM3 discontinuation]]. The route is documented in an MTA Board Action Request staff summary dated April 4, 2024, which sought authorization to hold a public hearing on the Queens Bus Network Redesign [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p002_c0005|April 4, 2024 staff summary]].

The Queens Bus Network Redesign process included an Existing Conditions report in August 2019 [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p002_c0013|August 2019 Existing Conditions report]], an Original Draft Plan in December 2019 that generated [[metric:metric_11000-comments-original-draft|Over 11,000 comments on Original Draft Plan]] before the COVID-19 pandemic paused the project [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p003_c0004|over 11,000 comments on Original Draft]], a New Draft Plan in March 2022 [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p003_c0005|New Draft Plan March 2022]], and a Proposed Final Plan in December 2023 [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p003_c0006|Proposed Final Plan December 2023]]. The MTA partnered with NYC DOT to identify [[metric:metric_24-priority-corridors-queens|24 priority corridors in Queens identified for bus priority]] for potential bus priority improvements [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p001_c0005|24 priority corridors identified]]. A phased implementation was targeted for mid-2025, with a Proposed Final Plan Addendum to follow the public hearing and precede the required MTA Board vote [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p001_c0006|mid-2025 phased implementation]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p003_c0007|Proposed Final Plan Addendum]]. The redesigned Queens network would serve [[metric:metric_queens-bus-ridership-800k|Queens bus network average weekday ridership ~800,000]] across the source-listed [[metric:metric_queens-113-bus-routes|113 bus routes in Queens]], including [[metric:metric_queens-30-express-routes|30 express bus routes in Queens]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138431#p002_c0012|Queens network statistics]].
