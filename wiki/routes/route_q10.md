The [[route:route_q10|Q10 bus route]] operates in Southwest Queens, traversing several Tier 1 and Tier 2 equity consideration areas under the Queens Bus Network Redesign equity framework [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p010_c0004|Q10 route description in equity evaluation]]. The existing Q10 has multiple service patterns all sharing the same route number: a direct Lefferts Boulevard route to JFK Airport, a South Ozone Park routing before continuing to the airport, and a third branch ending in South Ozone Park without reaching the airport, creating passenger confusion [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p010_c0004|Q10 service patterns described]].

As part of the Redesign's "Simplify Variants" strategy, the Q80 has been introduced as a new route label for the Lefferts Boulevard direct-to-airport branch, serving a population that is over 80% non-white [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p010_c0004|Q80 introduced for Lefferts Blvd]]. The Q80 offers more frequent and new overnight service along Lefferts Boulevard for airport workers with non-traditional schedules [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p010_c0004|Q80 frequency and overnight service]]. The Q10 maintains its pattern between Kew Gardens and JFK Airport, continuing to serve residents along Rockaway Boulevard and 130th Street, and is restructured as a "Rush" route providing faster service for South Ozone Park residents traveling to Kew Gardens while maintaining the airport connection [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p010_c0004|Q10 restructured as Rush route]]. The Q10 also appears in the route improvements summary table under the "Simplify Variants" strategy [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p018_c0003|Q10 route improvements table]].

Additionally, the Q10 is listed in the MTA's Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program along Lefferts Boulevard, with the source noting a 60-day warning period before fines begin [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|ACE listing for Q10/Q80 on Lefferts Blvd]].
