The proposed [[route:route_b3-draft-plan|Proposed B3 Local - Avenue U]] maintains its existing routing, serving the Avenue U corridor between Gravesend and Bergen Beach [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0003|B3]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0004|Avenue U]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0005|Service between Gravesend and Bergen Beach]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0019|maintain existing routing]]. As a Local route, stops would be spaced farther apart than existing -- from 640 feet to 1,127 feet on average -- to speed up buses and improve reliability [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0010|Existing: 640 feet Proposed: 1,127 feet]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0020|stops slightly farther apart]]. The route length stays unchanged at 5.3 miles and turns per mile remain at 0.7 [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0008|Existing: 5.3 miles Proposed: 5.3 miles]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0012|Existing: 0.7 per mile Proposed: 0.7 per mile]]. No frequency or service span changes are proposed at this time [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0021|No frequency or service span changes]]. The sole listed route improvement is improved stop spacing [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0023|Improved stop spacing]]. The proposed B3 is identified as the existing B3 route with the same label [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0006|Related Routes: B3]]. Proposed connections include bus routes B1, B4, B5, B6, B6 LTD, B9, B31, B36, B40, B41, B41 XT, B44, B44 SBS, B46, B46 SBS, B47, B49, B64, B68, B82, B82 SBS, and Q35, as well as subway service on the D, N, F, and Q lines [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0015|bus connections]] [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p071_c0017|subway connections]]. (The proposed B3 appears in the Brooklyn Bus Network Redesign Draft Plan published December 2022 [[cite:brooklyn_bus_network_draft_plan_with_route_profiles#p001_c0003|Draft Plan 12/01/22]].)
