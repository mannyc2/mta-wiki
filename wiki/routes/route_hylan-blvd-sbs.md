Hylan Boulevard Select Bus Service is one of 16 Select Bus Service routes implemented throughout New York City, launched in September 2012 on Staten Island [[cite:brt_route_index#p001_b0001|NYC DOT SBS route index]]. The route includes streamlined bus service, four miles of bus lanes, left-turn lanes, pedestrian refuge islands, and new bus shelters [[cite:brt_route_index#p001_b0001|NYC DOT SBS route index]]. A final report on the Hylan Boulevard project was published by NYC DOT in 2014 [[cite:brt_route_index#p001_b0001|NYC DOT SBS route index]].

The packet does not contain Hylan-specific ridership, speed, or satisfaction metrics comparable to those reported for other SBS routes such as the [[metric:metric_fordham-rider-satisfaction-98pct|Fordham Road 98% rider satisfaction]] or the [[metric:metric_first-second-ave-speed-increase-15to18|15-18% speed improvement on First/Second Avenues]].
