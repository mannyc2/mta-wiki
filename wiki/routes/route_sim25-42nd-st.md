SIM25 is a Staten Island Express Bus Route using 42nd Street, one of six Staten Island express routes that travel along this corridor. [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p006_c0003|Eight MTA bus routes across three boroughs use 42nd Street, carrying about 16,000 daily passengers with up to 210 buses per hour.]] [[cite:42nd_st_cb6_jun032019#p007_c0001|A map shows SIM25 running north-south in southern Staten Island, with the 42nd St Project Corridor along the M42 route in Manhattan.]]

The 42nd Street Transit Improvements project serves [[route:route_sim25-42nd-st|SIM25]] along with the M42, SIM8, SIM8X, SIM22, SIM26, and SIM30 routes. [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p029_c0006|The 2.0-mile corridor has average bus speeds of 4.2 mph in the AM and 2.9 mph in the PM.]] On 42nd Street, [[metric:metric_bus-people-79-percent|buses carry 79% of people using only 33% of street space]] [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p008_c0002|as shown in a street-space comparison chart.]]

M42 running time data illustrates the congestion that SIM25 and other express routes encounter on 42nd Street. [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p010_c0003|The M42 eastbound weekday running time from 10am-3pm is 34.4 minutes]] ([[metric:metric_m42-eb-weekday-10am-3pm-running-time|M42 EB Weekday 10am-3pm running time 34.4 min]]), and the westbound 3-7pm running time is 35.2 minutes ([[metric:metric_m42-wb-weekday-3-7pm-running-time|M42 WB Weekday 3-7pm running time 35.2 min]]). [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p010_c0005|Bus travel times are nearly double overnight times throughout the day.]]

Better Buses identified 42nd Street as a priority corridor in 2017 due to slow bus speeds. [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p029_c0005|The project investigates upgrades to existing curbside bus lanes and safety improvements.]] Proposed changes include offset bus lanes (24/7), curbside bus lanes (7 AM-7 PM), overnight commercial loading allocations, and bus stop consolidation. [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p018_c0002|Offset bus lanes are proposed to operate 24/7 with curbside bus lanes in effect 7 AM to 7 PM all days.]]
