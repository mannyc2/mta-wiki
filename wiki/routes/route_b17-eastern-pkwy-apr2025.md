[[route:route_b17-eastern-pkwy-apr2025|B17 (NYCT)]] is a local bus route operated by MTA NYCT in Brooklyn, running between Crown Heights and Canarsie [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0008|B17 listed as Crown Heights-Canarsie]]. Along with the B14, it serves Eastern Parkway between Schenectady Avenue and Ralph Avenue, where a transit priority proposal was presented to Brooklyn Community Board 8 on April 10, 2025, and to Brooklyn Community Board 9 on April 9, 2025 [[cite:eastern_pkwy_schenectady_ave_ralph_ave_cb8_apr2025#p001_c0003|CB8 presentation date]] [[cite:eastern_pkwy_schenectady_ave_ralph_ave_cb9_apr2025#p001_c0003|CB9 presentation date]].

Bus service along Eastern Parkway in this corridor began in 1925, operated by the Eastern Parkway, Brownsville & East New York Transit Relief Association [[cite:eastern_pkwy_schenectady_ave_ralph_ave_cb9_apr2025#p005_c0002|historical bus service note]]. [[metric:metric_3000-plus-board-b14-b17-utica-ave|3,000+ people board B14 or B17 at Eastern Parkway & Utica Avenue every weekday]], a high-ridership stop that also sees the B17's eastbound route shown on project routing maps [[cite:eastern_pkwy_schenectady_ave_ralph_ave_cb8_apr2025#p009_c0005|B17 route map]] [[cite:eastern_pkwy_schenectady_ave_ralph_ave_cb9_apr2025#p005_c0002|3,000+ daily boardings at Eastern Parkway & Utica Avenue]].

In a December 2024 staff summary supporting congestion pricing, the MTA identified [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-additional-buses|approximately 29 additional buses]] for local improvements and listed the [[route:route_b17-eastern-pkwy-apr2025|B17 (NYCT)]] as one of 16 local bus routes/route combinations selected for running time adjustments, planned for implementation in the summer 2025 schedule [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0008|B17 listed with running time adjustments]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0007|fleet increase and local route details]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0013|local bus routes Summer 2025]]. The recommended service package's [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-total-annual-cost|total annual operating cost]] increased by approximately $8 million, with the [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-local-annual-cost|local bus annual cost]] roughly $6.6 million per year and the [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-nyct-annual-cost|NYCT annual cost allocation]] approximately $5.1 million per year [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|annualized operating cost details]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0010|NYCT vs MTA Bus Company cost split]].
