The [[route:route_q84-queens-bus-redesign|Q84]] appears in the Queens Bus Network Redesign as a case study for the "Better bus stop spacing" strategy within a system of [[metric:metric_115-bus-routes-queens|115 bus routes in Queens]] serving [[metric:metric_800k-riders-queens-weekday|800,000 weekday riders in Queens]] [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p010_c0002|Case Study: Q84]] [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p002_c0003|115 bus routes that serve 800,000 customers]]. The route is proposed as a new "Rush" type service connecting Cambria Heights and Downtown Jamaica [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p010_c0003|Creating a new Rush route to connect Cambria Heights and Downtown Jamaica]].

As defined in the redesign, "Rush" Routes are designed to connect outer borough neighborhoods quickly to the subway [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p005_c0005|Rush Routes connect outer borough neighborhoods quickly to the subway]]. The Q84 stop spacing reflects this logic: stops are closer together in residential Cambria Heights and farther apart near the Jamaica LIRR/Subway hub [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p010_c0003|Stops are closer together in residential Cambria Heights and farther apart near the Jamaica LIRR/Subway hub]]. The redesign's proposed stop spacing for Rush Routes is 1,250 feet [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p010_c0005|Rush Routes NA → 1,250 feet]], a change from the Local Route target of [[metric:metric_local-stop-spacing-800-1200|Local route stop spacing 800-1,200 feet]] also shown in the same table.

The Q84 was presented during the public outreach phase following the New Draft Plan release in March 2022 [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p003_c0010|Released New Draft Plan (Mar 2022)]], as part of the MTA Queens Bus Network Redesign process that began with open houses in April 2019 [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p003_c0002|Launched project with open houses and surveys (Apr 2019)]]. Because the Q84 is a proposed redesign route rather than a current operating route, its final routing, stop locations, and service frequency depend on community feedback and adoption of the redesign plan.
