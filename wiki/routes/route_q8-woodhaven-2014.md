The [[route:route_q8-woodhaven-2014|Q8]] is a local bus route in Queens [[cite:2014_06_25_brt_woodhaven_publicworkshop2_summary#p005_c0006|mentioned as a connection point at 101st Avenue during community planning for Woodhaven/Cross Bay Boulevards Select Bus Service]]. At the second public SBS workshop on June 25, 2014, workshop participants proposed adding an SBS stop at 101st Avenue specifically to connect with the Q8 route [[cite:2014_06_25_brt_woodhaven_publicworkshop2_summary#p005_c0006|add stop at 101 Av to connect to the Q8]].

The Q8 also appears as one of six bus routes on a corridor map for the Linden Blvd Traffic Safety and Bus Priority Improvements project, which covers the segment from Fountain Avenue to Conduit Avenue [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p020_c0003|Q8 (purple) route shown on Linden Blvd map]]. That corridor serves approximately [[metric:metric_60000-daily-riders|60,000 daily riders]] across six routes—including the [[route:route_q8-woodhaven-2014|Q8]]—and has a [[metric:metric_bus-speed-4-mph|corridor bus speed low point]] of 4 mph due to congestion and double-parking [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p004_c0002|Bus speeds as low as 4 mph across 6 bus routes serving 60,000 daily riders]]. The [[metric:metric_corridor-length-1-1-miles_3|Linden Blvd corridor length]] is 1.1 miles, with a [[metric:metric_road-width-140-ft|140-foot]] wide roadway spanning [[metric:metric_10-lanes-corridor|10 travel lanes]], and crosswalks spaced up to [[metric:metric_crosswalk-spacing-1200-ft|1,200 feet apart]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p009_c0002|140 feet wide, 10 car lanes, crosswalks up to 1,200 feet apart]].

Demographic data for census tracts bordering this corridor indicates that [[metric:metric_34000-residents|34,000 residents]] live nearby, [[metric:metric_households-no-car-57-pct|57% of households have no vehicle]], and [[metric:metric_public-transit-commute-54-pct|54% commute via public transit]], with an [[metric:metric_avg-travel-time-46-min|average commute travel time]] of 46 minutes [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0002|34,000 residents, 46-minute avg travel time]][[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0005|57% no vehicle available]][[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0003|54% public transportation]].
