[[route:route_qm34-fresh-pond-rd|QM34]] is an express bus route serving Middle Village, Ridgewood, and Glendale in Queens, operating express to Midtown and Downtown Manhattan via the Long Island Expressway, according to NYC DOT project materials describing the Fresh Pond Road corridor [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_may2019#p006_c0002|Fresh Pond Rd bus route map]]. The route operates along Fresh Pond Road, a corridor that was identified as a 2019 priority project by NYC DOT's Better Buses Action Plan because buses on Fresh Pond Road averaged only 3.0 mph during the PM peak hour, well below the Queens average of 6.4 mph [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p005_c0002|Fresh Pond Rd priority justification]].

Speed data from May 2019 shows [[route:route_qm34-fresh-pond-rd|QM34]] buses reached their slowest speeds in the late afternoon and early evening, with a recorded speed of 3.4 mph at 5:00 PM, 3.5 mph at 6:00 PM, and 3.8 mph at 4:00 PM [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_may2019#p008_c0003|QM34 speed table]]. The corridor carried roughly 30,000 daily riders across all routes including the QM24, QM25, and QM34 express services and the Q58 local [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_tc_jun2019#p005_c0002|Fresh Pond Rd priority justification]]. The proposed transit improvements for Fresh Pond Road included a southbound-only curbside bus lane from Bleecker Street to Putnam Avenue, active during PM peak hours, along with bus stop consolidation and parking and loading zone changes [[cite:fresh_pond_rd_may2019#p018_c0002|proposed bus lane description]].
