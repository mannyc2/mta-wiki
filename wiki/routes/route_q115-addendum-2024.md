In the Queens Bus Network Redesign Proposed Final Plan Addendum published in December 2024, [[route:route_q115-addendum-2024|Q115]] is classified as a Local route serving as the underlying local service for the Q111 Rush Route [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p007_c0004|Underlying local service provided by the Q115]]. The Q111 Rush Route follows the Guy R Brewer Blvd corridor with a rush segment on Guy R Brewer Blvd, local stop spacing on 147th Avenue, and provides faster service to key transfer points in Jamaica [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p007_c0004|Serves Guy R Brewer Blvd corridor...rush segment on Guy R Brewer Blvd]]. The addendum proposed [[metric:metric_94-local-routes-proposed|94 Local routes proposed]] (up from 83 existing), including 25 Rush Routes, as part of [[metric:metric_124-total-routes-proposed|124 total routes proposed]] across the Queens network [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p008_c0002|124 total routes (vs. 113 existing)]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p008_c0003|94 Local routes (vs. 83 existing) including 25 Rush Routes]]. The Q115 as a Local route connects local neighborhoods with standard stop spacing, while the Q111 provides a time-sensitive rush variant along the same corridor [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p005_c0002|Local Routes (Green)]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p005_c0003|Connecting local neighborhoods]]. The addendum identifies the Q115 by its route label Q115 with a Local route type and local service variant [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p007_c0004|Underlying local service provided by the Q115]]. The proposed network includes [[metric:metric_36-major-routing-changes|36 major routing changes]], [[metric:metric_17-routes-increased-frequencies|17 routes with increased frequencies]], and [[metric:metric_8-routes-increased-spans|8 routes with increased spans]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p008_c0006|36 major routing changes]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p008_c0007|17 routes with increased frequencies]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p008_c0008|8 routes with increased spans]]. The planned MTA Board Vote was scheduled for Winter 2025, with implementation in two phases aligned with regular seasonal service changes [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p009_c0005|Board Vote]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p009_c0006|Winter 2025]] [[cite:queens_proposed_final_plan_addendum_2024#p009_c0008|Two phases aligned with regular seasonal service changes]].
