The [[route:route_b20-linden-blvd-apr2026|B20]] is a local bus route in Brooklyn that operates on the Linden Blvd corridor between Fountain Avenue and Conduit Avenue. It is shown as a green route on the proposed service map alongside the B13, B14, B15, and Q8 routes [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p020_c0003|B20 (green) on route map]].

The B20 is one of six bus routes serving the 1.1-mile corridor, which carries [[metric:metric_60000-daily-riders|60,000 daily riders]] across Brooklyn and Queens [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p004_c0002|corridor overview]]. Under the proposed Linden Blvd Traffic Safety and Bus Priority Improvements presented to Brooklyn Community Board 5 in April 2026, the B20 would travel in the main roadway instead of the service road, with new boarding island bus stops at Euclid Avenue, Crescent Street, Eldert Lane, and 79 Street [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p020_c0002|proposed routing and stops]]. The B20's routing would otherwise remain unchanged [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p020_c0002|routing unchanged]].

The corridor is a designated Vision Zero Priority Corridor with severe safety and mobility challenges: the roadway is [[metric:metric_road-width-140-ft|Road width 140 feet]] with [[metric:metric_10-lanes-corridor|10 travel lanes]], crosswalks up to [[metric:metric_crosswalk-spacing-1200-ft|Crosswalks up to 1,200 feet apart]], and bus speeds as low as [[metric:metric_bus-speed-4-mph|Bus speeds as low as 4 mph]] due to traffic congestion and double parking [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p009_c0002|existing conditions]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p004_c0002|bus speeds]]. Between 2019 and 2023, the corridor recorded [[metric:metric_safety-total-injuries-330|330 total injuries (2019-2023)]], [[metric:metric_safety-severe-injuries-13_2|13 severely injured (2019-2023)]], and [[metric:metric_safety-fatalities-2|2 killed (2019-2023)]], including [[metric:metric_safety-pedestrian-injuries-41|41 pedestrian injuries (2019-2023)]] and [[metric:metric_safety-pedestrian-fatalities-1|1 pedestrian killed (2019-2023)]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p011_c0002|safety record]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p011_c0005|safety breakdown by mode]].

Of [[metric:metric_34000-residents|34,000 residents near corridor]] near the corridor, [[metric:metric_households-no-car-57-pct|57% of households no vehicle]] and [[metric:metric_public-transit-commute-54-pct|54% commute via public transit]], with an average travel time of [[metric:metric_avg-travel-time-46-min|46-minute average travel time to work]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0002|demographics]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0005|vehicle access]] [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p010_c0003|commute mode]]. Implementation is planned in phases: Spring 2026 for proposal refinement based on community feedback, late summer/fall 2026 for concrete construction, and spring/summer 2027 for repaving, final roadway markings, and signage [[cite:linden_blvd_fountain_ave_conduit_ave_bkcb5_apr2026#p024_c0002|project timeline]].
