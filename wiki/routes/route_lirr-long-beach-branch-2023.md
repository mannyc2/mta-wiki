The Long Beach Branch is a LIRR rail branch between Valley Stream and Long Beach where scheduled track maintenance occurred in February 2023 [[cite:meeting_doc_104751#p001_c0004|LIRR plan to adjust schedules for track maintenance on Long Beach Branch]]. On Saturday, February 11, 2023, both main tracks between Valley Stream and Long Beach were taken out of service for approximately 24 hours while track maintenance was performed, with bus service replacing trains in both directions [[cite:meeting_doc_104751#p001_c0007|Track Maintenance – Long Beach Branch February 11 bus replacement]].

In the LIRR Annual Operating Report 2023, the Long Beach Branch recorded an overall on-time performance of 95.9% for 2023, compared to 92.3% in 2022, against an annual goal of 97.0% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Performance Summary - Long Beach Branch OTP 2023]]. By comparison, the system-wide [[metric:metric_lirr-otp-overall-2023|LIRR Overall On Time Performance 2023]] was 94.0%, against an annual goal of [[metric:metric_lirr-otp-goal-2023|LIRR Annual Goal OTP 2023]] of 95.8% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|System Overall OTP 2023]]. Branch-specific peak performance was lower, with AM Peak at 91.3% and PM Peak at 91.7%, while weekend service achieved 96.2% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Long Beach Branch OTP by period 2023]].

The branch's elevator availability was 99.3% in 2023, down slightly from 99.5% in 2022, while escalator availability was 97.2% in 2023 compared to 97.4% in 2022 [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0004|Elevator Availability - Long Beach Branch 2023]][[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0005|Escalator Availability - Long Beach Branch 2023]]. Both measures were near the system-wide averages of [[metric:metric_lirr-elevator-overall-2023|Overall Elevator Availability 2023]] at 99.1% and [[metric:metric_lirr-escalator-overall-2023|Overall Escalator Availability 2023]] at 95.7% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0004|Overall elevator average 2023]][[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0005|Overall escalator average 2023]].

The Annual Operating Report 2023 does not include branch-level breakdowns for delay categories, mean distance between failures, or trip-completion statistics for the Long Beach Branch; those metrics are reported at the system level only [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p005_c0002|System Categories of Delay table - no branch breakdown]][[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p006_c0004|MDBF table - fleet-level only]].
