The [[route:route_lirr-oyster-bay-branch-2023|Oyster Bay Branch]] is an LIRR branch line whose on-time performance (OTP) data appears in the MTA Long Island Rail Road's 2023 Annual Operating Report [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p001_c0003|Annual Operating Report 2023]]. For the year through December 2023, the branch reported an overall OTP of 94.1%, compared to 94.3% in 2022, against an annual goal of 95.5% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|Oyster Bay Branch OTP table]]. Peak-period performance was notably lower than the overall figure: the AM peak registered 92.5% and the PM peak 91.7%, while off-peak weekday service achieved 95.2% and weekend service 95.1% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|Oyster Bay Branch OTP table]]. By comparison, the LIRR system-wide [[metric:metric_lirr-otp-overall-2023|overall OTP]] for 2023 was 94.0%, with an annual goal of 95.8% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Performance Summary table]]. The same source page also records system-wide operating statistics for 2023: 303,519 trains scheduled, 2,465 trains arriving over 15 minutes late, 742 trains canceled, and a 99.6% rate of scheduled trips completed [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|Operating Statistics section]]. Branch-specific elevator and escalator availability figures are not listed separately for the Oyster Bay Branch in the elevator/escalator tables on page 7 of the report [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0004|Elevator Availability table]] [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0005|Escalator Availability table]].
