The [[route:route_q51-queens|Q51 bus route]] is a proposed brand-new east-west route in southeast Queens that would connect Southeast Queens to East New York, Brooklyn [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p008_c0003|Brand new route connecting Southeast Queens and East New York, Brooklyn]]. It is described as having fewer stops for faster service [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p008_c0003|Fewer stops for faster service]]. The Q51 would serve Tier 1 and Tier 2 equity areas as defined in the Queens Bus Network Redesign equity framework, and would facilitate connections with numerous existing bus routes and the A train at Rockaway Boulevard [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|Q51 serves Tier 1 and 2 equity areas, connects with A train]]. This would eliminate the need for passengers to travel to downtown Jamaica for transfers, providing enhanced cross-borough access [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|Eliminates need to travel to downtown Jamaica for transfers]]. The route's service type is classified as Crosstown [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|New east-west route in southeast Queens]].

In the redesign materials, the Q51 is presented as a new route introduced to address network gaps, particularly for southeast Queens riders who would otherwise rely on the Jamaica transfer hub for some connections [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|Q51 is a new east-west route introduced to address network gaps]]. As a proposed new route, the Q51 is part of the redesign's broader approach of adding connections where the existing network left transfer or coverage gaps [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|Another approach the Redesign used to address gaps in the network includes the introduction of new routes]].

The packet documents the Q51 as a planning proposal connecting Southeast Queens and East New York, Brooklyn; it does not by itself establish final implementation status [[cite:meeting_doc_87961#p008_c0003|Brand new route connecting Southeast Queens and East New York, Brooklyn]] [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p014_c0007|Q51 route proposal context]].
