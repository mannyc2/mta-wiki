The [[route:route_qm2-201110-qbb|QM2 Express Bus Route]] operates as an express bus in Queens, with a recorded daily ridership of 1,160 boardings on the Queensboro Bridge (QBB) as documented by the New York City Department of Transportation's Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|QM2 daily ridership 1,160]]. The QM2 was one of eighteen express routes surveyed in that study, which together carried approximately 7,920 express riders daily across the bridge [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|express bus total 7,920]].

The study found that congestion on the QBB is a significant cause of bus delay, with over 75% of express and local bus riders reporting that traffic congestion regularly delays their bus [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p004_c0003|over 75% of riders report congestion delay]]. Across all bus services, the Queensboro Bridge carries roughly 16,000 bus trips per day including about 8,000 local riders, with 110 buses crossing during the PM rush hour—approximately one bus every 30 seconds [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p004_c0003|QBB bus corridor statistics]].

In a rider survey conducted for the study, [[metric:metric_express-rider-survey-respondents|511 express bus riders responded]], of whom [[metric:metric_express-rider-work-trip-pct|88% reported traveling to or from work]] and [[metric:metric_express-rider-daily-bus-pct|81% said they ride the bus daily]] [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|express rider survey demographics]]. Among those express riders who perceived delay on the bridge, 62% reported an average delay of 5 to 15 minutes [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|express rider 5-15 minute delay]]. When asked for suggested improvements to trip speed, express riders most frequently cited bus lanes, enforcement of bus lanes, and routing suggestions [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|express rider suggested improvements]]. The [[metric:metric_express-bus-total-daily-ridership|total daily express bus ridership on the QBB]] across all routes was 7,920 [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|express bus total 7,920]], while the combined local bus routes (Q60, Q101, Q32) carried approximately 7,900 daily riders [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p007_c0002|local bus total 7,900]].
