The [[route:route_bm4-church-ave-cb12|BM4]] is an express bus route that provides direct service along Ocean Avenue between Brooklyn and Midtown & Downtown Manhattan, as documented in NYC DOT's 2019 Church Ave Transit & Traffic Improvements presentations to Community Boards 12 and 14 [[cite:church_ave_cb12_jun2019#p007_c0002|BM3 & BM4 express buses]]. The route is listed alongside the BM3 express bus and the B35 and B103 local/limited routes in materials describing current service on the Church Ave corridor [[cite:church_ave_project_update_oct2019#p007_c0002|BM3 & BM4 express buses]].

The Church Ave corridor (Flatbush Ave to Ocean Parkway) was identified by NYC DOT as a 2019 priority project due to slow bus speeds of 4.25 mph during peak (compared to a 6.7 mph Brooklyn average) and high combined ridership of 45,000 daily riders across all routes [[cite:church_ave_public_forum_jul2019#p005_c0002|Church Ave 2019 priority project]]. Along this corridor, bus passengers make up the dominant traveler share during both the AM and PM peaks [[metric:metric_bus-passengers-72-percent-am-peak|AM peak bus traveler share]] [[metric:metric_bus-passengers-69-percent-pm-peak|PM peak bus traveler share]] [[cite:church_ave_public_forum_jul2019#p009_c0002|Bus passengers 72% AM, 69% PM peak]]. The BM4 contributes to this ridership as one of the express bus options serving the corridor via Ocean Avenue [[cite:church_ave_cb12_jun2019#p007_c0002|BM3 & BM4 express buses]].

The treatment components proposed for the Church Ave project — curbside bus lanes, loading zone adjustments, and metered parking changes — focused on the local B35 route rather than the BM4, but the project materials consistently reference the BM4 as part of the existing transit network along the corridor [[cite:church_ave_project_update_oct2019#p028_c0002|Curbside bus lanes]].
