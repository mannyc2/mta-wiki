The [[route:route_bx39-soundview|Bx39]] is a Bronx bus route that was identified by the Bronx Bus Network Redesign as operating on congested Soundview corridors including Story Avenue, Bronx River Avenue, and White Plains Road [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]]. In December 2021, NYC DOT completed the installation of [[metric:metric_three-miles-bus-lanes|three miles]] of new dedicated bus lanes along these corridors to serve the Bx39, Bx5, and Bx36 routes, benefiting [[metric:metric_45000-daily-riders-served|metric_45000_daily_riders_served]] [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]]. Before the conversion, buses on these streets averaged [[metric:metric_peak-speed-5-mph-before|peak-period speeds of about 5 MPH]], with [[metric:metric_daytime-speed-5-mph-before|daytime speeds also as low as 5 MPH]] [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]].

The project included curbside bus lanes on Story Avenue and White Plains Road (effective weekdays 7 AM–7 PM), a full-time bus lane on Bronx River Avenue, transit signal priority, painted curb extensions at five intersections, updated parking regulations, and angled parking on adjacent side streets [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]]. Enforcement is conducted by the NYPD and DOT automated cameras, with a 60-day warning period followed by fines [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]].

This Soundview project contributed to the [[metric:metric_seven-bus-lane-miles-bronx-since-2020|seven bus lane miles added in the Bronx since 2020]], part of citywide bus priority efforts serving [[metric:metric_half-million-daily-riders-citywide|over half a million daily riders]] [[cite:soundview_bus_priority_press_release_2021#p001_b0001|press release]].
