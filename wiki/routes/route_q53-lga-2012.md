The Q53 was identified by workshop participants as a potential route for future BRT access to LaGuardia Airport, listed as running "from Rockaway Park to LGA" [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p003_c0006|Q53 from Rockaway Park to LGA]]. This suggestion emerged during the second public meeting of the LaGuardia Airport Access Alternatives Analysis, held on November 2, 2011, where BRT had already been selected as the locally preferred mode [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p001_c0013|BRT selected as locally preferred mode]]. The workshop drew [[metric:metric_workshop-23-participants|23 workshop participants]] across four small groups and collected [[metric:metric_survey-18-returned|18 surveys returned]] [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p001_c0005|23 participants]], [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p006_c0008|18 surveys returned]]. In rider preference voting, participants showed strong interest in a [[metric:metric_rider-pref-35min-dedicated-lane-16|Rider Preference: 35 minute ride in dedicated lane]] and [[metric:metric_rider-pref-express-bus-16|express bus service]], each receiving 16 votes [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p005_c0006|rider preference table]]. Other participant-identified routes in the same exercise included the existing Q48 and Q72, along with proposed BRT corridors from Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Avenue and Citi Field [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p003_c0006|participant-identified routes list]].
