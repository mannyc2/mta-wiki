The [[route:route_sim23-madison-ave-cb6-jun2025|SIM23 Express Bus]] is an NYCT-operated express bus route serving Staten Island between Annadale (Arden Avenue) and Midtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p003_c0004|SIM23 (NYCT): Staten Island: Annadale-Arden Av-Midtown]]. Starting in 2022, the MTA took over operation of the SIM23 and the SIM24 routes, bringing riders the same features available to other Staten Island express bus riders; the two routes together transport about 300 passengers daily to and from Midtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_79331#p100_c0005|MTA took over SIM23 and SIM24 in 2022]].

The SIM23 is one of 29 express bus routes serving Madison Avenue between East 23rd and East 42nd Street, and is represented as a southbound route beginning at 34th Street and continuing south to Madison Square Park on route diagrams [[cite:madison_ave_e23_st_e42_st_cb6_jun2025#p009_c0003|SIM23 shown as southbound route on Madison Avenue]]. As one of the six express bus routes with the highest single-hour utilization on an average weekday, the SIM23 was selected for additional service in Summer 2024 as part of the MTA's response to anticipated congestion pricing demand, adding one peak-period, peak-direction trip in both the AM and PM peaks [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0009|Add trips to SIM23 and five other express routes]][[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p003_c0004|SIM23: 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks]].

The Spring 2025 bus service enhancements continued these service levels on the SIM23 [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0005|SIM23 Spring 2025: 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks]]. The total annualized operating cost increase of the Spring 2025 bus service enhancement package is approximately $8 million across express and local routes [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-total-annual-cost|Annualized operating cost increase - total]]. Approximately $5.1 million per year is for NYCT bus routes, including $496,000 for the SIM23 and SIM24 funded by the City of New York [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-nyct-annual-cost|Annual operating cost - NYCT bus routes portion]][[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0010|$496,000 for SIM23 and SIM24 funded by the City of New York]]. The first-year cost includes approximately $5 million to rehabilitate and extend the life of 29 local buses [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-first-year-bus-rehab|First year cost - rehabilitate 29 local buses]][[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|$5 million first-year rehabilitation cost]].
