[[route:route_sim11-lex-ave-oct2025|SIM11]] is one of eight MTA bus routes that travel on Lexington Avenue between 60th Street and 52nd Street in Manhattan, listed alongside BxM1, M101, M102, M103, SIM22, SIM26, and SIM6 [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p004_c0002|8 MTA bus routes on Lexington Ave between 60th and 52nd]]. These eight routes carry approximately 71,000 riders daily on this segment, where the average bus speed is 6 mph overall and 5 mph during daytime hours, and the existing curbside bus lane is often blocked by loading and passenger pick-up/drop-off activity [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p004_c0002|71,000 riders, 6 mph avg speed, blocked bus lane]].

In the past five years, 88 injuries occurred in the project area, including [[metric:metric_6-severe-injuries-lex-ave|6 severe injuries]] [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p004_c0002|88 injuries, 6 severe]]. On an average weekday, close to 6,000 daily riders travel on this slow 8-block segment, with about [[metric:metric_1900-boarding-lex-ave-60th-52nd|1,900 riders boarding]] and [[metric:metric_1800-alighting-lex-ave-60th-52nd|1,800 riders alighting]] [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p006_c0002|6,000 daily riders, 1,900 boarding, 1,800 alighting]]. NYC DOT has proposed upgrading the existing curbside bus lane to a 24/7 offset bus lane, following a design similar to the offset lane implemented on Lexington Avenue between 96th and 60th Streets in 2019, which resulted in bus speed increases of up to 26% and a 35% reduction in pedestrian injuries [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p007_c0002|offset bus lane proposal with 96th-60th reference]]. The project is a joint effort between NYC DOT and MTA, with enforcement through bus-mounted cameras under the MTA Automated Camera Enforcement Program [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p009_c0003|offset bus lane enforcement by camera]].

Presentations were made to Community Board 5 on September 15, 2025, Community Board 6 on October 6, 2025, and Community Board 8 on October 8, 2025 [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_sept2025#p001_c0002|CB5 presentation September 15, 2025]] [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p001_c0003|CB6 presentation October 6, 2025]] [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_cb8_oct2025#p001_c0003|CB8 presentation October 8, 2025]]. Implementation is targeted for Fall 2025 or Spring 2026, with curb regulations to be refined in 2026 after monitoring [[cite:lexington_ave_60_st_52_st_oct2025#p012_c0003|Fall 2025/Spring 2026 implementation, curb regulations refine 2026]].
