The [[route:route_b82e-express-cb18-jun2017|B82 Express (B82E)]] is documented as an existing express bus service operating on Flatlands Avenue from East 103 Street to Pennsylvania Avenue in Brooklyn [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p037_c0001|Existing: Flatlands Ave (E 103 St to Pennsylvania Ave)]]. Along this portion of Flatlands Avenue, three city bus routes and one express bus route share the roadway, which features a wider cross-section with concrete medians [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p037_c0002|3 city bus routes and 1 express bus route]]. The corridor also provides a transit connection from Pennsylvania Avenue and Starrett City to the Canarsie-Rockaway Parkway L train station [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p037_c0002|Transit connection from Pennsylvania Ave/Starrett City to L Canarsie-Rockaway Pkwy station]]. A bus stop signpost at Flatlands Avenue and East 105 Street displays signage for B82E alongside B82, B103E, and BM2 routes, confirming the express service's presence at that location [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p037_c0004|a purple sign for B82E]].

The B82E appears in the source as part of the existing conditions documentation for the B82 Southern Brooklyn Select Bus Service project, presented to Community Board 18 on June 21, 2017 [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p001_c0002|June 21, 2017 – Community Board 18]]. The B82 Limited and Local routes together served approximately [[metric:metric_ridership-32000-b82-cb18-jun2017|B82 daily ridership - 32,000]] at the time [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p003_c0003|Serves 32,000 daily riders on B82 Limited and Local]]. Proposed Select Bus Service improvements along the corridor—including bus lanes, intersection redesigns, and re-routing via Glenwood Road and Avenue K [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82_cb18_jun2017#p012_c0002|Proposed re-route via Glenwood Rd streamlines existing routing]]—were designed to serve both B82 Limited and B82 Local riders, with the B82E operating as a separate express service on the wider portion of Flatlands Avenue.
