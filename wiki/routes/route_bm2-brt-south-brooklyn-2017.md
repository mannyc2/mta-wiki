The [[route:route_bm2-brt-south-brooklyn-2017|BM2 Express]] is an MTA Bus Company-operated express route running between Canarsie-Avenue H in Brooklyn and Midtown/Downtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0005|BM2 service description]]. Along Pennsylvania Ave, the BM2 is one of five bus routes (B82 Local, B82 Limited, B83, BM2, BM5) serving a corridor with slow bus speeds, long pedestrian crossings, and narrow concrete medians [[cite:brt_south_brooklyn_b82#p020_c0002|Pennsylvania Ave corridor conditions]]. The BM2 is also a secondary route on Flatbush Ave, providing connections to midtown Manhattan alongside the B41, B9, B67, B103, and Q35 [[cite:flatbush_ave_cb9_sept2022#p011_c0002|Flatbush Ave secondary routes]].

In Summer 2024, the BM2 was among six express bus routes selected for frequency increases in anticipation of Congestion Pricing, with one Manhattan-bound trip added in the AM peak at an annualized cost of $254,000 for MTA Bus routes [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0009|BM2 summer 2024 trip addition]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0011|MTA Bus cost detail]]. In Spring 2025, under a broader congestion-pricing service enhancement package, the BM2 again received one additional Manhattan-bound AM peak trip as part of frequency increases on eight express bus routes [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0005|BM2 spring 2025 trip addition]]. That package carries an [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-total-annual-cost|annualized operating cost increase of approximately $8 million]] total, including roughly [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-mtabus-annual-cost|$2.9 million per year for MTA Bus Company routes]], and a first-year cost of about [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-first-year-bus-rehab|$5 million to rehabilitate 29 local buses]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|total annualized cost, first-year bus rehab]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0010|MTA Bus Company share]].
