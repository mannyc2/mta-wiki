The [[route:route_qm12-qbb-study|QM12 Express Bus Route]] was included in the Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study conducted by NYC DOT and presented to local Community Boards in spring-summer 2010 [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p001_c0002|The Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study]]. The study documented a daily ridership of 220 passengers on the QM12, contributing to the total of 7,920 daily express bus riders on all routes using the Queensboro Bridge [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Bus Routes table]] — a figure that can be related to the [[metric:metric_express-bus-total-daily-ridership|Express Bus Total Daily Ridership on QBB]] metric. A map of the Third Avenue and 57th Street intersection shows a bus stop serving QM12 together with QM4, QM10, and QM24 [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p010_c0005|Third Avenue at 57th Street map]].

The study identified five focus areas, including Manhattan-side locations near 60th Street and 2nd Avenue (bus safety) and 57th Street and 3rd Avenue (bus priority opportunity) [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p005_c0003|Study Focus Areas]]. The study schedule ran from spring 2010 data collection through fall 2011 implementation of short-term recommendations [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p004_c0003|Spring 2010 data collection]] [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p004_c0012|Fall 2011 implementation]], with Recommendations #1-4 implemented in fall 2011 [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p017_c0004|Recommendations #1-4 implementation]]. Recommendation #5, which examined express bus lane options on the Thomson Avenue ramps, remained pending additional analysis by NYC DOT [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p017_c0006|Recommendation #5]].

The QM12 operates between Manhattan and Queens as express bus service [[cite:201110_qbb_summary_recommendations#p010_c0005|Third Avenue at 57th Street map]] and was one of several QM-series express routes evaluated in the study, along with QM1, QM1A, QM2, QM2A, QM3, QM4, QM10, QM15, QM16, QM17, QM18, QM21, and QM24 [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Bus Routes table]].
