The X28 express bus is included among the routes served by the Bay Parkway and Cropsey Avenue Bus Priority and Safety Improvements draft proposal, presented to Brooklyn Community Board 11 on June 16, 2025 [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p001_c0002|CB 11 Transportation Committee]] [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p001_c0003|June 16, 2025]]. The [[route:route_x28-cb11-jun2025|X28]] and X38 express buses operate on these corridors alongside the B6 Local and Limited and B82 Local and Select Bus Service, serving [[metric:metric_35k-daily-bus-riders|35,000 daily bus riders]] [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p004_c0002|study area summary]]. The study area covers Bay Parkway from Avenue J to Shore Parkway (2.4 miles) and Cropsey Avenue from Bay Parkway to 26th Avenue (0.6 miles), with connections to F, N, and D trains [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p004_c0002|study area summary]].

A photo in the proposal captures an X28 express bus on Cropsey Avenue at 26th Avenue behind a school bus and truck, illustrating congestion along the corridor [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p005_c0004|Cropsey Avenue photo caption]]. Bus speeds on Bay Parkway are as slow as [[metric:metric_bus-speed-5-mph-throughout-day|Bus speeds 5 mph throughout day on Bay Parkway]] and [[metric:metric_bus-speed-3-mph-busiest-hours|Bus speeds 3 mph during busiest hours on Bay Parkway]], with double parking as a significant contributor [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p022_c0002|bus speed description]].

The corridor is a Vision Zero Priority Corridor, with [[metric:metric_ksi-22-2020-2024|22 people killed or seriously injured 2020-2024]] and [[metric:metric_2-more-deaths-2025|2 more deaths in 2025]] [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p004_c0002|safety summary]]. Overall [[metric:metric_517-injuries-bay-pkwy-2020-2024|517 people injured in crashes on Bay Parkway 2020-2024]] on Bay Parkway during 2020-2024, including [[metric:metric_20-severe-injuries-bay-pkwy-2020-2024|20 severely injured on Bay Parkway 2020-2024]] and [[metric:metric_2-fatalities-bay-pkwy-2020-2024|2 fatalities on Bay Parkway 2020-2024]] [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p023_c0004|injury table]]. The draft proposal includes repurposing one of two general travel lanes for an offset bus lane on Bay Parkway, with implementation proposed for late 2025 or 2026 [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p009_c0002|offset bus lane proposal]] [[cite:bay_pkwy_cropsey_ave_cb11_jun2025#p017_c0008|proposed implementation timeline]].
