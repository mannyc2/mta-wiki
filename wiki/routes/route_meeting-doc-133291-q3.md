The Q3 is a local bus route in Queens. A February 2024 MTA staff summary proposes modifications to the weekday schedule of the [[route:route_meeting-doc-133291-q3|Q3]], along with the B82, Bx19, and Q29 [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0006|Service Changes: Spring 2024 Bus Schedules]]. Under the proposal, the frequency of service on the Q3 would decrease and running time would increase [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p002_c0010|Staff Summary discussion]]. The Q3 is operated by New York City Transit and, together with the B82 and Bx19, the combined cost impact is $89,400 [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0010|Budget Impact]]. The proposed implementation date is April 2024 [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p001_c0012|Proposed Implementation Date]].

On the Q3, the [[metric:metric_q3-am-peak-headway-current|Q3 AM Peak headway current - 8 min]] would improve to [[metric:metric_q3-am-peak-headway-proposed|Q3 AM Peak headway proposed - 7 min]], while the [[metric:metric_q3-am-peak-capacity-current|Q3 AM Peak capacity current - 75%]] would decrease to [[metric:metric_q3-am-peak-capacity-proposed|Q3 AM Peak capacity proposed - 67%]] [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0003|Attachment 1 schedule table]]. Midday, PM Peak, and Evening headways and capacity percentages would remain unchanged [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0003|Attachment 1 schedule table]]. The Q29, noted as having running time changes only, shows dashes across all table cells, indicating no headway or capacity changes in the table [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0003|Attachment 1 schedule table]] [[cite:meeting_doc_133291#p004_c0005|Q29 footnote]].
