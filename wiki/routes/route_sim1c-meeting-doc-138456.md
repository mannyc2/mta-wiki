[[route:route_sim1c-meeting-doc-138456|SIM1C (NYCT)]] is a Staten Island express bus route operated by NYCT, running from Eltingville along Hylan Boulevard to Midtown/Downtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p002_c0012|route description]]. As part of the Express Bus Service Additions package proposed alongside the planned June 2024 launch of Congestion Pricing, the MTA identified SIM1C as one of six express routes with the highest per-hour utilization and recommended adding trips to these routes [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0006|ample capacity context]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0009|recommendation]]. For SIM1C, the proposal added [[metric:metric_sim1c-1-trip-afternoon-manhattan|1 Manhattan-bound trip in the weekday afternoon]], with implementation targeted for June 2024 [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0013|June 2024 implementation]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p002_c0012|trip addition details]]. The full package's [[metric:metric_cost-total-annual-883k|total annual operating cost increase]] was approximately $883,000 per year, with $629,000 on NYCT routes (including $496,000 for SIM23/SIM24 funded by the City of New York) and $254,000 on MTA Bus routes, funded through the Outer Borough Transportation Account [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0011|cost breakdown]]. The MTA committed to monitoring ridership on these routes after the planned tolling start to evaluate whether the added service was justified [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0007|ridership monitoring]]. The same SIM1C trip addition was later listed again in a subsequent Spring 2025 bus service enhancement document covering eight express bus routes [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0005|Spring 2025 listing]]. Added trips were subject to regular ridership review to assess whether they should continue [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p003_c0006|regular evaluation]].
