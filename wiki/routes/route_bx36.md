The [[route:route_bx36|BX36]] is a local bus route in the Bronx, designated as item "BX36" in the ACE routes dataset [[cite:ace_routes_dataset_dictionary#p001_b0049|ACE routes dataset]]. It operates on Tremont Avenue from University Avenue to White Plains Road and is the 5th busiest route in the Bronx, carrying approximately 34,000 bus riders per day [[cite:tremont_ave_bus_priority_cb5_nov2024#p004_c0002|Tremont Av Bus Priority CB5 Nov 2024]]. It serves as a critical east-west transit connection across the borough [[cite:tremont_ave_bus_priority_cb6_feb2025#p004_c0002|Tremont Av Bus Priority CB6 Feb 2025]].

The route is part of the MTA's Automated Bus Lane Enforcement (ABLE) program, with cameras operating on the route through at least 2023 along E Tremont Avenue and White Plains Road [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|MTA Automated Camera Enforcement]] [[cite:bus_lane_camera_report_2024#p008_c0004|Bus Lane Camera Report 2024]]. The BX36 serves over 39,000 daily riders according to the Tremont Avenue Busway project page, which aims to increase bus speeds and reliability for these riders [[cite:busway_tremontavenue#p001_b0001|Tremont Avenue Busway]].

At E Tremont Avenue and Webster Avenue, the BX36 provides a transfer point to the Bx41, as documented during the Webster Avenue Select Bus Service community planning process [[cite:2012_10_brt_webster_cac3_meeting_summary#p002_c0006|Webster SBS CAC3 Meeting Summary]]. The route also serves the University Avenue corridor, where combined weekday ridership across all routes (Bx3, Bx18, Bx36) reaches approximately 39,000 [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_2019#p031_c0004|Bronx Bus Network Final Plan 2019]] [[cite:bx_cb5_projects_dec032019#p013_c0003|2020 Bus Priority Improvements CB5]].

BX36 bus stops were proposed for relocation to Valentine Avenue as part of the Webster Avenue SBS project design [[cite:2013_03_sbs_webster_bx_cb5#p033_c0003|Webster SBS CB5 2013]]. A June 2022 Tremont Avenue bus priority project straightened BX36 routing so that it serves Tremont Avenue from University Avenue to White Plains Road, while Bx40/Bx42 serves Tremont Avenue east of Rosedale Avenue; the routes no longer switch streets at Webster Avenue [[cite:tremont_ave_bus_priority_cb6_feb2024#p006_c0002|Tremont Av Bus Priority CB6 Feb 2024]]. The simplified routing has been associated with reduced travel times [[cite:meeting_doc_98311#p005_c0008|Meeting Doc 98311]].
