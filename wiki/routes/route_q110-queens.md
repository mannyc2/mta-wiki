The Q110 bus route in Southeast Queens is described in the Queens Bus Network Redesign equity evaluation as a route that has been streamlined for more direct routing [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|More Direct Routing]]. The existing Q110 connects passengers from Parsons Boulevard in Jamaica to Belmont Park [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|Q110 existing routing]]. Under the Proposed Final Plan Addendum, the Q110 is rerouted and extended to Floral Park, offering a more direct path and expanded coverage [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|Q110 redesign proposal]]. This change would add a connection to the Queens Village LIRR station [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|Queens Village LIRR connection]]. The redesign serves an area where 94% of the population is non-white and 40% is transit dependent, and is intended to enhance travel efficiency in this Southeast Queens corridor [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|Southeast Queens demographics]]. Passengers on the discontinued portion of Hempstead Avenue would be served by the new Q82 route, ensuring continuity of service [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|Q82 replacement service]].
