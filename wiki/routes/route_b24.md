[[route:route_b24|B24]] is a Williamsburg Bridge Plaza-bound local bus route serving Brooklyn along the Marcy Avenue corridor. Its average daily ridership was 2,359 in 2024 [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|CB1 June 2025 presentation]]. The B24 is one of three bus routes — along with the B60 and Q54 — that converge on Marcy Av between Borinquen Pl and Broadway, where each route experiences delays and slower speeds along this tail-end stretch; the delays compound and result in buses arriving late to their terminal, delaying the next scheduled run [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|CB1 June 2025 presentation]]. The BQE North and South (2024) study recommended providing a bus priority lane to alleviate these delays [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p002_c0002|CB1 June 2025 presentation]].

Traffic congestion along this segment is common, especially on weekday late afternoons before the PM rush hour lane along the east curb goes into effect [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p003_c0008|CB1 June 2025 presentation]]. Buses must also navigate a very tight right turn when continuing from southbound Marcy Av onto westbound Broadway, competing for space with other turning vehicles and improperly stopped traffic on the eastbound side [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p005_c0004|CB1 June 2025 presentation]]. On the west side of Marcy Av between S 4 St and S 5 St, multiple vehicles load and perform deliveries daily while occupying the bus stop for extended periods [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p004_c0008|CB1 June 2025 presentation]].

A Bus Priority Street Improvement Project (SIP ID: 12263) was presented to Community Board 1's Transportation Committee in June 2025 and re-presented with a detailed design in September 2025 [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_jun2025#p001_c0002|CB1 June 2025 presentation]]. The September 2025 design proposes a bus priority lane on Marcy Av from Borinquen Pl to Broadway (all hours), a travel lane widened to facilitate safer turns for trucks and buses, the bus lane merging into the general travel lane, a new Neighborhood Loading Zone, and a part-time parking lane replaced with a full-time general travel lane [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_sept2025#p007_c0003|CB1 September 2025 pavement markings drawing]] [[cite:ace_able_pdf_marcy_ave_borinquen_pl_broadway_cb1_sept2025#p011_c0002|CB1 September 2025 treatment summary]].
