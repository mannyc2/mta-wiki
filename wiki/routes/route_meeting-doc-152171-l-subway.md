The [[route:route_meeting-doc-152171-l-subway|L Subway Line]] runs along 14th Street in Manhattan and through Brooklyn, and appeared in the September 25, 2024 MTA Board Meeting as one of the subway lines receiving station upgrades at 14 St [[cite:meeting_doc_152171#p005_c0006|14 St F/M/L Upgrades]]. The meeting also presented a public service announcement about using mass transit during UN Week gridlock September 22-29 [[cite:meeting_doc_152171#p011_c0002|UN WEEK SEPT 22-29 GRIDLOCK DELAYS USE MASS TRANSIT]].

NYCT Operations Planning, under Acting Chief Sarah Wyss, proposed Fall 2025 schedule changes to the L weekday, Saturday, and Sunday timetables, with implementation planned for November 2025 [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p001_c0005|Subway schedules are regularly reviewed]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p001_c0011|proposed minor subway schedule changes would be implemented in November 2025]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p001_c0003|Sarah Wyss, Acting Chief, Operations Planning, NYCT]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p004_c0003|Summary of Proposed A and L Weekday Scheduled Trip Changes as of November 2025]].

Weekday AM peak service would increase by four additional round trips, adding two trains at the peak to raise service from 20 to 22 trains between approximately 8 a.m. and 9 a.m., enabled by traction power upgrades [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p002_c0012|Weekday AM peak period service would be increased by four additional round trips total]]. The weekday PM peak would shift the span of 4-minute headways about 30 minutes earlier [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p003_c0003|In the weekday PM peak period, we are proposing to shift the span of 4-minute headways approximately 30 minutes earlier]]. Altogether, weekday service adds four round trips and one additional crew [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p003_c0004|Weekday service on the L would be increased by a total of four round trips, and one additional crew would be required]].

On Saturdays, four trips shift from early morning to late evening, extending 8-minute headways through the 11 p.m. hour while maintaining 8-minute-or-better headways in the morning [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p003_c0003|On Saturdays, we are proposing to shift four trips from the early morning to late evening hours]]. On Sundays, peak 4-minute service shifts two hours earlier, reaching the 4-minute headway by the 11 a.m. hour [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p003_c0003|On Sundays, we are proposing to shift the span of peak 4-minute service two hours earlier]]. There is no net change in total daily trips on either weekend day, and weekend adjustments are cost neutral [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p003_c0004|There is no net change in the total number of daily trips on either the Saturday or Sunday L timetables]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p001_c0009|The weekend schedule adjustments are cost neutral]]. The weekday adjustments have an estimated annual cost of $600,000 [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p001_c0009|The weekday adjustments have an estimated annual cost of $600,000]].

The proposed headways and guideline load metrics are detailed in a table. For the L Northbound at Bedford Av, [[metric:metric_md176411-l-nb-8am-current-headway|current headway]] is 3 minutes with a proposed 2.75 minutes [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p004_c0004|L Northbound Bedford Av 8:00 AM 3 2.75]], and [[metric:metric_md176411-l-nb-ampeak-current-headway|AM Peak current headway]] is 3.75 minutes proposed at 3.5 minutes [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p004_c0004|AM Peak* 3.75 3.5]]. Saturday Northbound at Bedford Av shows a 7:00 AM [[metric:metric_md176411-l-sat-nb-7am-current-headway|current headway]] of 6 minutes proposed to 7.5 minutes (reducing trips) and an 11:00 PM [[metric:metric_md176411-l-sat-nb-11pm-current-headway|current headway]] of 12 minutes proposed to 7.5 minutes (adding trips), with guideline load at 11:00 PM dropping from 128% to 80% [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p004_c0008|L Northbound Bedford Av ... 11:00 PM 12 7.5 3 128% 80%]]. Peak period guideline load is 145 passengers per car; off-peak is 53 passengers per car [[cite:meeting_doc_176411#p004_c0006|Peak period guideline load is 145 passengers/car. Off-peak guideline load is 53 passengers/car]].
