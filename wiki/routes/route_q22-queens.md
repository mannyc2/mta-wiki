The [[route:route_q22-queens|Q22 bus route]] is a local bus route in Queens, operating along Rockaway Beach Boulevard in the Rockaways [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p012_c0004|Queens Bus Network Redesign Equity Evaluation]]. It is one of five bus routes serving various sections of Rockaway Beach Boulevard, along with Q52 SBS, Q53 SBS, QM16, and QM17 [[cite:rockaway_beach_blvd_jun2019#p010_c0005|Rockaway Beach Boulevard Improvements]].

Under the Queens Bus Network Redesign, the Q22 is proposed to be truncated at Beach 116 St on its western end, with the Q35 realigned to serve the segment of Rockaway Beach Boulevard west of Beach 116 St due to low ridership on that portion [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p012_c0004|Q22 truncation rationale]]. The eastern end of the Q22 would be extended to the Far Rockaway LIRR station, providing Rockaways residents with a new connection to the Long Island Rail Road via a discounted LIRR Far Rockaway Ticket [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p012_c0005|Q22 extension to Far Rockaway LIRR]]. The portion of the existing Q22 that would remain primarily serves communities within Equity Tier 1 and 2 areas [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p012_c0004|Equity Tier coverage]].

Rockaway Beach Boulevard was identified as a priority project in the 2019 Better Buses Action Plan, driven by slow bus speeds, unreliable travel times, high ridership, and safety concerns raised by the community and elected officials [[cite:rockaway_beach_blvd_jun2019#p003_c0002|2019 priority project rationale]]. The Q22 is one of the routes served by the Rockaway Beach Boulevard Improvements project [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p041_c0011|Corridor routes and ridership]].
