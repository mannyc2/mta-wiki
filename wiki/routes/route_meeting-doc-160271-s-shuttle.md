The [[route:route_meeting-doc-160271-s-shuttle|S Shuttle (Rockaway Park Shuttle)]] operates between Broad Channel and Rockaway Park – Beach 116 Street in Queens, serving as a vital link on the Rockaway Line along with the A Train, which together carry over 9,000 average weekday riders [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p001_c0004|Rockaway Line weekday ridership]]. In 2012, the line suffered extensive damage from Superstorm Sandy, and A Train service was suspended for seven months; while emergency repairs restored service, the MTA determined that significant permanent work was still needed [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p001_c0004|Sandy damage description]].

The Rockaway Line Rehabilitation & Resiliency Project was described as a $393 million investment to reconstruct the elevated Hammels Wye and rehabilitate electrical and mechanical systems on the South Channel Bridge [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p001_c0005|$393M project details]]. C&D President Torres-Springer reported that fully reconstructing the 65-year-old Hammels Y was necessary to continue providing service to the Rockaway Peninsula for the next 50 years [[cite:meeting_doc_160096#p008_c0007|Hammels Y reconstruction context]]. The cited material planned a 17-week service outage beginning January 2025 and ending May 2025 to complete the work safely and cost-efficiently [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p001_c0005|17-week outage timeline]].

During this outage, the S Shuttle service between Broad Channel and Rockaway Park - Beach 116 Street was to be suspended [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p002_c0001|S Shuttle suspension]]. It was to be replaced by a special fare-free S Shuttle train serving all stations on the peninsula between Far Rockaway - Mott Avenue and Rockaway Park - Beach 116 Street [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p002_c0001|fare-free replacement shuttle]]. Separately, the MTA said LIRR would discount the [[metric:metric_lirr-far-rockaway-ticket-peak|peak Far Rockaway Ticket]] from $7 and the [[metric:metric_lirr-far-rockaway-ticket-offpeak|off-peak Far Rockaway Ticket]] from $5 to a [[metric:metric_lirr-far-rockaway-ticket-discounted|discounted Far Rockaway Ticket]] price of $2.75 for trips to and from the Far Rockaway LIRR Station, allowing A Train customers to use LIRR service as an alternative [[cite:meeting_doc_160271#p002_c0001|LIRR Far Rockaway discounted ticket]].
