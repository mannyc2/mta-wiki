The [[route:route_meeting-doc-160136-newburgh-beacon-ferry|Newburgh-Beacon Ferry]] is operated by Metro-North Railroad, using parking and ferry landing facilities in Newburgh that the City of Newburgh leases from a private landowner [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|Discussion of 2004 agreement and 250-space parking facility]]. The facility includes a [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160136-parking-spaces-250|Parking facility capacity: 250 spaces]] for ferry riders [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|250-space parking facility]].

The service's current funding structure traces to an August 2004 agreement between Metro-North and the City of Newburgh concerning mooring, docking, and facility use [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|2004 agreement]]. The City initially received rent reimbursement from NYSDOT under a separate contract, but after that contract expired Metro-North began reimbursing the City through a series of memoranda of understanding [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|NYSDOT contract expired and MOU series began]]. The first MOU, dated May 26, 2015, set a monthly lease reimbursement rate of [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160136-monthly-lease-21278-2015|Monthly lease payment rate under first MOU: $21,278]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|$21,278 per month]]. After a second new lease took effect July 1, 2019, the monthly rate rose to [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160136-monthly-lease-21916-2019|Monthly lease payment rate under Second New Lease: $21,916]], which has continued through the Seventh through Tenth MOUs and into the proposed Eleventh MOU [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0010|$21,916 per month]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0004|Seventh through Tenth MOUs at same rate]].

The Eleventh MOU, scheduled for Metro-North Committee review on December 16, 2024 and MTA Board action on December 18, 2024, would authorize Metro-North to reimburse the City for lease payments from January 1, 2025 through December 31, 2025 at the same monthly rate of $21,916 [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0009|Purpose of Eleventh MOU]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p001_c0006|Board action schedule]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0005|Eleventh MOU terms at same rate]]. The reimbursement would be funded by NYSDOT Congestion Mitigation/Air Quality (CMAQ) funds, with [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160136-cmaq-funding-263000|CMAQ funds: $263,000 annually for lease reimbursement]] committed annually upon proof of payment [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0008|CMAQ funds and $263,000 annually]]. Because Metro-North applies for reimbursement from NYSDOT as payments are made, there is zero net impact on the operating budget [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0009|Zero net operating budget impact]]. The Eleventh MOU also commits the City and Metro-North to continue identifying alternative locations and funding sources for the ferry and parking facility [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0006|Alternative location search]]. Without the Eleventh MOU, the City states it would terminate the lease, leading to interruption of ferry service while a new site is identified and developed [[cite:meeting_doc_160136#p002_c0010|Alternative: City would terminate lease]].
