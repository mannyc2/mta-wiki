The Q100 is a bus route in Queens identified in the October 2022 NYC Transit & Bus Committee performance metrics document [[cite:meeting_doc_98311#p003_c0001|October 2022 NYC Transit & Bus Committee meeting]]. New bus lanes along 21st Street in western Queens, running from Long Island City to Astoria, were planned to serve the Q100 along with the Q66 and Q69 routes, with the goal of improving service for 29,000 riders in that community [[cite:meeting_doc_98311#p005_c0002|21st Street bus lanes serving Q100]]. The Q100 is one of three routes on this corridor slated to benefit from these bus lanes, alongside the Q66 and Q69 [[cite:meeting_doc_98311#p005_c0002|Q66, Q69, and Q100 on 21st Street]]. This record is scoped to the single mention of the Q100 in the October 2022 document; no other route-level performance data such as ridership, speed, or on-time performance is reported for the Q100 individually within this source [[cite:meeting_doc_98311#p005_c0002|Q100 on 21st Street bus lanes]].
