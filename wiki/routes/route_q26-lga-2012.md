The [[route:route_q26-lga-2012|Q26]] appears in the record of the LaGuardia Airport Access Alternatives Analysis (LGA AA), a study that identified Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) as the locally preferred mode for improving airport access [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p001_c0013|BRT selected as locally preferred mode]]. During the second public meeting, held on November 2, 2011 in workshop format with [[metric:metric_workshop-23-participants|23 workshop participants]], attendees were asked to identify future BRT-based access routes to LaGuardia Airport [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p001_c0005|23 workshop participants]]. One participant-identified route listed "Q48 (or Q26) from North Shore Towers to LGA," indicating that the Q26 was discussed as a potential corridor for enhanced service to the airport, alongside the existing Q48 route [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p003_c0006|Q48 (or Q26) from North Shore Towers to LGA]]. The Q26 is a Queens bus route; the route record established here captures this single 2011-era workshop mention and should be considered a narrow-scope reference tied specifically to the LGA AA process [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p003_c0006|Q26 as participant-identified route]]. At the workshop's conclusion, [[metric:metric_survey-18-returned|18 surveys returned]] showed that a majority of participants either supported BRT or supported it with concerns [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p006_c0010|Majority supported BRT or supported with concerns]]. Workshop participants expressed a range of rider preferences during the voting exercise, most notably [[metric:metric_rider-pref-60min-1seat-16|Rider Preference: 60 Min, 1 Seat Ride]], [[metric:metric_rider-pref-express-bus-16|Rider Preference: Express Bus]], [[metric:metric_rider-pref-35min-dedicated-lane-16|Rider Preference: 35 minute ride in dedicated lane]], and [[metric:metric_rider-pref-highway-15|Rider Preference: Highway]] [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p005_c0006|Rider Preferences table]]. The primary source for this page is a meeting summary published by consultant URS for NYCDOT project manager Aaron Sugiura, dated January 17, 2012 [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p001_c0002|Client: NYCDOT]] [[cite:2012_04_20_brt_lga_workshop_summary#p002_c0002|URS]].
