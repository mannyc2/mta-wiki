The West Hempstead Branch is a commuter rail branch operated by the MTA Long Island Rail Road [[cite:meeting_doc_100336#p001_c0002|LIRR]]. In December 2022, during Montauk Branch switch maintenance between Valley Stream and Rockville Centre, bus service replaced train service on the West Hempstead Branch for approximately 8 hours each overnight period from December 6-8 [[cite:meeting_doc_100336#p001_c0008|West Hempstead Branch bus replacement]]. In April 2023, Montauk Branch track maintenance between Valley Stream and Jamaica required West Hempstead Branch trains to operate on adjusted schedules [[cite:meeting_doc_107636#p001_c0007|West Hempstead adjusted schedules]]. During the Van Wyck Bridge replacement on weekends of April 15-16 and April 22-23, West Hempstead Branch trains originated and terminated at Grand Central Madison [[cite:meeting_doc_107636#p001_c0007|Van Wyck Bridge adjustment]].

The LIRR Annual Operating Report for 2023 reports the West Hempstead Branch achieved an overall on-time performance of 95.8%, compared to 94.1% in 2022 and an annual goal of 98.4% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|West Hempstead OTP table]]. PM Peak performance was 81.8% in 2023, the lowest period, while Total Peak reached 86.1% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|West Hempstead OTP table]]. Off Peak Weekday OTP was 95.2% and Weekend OTP was 97.8% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|West Hempstead OTP table]]. The branch completed 99.6% of scheduled trips in 2023, consistent with the prior year [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p004_c0001|Percent Scheduled Trips Completed]]. The system-wide [[metric:metric_lirr-otp-overall-2023|LIRR Overall On Time Performance 2023]] was 94.0%, meaning the West Hempstead Branch outperformed the system average [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|System OTP table]].
