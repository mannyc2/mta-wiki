The [[route:route_bx18a-b|Bx18A/B]] is a local bus route in the Bronx, serving the Mt. Eden, Highbridge, and Morris Heights neighborhoods [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p002_c0006|Fare-Free Routes Map]] [[cite:meeting_doc_147096#p017_c0002|Bronx Bx18A/B]]. It was selected as the Bronx representative in MTA's Fare-Free Bus Pilot, one of five routes chosen based on equity criteria, existing capacity, limited overlap with other services, and fare evasion levels [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p002_c0004|Selection Criteria]] [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p002_c0005|Pilot Dates]].

The pilot ran from September 24, 2023 through August 31, 2024 [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p002_c0005|Pilot Dates]] [[cite:meeting_doc_147096#p002_c0005|Pilot Dates]]. During the school-year evaluation period (September–May), weekday ridership on the [[route:route_bx18a-b|Bx18A/B]] rose from 4,703 to 6,072, a 29% increase [[cite:meeting_doc_147096#p004_c0003|Weekday Ridership Table]]. Weekend ridership over the same period increased from 2,072 to 2,860, also climbing 38% [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p005_c0006|Weekend Ridership Table]].

Operator assaults on the [[route:route_bx18a-b|Bx18A/B]] fell from 7 in the pre-pilot period to 2 during the pilot, a 71.4% decline — the largest drop of any pilot route against a fare-free average of 31.9% and a systemwide decline of 15.4% [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p024_c0003|Bus Operator Assaults Table]]. The total cost attributed to the Bx18 route was about $1.27 million, including $722,000 in foregone farebox revenue, $148,000 in lost revenue on nearby services, $182,000 in incremental Access-A-Ride costs, and $217,000 in one-time and upfront costs [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p019_c0002|Cost Breakdown Table]].

The evaluation found that across all pilot routes, ridership increased but most growth came from existing riders using the service more often rather than from new riders [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p003_c0003|Key Findings]] [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p007_c0002|12% New Riders]]. Service performance showed mixed results, with pilot journey times improving while dwell times increased [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p003_c0003|Key Findings]]. The total 12-month cost of the fare-free pilot across all five routes was [[metric:metric_total-cost-16-475-m|$16,475,000]] [[cite:fare_free_bus_pilot_evaluation#p019_c0002|Total Cost Table]].
