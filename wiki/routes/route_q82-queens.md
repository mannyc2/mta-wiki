The [[route:route_q82-queens|Q82]] is a new bus route proposed in the MTA Queens Bus Network Redesign, introduced to maintain service continuity after the Q110 was streamlined along a more direct alignment [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|More Direct Routing]]. The existing Q110 connected passengers from Parsons Boulevard in Jamaica to Belmont Park; under the redesign, the Q110 is rerouted and extended to Floral Park for a more direct path and expanded coverage, and the new Q82 serves the discontinued portion of Hempstead Avenue [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|More Direct Routing]]. The Q110 change serves an area where 94% of the population is non-white and 40% is transit dependent, and adds a connection to the Queens Village LIRR station [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|More Direct Routing]].

The Q82 is part of a broader Queens Bus Network Redesign equity framework that assigns census block groups to three Equity Tiers based on socioeconomic indicators including transit dependency, minority representation, income, access to opportunity, and mobility needs [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p002_c0005|MTA's Equity Framework]]. Under the redesign, the all-day frequent network (stops with 10-minute-or-better frequency from 6am to 9pm) would expand boroughwide: the proposed network would serve 445,000 people in Tier 1 areas (71% of the Tier 1 population), up from 419,000 (67%) under the existing network [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p007_c0007|All-Day Frequent Network Coverage]]. The Queens population with access to all-day frequent service would rise from 56% to 61% [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p007_c0008|All-Day Frequent Network Coverage]]. The route's precise alignment, terminal locations, frequency, and span of service are not described in the source equity appendix beyond its role as a replacement for the discontinued Q110 segment along Hempstead Avenue [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p009_c0005|More Direct Routing]].
