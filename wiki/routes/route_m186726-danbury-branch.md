The [[route:route_m186726-danbury-branch|Danbury Branch]] is a Metro-North commuter rail branch operated as part of the New Haven Line network. In the October 2025 Schedule Change and Fall/Winter Programs document published by Metro-North's Service Design department, the branch appears solely in the Christmas Day 2025 holiday service plan [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p001_c0002|Staff Summary header]]. Christmas Day has one of the lowest ridership levels of the year, prompting a special holiday schedule [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p004_c0007|Christmas Day ridership level]]. Under that plan, the main lines (Lower Hudson, Upper Hudson, Mid-Harlem, Upper Harlem, Inner New Haven, Outer New Haven) run hourly, the New Canaan Branch runs hourly, the Wassaic Branch runs every two hours, and both the Danbury and Waterbury Branches run with service every three hours [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p004_c0008|Christmas Day service frequencies]]. No other schedule changes, service improvements, ridership metrics, or infrastructure project impacts specific to the Danbury Branch appear in this source document, which otherwise focuses on timetable changes effective October 5, 2025 affecting the Hudson, Harlem, and New Haven Lines and their support for the Park Avenue Viaduct and Walk Bridge replacement projects [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p001_c0010|schedule change purpose]]. The Danbury Branch is classified as a commuter rail branch with a route record scope of true_route.
