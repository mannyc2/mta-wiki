The [[route:route_meeting-doc-160441-b74|B74]] is a local NYCT bus route between Sea Gate and Stillwell Avenue Station in Brooklyn. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0008|B74 listed with Sea Gate-Stillwell Avenue Station description]]

The B74 was one of 16 local bus routes identified for service enhancements in support of the Central Business District Tolling (Congestion Pricing) program. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0007|Recommendation to provide frequency increases on 16 local routes]] The specific improvement for the route is frequency increases. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0008|B74 • Frequency increases]]

The local bus service package requires an increase of approximately [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-additional-buses|Additional buses needed for local improvements]], to be secured by rehabilitating buses planned for retirement. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0007|29 additional buses via rehabilitation of retiring buses]] The frequency increases and running time adjustments on local routes were planned for implementation in the Summer 2025 schedule. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0013|Local bus routes: Summer 2025]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p005_c0006|Local bus routes: Summer 2025]]

The overall recommended revision represents an annualized operating cost increase of approximately [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-total-annual-cost|Annualized operating cost increase - total]], including [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-local-annual-cost|Annual operating cost - local bus routes]] for local bus routes. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|$8 million total, $6.6 million local]] In the first year an additional [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-first-year-bus-rehab|First year cost - rehabilitate 29 local buses]] is required to rehabilitate 29 local buses. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|$5 million first year rehab]] Of the $8 million total, approximately [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-nyct-annual-cost|Annual operating cost - NYCT bus routes portion]] is for NYCT routes (including $496,000 for SIM23 and SIM24 funded by the City of New York) and approximately [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-mtabus-annual-cost|Annual operating cost - MTA Bus Company portion]] is for MTA Bus Company routes. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0010|NYCT vs MTA Bus Company cost split]] The staff summary requesting approval for these changes, prepared by Robert Lai of the Operations Planning Department, was dated December 10, 2024. [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p002_c0004|Subject, department, and project manager]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p002_c0005|Date December 10, 2024]]
