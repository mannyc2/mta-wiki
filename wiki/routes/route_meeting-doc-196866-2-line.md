The [[route:route_meeting-doc-196866-2-line|2 line]] is included in a Spring 2026 NYCT proposal for minor weekday schedule changes on the 2, 3, 4, and 5 lines, documented in a January 15, 2026 staff summary prepared by Acting Chief of Operations Planning Sarah Wyss [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p001_c0005|Spring 2026 Proposed Subway Schedule Changes]]. The proposal responds to shifts in passenger demand and aims to improve operational reliability, with implementation planned for May 2026 [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p001_c0011|Proposed Implementation Date]]. The adjustments are expected to be cost neutral [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p001_c0009|Budget Impact]].

For the northbound 2 line, measured at 14 St (AM) and Times Square-42 St (PM), the proposal adds one trip each to the 6:00 AM, 7:00 AM, 8:00 AM, and 5:00 PM hours, removes one trip from the 5:00 AM and 9:00 AM hours, and removes two trips from the 6:00 PM hour [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p003_c0004|2 line trip changes]]. The corresponding headway changes include a reduction from 10.0 to 8.6 minutes at 7:00 AM ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-2nb-8am-headway|2 NB 8 AM headway change]]), from 6.7 to 6.0 minutes at 8:00 AM ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-2nb-8am-headway|2 NB 8 AM headway change]]), and from 6.7 to 6.0 minutes at 5:00 PM ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-2nb-5pm-headway|2 NB 5 PM headway change]]), while the 6:00 PM headway would widen from 5.5 to 6.7 minutes with a net loss of two trips ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-2nb-6pm-change|2 NB 6 PM trip change]]) [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p005_c0004|2 line headway table]]. At the combined 2/3 level northbound, the 6:00 PM hour would lose three trips as headways shift from 2.9 to 3.3 minutes ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-23combined-nb-6pm-change|2/3 combined NB 6 PM trip change]]), while the 7:00 AM combined headway would improve from 5.0 to 4.3 minutes ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-196866-23combined-nb-7am-headway|2/3 combined NB 7 AM headway change]]) [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p006_c0003|2/3 combined table]].

The proposal also includes operating five southbound 2 line trips local between Times Sq-42 St and Chambers St between approximately 11:15 PM and 12:15 AM, intended to reduce delays caused by 3 trains terminating at 34 St-Penn Station on the downtown express track [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p003_c0006|2 line local ops]]. These schedule changes are part of a broader effort to spread peak service, create more even headways, and reduce delays through the Nostrand Junction bottleneck [[cite:meeting_doc_196866#p002_c0010|Nostrand Junction bottleneck relief]].
