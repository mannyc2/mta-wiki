[[route:route_sim30-42nd-st|SIM30]] is a Staten Island Express Bus Route that operates along 42nd Street as part of the 42nd Street Transit Improvements project corridor [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p006_c0003|Staten Island Express Bus Routes on 42nd St]]. It is one of six Staten Island express bus routes (along with SIM8, SIM8X, SIM22, SIM25, and SIM26) that use 42nd Street, identified in 2017 as a Bus Forward priority corridor due to slow bus speeds [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p029_c0005|42nd St Bus Forward priority]]. The corridor runs 2.0 miles from 12th Avenue to FDR Drive and carried approximately 16,000 daily MTA bus passengers across all eight routes that use it [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p029_c0006|corridor length and ridership]]. Average bus speeds on the corridor were 4.2 mph in the AM peak and 2.9 mph in the PM peak [[cite:better_buses_action_plan_2019#p029_c0006|corridor bus speeds]].

The 42nd Street Transit Improvements project, part of the Better Buses Action Plan, proposed offset bus lanes in effect 24/7, curbside bus lanes in effect 7 AM–7 PM, and commercial metered loading from 7 PM–7 AM [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p018_c0002|proposed curb regulations]]. The project also proposed bus stop consolidation at Lexington Avenue and 3rd Avenue [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p022_c0003|bus stop consolidation]], bus boarders at select stops [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p014_c0013|bus boarders]], and 12-foot-wide bus lanes with investigation of physical protection in certain segments [[cite:42nd_st_cb6_jun032019#p019_c0006|wider bus lanes]].

In a June 2019 presentation to Community Board 6, the route was shown on a map of Staten Island running east-west in the center of the borough, with a red line indicating the 42nd St Project Corridor along the M42 Manhattan Route [[cite:42nd_st_cb6_jun032019#p007_c0001|SIM30 on Staten Island map]]. A separate presentation to Community Board 4 included the same map, labeling SIM30 as running east-west in the center of Staten Island [[cite:42nd_st_cb4_jun192019#p006_c0001|SIM30 map label on CB4 slide]].
