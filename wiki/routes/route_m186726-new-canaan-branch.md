The [[route:route_m186726-new-canaan-branch|New Canaan Branch]] is a Metro-North Railroad commuter rail branch. The only service information in this source document (an MTA Metro-North Staff Summary from the Service Design department covering the October 5, 2025 schedule change and fall/winter holiday programs) relates to the Christmas Day holiday schedule [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p001_c0002|October 5, 2025 Staff Summary]]. On Christmas Day (Thursday, December 25, 2025), Metro-North planned to operate service every hour on the New Canaan Branch, matching the hourly frequency provided on the Lower Hudson, Upper Hudson, Mid-Harlem, Upper Harlem, Inner New Haven, and Outer New Haven Lines [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p004_c0008|Christmas Day hourly service on New Canaan Branch]]. This was a reduced service relative to normal weekday operations, consistent with Christmas Day having one of the lowest ridership levels of the year [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p004_c0007|Christmas Day low ridership]]. Other branches served that day included the Wassaic Branch (every two hours) and the Danbury and Waterbury Branches (every three hours) [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p004_c0008|Christmas Day branch service frequencies]].

The broader October 5, 2025 timetable change updated schedules on the Hudson, Harlem, and New Haven Lines to improve reliability and accommodate infrastructure projects including the Park Avenue Viaduct and Walk Bridge replacements [[cite:meeting_doc_186726#p001_c0010|schedule change purpose]], but the source document does not mention New Canaan Branch-specific changes in the regular timetable or any holiday period other than Christmas Day. No other service-level claims, ridership metrics, or infrastructure projects are attributed directly to the New Canaan Branch in this source.
