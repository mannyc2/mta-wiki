[[route:route_sim4x-meeting-doc-176441|SIM4X]] is a weekday peak-period express bus variant operating between Staten Island's South Shore along Richmond Avenue and Downtown Manhattan, serving only two stops on Staten Island (the Staten Island Mall Park-and-Ride and one other stop) but all same stops as the SIM4 in Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0005|route description]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0009|route detail]]. It was introduced as part of the August 2018 Staten Island Express Bus Redesign to leverage the Park-and-Ride facility to attract ridership, and in January 2019 one additional Staten Island stop was added in each direction [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0006|2018 redesign]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0010|2019 stop addition]]. As of the June 17, 2025 staff summary authored by project manager Robert Lai of the Operations Planning Department, the SIM4X operates [[metric:metric_sim4x-15-trips-weekday|SIM4X 15 trips per weekday]]: [[metric:metric_sim4x-7-morning-trips|SIM4X 7 morning trips to Manhattan]] and [[metric:metric_sim4x-8-afternoon-trips|SIM4X 8 afternoon/evening trips to Staten Island]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0011|current trips]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0002|staff summary]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p002_c0003|date]].

The SIM4X trips are underutilized with an average load factor of [[metric:metric_sim4x-load-factor-15pct|SIM4X average load factor 15%]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0007|load factor]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0003|load factor detail]]. The recommendation documented in this staff summary is to discontinue the SIM4X and its counterpart SIM8X and reinvest into [[metric:metric_proposed-7-sim4-trips|Proposed 7 additional SIM4 trips]] ([[metric:metric_proposed-3-sim4-manhattan-trips|Proposed 3 SIM4 trips to Manhattan]] and [[metric:metric_proposed-4-sim4-si-trips|Proposed 4 SIM4 trips to Staten Island]]) and [[metric:metric_proposed-2-sim8-trips|Proposed 2 additional SIM8 trips]] ([[metric:metric_proposed-1-sim8-manhattan-trip|Proposed 1 SIM8 trip to Manhattan]] and [[metric:metric_proposed-1-sim8-si-trip|Proposed 1 SIM8 trip to Staten Island]]) while meeting schedule loading guidelines [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0009|recommendation]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0005|recommendation detail]]. The net annual operating cost savings from this change would be [[metric:metric_net-decrease-operating-costs-2-5m|net annual operating cost decrease]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0011|cost savings]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0009|cost savings detail]]. The proposed implementation date is Fall 2025; a "do nothing" alternative was also considered [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p001_c0013|implementation date]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0007|do nothing alternative]] [[cite:meeting_doc_176441#p003_c0011|implementation date p3]].
