The [[route:route_sim24-madison-ave-cb6-jun2025|SIM24 Express Bus]] is an NYCT-operated express bus route connecting Prince's Bay and Huguenot Avenue on Staten Island to Midtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p003_c0004|SIM24 route description]]. It is among the bus routes that operate on Madison Avenue within the East 23rd to East 42nd Street study area, as shown in the Madison Avenue bus route diagram [[cite:madison_ave_e23_st_e42_st_cb6_jun2025#p009_c0003|Madison Avenue bus routes diagram]].

Starting in 2022, the MTA took over operation of the SIM24 and SIM23 routes, extending standard Staten Island express bus features to riders on these lines [[cite:meeting_doc_79331#p100_c0005|MTA takeover of SIM23 and SIM24]]. The two routes together transport about 300 passengers daily to and from midtown Manhattan [[cite:meeting_doc_79331#p100_c0005|SIM23/SIM24 daily ridership]].

A proposed service enhancement would add 1 peak period, peak direction trip each in the AM and PM peaks on the SIM24, as part of a package adding trips to express bus routes with the highest single-hour utilization [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p001_c0009|Add trips to express bus routes]] [[cite:meeting_doc_138456#p003_c0004|SIM24 trip additions]]. This same trip addition was carried into a Spring 2025 express bus service enhancement proposal that provided frequency increases or running time adjustments on eight express routes [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0005|Spring 2025 express bus enhancements]]. The broader service revision records package-level cost measures for the total annualized operating increase, the NYCT annualized share, the MTA Bus Company annualized share, and first-year bus rehabilitation; the NYCT line item includes a City-funded SIM23/SIM24 component, but these are not SIM24-only costs [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-total-annual-cost|total annualized operating cost increase]] [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-nyct-annual-cost|NYCT annualized operating cost share]] [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-mtabus-annual-cost|MTA Bus Company annualized operating cost share]] [[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-first-year-bus-rehab|first-year bus rehabilitation cost]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0010|NYCT and MTA Bus Company cost breakdown]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|First year bus rehabilitation cost]].
