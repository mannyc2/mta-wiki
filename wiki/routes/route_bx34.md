The [[route:route_bx34|Bx34 Local]] is a Bronx bus route operating on Bainbridge Avenue and Valentine Avenue, with a route label of Bx34 Bainbridge Avenue/Valentine Avenue [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p010_c0001|Bx34 Bainbridge Avenue/Valentine Avenue map label]]. During the 2012 Webster Avenue Select Bus Service planning process, the proposed service plan included rerouting the southern portion of the Bx34 to Webster Avenue [[cite:2012_11_sbs_webster_cac4#p033_c0002|Reroute the southern portion of the Bx34 route to Webster Avenue]], with the Bx34 listed under the "proposed" category as "rerouted" [[cite:2012_11_sbs_webster_cac4#p030_c0007|Bx34 (Local) Rerouted]].

The Bronx Bus Network Redesign proposed Final Plan (October 2019) again called for streamlining the Bx34, making it more direct by avoiding circuitous segments and narrow one-way streets, and moving service to the larger Webster Avenue corridor [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p002_c0005|Bx34 retains original routing]] [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p007_c0007|Bx34 routing will remain as-is]]. However, strong community opposition from local senior customers demonstrated that the current alignment is valued by existing Bx34 bus customers. After field visits from agency staff members and after hearing from the community during the February 2020 public hearing, it was agreed that the Bx34 would no longer change and would remain as-is, with frequencies staying the same [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p002_c0005|Bx34 retains original routing]] [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p007_c0007|Bx34 routing will remain as-is]].

The Bx34 also serves the Fordham Road corridor and is listed among routes that travel on Fordham Road alongside the Bx9, Bx12 SBS/Local, Bx17, Bx22, and Bee-Line 60-62 buses [[cite:better_buses#p001_b0001|Bx34 listed on Fordham Road]] [[cite:fordham_rd_major_deegan_expwy_boston_rd_jun2021#p028_c0002|Bx34 listed on Fordham Road]]. The Bx34 is not listed among routes on any of the 10 bus priority corridors identified by NYC DOT in the Bronx [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p005_c0003|Bus Priority Corridors table]], and the addendum table of route-level changes shows only bus stop changes (no alignment or schedule changes) for the Bx34 [[cite:bronx_bus_network_final_plan_addendum_2021#p011_c0001|Route changes table showing Bx34 with only bus stop changes]].
