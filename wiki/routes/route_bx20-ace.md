The [[route:route_bx20-ace|Bx20]] is a local MTA bus route that operates on Broadway and Henry Hudson Parkway in the Bronx and Manhattan [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|Bx20 listed as ACE route with major streets]]. The route serves the Broadway corridor from approximately 157th Street to 220th Street, alongside the Bx7, M5, M4, and M100 [[cite:broadway_157_st_220_st_cb12_mar2025#p005_c0002|Bx20 identified among Broadway corridor bus routes]]. These routes connect to the 1, A, and C subway lines and Metro-North Railroad [[cite:broadway_157_st_220_st_cb12_mar2025#p005_c0002|routes connect to 1 A C and Metro North]].

Weekday ridership in 2018 was 813, based on New York City Transit data cited in the NYC DOT Better Buses Broadway Improvements presentation to Community Board 8 [[cite:better_buses_pdf_better_buses_broadway_improvements_cb8_2019#p006_c0005|Bx20: 813 2018 weekday ridership]]. Across all five bus routes operating on the corridor, the Broadway study area carries over [[metric:metric_15000-daily-riders-171-186|15,000 daily riders]] between 171st and 186th streets, where speeds are lowest—as slow as 4 MPH [[cite:broadway_157_st_220_st_cb12_mar2025#p006_c0002|ridership and speed data for Broadway between 171st and 186th St]].

The NYC Department of Transportation is pursuing bus and safety improvements on Broadway from 157th Street to 220th Street, a Tier 1 Priority Investment Area under the NYC Streets Plan [[cite:broadway_157_st_220_st_cb12_mar2025#p004_c0002|Broadway bus priority corridor context]]. Existing conditions were presented to Community Board 12 in March 2025, with DOT and MTA planning to develop a design proposal with continued community feedback [[cite:broadway_157_st_220_st_cb12_mar2025#p017_c0002|next steps for Broadway corridor improvements]]. The Bx20 is also listed on the MTA's automated camera enforcement (ACE) page as a route where bus lane and bus stop camera enforcement applies [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|Bx20 on ACE route list]].
