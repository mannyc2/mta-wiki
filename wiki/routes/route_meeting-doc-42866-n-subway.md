The [[route:route_meeting-doc-42866-n-subway|N Subway]] appears in the June 23, 2021 MTA Board Update as one of four routes listed on the station sign at the 57 Street / 7 Av ADA Subway Station entrance, alongside Q, R, and W [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p001_c0002|MTA Board Update June 23, 2021]] [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p004_c0002|57 Street Station serves N Q R W]]. The photograph in the update shows an accessibility-focused event at the station entrance, with an MTA "Improving Accessibility" sign and attendees including construction workers and mobility device users [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p004_c0002|57 Street ADA event photo]]. The update was presented by MTA Construction & Development [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p001_c0003|MTA C&D logo]] and also reported that major construction was completed on East Side Access, ADA elevator enhancements were underway at Floral Park station by the Long Island Rail Road, and utilities work continued on the East End Gateway project [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p002_c0001|East Side Access Major Construction Completed]] [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p003_c0001|Floral Park ADA Enhancements]] [[cite:meeting_doc_42866#p005_c0001|East End Gateway Utilities Work]]. The 57 Street station serves multiple Broadway Line subway routes, and this source confirms the N Subway's presence at that station as of mid-2021.
