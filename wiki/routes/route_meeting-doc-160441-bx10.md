The [[route:route_meeting-doc-160441-bx10|Bx10]] is an NYCT local bus route operating between Riverdale and Norwood that was identified for running time adjustments as part of an MTA service enhancement package in support of Congestion Pricing [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p004_c0004|Bx10 running time adjustments]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0005|Congestion Pricing rationale]]. It was among 16 local bus routes and route combinations selected for frequency increases and/or running time adjustments where utilization is high or where routes have reliability issues [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0007|local route selection criteria]]. The details of the adjustments were under development at the time of the staff summary and were planned for implementation in the summer 2025 schedule [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0007|summer 2025 planned]] [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0013|local routes summer 2025]]. To support these local improvements, an increase in the overall fleet by approximately 29 additional buses was needed, secured by rehabilitating buses planned for retirement ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-first-year-bus-rehab|First year cost - rehabilitate 29 local buses]]) [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p003_c0007|29 buses via rehabilitation]]. The annualized operating cost increase for local bus routes was approximately $6.6 million per year ([[metric:metric_meeting-doc-160441-local-annual-cost|Annual operating cost - local bus routes]]) [[cite:meeting_doc_160441#p001_c0009|$6.6 million local bus annual cost]].
