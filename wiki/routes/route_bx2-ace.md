The [[route:route_bx2-ace|Bx2]] is a Bronx bus route listed on Grand Concourse in the Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|ACE route list]]. The route is one of 60 bus routes in the ACE program, a bus-mounted camera system that issues civil penalties to vehicles blocking bus lanes, bus stops, or double-parked along bus routes [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|ACE program description]]. The ACE program, administered jointly by the MTA, NYC Department of Finance, and NYC Department of Transportation, expanded through 2023 state legislation and benefits over one million average weekday riders across all participating routes [[metric:metric_ace-weekday-riders|over 1M weekday riders]] [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|ACE program scope]]. On each ACE route, including the [[route:route_bx2-ace|Bx2]], the MTA and City issue warning notices for 60 days before fine-bearing violations begin [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|60-day warning period]]. Fines start at $50 and escalate by $50 per additional violation up to $250 maximum [[metric:metric_ace-fine-start-50|fines start at $50]] [[metric:metric_ace-fine-increment-50|$50 increment]] [[metric:metric_ace-fine-max-250|$250 maximum]] [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|fine structure]].

The ACE enforcement page lists an update date of May 15, 2026 [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|page update date]]. The Bx2 route is listed under the Bronx section of ACE-enforced routes with "Grand Concourse" noted as its major street, so this page treats the ACE listing as program participation evidence rather than as a full route profile [[cite:mta_automated_camera_enforcement#p001_b0001|Bx2 route entry]].
