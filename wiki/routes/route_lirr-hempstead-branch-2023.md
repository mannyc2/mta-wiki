The [[route:route_lirr-hempstead-branch-2023|Hempstead Branch]] is operated by the MTA Long Island Rail Road and is documented in the 2023 Annual Operating Report [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Performance Summary table]]. In its 2023 performance data, the branch recorded an overall on-time performance of 96.5%, exceeding the prior year's 94.4% and narrowly surpassing the 96.3% annual goal [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Hempstead Branch OTP row]]. The branch's OTP varied by period: AM Peak at 90.9%, PM Peak at 94.4%, Total Peak at 92.6%, Off Peak Weekday at 94.3%, and Weekend at 96.0% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p003_c0004|Hempstead Branch OTP breakdown]].

Elevator availability on the Hempstead Branch improved from 99.2% in 2022 to 99.5% in 2023, above the system overall average of 99.1% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0004|Elevator Availability table]] ([[metric:metric_lirr-elevator-overall-2023|Overall Elevator Availability 2023]]). Escalator availability held steady at 97.3% in both 2022 and 2023, matching the system average of 95.7% [[cite:meeting_doc_133321#p007_c0005|Escalator Availability table]] ([[metric:metric_lirr-escalator-overall-2023|Overall Escalator Availability 2023]]).

In spring 2023, the branch experienced temporary service adjustments due to the Van Wyck Bridge Replacement project on the Main Line. During the weekends of April 15-16 and April 22-23, two of four main tracks between Jamaica and Harold Interlocking were taken out of service, and Hempstead Branch trains were adjusted to terminate or originate at either Atlantic Terminal or Jamaica [[cite:meeting_doc_107636#p001_c0007|Van Wyck Bridge service adjustments]]. No branch-specific on-time performance, delay category, or fleet reliability metrics were disaggregated for the Hempstead Branch beyond the performance summary and elevator/escalator data in the available 2023 annual report.
