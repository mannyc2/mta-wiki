The [[route:route_meeting-doc-164866-greenport-branch|Greenport Branch]] is an MTA Long Island Rail Road branch in Suffolk County, New York, served by the LIRR's Ronkonkoma line east of Ronkonkoma [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0002|LIRR]]. A February 3, 2025 staff summary to the LIRR Long Island Committee documented a March 3, 2025 timetable change under which bus service replaced weekday/midday train service between Ronkonkoma and Greenport to support track maintenance and crossing rehabilitations east of Ronkonkoma [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0005|timetable change effective March 3 through May 19, 2025]] [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p002_c0003|bus replacement Ronkonkoma to Greenport]]. The Track Maintenance and Crossing Rehabilitation project between Ronkonkoma and Greenport was one of several projects supported during this timetable period [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p001_c0006|projects supported]].

During the same March–April 2025 period, four weekends of switch and signal work near Ronkonkoma station also affected the [[route:route_meeting-doc-164866-greenport-branch|Greenport Branch]]: on March 1–2, March 8–9, March 15–16, and March 22–23, the single main track between Ronkonkoma and Greenport was taken out of service, with bus service replacing trains for all stations between Ronkonkoma and Greenport [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p002_c0006|switch and signal work weekend service adjustments]]. Funding for these projects was contained in the LIRR Operating and MTA Capital Program budgets [[cite:meeting_doc_164866#p002_c0010|funding source]].
