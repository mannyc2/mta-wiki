The [[route:route_q61-queens|Q61 bus route]] is a proposed new bus route under the Queens Bus Network Redesign, introduced as part of a route-combination strategy to eliminate redundant service in the existing network [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p011_c0004|Combine Routes - Q61 description]]. Under the redesign, the proposed Q25 would absorb existing Q34 trips along Kissena and Parsons Boulevards, while the remaining portion of the Q34 not included in the Q25 proposal would be served by the new Q61 [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p011_c0004|Combine Routes - Q25 and Q61 allocation]]. The Q61 is designed to improve reliability and introduce weekend service to Linden Hill, a neighborhood in Flushing described as having moderate to significant equity concerns [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p011_c0004|Combine Routes - Linden Hill service]]. A map accompanying the equity evaluation shows the new Q61 route covering the northern portion of the discontinued Q34 alignment [[cite:queens_addendum_equity_evaluation_appendix_d#p011_c0005|Combine Routes - map description]]. The Q61 is one of several new routes introduced in the Queens Bus Network Redesign Proposed Final Plan Addendum aimed at improving network coverage and addressing equity considerations across the borough.
