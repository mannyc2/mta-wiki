The [[route:route_b47-grand-ave-2024|B47 bus]] is one of ten bus routes that operates out of the MTA Grand Av Bus Depot in Queens. [[cite:grand_ave_metropolitan_ave_queens_blvd_cb5_dec2024#p007_c0005|listed as one of 10 routes from the depot]] The depot serves 1,032 buses pulling in and out every weekday, including over 80 movements during the busiest hour. [[cite:grand_ave_metropolitan_ave_queens_blvd_cb5_dec2024#p007_c0005|1,032 buses, 80+ movements]] Truck traffic and congestion from the Grand St Bridge cause significant operational difficulties for pull-in and pull-out, resulting in longer wait times and less reliable service for riders. [[cite:grand_ave_metropolitan_ave_queens_blvd_cb5_dec2024#p007_c0005|operational difficulties from truck traffic and Grand St Bridge congestion]] Improvements along Grand Avenue that prioritize bus service and depot access are expected to deliver more reliable, faster buses in an area that lacks direct subway service. [[cite:grand_ave_metropolitan_ave_queens_blvd_cb5_dec2024#p007_c0005|area lacking direct subway service]]

The same findings appear consistently in presentations delivered to Queens Community Board 4 on November 26, 2024, Queens Community Board 5 on December 17, 2024, and Brooklyn Community Board 1 on November 12, 2024, as part of the Grand St/Grand Av Safety & Bus Priority Improvements initiative by NYC DOT and MTA.
