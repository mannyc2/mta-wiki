The [[route:route_bx12-local-2015-webster-map|Bx12 Local]] appears on the legend of a 2015 Webster Avenue Select Bus Service service plan map as one of the routes serving the corridor [[cite:2015_07_webster_sbs_service_plan#p001_c0005|Webster SBS Service Plan Legend]]. Along Fordham Road, the Bx12 Local and Bx12 SBS together carried approximately 53,100 daily riders, with average bus speeds of 6.8 mph during AM peak and 5.9 mph during PM peak [[cite:bx_cb5_projects_dec032019#p025_c0003|Fordham Rd Bus Priority Performance Data]].

Historical ridership and speed data for the combined Bx12 Local and SBS service from 2007 through 2019 shows peak combined daily ridership of 48,500 riders in 2014 and 2015, with average speeds declining from 9.1 mph in the 2009-2012 period to 7.5 mph in 2018 and 2019 [[cite:fordham_rd_inwood_cab_mar2022#p009_c0004|Bx12 Lcl/SBS Ridership and Speed Table]]. By 2023, the Bx12 Local specifically was reported to have approximately 5,100 daily riders, while the Bx12 SBS carried about 37,000 daily riders [[cite:fordham_rd_inwood_cb6_jun2023#p007_c0002|Bronx Bus Routes Serving Fordham Road Ridership]], and across all five Fordham Road bus routes (Bx12-SBS + Local, Bx9, Bx17, Bx22) the corridor serves about 85,000 average daily bus riders [[cite:fordham_rd_inwood_cb11_jun2023#p004_c0002|Fordham Rd Corridor Daily Ridership]].

Following the implementation of Automated Bus Lane Enforcement (ABLE) cameras on the Bx12 SBS on November 18, 2022, the route experienced a [[metric:metric_able-speed-change-april2023|+4.7% speed increase in April 2023 compared to October 2022]], with average Fordham Road speeds rising from 6.0 mph to 6.3 mph between Sedgwick Avenue and Southern Boulevard [[cite:fordham_rd_inwood_cb11_jun2023#p016_c0002|ABLE Speed Evaluation Results]]. The Bx12 Local is one of several routes listed as serving the Fordham Road corridor alongside the Bx9, Bx17, Bx22, Bx34, and Bee-Line 60-62 buses [[cite:fordham_rd_major_deegan_expwy_boston_rd_jun2021#p028_c0002|Fordham Rd Corridor Route List]].
