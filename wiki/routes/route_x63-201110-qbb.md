The [[route:route_x63-201110-qbb|X63 Express Bus Route]] is a Queens-based express bus service that crosses the Queensboro Bridge (QBB). This route was documented in 2011 as part of the NYC DOT Queensboro Bridge Bus Priority Study, which identified cost-effective ways to improve bus speed and reliability for local and express buses using the bridge [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p003_c0004|Study Goal]]. The study recorded the X63 with an estimated daily ridership of 380, marked with an asterisk indicating estimated volume [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Route Ridership Table]]. The X63 appeared alongside other express routes such as the X64, X68, X51, and numerous QM-series routes in the study's express bus inventory [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Route Ridership Table]]. The total daily express bus ridership across all QBB routes was 7,920 [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Route Ridership Table]].

At the time of the study, the QBB carried approximately 16,000 bus trips daily, including about 8,000 local riders and 110 buses during PM rush hour [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p004_c0003|QBB Corridor Description]]. Over 75% of QBB express and local bus riders reported that traffic congestion regularly delayed their bus [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p004_c0003|Congestion Impact]]. An express rider survey conducted as part of the study found that of 511 express respondents, 88% were traveling to or from work, 81% rode the bus daily, and 52% sometimes used a subway alternative [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|Express Rider Survey]]. Among express riders who perceived delay on the bridge, 62% reported delays of 5-15 minutes [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|Express Rider Survey]]. The most cited improvement suggestions from express riders were bus lanes, enforcement of bus lanes, and routing changes [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p012_c0004|Express Rider Survey]].

The [[metric:metric_express-bus-total-daily-ridership|Express Bus Total Daily Ridership on QBB]] metric captures the aggregate express rider volume across all QBB express routes [[cite:201110_qbb_approach_summary#p008_c0003|Express Ridership Table]].
