The [[route:route_b36-2015-sbk-corridor|B36]] was identified as one of thirteen east-west bus routes serving south Brooklyn as part of the South Brooklyn Crosstown SBS corridor study presented at a June 2015 NYC DOT kick-off meeting [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p002_c0005|B36 listed among east-west routes]]. These routes carried a combined daily ridership of 178,000 [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p002_c0007|178,000 per day]]. The Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study (2009) had previously identified the south Brooklyn east-west corridor as a priority service need, based on public workshops that raised concerns about slow, long bus trips and underserved areas [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p002_c0009|BRT Phase II Study identified priority]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p002_c0011|issues raised at 2009 workshops]].

At the time of the kick-off, existing SBS routes citywide had demonstrated measurable improvements: [[metric:metric_sbs-faster-bus-15-23-pct|SBS bus service speed improvement]] of 15-23%, [[metric:metric_sbs-ridership-increase-110-pct|SBS ridership increase]] averaging up to 110%, [[metric:metric_sbs-rider-satisfaction-95-pct|SBS rider satisfaction]] at 95%, and up to a [[metric:metric_sbs-crash-reduction-20-pct_3|SBS crash reduction]] of 20% along SBS corridors [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p001_c0007|15-23% faster]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p001_c0009|↑110% ridership]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p001_c0013|95% satisfaction]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p001_c0015|↓20% crash reduction]]. The proposed SBS toolbox for the corridor included dedicated bus lanes, off-board fare collection, all-door boarding, transit signal priority, real-time passenger information, improved station amenities, and pedestrian safety improvements [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p003_c0001|Ways to Improve Bus Service]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p004_c0007|Real-Time Passenger Information]] [[cite:2015_06_17_brt_southbrooklyn_kickoff_boards#p004_c0009|Pedestrian Safety Improvements]].
