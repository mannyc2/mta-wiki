### [p003_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p003_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p003_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p003_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes

### [p010_c0001] Project Background
[p010_c0002] The Utica Avenue B46 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit: Phase II Study.
- [p010_c0003] • Ranked by community as one of two corridors most in need of bus improvements • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Second-busiest bus route in NYC, carrying nearly 50,000 passengers per day • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains • Connection to 30 bus routes including B35, B6, B12 • B35-to-B46 transfer is the most frequent bus-to-bus transfer in NYC
### [p010_c0004] Residents Mode to Work
> [p010_c0005] Mode Percentage Transit 61% Driving 24% Walk / Bicycle 10% Other 5%
[p010_c0006] Source: 2008-2012 ACS 5-Year Estimates ¼ mile from Utica corridor
