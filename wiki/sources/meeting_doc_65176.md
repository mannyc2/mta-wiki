## Page 3

[p003_c0001] Lapsed customers Why aren't they riding like they used to?
[p003_c0002] Picture of a Lapsed Express Bus Survey Respondent in Staten Island
### [p003_c0003] WFH, but work locations are open
- [p003_c0004] • More than 80% of lapsed MTA customers reported that their work locations never closed, have re-opened or will be re-opening.
### [p003_c0005] Personal security and COVID concerns
- [p003_c0006] • Lapsed customers cited crime and harassment/ personal security, cleanliness and disinfection efforts/health as very important factors as they considered returning to transit.
### [p003_c0007] Using different mode than pre-COVID
- [p003_c0008] • Subway (41%), Local Bus (42%), and Express Bus (17%) customers reported using different modes, cars, FHVs, bikes.
> [p003_c0009] The MTA logo, consisting of the letters 'MTA' in white inside a dark blue circle.

## Page 4

[p004_c0001] Current customers
[p004_c0002] Why are they riding less?
[p004_c0003] Picture of a Current AAR Survey Respondent in Brooklyn
### [p004_c0004] WFH, but taking discretionary trips
- [p004_c0005] • More trips: shopping, dining, visiting friends, going to medical appointments.
### [p004_c0006] Using different travel modes
- [p004_c0007] • About one-third of Subway (36%) and Local Bus (35%) current customers who are using the subway less reported traveling differently.
### [p004_c0008] Service-related attributes
- [p004_c0009] • Among current Subway, Bus, and SIR customers riding less and using alternative modes, a major reason cited was that MTA services were unreliable – unpredictable travel times and unexpected delays.
