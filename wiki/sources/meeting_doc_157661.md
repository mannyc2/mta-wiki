### [p002_c0016] Key Performance Metrics Reports
[p002_c0017] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p002_c0018] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0019] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0020] Work Plan
[p002_c0021] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0022] President's Report
[p002_c0023] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0024] Chief's Report
[p002_c0025] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0026] Safety Report
[p002_c0027] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators
### [p002_c0028] Police Report
[p002_c0029] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
