### [p001_c0002] Standard Follow-Up Report: Transit Adjudication Bureau, 2 nd Quarter 2024
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this quarterly report is to update the Transit Committee on Transit Adjudication Bureau (TAB) activities and outcomes, as reflected by several Key Indicators.
[p001_c0004] TAB is the statutory administrative tribunal that receives, processes, adjudicates and pursues collection of civil penalties arising from the tens of thousands of civil summonses that are issued each year for violations of the Transit Rules of Conduct.
[p001_c0005] This quarterly reporting on TAB activities and outcomes commenced in 1992. The report provides the Committee with metrics, covering the most recently completed quarter, for the following Key Indicators:
- [p001_c0006] • Number of TAB violations received by TAB • Number and dollar amount of payments TAB received • TAB revenues and expenses for the quarter • Number of cases adjudicated

### [p002_c0001] STANDARD FOLLOW-UP REPORTS: TRANSIT ADJUDICATION BUREAU SECOND QUARTER 2024
[p002_c0002] The following is a comparison of the key indicators for the second quarter of 2024 as compared to the same period in 2023:
- [p002_c0003] • Summonses issued in the second quarter increased by 17.99% (from 59,429 in 2023 to 70,122 in 2024). • TAB received 53,960 payments in 2024, a 5.76% decrease from the 57,256 received in 2023. Direct payments increased by 21.63% (28,184 to 34,280) from the second quarter of 2023 and payments received from state tax refunds decreased by 32.31% from 29,072 to 19,680. • Overall total revenue for the second quarter totaled $6,143,832, a 17.77% decrease from the 2023 second quarter revenue of $7,471,164. Receipts from direct payments increased by 22.53% to $3,113,079 in 2024 as compared to $2,540,582 in the second quarter of 2023. The $2,976,754 in second quarter receipts from state tax refunds relating to outstanding judgments from prior years, represented a 38.92% decrease from second quarter state tax refunds of $4,873,522 in 2023. • Expenses increased by 13.19% ($1,271,431 compared to $1,439,093) from the second quarter of 2023. Please note that starting from Q1 2024, the segment of incoming data utilized to generate this section of the report is rounded to the nearest thousand. • TAB revenue exceeded expenses by $4,704,739 compared to $6,199,733 for the second quarter of 2023.
