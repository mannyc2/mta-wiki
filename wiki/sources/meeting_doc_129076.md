COMPENSATION: None
### [p008_c0009] COMMENTS:
[p008_c0010] MNR is seeking to continue operations of its material and equipment storage under a short-term agreement until a long-term agreement is executed.
[p008_c0011] Pursuant to the Board-approved Real Estate Department Policy "Agreements under $100K where the MTA is the grantee" dated June 27, 2023, MTA Real Estate is authorized to enter into a license, lease or acquire property rights in support of MTA capital projects or ongoing operations when it is estimated that MTA's aggregate obligation to pay compensation to the licensor or grantor is less than $100,000 over the term.
