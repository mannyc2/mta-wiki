### [p001_c0002] December 2022 Crime Report
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this report is to provide Committee Members with statistical information regarding the number of major felonies including: homicide, robbery, assault, rape in addition to hate crime incidents occurring in the NYCT Subway system.
