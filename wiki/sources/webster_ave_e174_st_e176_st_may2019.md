### [p007_c0001] TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT
[p007_c0002] Webster Av & E 175 St, looking South
- [p007_c0003] • On/off ramps for the Cross Bronx Expressway create traffic pinchpoint • Unclear lane designations lead to sideswipes and rear-ends (51.6% of vehicle crashes on this stretch of Webster Av) • In southbound direction, similar amount of vehicles turning right onto the Cross Bronx Expressway and going straight on Webster Av • On average 26% of people traveling southbound are on transit vehicles (33% in AM Peak hour)
