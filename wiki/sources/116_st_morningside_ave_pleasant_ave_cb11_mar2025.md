### [p014_c0001] Public Health and Air Quality
- [p014_c0002] • Residents in neighborhoods near 116 th St. have high rates of asthma, diabetes, and heart disease, in part due to exposure to vehicular pollution. • Increased transit use can contribute to improved air quality by reducing reliance on personal vehicles and decreasing associated exhaust pollution. • Research shows that transit may promote social connection and lower rates of depressive symptoms in older adults, and that transit is linked to increased walking.
