[p003_c0004] Net preliminary operating results for YTD December were favorable to the November Forecast by $517 million, with the month of December favorable by $224 million. Overtime spending YTD December was $18 million (2%) unfavorable and $6 million (7%) unfavorable in December, both primarily due to vacancy/absentee coverage at NYCT.
