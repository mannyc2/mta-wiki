### [p005_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p005_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
[p005_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p005_c0004] • 10-30% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p005_c0005] There are 16 Select Bus Service corridors in operation serving all 5 boroughs.

### [p008_c0001] B82 Limited is Now B82 SBS
- [p008_c0002] • B82 SBS service launched on 10/01/18, replacing the B82 Limited • B82 SBS begins at Cropsey Ave & Bay 37 St and serves many of the same stops as the existing B82 Limited , running to Pennsylvania Ave in Starrett City • B82 SBS operates for more hours of the day than the B82 Limited did (service now operates from 4:45 AM to 11:00 PM) • All current stops are served by the B82 Local , except for three stops planned for removal (due to close proximity to nearby stops and no corresponding stop in opposite direction): – Kings Hwy at W 9 St, eastbound – Kings Hwy at Van Sicklen, eastbound – Kings Hwy at E 9 St, eastbound
