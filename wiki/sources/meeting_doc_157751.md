### [p001_c0002] Standard Follow-Up Report: Transit Adjudication Bureau, 3rd Quarter 2024
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this quarterly report is to update the Transit Committee on Transit Adjudication Bureau (TAB) activities and outcomes, as reflected by several Key Indicators.
[p001_c0004] TAB is the statutory administrative tribunal that receives, processes, adjudicates and pursues collection of civil penalties arising from the tens of thousands of civil summonses that are issued each year for violations of the Transit Rules of Conduct.
[p001_c0005] This quarterly reporting on TAB activities and outcomes commenced in 1992. The report provides the Committee with metrics, covering the most recently completed quarter, for the following Key Indicators:
- [p001_c0006] • Number of TAB violations received by TAB • Number and dollar amount of payments TAB received • TAB revenues and expenses for the quarter • Number of cases adjudicated

### [p002_c0001] STANDARD FOLLOW-UP REPORTS: TRANSIT ADJUDICATION BUREAU THIRD QUARTER 2024
[p002_c0002] The following is a comparison of the key indicators for the third quarter of 2024 as compared to the same period in 2023:
- [p002_c0003] • Summonses issued in the third quarter increased by 39.53% (from 51,199 in 2023 to 71,437 in 2024). • TAB received 39,702 payments in 2024, a 45.06% increase from the 27,370 received in 2023. Direct payments increased by 44.06% (25,888 to 37,293) from the third quarter of 2023 and payments received from state tax refunds increased by 62.55% from 1,482 to 2,409. • Overall total revenue for the third quarter totaled $3,627,814, a 39.40% increase from the 2023 third quarter revenue of $2,602,464. Receipts from direct payments increased by 39.72% to $3,308,790 in 2024 as compared to $2,368,099 in the third quarter of 2023. The $319,024 in third quarter receipts from state tax refunds relating to outstanding judgments from prior years, represented a 36.12% increase from third quarter state tax refunds of $234,365 in 2023. • Expenses increased by 8.29% ($1,454,560 compared to $1,343,178) from the third quarter of 2023. Please note that starting from Q1 2024, the segment of incoming data utilized to generate this section of the report is rounded to the nearest thousand. • TAB revenue exceeded expenses by $2,296,300 compared to $1,407,267 for the third quarter of 2023.
