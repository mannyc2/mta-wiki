### [p004_c0001] Where Does E-Hail Fit In?
### [p004_c0002] Adding A Flexible and Spontaneous Option
- [p004_c0003] • On-demand service in a taxi or for-hire vehicle • Customer books directly with provider by app or phone • MTA subsidizes portion of trip for a set number of trips per month
### [p004_c0004] Current E-Hail Phase 2 Pilot
- [p004_c0005] • Launched August 2023 • 5 taxi and FHV providers available • Customer pays $4 initial charge and any amount over MTA subsidy, if applicable • Two programs: • Distance: 25 monthly trips, $40 MTA subsidy per trip • High Volume: 40 monthly trips, $25 MTA subsidy • 10,000 customers invited; 1,600 registered
> [p004_c0006] The MTA logo, consisting of the letters 'MTA' in white inside a black circle.

## Page 5

### [p005_c0001] Lessons from E-Hail Phase 2
- [p005_c0002] • Many customers chose not to register for E-hail because of satisfaction with current AAR service • Customer satisfaction is very high for those using E-Hail: 81% overall satisfaction among participants • Monthly trip and subsidy allocations reduced E-Hail costs by more than 50% and enabled MTA to expand the benefits to more customers • On average, active pilot customers take about 15 E-Hail trips per month • Phase 2 pilot customers continue to use traditional AAR for some trips
> [p005_c0003] The MTA logo, consisting of the letters 'MTA' in white inside a black circle.

## Page 6

### [p006_c0001] Phase 2: August 2023 – present
- [p006_c0002] • Customers: 1,600 • Initial customer fare: $4 • Monthly trips: 25 or 40 • Monthly per trip subsidy: $40 or $25 • Annual MTA cost: $4.5M per year at $21 average cost per trip
### [p006_c0003] Phase 3 Plan: Starting July, 2024
- [p006_c0004] • Customers: 1,600 from Phase 2 • Consider adding new customers starting in the fall • Initial customer fare: $4 • Monthly trips: 25 or 40 • Monthly per trip subsidy: $60 • Estimated annual MTA cost: $6-$7M per year at $35 per trip

### [p007_c0001] Goals for E-Hail Phase 3
### [p007_c0002] Customers: Flexibility
- [p007_c0003] • Provide flexible transportation to customers who depend on Access-A-Ride the most
### [p007_c0004] Budget: Cost-Effective E-Hail Program
- [p007_c0005] • Phase 2 E-Hail provided a useful tool for customers, within budget parameters • Benefit is high for most frequent AAR travelers
### [p007_c0006] MTA: Improved Operations
- [p007_c0007] • Access-A-Ride is scheduling up to 36,500 trips per weekday, an all-time record high • E-Hail Phase 3 can potentially augment existing capacity, cost effectively, to help Access-A-Ride accommodate demand
