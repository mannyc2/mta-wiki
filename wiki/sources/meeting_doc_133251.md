### [p001_c0002] January 2024 Crime Report
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this report is to provide Committee Members with statistical information regarding the number of major felonies (including homicide, robbery, assault, and rape) in addition to hate crimes occurring in the NYCT Subway system. The report is submitted by NYPD's Transit Bureau on a monthly basis for the month prior to the reporting period.
