### [p003_c0001] Work Plan
[p003_c0002] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p003_c0003] President's Report
[p003_c0004] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments, safety, operations and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p003_c0005] Police Report
[p003_c0006] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p003_c0007] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0008] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0009] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0010] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0011] Finance Report
[p003_c0012] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0013] Procurements
[p003_c0014] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0015] II. REGULAR INFORMATION ITEMS
### [p003_c0016] Key Performance Metric Report
[p003_c0017] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p003_c0018] III. ANTICIPATED INFORMATION ITEMS
### [p003_c0019] JANUARY 2026
### [p003_c0020] Winter Track Work Programs
