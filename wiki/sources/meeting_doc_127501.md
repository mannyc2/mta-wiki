### [p001_c0002] Standard Follow-Up Report: Transit Adjudication Bureau, 3rd Quarter 2023
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this quarterly report is to update the Transit Committee on Transit Adjudication Bureau (TAB) activities and outcomes, as reflected by several Key Indicators.
[p001_c0004] TAB is the statutory administrative tribunal that receives, processes, adjudicates and pursues collection of civil penalties arising from the tens of thousands of civil summonses that are issued each year for violations of the Transit Rules of Conduct.
[p001_c0005] This quarterly reporting on TAB activities and outcomes commenced in 1992. The report provides the Committee with metrics, covering the most recently completed quarter, for the following Key Indicators:
- [p001_c0006] • Number of TAB violations received by TAB • Number and dollar amount of payments TAB received • TAB revenues and expenses for the quarter • Number of cases adjudicated

### [p002_c0001] STANDARD FOLLOW-UP REPORTS: TRANSIT ADJUDICATION BUREAU THIRD QUARTER 2023
[p002_c0002] The following is a comparison of the key indicators for the third quarter of 2023 as compared to the same period in 2022.
- [p002_c0003] • Summons issued in the third quarter increased by 18% (from 43,506 in 2022 to 51,199 in 2023). • TAB received 27,370 payments in Q3 2023, a 19% increase from the 23,064 received in Q3 2022. Direct payments increased by 21% (from 21,446 to 25,888) compared to the third quarter of 2022. The number of third quarter payments received from state tax decreased by 8% from 1,618 to 1,482. • The total amount paid for the third quarter of 2023 totaled $2,602,464, a 24% increase from the amount paid in the third quarter of 2022, $2,098,359. Receipts from direct payments increased by 23% to $2,368,099 in 2023 as compared to $1,929,501 in the third quarter of 2022. Receipts from state tax refunds for Q3 2023 relating to outstanding judgments from prior years totaled $234,365, representing a 39% increase from Q3 2022 state tax refund receipts of $168,859. • Expenses increased by 54% ($1,343,178 compared to $870,625) from the third quarter of 2022. • Revenue increased by 30% ($2,750,446 compared to $2,113,898) from the third quarter of 2022. • TAB revenue exceeded expenses by $1,407,267 compared to $1,243,273 for the third quarter of 2022.
