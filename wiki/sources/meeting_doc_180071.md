### [p014_c0001] Ticketing policies on the commuter railroads
### [p014_c0002] ► Customers will no longer need to activate one-way tickets
- [p014_c0003] • All mobile tickets will auto-activate upon purchase • Tickets will remain valid for 4 hours
### [p014_c0004] Benefits
- [p014_c0005] • Simplifies the user experience • Speeds up the ticket validation process • Eliminates customers ability to reuse tickets on a later trip

## Page 15

### [p015_c0001] Ticketing policies on the commuter railroads
### [p015_c0002] ► A reduced fare product will be available 24/7 for seniors, people with disabilities, and people on Medicare
- [p015_c0003] • A reduced fare ticket will be valid at all times, even in the morning peak • Maintain the 50% discount on reduced fares
### [p015_c0004] Benefits
- [p015_c0005] • Simplifies the customer experience • Reduces onboard transactions • Reduces the fare for many customers in the AM peak

## Page 16

### [p016_c0001] Ticketing policies on the commuter railroads
### [p016_c0002] ► Customers can buy a Day Pass for unlimited travel
- [p016_c0003] • Valid until 4 a.m. the next day. • Weekday pass: 10% discount on the price of two peak one-way tickets • Weekend passes: Price of two off-peak tickets
### [p016_c0004] Benefits
- [p016_c0005] • Ideal for the day-tripper and remote worker with occasional in-office days • Simplifies the user experience • Allows for additional stops enroute and may encourage new, hop-on / hop-off discretionary travel

## Page 17

### [p017_c0001] Ticketing policies on the commuter railroads
### [p017_c0002] ► After 10 trips, you'll automatically get an 11 th ticket for free
- [p017_c0003] • Available on TrainTime only • All trips need to be taken within 2 weeks between the same zones • Free trip can be used in peak or off-peak
### [p017_c0004] Benefits
- [p017_c0005] • Ideal for the hybrid commuter, offering a more flexible fare • Discount available with any combination of peak or off-peak trips • Avoids need to pre-pay for 10 tickets to receive a discount
[p017_c0006] _17_

### [p018_c0001] Ticketing policies on the commuter railroads
### [p018_c0002] ► Recurring late mobile ticket purchasers will be subject to a surcharge, just like onboard paper tickets

### [p018_c0004] Benefits
- [p018_c0005] • Reduces onboard conflict and helps crews move more quickly through the train • Avoids punishing new or infrequent customers
