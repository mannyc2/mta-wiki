### [p001_c0003] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0004] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to adjust schedules for a Switch Installation in Queens Interlocking in support of the Queens Reconfiguration Project, A Signal Cutover in Mineola in support of the Main Line Expansion Project, Switch Maintenance in Valley Stream, Concrete Tie Replacement near Floral Park, and Track Maintenance near Patchogue.
### [p001_c0005] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0006] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0007] • Main Line – Switch Installation – On the weekend of September 24-25, all main tracks on the Main Line will be out of service between New Hyde Park and Hicksville for approximately 48 hours for a switch installation in Queens Interlocking as part of the Queens Reconfiguration Project. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: ▪ Eastbound, no MU service will operate between Penn Station and Hicksville, and two-hourly Oyster Bay Branch diesel service will operate between East Williston and Oyster Bay. For Eastbound customers from western terminals traveling to Ronkonkoma Branch stations Farmingdale through Ronkonkoma, and to Port Jefferson Branch stations Hicksville through Huntington/Port Jefferson, customers will board electric MU express service to Babylon, where they will transfer to diesel shuttle trains operating between Babylon and Hicksville via the Central Branch, then transfer at Hicksville to trains operating to either Ronkonkoma or Huntington/Port Jefferson. Eastbound Bethpage customers will board the electric MU express service to Babylon, then transfer to the diesel shuttle train, which will stop at Bethpage on its way to Hicksville.
