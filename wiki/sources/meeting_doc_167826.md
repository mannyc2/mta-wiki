### [p023_c0001] The MTA is doing more on its own to fund safety and security measures
[p023_c0002] MTA moves 43% of the nation's transit riders and receives just 17% of all federal formula funding.
[p023_c0003] Despite limited federal assistance and shrinking national commitment to transit security, MTA invested $513M on safety and security in 2024, 35 times more than the minimum federal requirements of its safety and security grants, on initiatives including:
- [p023_c0004] • Camera and communication systems • LED lighting in stations • Property protections • Fire system upgrades • Detection systems • Emergency exits
[p023_c0005] This level of investment will continue even as federal support declines.
