### [p014_p0001] Response to Community Feedback
- [p014_p0002] • Concerns about congestion
- [p014_p0003] • Analysis of traffic counts show that peak volumes can be accommodated with three lanes of traffic
- [p014_p0004] • Requests to retain parking
- [p014_p0005] • Parking will be retained as much as possible with floating parking lane and offset bus lane
- [p014_p0006] • Corridor will be studied for loading zones to improve curb access
- [p014_p0007] • Parking will only be affected to improve visibility, shorten crossings for pedestrians.
- [p014_p0008] • Concerns about unpredictable cyclist behavior
- [p014_p0009] • Adding a dedicated bike lane will increase predictability and reduce conflicts between motorists and cyclists
- [p014_p0010] • Need for pedestrian improvements
- [p014_p0011] • Project includes pedestrian improvements at intersections
- [p014_p0012] • Concerns about turning buses, specifically at 106 St, 116 St, and 125 St intersections
- [p014_p0013] • Design changes ie: offset crossings + daylighting will increase visibility and slow turning vehicles
