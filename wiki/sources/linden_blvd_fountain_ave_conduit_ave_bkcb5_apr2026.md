- [p022_c0002] 1. Count traffic and pedestrians at approx. 30 intersections on Linden Blvd and nearby streets 2. Analyze vehicle origins and destinations with anonymized GPS data from drivers on Linden Blvd 3. Model each intersection and analyze: • Signal timing • Number of lanes and turning movements (left, straight, right) • Pedestrian volumes • Existing conditions and multiple potential future scenarios
