### [p001_c0004] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0005] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to temporarily adjust schedules to support state of good repair track maintenance at Bayside and state of good repair switch replacement at East New York.
### [p001_c0006] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0007] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0008] Port Washington Branch – Tie Replacement and Surfacing – Bay Interlocking - On the weekend of June 28-29, one of two main tracks between Mets-Willets Point and Great Neck will be taken out of service while we install new concrete ties, perform track surfacing and switch maintenance activities at Bay Interlocking in Bayside to maintain state of good repair and provide a smoother, quieter ride for our customers. Temporary Service Adjustments: With one of two main tracks out of service between Mets-Willets Point and Great Neck on the weekend of June 28-29, train service on the Port Washington Branch will be reduced to hourly to and from Penn

- [p002_c0002] • East New York – Switch Replacement – On the weekends of July 26-27 and August 2-3, all tracks will be out of service between Jamaica and Atlantic Terminal while we perform switch replacement work at East New York in order to maintain this portion of our system to a state of good repair and improve reliability. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With all tracks out of service between Jamaica and Atlantic Terminal, there will be no LIRR train service to East New York, Nostrand Avenue or Atlantic Terminal. NYCT will cross honor LIRR fares.
### [p002_c0003] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p002_c0004] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
