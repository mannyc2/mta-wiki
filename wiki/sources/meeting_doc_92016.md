### [p003_c0001] Traffic Mobility Review Board (TMRB): What It Is
[p003_c0002] The TBTA Board (statutorily consisting of same members as MTA Board) establishes the TMRB and names its Chair and five members:
- [p003_c0003] One recommended by the Mayor of the City of New York One must reside in the Metro-North Railroad Region One must reside in the Long Island Rail Road Region Each must have experience in at least one of the following areas: public finance; transportation; mass transit; or management
[p003_c0004] TMRB shall conduct its meetings open to the public in accordance with Open Meetings Law (schedule TBD)
[p003_c0005] Appointing TMRB members today allows them to observe and benefit from the August public hearings and to begin reviewing and understanding information provided by the CBDTP Team
[p003_c0006] _Bridges and Tunnels_

## Page 4

### [p004_c0001] Traffic Mobility Review Board: Their Responsibilities
[p004_c0002] TMRB shall consider factors including but not limited to traffic patterns, traffic mitigation measures, operating costs, public impact, public safety, hardships, vehicle type, discounts for motorcycles, peak and off-peak rates and environmental impacts, including but not limited to air quality and emissions trends.
[p004_c0003] TMRB shall recommend:
- [p004_c0004] CBD toll amounts that reduce congestion and ensure collection of annual net revenues and fees needed to fund at least $15 billion for 2020-24 capital program A plan for credits, discounts, and/or exemptions for tolls paid on bridges & crossings A plan for credits, discounts, and/or exemptions for for-hire vehicles
[p004_c0005] TMRB must also produce a report on its review and analysis underlying its recommendations
