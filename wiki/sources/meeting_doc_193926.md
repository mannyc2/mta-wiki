### [p004_c0001] Metro-North Railroad
### [p004_c0002] Annual Emergency Management Exercise
### [p004_c0003] Scenario
[p004_c0004] A portable electric scooter fire on a passenger train. Crew had to evacuate riders, secure the scene, report to Operations Control, and coordinate with local fire and MTAPD responders.
### [p004_c0005] Agencies Involved
[p004_c0006] Metro-North Railroad, MTA Police, Poughkeepsie Fire Department, Amtrak Emergency Management.
### [p004_c0007] Objectives
- [p004_c0008] 1. Practice Metro-North's Passenger Train Emergency Preparedness Plan and use of emergency gear. 2. Test how well each agency handles rescue operations during an onboard fire. 3. Prove they can coordinate effectively during a multi-agency incident.
