### [p002_c0001] Security Grant Program – Funding Sources
### [p002_c0002] □ Transit Security Grant Program (TSGP)
- [p002_c0003] □ For eligible public transportation systems. □ MTA is a Direct Recipient from FEMA. □ Protect critical transportation infrastructure and the travelling public from terrorism.
### [p002_c0004] □ Port Security Grant Program (PSGP)
- [p002_c0005] □ For port authorities, facility operators, and state and local agencies. □ MTA is a Direct Recipient from FEMA. □ Implementing Area Maritime Security Plans, facility security plans, and other port-wide risk management efforts.
### [p002_c0006] □ Urban Area Security Initiative (UASI)
- [p002_c0007] □ For high-risk urban areas. □ MTA is a Sub-Recipient from NYSDHSES □ Capabilities necessary to prevent, prepare for, protect against, and respond to acts of terrorism.
