## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] 34 th Street Project Need
- [p003_c0002] • Slow Bus Speeds: avg. speed of 4.5 mph, over 33,000 daily bus riders • Pedestrian Safety: some of the most congested sidewalks in NYC • Curbside loading: key concern of residents and businesses • Future growth: tens of thousands of new residents and jobs coming to 34 th St.
[p003_c0003] _New York City Transit_

## Page 4

### [p004_c0001] Community Feedback
- [p004_c0002] • Loading/curb access is a key issue – improve curbside access wherever possible • Traffic on side streets is already an issue – maintain 2-way traffic on 34 th Street if possible • Pedestrian improvements are very important – and should be focused where they are needed most • Each improvement should be fully evaluated – and the community kept involved throughout the process
[p004_c0003] _New York City Transit_

## Page 19

### [p019_c0001] Project Benefits
- [p019_c0002] • Bus Service : improves bus reliability and increase bus speeds for over 33,000 daily riders • Pedestrians : adds 18,000 sq. ft. of new pedestrian space, reducing crowding and improving safety • Loading : increases daytime loading from 55 to 355 spaces with a loading zone on every block • Design : uses standard bus and pedestrian design elements; emergency vehicles could use the improved bus lanes • Traffic : maintains 2-way traffic from river to river, avoids traffic spillover onto parallel side streets
[p019_c0003] _New York City Transit_
