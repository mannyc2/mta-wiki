### [p006_c0001] Bus Rapid Transit & Select Bus Service
[p006_c0002] Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) – a cost-effective approach to transit service that cities around the world have used to make riding the bus more like a subway. BRT improves:
- [p006_c0003] • Speed • Reliability • Passenger comfort & convenience
[p006_c0004] Select Bus Service (SBS) - New York City's version of BRT, first used on the Fordham Road-Pelham Parkway Bx12 SBS route
