### [p001_c0002] February 2025 Crime Report
[p001_c0003] The purpose of this report is to provide Committee Members with statistical information regarding the number of major felonies (including homicide, robbery, assault, and rape) in addition to hate crimes occurring in the NYCT Subway system.
