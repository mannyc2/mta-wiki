### [p003_c0001] 3. APPROVAL OF MEETING MINUTES
[p003_c0002] Upon motion duly made and seconded, the Board approved the Minutes of the Committee meeting held on March 27, 2023.
[p003_c0003] Refer to the video recording of the meeting, produced by the MTA and maintained in MTA records, for the details.
### [p003_c0004] 4. WORK PLAN REPORT
[p003_c0005] President Richard Davey stated that there were no changes to the 2023 Work Plan.
### [p003_c0006] 5. PRESIDENT’S REPORT
[p003_c0007] President Davey delivered the President’s Report.
[p003_c0008] President Davey reported that the MTA had received an FTA “Champion of the Challenge” award for its Zero-Emission Bus Transition Plan.
[p003_c0009] President Davey commented that the Faster, Cleaner Safer initiatives continue to put the focus on customer experience. He noted as an example that the coming expansion of the automated bus lane enforcement camera program will increase bus speeds and bus customer satisfaction. He also referred to record high subway on-time performance for three straight months.
[p003_c0010] President Davey stated that subway station agents have begun to support customers outside of station booths and that three additional Customer Service Centers have opened.
[p003_c0011] Department of Buses Chief of Staff and Vice President, Business Strategies Zafira Lateef and Deputy General Manager James Coyle reported on an initiative for targeted improvements on 28 lower performing routes that will benefit 20% of bus ridership.
[p003_c0012] Refer to the video recording of the meeting, produced by the MTA and maintained in MTA records, for the details.
### [p003_c0013] 6. ACCESSIBILITY
[p003_c0014] Quemuel Arroyo, Chief Accessibility Officer, delivered the Accessibility Report.
[p003_c0015] Arroyo reported that that the wide aisle fare gate pilot had begun at two stations.
[p003_c0016] Arroyo also referred to the new and improved My AAR app that allows trips to be booked on-line.
### [p003_c0017] 7. SAFETY & SECURITY REPORT
[p003_c0018] Deputy Chief Norman Grandstaff delivered the Safety and Security Report. He provided the latest crime statistics.
