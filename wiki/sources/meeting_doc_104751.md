### [p001_c0003] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0004] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to adjust schedules to support track maintenance on the Long Beach Branch.
### [p001_c0005] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0006] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0007] • Track Maintenance – Long Beach Branch – On Saturday, February 11 th both main tracks will be taken out of service between Valley Stream and Long Beach for a period of approximately 24-hours while track maintenance is performed. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With 2 main tracks out of service between Valley Stream and Long Beach, bus service will replace train service in both directions for a period of approximately 24 hours.
### [p001_c0008] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p001_c0009] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget
