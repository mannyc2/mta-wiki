### [p003_c0001] MONTHLY MEETING OF TRIBOROUGH BRIDGE AND TUNNEL AUTHORITY COMMITTEE
[p003_c0002] December 15, 2025
[p003_c0003] Minutes of TBTA Committee held December 15, 2025 at 9:30 a.m. A list of those in attendance is attached.
### [p003_c0004] Public Speakers
[p003_c0005] The following speakers commented during the public speakers' session:
- [p003_c0006] • Mr. X about elevating BQE lanes to construct an additional subway station on the 7 line. • Joseph Morales about ghost plate taskforce expansion, HLC project noise potential, and developing equity-driven solutions to toll evasion. • Jesse Figueroa about continuing to prioritize accessibility and affordability. • Aleta Dupree about congestion pricing and E-ZPass usage.
### [p003_c0007] Minutes
[p003_c0008] As a quorum of the Committee was not in attendance, Acting Chair O'Donnell postponed the approval of the TBTA portion of the minutes of the Joint-agency Committee Meeting held on November 19, 2025.
### [p003_c0009] Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0010] President Sheridan stated that there are no changes to the TBTA Committee Work Plan, noting that TBTA's proposed 2026 Committee Work Plan was submitted for review at the November meeting. President Sheridan postponed the approval of the proposed 2026 Committee Work Plan as a quorum of the Committee was not in attendance.
### [p003_c0011] Overview of TBTA's Eastern Facilities
[p003_c0012] Senior Vice President for Facility Management, Marlene Thompson, provided an overview of TBTA's Eastern facilities, which encompass the Bronx-Whitestone and Throgs Neck bridges. Refer to the video recording of the meeting, produced by the MTA and maintained in MTA records, for the details of the presentation.
### [p003_c0013] Recognition of Donald Look
[p003_c0014] Recognizing a milestone for one of the TBTA Executive Staff, President Sheridan congratulated Senior Vice President and Chief of Internal Affairs, Donald Look, on 25 years of dedicated service to TBTA.
### [p003_c0015] 2025 Closing Remarks
[p003_c0016] President Sheridan expressed her gratitude to all of TBTA's employees for their hard work and dedication, and extended her thanks to TBTA's MTA business partners who support TBTA's operations. President Sheridan also thanked the TBTA Committee Members for their guidance and support. Looking forward to a productive 2026 with more new achievements and milestones to celebrate, President Sheridan wished everyone a peaceful, safe, and festive holiday season, and a very Happy New Year.
