### [p001_c0001] What's Happening Here?
[p001_c0002] Improve bus travel times
[p001_c0003] Improve bus reliability
### [p001_c0004] WOODHAVEN BOULEVARD BUS LANE IMPROVEMENTS
[p001_c0005] Organize vehicle movements
[p001_c0006] Improve mobility
[p001_c0007] DOT will implement offset (one lane away from the curb) bus lanes on Woodhaven Boulevard between Dry Harbor Road to Metropolitan Avenue:
- [p001_c0008] ▪ Bus lanes will be in effect Monday through Friday from 7-10 AM and from 4-7 PM ▪ Bus lanes will improve bus travel times and reliability for the 30,000 daily bus riders on this corridor ▪ Parking will remain at the curb with the same regulations as existing ▪ Vehicles can enter or cross over a bus lane to make right turns, access a curb cut or driveway within 200', enter a parking space, or to quickly drop-off or pick up passengers
### [p001_c0009] Implementation begins Mid-July
> [p001_c0010] The diagram illustrates the proposed changes to bus lanes on Woodhaven Boulevard. It is divided into two horizontal sections: EXISTING and PROPOSED . EXISTING Configuration: West Sidewalk: Parking / Bus Stop (20' wide), followed by four 11' wide Travel Lanes. Median Island: 14' wide, 10' Turn Bays. East Sidewalk: Four 11' wide Travel Lanes, followed by Parking / Bus Stop (20' wide). PROPOSED Configuration: West Sidewalk: Parking / Bus Stop (8' wide), followed by a 12' wide Bus Lane (7-10 AM, 4-7 PM Mon-Fri; Travel Lane Other Times), followed by three 11' wide Travel Lanes. Median Island: 14' wide, 10' Turn Bays. East Sidewalk: Three 11' wide Travel Lanes, followed by a 12' wide Bus Lane (7-10 AM, 4-7 PM Mon-Fri; Travel Lane Other Times), followed by Parking / Bus Stop (8' wide). A total width of 120' is indicated for the entire corridor.
