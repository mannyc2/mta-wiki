### [p003_c0002] Procurements
[p003_c0003] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.

[p003_c0005] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key operating and performance statistics and indicators.

[p003_c0007] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast both on an accrual and cash basis.
### [p003_c0008] Ridership
[p003_c0009] A monthly report will be provided that includes a comparison of actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues with the budget and prior year results.
### [p003_c0010] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0011] OCTOBER 2023
### [p003_c0012] 2024 Preliminary Budget
[p003_c0013] Public comment will be accepted on the 2024 Budget.
### [p003_c0014] LIRR/MNR PTC Project Update
[p003_c0015] The Committee will be briefed on the status of project implementation and close-out following full Positive Train Control functionality for both railroads which went into effect in December 2020.
### [p003_c0016] Track Program Quarterly Update
[p003_c0017] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
### [p003_c0018] NOVEMBER 2023
### [p003_c0019] Joint Railroad Strategic Priorities Update
[p003_c0020] In alignment with MTA's strategic priorities, a biannual report to the Committee on the Railroads' progress in delivering safe and reliable transportation and excellent customer service.
### [p003_c0021] DECEMBER 2023
### [p003_c0022] 2024 Final Proposed Budget
[p003_c0023] The Committee will recommend action to the Board on the Final Proposed Budget for 2024.
### [p003_c0024] 2024 Proposed Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0025] The Committee Chair will present a draft Metro-North Committee Work Plan for 2024 that will address initiatives to be reported throughout the year.
### [p003_c0026] Diversity & EEO Report– 3 rd Quarter 2023
[p003_c0027] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
