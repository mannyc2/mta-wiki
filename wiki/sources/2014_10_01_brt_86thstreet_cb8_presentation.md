### [p003_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p003_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p003_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p003_c0004] • 15-20% faster bus speeds • 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Improved passenger comfort • and convenience
[p003_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.

### [p010_c0001] Project Background
[p010_c0002] The M86 bus corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the “Bus Rapid Transit: Phase II Study” (2009).
- [p010_c0003] • Most passengers per mile of any NYCT bus route • Heavily used route with slow trips • Carries over 25,000 passengers per day, making it the second-busiest cross-town bus route • Crucial connection to 1 4 5 6 B C trains • Connection to 12 bus routes including M15 SBS, M101 and M4
