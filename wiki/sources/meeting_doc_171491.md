## Page 2

### [p002_c0001] A NJ Transit strike would affect West of Hudson Metro-North riders on the Pascack Valley and Port Jervis Lines
- [p002_c0002] • Metro-North customers on West of Hudson service depend on NJ Transit to reach Hoboken or Secaucus Junction, and ultimately NY Penn Station • A strike by NJ Transit train operators could begin on Friday May 16 at 12:01 AM • In the event of a strike, there will be no Metro-North West of Hudson service

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] West of Hudson riders have a great alternative: The Hudson and Harlem Lines
[p003_c0002] Metro-North will accommodate its West of Hudson customers on the Hudson and Harlem Lines:
- [p003_c0003] • All fare tickets will be cross-honored • Parking will be cross-honored at five stations (Cortlandt, Beacon, New Hamburg, Poughkeepsie and North White Plains) • Free bus and ferry connections for West of Hudson ticket holders • The Hudson Line will operate with additional train cars
[p003_c0004] Metro-North will monitor ridership to determine additional service needs for:
- [p003_c0005] • Shuttle bus or ferry service across the river • Train service on Hudson or Harlem Lines

### [p004_c0001] Example: Salisbury Mills to Manhattan
### [p004_c0002] Current West of Hudson trip
- [p004_c0003] • Drive to Salisbury Mills Station on the Port Jervis Line • Choice of 5 morning AM rush trains • 87 minutes to Secaucus Transfer and then NY Penn (March OTP: 90.9%)
### [p004_c0004] East of Hudson alternative
- [p004_c0005] • Drive to Beacon Station on the Hudson Line • Parking is cross-honored • Choice of 12 morning AM rush trains from Beacon • 78-minute, one-seat ride to Grand Central (March OTP: 99.1%)
