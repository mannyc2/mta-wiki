### [p002_c0003] Completed Fall 2015
### [p002_c0004] Dedicated Bus Lanes
[p002_c0005] B46 buses now have their own dedicated lane , moving passengers faster while making the street more predictable for drivers.
### [p002_c0006] Bus Priority at Lights
[p002_c0007] B46 SBS buses are equipped with transit signal priority (TSP) technology which communicates with traffic signals so the bus spends less time waiting at traffic lights.
### [p002_c0008] Coming Spring 2016
### [p002_c0009] Faster Fare Payment
[p002_c0010] With off-board fare payment, riders will pay their fare before boarding the bus with a MetroCard or coins and board through any door, greatly reducing the time waiting to pay at the front of the bus.
### [p002_c0011] Real-Time Information
[p002_c0012] Bus Time displays and neighborhood maps are coming to most B46 SBS stops, so riders will know when the next SBS or Local bus is coming in real time.
### [p002_c0013] Coming 2017-2018
### [p002_c0014] Bus Bulbs (Curb Extensions)
[p002_c0015] By extending the sidewalk into the street to meet the bus, riders have more room to wait and board safely and comfortably.

### [p003_c0002] How Does Off-Board Fare Payment Work?
[p003_c0003] B46 SBS riders must dip their MetroCards or use coins to get a ticket to ride before boarding the bus. The cost is the same as a Limited bus, Local bus or the subway. Transfers are stored on the MetroCard. If paying by coins, show the ticket to the bus operator to get a paper transfer MetroCard. Riders then board the bus at any door, greatly reducing wait times at bus stops.
