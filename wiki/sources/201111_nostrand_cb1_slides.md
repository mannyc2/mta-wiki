### [p002_c0001] Nostrand Ave/Rogers Ave Corridor
[p002_c0002] 9.3 miles from Williamsburg Bridge to Sheepshead Bay
[p002_c0003] Currently served by B44 bus route
- [p002_c0004] • 42,000 weekday riders – 7th busiest bus route in city • 2,000 people board in CB 1 • Buses travel at an average speed of 7-8 mph
[p002_c0005] Within a ¼ mile:
- [p002_c0006] • 300,000 residents • 62% of households do not own a car (69% in CB 1) • 60% of residents commute by transit (58% in CB1, compared to 21% by car, truck, or van)

### [p005_c0001] SBS Results in NYC
