### [p017_c0004] Advance Signal
- [p017_c0005] ■ Green time for Hylan Boulevard is limited at busy intersections ■ Goal: Move as many vehicles as possible on green signal ■ Issue: Weaving of cars and buses clogs the intersection
> [p017_c0006] The diagram illustrates a traffic intersection with three lanes. The top lane contains four cars moving right. The middle lane contains two cars moving right. This weaving movement is the issue being addressed by the 'Advance Signal' study.

### [p018_c0001] Hylan Boulevard Transportation Improvement Study
### [p018_c0002] Advance Signal
- [p018_c0003] ■ Solution: Separate bus and car weaving before light turns green ■ Reduced weaving allows more efficient (and safer) flow through intersection ■ Allows buses to avoid congestion without having a bus lane at a major intersection.
> [p018_c0004] The diagram illustrates the 'Advance Signal' solution for Hylan Boulevard. It shows a bus lane (red) and two car lanes (grey) merging into a single lane. The bus lane is highlighted in red, and the car lanes are grey. The diagram shows the bus and cars weaving before the light turns green, illustrating the solution for congestion.
