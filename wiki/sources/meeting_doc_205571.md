### [p004_c0001] Recent Accomplishments
> [p004_c0002] System-wide camera expansion • 10,000 new or upgraded cameras have been installed systemwide since May 2025. An estimated 800 additional conductor cab camera systems will be in service by Jan 2027. Capital Plan Projects 2025-2029 • MTA's Capital Plan Projects for 2025-2029 focuses on expanding our camera infrastructure to enhance rider safety. Grant Funding • In 2025, a $3.6 million Transit Security Grant (FEMA) was awarded for the purchase of a Tactical Deployable Camera System. Enhanced Video Sharing • MTA has enhanced video sharing with our law enforcement partners for improved information sharing and investigative support.
