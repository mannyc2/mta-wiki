### [p002_c0026] DETAILED SUMMARY
### [p002_c0027] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0028] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0029] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0030] Work Plan
[p002_c0031] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics anticipated to be presented to the Committee for action or for information. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
[p002_c0032] .
### [p002_c0033] President's Report
[p002_c0034] A monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments including safety, operations and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0035] Police Report
[p002_c0036] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p002_c0037] Information Items (if any)
[p002_c0038] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p002_c0039] Action Items (if any)
[p002_c0040] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p002_c0041] Financial Report
[p002_c0042] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.

### [p003_c0001] Procurements
[p003_c0002] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0003] II REGULAR INFORMATION ITEMS
### [p003_c0004] Key Performance Metric Report
[p003_c0005] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p003_c0006] III INFORMATION ITEMS
### [p003_c0007] JANUARY 2026
### [p003_c0008] 2025 Operations Year in Review
[p003_c0009] A review of the prior year's performance of railroad will be provided to the Committee.
### [p003_c0010] FEBRUARY 2026
### [p003_c0011] Adopted Budget/Financial Plan 2026
[p003_c0012] The Agency will present its revised 2026 Financial Plan. These plans will reflect the 2026 Adopted Budget and an updated Financial Plan for 2026 reflecting the out-year impact of any changes incorporated into the 2026 Adopted Budget.
### [p003_c0013] MARCH 2026
### [p003_c0014] Annual Elevator/Escalator Report
[p003_c0015] Annual report to the Committee on system-wide reliability and availability for elevators and escalators throughout the system.
### [p003_c0016] Diversity & EEO Report– 4 th Quarter 2025
[p003_c0017] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p003_c0018] Schedule Change
[p003_c0019] The Committee will be advised of the upcoming schedule changes for the Railroad.
### [p003_c0020] APRIL 2026
### [p003_c0021] 2025 Annual Ridership Report
[p003_c0022] A report will be presented to the Committee on Metro-North's ridership trends during 2025 based on monthly ticket sales data and the results of train ridership counts conducted by Metro-North.
### [p003_c0023] MAY 2026
### [p003_c0024] Final Review of 2025 Operating Budget Results
[p003_c0025] A review of the prior year's budget results and their implications for current and future budget performance will be presented to the Committee.
