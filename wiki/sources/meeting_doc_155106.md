[p003_c0008] AGENCY: MTA Long Island Rail Road (“LIRR”) PERMITTEE: Kew Gardens Flea and Craft Market, Inc. LOCATION: LIRR’s Kew Gardens Station (“Station”), North Parking Lot PREMISES: Approximately 30 parking spaces USE: Use of the Station’s North Parking Lot for flea market event TERM: Saturday, September 21, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. Rain Date: Saturday, September 28, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. Saturday, October 12, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. Rain Date: Saturday, October 19, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. Saturday, December 7, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. Rain Date: Saturday, December 14, 2024, 5:30 a.m. to 11:59 p.m.
[p003_c0009] COMPENSATION: $5.19 per space or $155.70 per day totaling $467.10 (for all three flea market events)
### [p003_c0010] COMMENTS:
[p003_c0011] Kew Gardens Flea and Craft Market, Inc., a not-for-profit organization, approached the MTA requesting a permit to use up to 30 parking spaces at the Station’s North Parking Lot on the above-noted dates for flea market events. Most vendors operate using tables, but a few with tents, were secured in place. Each vendor was allowed up to three people seated behind their tables or within their tents. All excess merchandise, packaging materials, and boxes were secured under the vendor’s tables and did not obstruct any pedestrian walkways or rights-of-way at any time.
[p003_c0012] This permit was granted pursuant to MTA Board-approved policy on January 24, 2011, governing the temporary use of MTA-parking facilities in connection with short-term commercial activities which allows for the issuance of short-term permits, MTA Real Estate has approved this transaction and entered into a Permit to Enter Upon and Use Property agreement with Kew Gardens Flea and Craft Market, Inc. as described above.

[p005_c0008] AGENCY: MTA Long Island Rail Road ("LIRR") GRANTOR: The Town of Islip (the "Town") LOCATION: Located on the north side of LIRR's Babylon Branch line, south of West Islip Boulevard, Babylon, NY. EASEMENT AREA: Easement 1: Approximately one hundred thirty four (134) square feet Easement 2: Approximately ninety (90) square feet USE: Installation, operation and maintenance of LIRR signal huts TERM: Perpetual COMPENSATION: $7,500.00
### [p005_c0009] COMMENTS:
[p005_c0010] MTA Real Estate received a property acquisition request from the LIRR to acquire two permanent easements from the Town of Islip in support of the LIRR's Babylon Interlocking Signal System and Upgrade Project. The two permanent easements are located on the Town's property, south of West Islip Boulevard, and are necessary for the installation of new signal huts.
[p005_c0011] This easement was acquired pursuant to the MTA Board-approved policy dated September 21, 2015, and last modified June 27, 2023, which authorizes MTA Real Estate to license, lease or acquire property rights in support of MTA capital projects or ongoing operations when it is reasonably estimated that MTA's aggregate obligation to pay compensation or reimbursement to the licensor, lessor, or grantor will be less than $100,000 over the term. The agreement was reviewed and approved as to form by MTA Legal.
