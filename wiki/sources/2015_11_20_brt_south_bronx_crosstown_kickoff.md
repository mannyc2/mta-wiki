### [p012_c0001] Opportunity: Select Bus Service
[p012_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is NYC's brand name for a package of improvements that result in faster and more reliable bus service.
[p012_c0003] SBS also brings:
- [p012_c0004] • High customer satisfaction • Improved passenger comfort and convenience • Safer streets and sidewalks • More consistent traffic flow
