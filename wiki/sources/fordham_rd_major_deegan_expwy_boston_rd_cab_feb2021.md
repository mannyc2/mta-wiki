### [p005_c0001] Why Fordham Road
### [p005_c0002] Background
- [p005_c0003] • Critical crosstown transportation corridor • 93,700 average daily bus riders on 9 routes, serving neighborhoods with high concentrations of essential workers • Bus riders and pedestrians are the majority of roadway users on Fordham Road • 62% of households on Bx12 corridor have no access to a private vehicle. 71% commute to work via public transit, walking, or biking • Residents in neighborhoods near Fordham Road have high rates of asthma, obesity, diabetes, and heart disease, in part due to exposure to pollution
