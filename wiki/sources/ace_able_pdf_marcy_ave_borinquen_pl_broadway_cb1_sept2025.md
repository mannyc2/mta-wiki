### [p016_c0001] Parking Impact
- [p016_c0002] The proposed changes would remove parking from two block faces: between S 4 St & S 5 St – 12 spaces between S 5 St & Broadway – 9 spaces Parking considerations: Overnight and AM Peak parking utilization rates on these two blocks are low, ranging from 11% to 58%. The adjacent land-uses (mostly parkland) do not draw high parking demand. There is spare on-street parking on adjacent overpass blocks (low parking demand on overpasses). There is off-street parking on adjacent blocks for the largest residential buildings: The Dime Residences – 124 spaces. The Williams – 153 spaces.
