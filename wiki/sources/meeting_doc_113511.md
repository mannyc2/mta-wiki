[p001_b0033] Row 28: [Sheet1!A28] *MTA’s fuel hedging program mitigates budget risk by dollar cost averaging approximately 50% of projected fuel expenses. The goal of the program is to be 50% hedged for the next 12 months. Monthly hedges are equal to approximately 4% of the projected fuel purchases expected to occur 13-24 months from each hedge execution date. The actual percentage hedged could be higher or lower than the 50% target depending on the actual levels of fuel consumption. While the fuel hedging program includes compressed natural gas, there are currently no active hedges.
