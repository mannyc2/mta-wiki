- [p013_c0003] • Two-way Transitway for length of 34 th Street aligned along curb • Single side alignment selected based on street width and corridor needs – street is too narrow to place Transitway down center of street, and allow parking on either side of the street. • Transitway occupies half of street, north half east of Fifth Avenue, south half east of Sixth Avenue, with a new plaza from Fifth to Sixth • General Traffic: One-Way Eastbound only east of Fifth Avenue One-Way Westbound only west of Sixth Avenue
