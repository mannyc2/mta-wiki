### [p002_c0001] Transit & Truck Priority
[p002_c0002] Tremont Ave. will become a busway:
- [p002_c0003] ■ Eastbound from Third Ave. to Southern Blvd. ■ Westbound from Southern Blvd. to Belmont Ave.
[p002_c0004] This busway is designed to:
- [p002_c0005] ■ Increase bus speeds and reliability for 34,000+ daily bus riders on the Bx36 on Tremont Ave. ■ Improve truck movement and deliveries to local businesses along Tremont Ave.
### [p002_c0006] New Tremont Ave. Busway Regulations
### [p002_c0007] 6 AM – 8 PM / 7 days a week
[p002_c0008] Only buses, trucks with 6+ wheels, Access-A-Ride vans, emergency vehicles, and bikes may travel all the way through on Tremont Ave., eastbound from Third Ave. to Southern Blvd., and westbound from Southern Blvd. to Belmont Ave.
[p002_c0009] Passenger vehicles, including for-hire vehicles, may make local trips by turning onto the busway from a side street. Passenger vehicles may expeditiously drop off and pick up passengers all along the busway.
[p002_c0010] After turning onto the busway, passenger vehicles must make the first possible right turn off the busway. All left turns off the busway are prohibited, except trucks may turn left at Third Ave. The map on the other side of this brochure shows how vehicles can access each block of the busway.
### [p002_c0011] Frequently Asked Questions
### [p002_c0012] Are passenger vehicles, including for-hire vehicles, allowed on the busway?
[p002_c0013] Yes, all passenger and for-hire vehicles are allowed to enter the Tremont Ave. Busway for access to all blocks. Vehicles must turn onto the busway from a side street and make the next available right turn.
### [p002_c0014] How will I know what the rules are when I'm driving on Tremont Ave.?
[p002_c0015] Every block of the busway will have red paint and “Bus Truck Only” markings. Posted signage on Tremont Ave. and intersecting streets explain the rules.
### [p002_c0016] If I can't drive all the way down Tremont Ave., what other routes can I use?
[p002_c0017] If you are traveling westbound, you can drive on 180th St., 179th St., 175th St., the Cross Bronx Expressway, or other routes. If you are traveling eastbound, you can drive on 180th St., Crotona Park North, the Cross Bronx Expressway, or other routes.
### [p002_c0018] Is parking still allowed on the busway?
[p002_c0019] Yes, parking is allowed on the busway. Follow the posted parking regulations.
### [p002_c0020] Which vehicles are considered trucks?
[p002_c0021] A truck is any vehicle that has more than two axles or six or more wheels.
### [p002_c0022] How is the busway enforced?
[p002_c0023] NYPD traffic agents and automated cameras enforce regulations by giving warnings and summonses. Violating bus lane rules results in a $50-$250 fine.
### [p002_c0024] How can cyclists use the busway?
[p002_c0025] Cyclists may ride in the Tremont Ave. bike lane for the length of the busway. They do not need to make any of the required turns.
