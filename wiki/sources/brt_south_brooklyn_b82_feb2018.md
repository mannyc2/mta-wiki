### [p003_c0001] B82 Project Background
[p003_c0002] The Southern Brooklyn B82 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p003_c0003] • Serves 28,000 daily riders on B82 Limited and Local • Heavily used route with slow trips • Important east-west transit connection across Southern Brooklyn • Connects to D N F B Q L subway lines and 21 bus routes, including B44 Nostrand SBS and B46 Utica SBS • Serves areas far from the subway

### [p030_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p030_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
