### [p004_c0001] Project Background
[p004_c0002] The Utica Avenue B46 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the 2009 Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study.
- [p004_c0003] • Characterized by slow and crowded trips • Busiest bus route in Brooklyn, carrying nearly 50,000 passengers per day • Crucial connection to 3 4 A C trains and 30 bus routes • Vision Zero Priority Corridor • 7 pedestrians killed and 57 pedestrians severely injured (2009-2013)

### [p009_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p009_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p009_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p009_c0004] • 15-23% faster bus speeds • About 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Customer satisfaction of 95% • Safer streets / reduction in crashes
[p009_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
