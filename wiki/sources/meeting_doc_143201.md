### [p001_c0003] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0004] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to temporarily adjust schedules to support a series of Switch Installations and Track Maintenance on the Port Jefferson Branch, ADA Station Rehabilitation at Laurelton, and Track Maintenance between Queens Village and Jamaica.
### [p001_c0005] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0006] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0007] • Port Jefferson Branch – Switch Installation and Track Maintenance – On the weekends of June 29-30, July 13-14, July 20-21, July 27-28, and August 3-4, both main tracks between Hicksville and Port Jefferson will be taken out of service for a period of approximately 48-hours to support a series of switch installations, as well as rail replacement and bridge maintenance at various locations on the Port Jefferson Branch. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With both main tracks out of service between Hicksville and Port Jefferson, bus service will replace train service for approximately 48-hours. • Atlantic Branch – ADA Station Rehabilitation – Laurelton – On the weekends of June 29-30, July 13-14, July 20-21, July 27-28, and August 3-4, both main tracks on the Atlantic Branch between Jamaica and Valley Stream will be out of service to support the ADA Accessibility Project at Laurelton. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With both main tracks out of service on the Atlantic Branch between Jamaica and Valley Stream, bus service will replace train service to/from Locust Manor, Laurelton, and Rosedale for a period of 48-hours.

## Page 2

- [p002_c0001] • Main Line – Maintenance – The local westbound track that services Elmont-UBS Arena, Queens Village, and Hollis will be taken out of service Monday through Thursday between July 8 th and July 25 th during the midday period, as track maintenance is performed. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the local westbound track out of service between Queens Village and Jamaica, NYCTA Bus will cross-honor customers at Queens Village and Hollis Stations. There will be no westbound service from Elmont-UBS Arena during these timeframes. Customers are encouraged to utilize Bellerose Station for westbound train service to Jamaica.
### [p002_c0002] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p002_c0003] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
