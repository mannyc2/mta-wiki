### [p002_c0003] MTA 2023 Strategic Priorities
### [p002_c0004] Keep bringing customers back to mass transit
### [p002_c0005] 1. Better service
- [p002_c0006] • Sustained high operational performance levels • Improved performance on targeted low-performing lines/routes • Modified schedules to better meet customer demand, including weekends
### [p002_c0007] 2. Safety and respect
- [p002_c0008] • Continued effective collaboration with police to reduce crime and disorder • Reduced fare evasion • Cameras on trains • Renewed approaches to homeless and erratic behavior in system
### [p002_c0009] 3. Increased appeal for customers
- [p002_c0010] • Customer service centers and roaming station agents • More OMNY function/benefits • Increased systemwide accessibility • Sharpened customer info and communications • Cleaner cars and stations
### [p002_c0011] 4. 21st Century bus systems
- [p002_c0012] • Dramatically increased bus speeds (with City DOT) • Queens and Brooklyn bus network redesign underway • Acceleration of electrification, zero-emission bus fleet • Fast-track depot electrical upgrades
### [p002_c0013] 5. Financial stability and viability
- [p002_c0014] • Secured long-term, recurring funding sources • Increased employee availability • Realized efficiencies in operating agencies and HQ • Maximized federal funding support • Implemented CBD tolling
### [p002_c0015] 6. Strengthening and expanding our system
- [p002_c0016] • Dynamic, prioritized management of crucial SOGR needs • Enhanced long-range planning, releasing robust 20-year Needs Assessment • Progress on Megaprojects (New Penn, Penn Station Access, SAS2) • Expanded MWBE and DBE pools • Integrated resiliency planning
### [p002_c0017] 7. Revived talent and culture
- [p002_c0018] • Stronger partnership with labor • Compensation system redesigned, with more compelling career pathways • Engaged employees • Diversity supported across the MTA • Magnet for new talent
### [p002_c0019] 8. Stronger administrative support
- [p002_c0020] • Consolidated functions reliably delivering crucial services for operating agencies and MTA staff (IT, HR, Procurement, Legal) • Hiring processes accelerated • Resilient payroll and accounting services • Improved handling of workers' compensation claims and benefits administration
