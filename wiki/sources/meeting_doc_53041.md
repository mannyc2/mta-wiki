### [p005_c0001] Transportation Modeling and Analysis
### [p005_c0002] Coding Includes
- [p005_c0003] • 61,000 network links for streets, arterials, and highways • 4,600 analysis zones across region • Models vehicle speeds, type, truck usage, and toll collection • Incorporates all regional transit service with routes, frequency, projected usage, and fares
### [p005_c0004] Model Runs (14 total scenarios)
- [p005_c0005] • No Action (1) for 2020 and 2045 (1 x 2 = 2) • NEPA Action Scenarios (6) for 2020 and 2045 (6 x 2 = 12)
### [p005_c0006] Adjustments and Assignments Based on BPM
- [p005_c0007] • Balance bridge and tunnel capacity • Identify peak hour of peak traffic periods • Assign peak hour volumes to study area intersections for all time periods • Analysis of diversions/identify worst case
### [p005_c0008] Synchro & VISSIM Models (2 scenarios)
- [p005_c0009] • Synchro: Geometric intersection analysis • 100+ Intersections • 4 time periods (AM, PM, midday, and 24-hr) • VISSIM: Highway lane capacity analysis • Multiple tunnels, bridges, and highways • Generates: VMT, changes in volumes, routes, and speeds for multiple vehicle types
