### [p007_c0001] Corridor Description
[p007_c0002] 9.3 miles from Williamsburg Bridge to Sheepshead Bay
[p007_c0003] Currently served by B44 bus route
- [p007_c0004] • 44,000 weekday riders – 5 th busiest bus route in city • 2,000 people board in CB1 • Buses travel at an average speed of 7-8 mph
[p007_c0005] Within a ¼ mile:
- [p007_c0006] • 300,000 residents • 62% of households do not own a car (69% in CB1) • 60% of residents commute by transit (58% in CB1, compared to 21% by car, truck, or van)

## Page 10

### [p010_c0001] Project Benefits
[p010_c0002] Over the length of the corridor, bus lanes, transit signal priority, and quicker boarding would lead to 25% faster trips.
[p010_c0003] A trip from Taylor St to Fulton St would be about 3 minutes faster, from 16 minutes today to 13 minutes with SBS.
[p010_c0004] More reliable service and less bus bunching.
[p010_c0005] With both local and SBS buses traveling the full route, the area north of Flushing Avenue would have more frequent bus service than today.
