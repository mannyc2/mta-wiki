### [p002_c0001] Project Need

- [p005_c0002] • High quality surface transit – faster and more reliable • Physical and operational improvements to bus service: – Low floor buses – High quality bus lanes – Prepayment fare collection – Transit signal priority – Other service enhancements • First implemented on Bx12 route – 20% travel time savings
