### [p001_c0004] KINGS HIGHWAY AT FRASER SQUARE TRAFFIC CIRCLE PEDESTRIAN AND TRAFFIC SAFETY IMPROVEMENTS
[p001_c0005] Install New Pedestrian Spaces and Bus Stops
[p001_c0006] Reconfigure Intersections and Lanes
[p001_c0007] NYC DOT will implement safety improvements on Kings Highway at E 34th Street, E 35th Street, and Avenue M:
- [p001_c0008] ▪ Shorten and install new pedestrian crossings ▪ Signal timing changes to improve traffic flow and pedestrian crossings ▪ Create more pedestrian space and safer bus stops ▪ Reconfigure intersections and lanes to better organize traffic and improve safety
[p001_c0009] Implementation begins in September
