### [p002_c0001] DRONE PROGRAM OVERVIEW
[p002_c0002] LIRR Office of Security created the agency's first Drone Program. Drones, also known as Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS), are a more efficient way to monitor and identify issues on the LIRR system. Using the latest technology, the program compliments and strengthens:
- [p002_c0003] • Emergency response • Planned incident response • Operational support • Infrastructure review
