> [p002_c0008] Service need: South Brooklyn Crosstown
[p002_c0009] The Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study (2009) identified the south Brooklyn east-west corridor as a priority service need .
[p002_c0010] Key issues raised at the 2009 public workshops included the following:
- [p002_c0011] • Bus trips are long and slow • Many parts of south Brooklyn are underserved by transit • A bus trip across south Brooklyn can take up to 2 hours
