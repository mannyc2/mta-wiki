### [p002_c0017] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0018] Key Performance Metrics Reports
[p002_c0019] The Key Performance Metrics book provides the MTA Board and members of the public with a comprehensive overview of monthly LIRR and Metro-North performance indicators including ridership, financial results, train performance, capital project progress, community events, and safety and security.
### [p002_c0020] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0021] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0022] Work Plan
[p002_c0023] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0024] President's Report
[p002_c0025] A Monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0026] Chief's Report
[p002_c0027] A monthly report will be given highlighting key operating performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0028] Safety Report
[p002_c0029] A monthly report will be given highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators
### [p002_c0030] Police Report
[p002_c0031] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.

### [p003_c0001] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0002] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0003] Information Items (if any)
[p003_c0004] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.
### [p003_c0005] Financial Report
[p003_c0006] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast.
### [p003_c0007] Procurements
[p003_c0008] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval and items for Committee and Board information. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
