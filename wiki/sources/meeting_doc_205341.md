### [p001_c0005] NON-REIMBURSABLE OVERTIME
### [p001_c0006] Agency Detail
- [p001_c0007] • NYCT: $211M spent YTD and $79M unfavorable, mainly driven by higher vacancy/absentee coverage and weather-related requirements. • LIRR: $54M spent YTD and $13M unfavorable, mainly due to higher weather-related and unscheduled maintenance requirements. • MNR: $25M spent YTD and $1M unfavorable, mainly due to higher weather-related requirements. • MTA Bus: $25M spent YTD and $1M unfavorable, mainly due to higher programmatic maintenance and weather-related requirements. • MTA HQ: $7M spent YTD and $2M unfavorable, mainly due to higher MTA PD-related safety/security/ enforcement and vacancy/absentee coverage requirements. • B&T: $9M spent YTD and $2M unfavorable, due to higher vacancy/absentee coverage and weather-related requirements. • SIR: $2M spent YTD and $0.4M unfavorable, mainly due to higher weather-related requirements.
