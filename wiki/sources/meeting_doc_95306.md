### [p002_c0018] I. RECURRING AGENDA ITEMS
### [p002_c0019] Summary of Actions (if any)
[p002_c0020] A summary is provided by agency of any procurement or action item included in the monthly agenda.
### [p002_c0021] Approval of Minutes
[p002_c0022] The Committee Chair will request a motion to approve the minutes of the prior month's meeting.
### [p002_c0023] Work Plan
[p002_c0024] The Work Plan will list, by month, the topics scheduled for review. The Committee will be advised if any changes have been made to the plan.
### [p002_c0025] President's Report
[p002_c0026] A monthly report will be provided highlighting major accomplishments and progress on key initiatives and performance indicators.
### [p002_c0027] Safety
[p002_c0028] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key safety performance statistics and indicators.
### [p002_c0029] Police Activity Report
[p002_c0030] MTA Police will highlight the significant police activities incurred during the month reported.
### [p002_c0031] Information Items (if any)
[p002_c0032] Materials presented to the Committee for review pertaining to certain agency initiatives and functions.

## Page 3

### [p003_c0001] Action Items (if any)
[p003_c0002] Staff summary documents presented to the Board for approval of items affecting business standards and practices.
### [p003_c0003] Procurements
[p003_c0004] List of procurement action items requiring Board approval. The Non-Competitive items will be first, followed by the Competitive items and then the Ratifications. The list will include items that need a 2/3 vote of the Board for approval.
### [p003_c0005] Operations
[p003_c0006] A monthly report will be provided highlighting key operating and performance statistics and indicators.
### [p003_c0007] Finance
[p003_c0008] A monthly report will be provided that compares the Railroad's actual financial performance against its budget and/or forecast both on an accrual and cash basis.
### [p003_c0009] Ridership
[p003_c0010] A monthly report will be provided that includes a comparison of actual monthly ticket sales, ridership and revenues with the budget and prior year results.
### [p003_c0011] II. SPECIFIC AGENDA ITEMS
### [p003_c0012] SEPTEMBER 2022
### [p003_c0013] 2023 Preliminary Budget
[p003_c0014] Public comment will be accepted on the 2023 Budget.
### [p003_c0015] 2022 Mid-Year Forecast
[p003_c0016] The agency will provide the 2022 Mid-Year Forecast financial information for revenue and expense by month.
### [p003_c0017] Diversity & EEO Report– 2 nd Quarter 2022
[p003_c0018] A quarterly report to the Committee providing data on key Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) and Human Resources indicators relating to MNR's EEO and Diversity efforts, such as composition of the agency's workforce, new hires, and discrimination complaints. In alternating quarters, the report will include additional information on diversity initiatives.
### [p003_c0019] OCTOBER 2022
### [p003_c0020] 2023 Preliminary Budget
[p003_c0021] Public comment will be accepted on the 2023 Budget.
### [p003_c0022] LIRR/MNR PTC Project Update
[p003_c0023] The Committee will be briefed on the status of project implementation and close-out following full Positive Train Control functionality for both railroads going into effect in December 2020.
### [p003_c0024] Track Program Quarterly Update
[p003_c0025] A quarterly report will be provided that highlights the progress made on track maintenance work to bring the infrastructure to a state of good repair.
### [p003_c0026] NOVEMBER 2022
### [p003_c0027] Progress on Way Ahead Strategic Plan
[p003_c0028] A biannual report to the Committee on Metro-North's progress in setting the standard for safety, reliability and innovation in the delivery of excellent customer service.
