### [p003_c0007] Minutes
[p003_c0008] Upon a motion duly made and seconded, the minutes of the Committee Meeting held on September 29, 2025 were approved.
### [p003_c0009] Committee Work Plan
[p003_c0010] President Sheridan stated that there are no changes to the Work Plan.
### [p003_c0011] President Sheridan's Remarks
[p003_c0012] President Sheridan began with an update on the targeted joint-agency enforcement operations, which have yielded more than 1,400 arrests, 63,000 summonses, and 5,700 vehicles towed. The operations now include the congestion relief zone. President Sheridan praised the work and dedication of the officers. President Sheridan also announced that Rockland County Sheriff's Office has now joined the task force.
[p003_c0013] President Sheridan reported that TBTA has strengthened its focus on tinted window enforcement. TBTA's officers have been equipped with new tint meters that measure compliance with the Vehicle and Traffic Law. The 2025 training initiative is nearly complete, with 89% of TBTA's law enforcement personnel trained in the use of these new meters. Through September, tinted window enforcement has increased by 24% over 2024 and 35% over 2023.
[p003_c0014] President Sheridan announced that TBTA and TransCore received three awards by the International Bridge, Tunnel and Turnpike Association ("IBTTA") for the successful Central Business District Tolling Program and the Congestion Relief Zone. TransCore received the Toll Excellence Award for Private Sector Innovation, and TBTA was awarded a Toll Excellence Award for Social Responsibility, as well as IBTTA's highest achievement, the President's Award for Excellence. President Sheridan thanked the entire team for their long and challenging work.
[p003_c0015] President Sheridan highlighted that October 15 marked the 30th anniversary of E-ZPass at the Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge, the first TBTA crossing to debut an electronic toll payment system. An exhibit curated by the Special Archive can be viewed at Grand Central Terminal's Vanderbilt Passage. President Sheridan thanked the maintenance crew for the installation and Metro-North colleagues for their logistical support.
[p003_c0016] Finally, President Sheridan noted that last week, she and other senior staff hosted the TBTA annual Safety and Employee Appreciation picnic. A team from many TBTA and C&D departments was awarded the inaugural President's Safety Award for their amazing work at the Marine Parkway Bridge.
