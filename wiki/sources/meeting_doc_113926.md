### [p001_c0004] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0005] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to adjust schedules to support summer trackwork programs.
### [p001_c0006] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0007] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0008] • Port Washington Branch – Rail Replacement, Track Surfacing – On the weekend of July 8-9, the single main track between Great Neck and Port Washington will be taken out of service for a period of approximately 48-hours while portions of the existing rail are replaced and track surfacing, track welding, and mud spot remediation are performed. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the single main track out of service between Great Neck and Port Washington, bus service will replace train service for approximately 48-hours. • Port Jefferson Branch – Concrete Tie Installation and Track Maintenance – On the weekends of July 15-16, July 22-23, July 29-30, August 5-6, August 12-13, and August 19-20 the single main track between Huntington and Port Jefferson will be taken out of service for a period of approximately 48-hours while new concrete ties are installed, and the existing rail is replaced. Installing the new concrete ties will offer greater reliability versus traditional wooden ties, and a longer lifespan, thus reducing the need for future outages. Additionally, over the course of the 6 weekends, the LIRR Engineering Department will also be upgrading switches, and performing track welding and surfacing. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the single main track out of service between Huntington and Port Jefferson, bus service will replace train service for approximately 48-hours.
### [p001_c0009] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p001_c0010] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
