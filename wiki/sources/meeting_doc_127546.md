### [p001_c0004] PURPOSE:
[p001_c0005] This is to inform the Long Island Committee of the MTA Long Island Rail Road’s plan to temporarily adjust schedules for a series of Switch Installations in West Side Yard.
### [p001_c0006] TRACK WORK PROGRAMS
[p001_c0007] Construction Activities (Short-term trackwork items requiring a special program)
- [p001_c0008] • West Side Yard – Switch Installation – On December 2-3, December 9-10, and December 16-17, all yard tracks within West Side Storage Yard, which is the primary storage facility for Penn Station, will be out of service to support a series of switch installations. Over the course of these three weekends, LIRR Engineering forces will be also piggybacking this outage to perform maintenance activities throughout the yard. ○ Temporary Service Adjustments: With the tracks in West Side Yard out of service each weekend, Far Rockaway Branch trains will be diverted from Penn Station to Grand Central Madison for a period of approximately 48-hours.
### [p001_c0009] IMPACT ON FUNDING
[p001_c0010] Funding for these projects is contained in the Long Island Rail Road Operating and MTA Capital Program budget.
