### [p003_c0001] Project Background
[p003_c0002] The M86 corridor was identified as a potential candidate for Select Bus Service in the “Bus Rapid Transit: Phase II Study” in 2009.
[p003_c0003] CB 7 resolved in favor of off-board fare collection in 2013.

### [p004_c0001] Select Bus Service in New York City
[p004_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is New York City's brand name for Bus Rapid Transit: an improved bus service that offers fast, frequent, and reliable service on high-ridership bus routes.
### [p004_c0003] SBS has brought:
- [p004_c0004] • 15-20% faster bus speeds • 10% increase in ridership • More reliable service • Improved passenger comfort and convenience
[p004_c0005] There are 7 Select Bus Service routes serving all 5 boroughs.
> [p004_c0006] A map of New York City showing the 7 Select Bus Service (SBS) routes.
