### [p003_c0001] About Select Bus Service
[p003_c0002] Select Bus Service (SBS) is NYC's brand name for a package of improvements that result in faster and more reliable bus service.
[p003_c0003] SBS also brings:
- [p003_c0004] • High customer satisfaction • Improved passenger comfort & convenience • Safer streets and sidewalks • More consistent traffic flow

### [p006_c0001] Project Background
[p006_c0002] The Bus Rapid Transit Phase II Study (2009) identified the South Brooklyn east-west corridor as a priority service need .
[p006_c0003] Key issues raised at public workshops included:
- [p006_c0004] • Bus trips are long and slow • Many parts of South Brooklyn are underserved by transit • A bus trip across South Brooklyn can take up to 2 hours
