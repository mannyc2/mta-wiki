- [p011_p0004] •     Add 11’ super sidewalk on the west curb
- [p011_p0005] • A super sidewalk is a painted extension of the sidewalk
- [p011_p0006] • Provides extra space for pedestrians on crowded and narrow sidewalk
- [p011_p0007] •     Add 9’ parking protected bike lane on the west curb
- [p011_p0008] • Provides safer dedicated space for cyclists that is separated from moving vehicles
- [p011_p0009] •     Add 11’ bus only lane
- [p011_p0010] • Provides dedicated space for buses to increase bus speeds and lower travel time for bus riders
